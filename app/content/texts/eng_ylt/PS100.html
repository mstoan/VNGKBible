100
1 A Psalm of Thanksgiving. Shout to Jehovah, all the earth. 2 Serve Jehovah with joy, come before him with singing. 3 Know that Jehovah He [is] God, He made us, and we are His, His people -- and the flock of His pasture. 4 Enter ye His gates with thanksgiving, His courts with praise, Give ye thanks to Him, bless ye His Name. 5 For good [is] Jehovah, to the age His kindness, And to generation and generation His faithfulness!
