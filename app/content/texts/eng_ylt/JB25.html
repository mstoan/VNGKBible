25
1 And Bildad the Shuhite answereth and saith: -- 2 The rule and fear [are] with Him, Making peace in His high places. 3 Is their [any] number to His troops? And on whom ariseth not His light? 4 And what? is man righteous with God? And what? is he pure -- born of a woman? 5 Lo -- unto the moon, and it shineth not, And stars have not been pure in His eyes. 6 How much less man -- a grub, And the son of man -- a worm!
