93
1 Jehovah hath reigned, Excellency He hath put on, Jehovah put on strength, He girded Himself, Also -- established is the world, unmoved. 2 Established is Thy throne since then, From the age Thou [art]. 3 Floods have lifted up, O Jehovah, Floods have lifted up their voice, Floods lift up their breakers. 4 Than the voices of many mighty waters, Breakers of a sea, mighty on high [is] Jehovah, 5 Thy testimonies have been very stedfast, To Thy house comely [is] holiness, O Jehovah, for length of days!
