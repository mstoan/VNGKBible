1  Và Naomi had a kinsman of her husband's, a mighty man of wealth, of the gia đình of Elimelech; Và his name was Boaz. 1 2  Và Ruth the Moabitess nói unto Naomi, Let me now go to the field, Và glean ears of corn after him in whose sight I shall find ân-điển. Và she nói unto her, Go, my daughter. 3  Và she went, Và came, Và gleaned in the field after the reapers: Và her hap was to light on a part of the field belonging unto Boaz, who was of the kindred of Elimelech. 2 4  And, kìa, Boaz đến from Beth–lehem, và nói unto the reapers, The CHÚA be with you. And Họ trả lời him, The CHÚA bless ngươi. 5  Then nói Boaz unto his servant that was set over the reapers, Whose damsel is this? 6  Và the servant that was set over the reapers trả lời và nói, It is the Moabitish damsel that came back with Naomi out of the country of Moab: 7  Và she nói, I pray you, let me glean Và gather after the reapers among the sheaves: so she came, Và hath continued even from the morning until now, that she tarried a little in nhà. 8  Then nói Boaz unto Ruth, Hearest ngươi not, my daughter? đi not to glean in another field, neither go from hence, but abide here fast by my maidens: 9  Let thine eyes be on the field that they do reap, Và go ngươi after them: have I not charged the young men that they shall not touch ngươi? and when ngươi art athirst, đi unto the vessels, Và drink of that which the young men have drawn. 10  Then she fell on her face, Và bowed herself to the ground, và nói unto him, Tại sao have I found ân-điển in thine eyes, that ngươi shouldest take knowledge of me, seeing I là a stranger? 11  Và Boaz trả lời và nói unto her, It hath fully been shewed me, all that ngươi hast done unto thy mother in law since the death of thine husband: and how ngươi hast left thy father Và thy mother, Và the land of thy nativity, Và art come unto a people which ngươi knewest not heretofore. 12  The CHÚA recompense thy work, Và a full phần thưởng be given ngươi of the CHÚA ĐCT của I-sơ-ra-ên, under whose wings ngươi art come to trust. 13  Then she nói, Let me find favour in thy sight, my lord; for that ngươi hast comforted me, and for that ngươi hast spoken friendly unto thine handmaid, though I be not like unto one of thine handmaidens. 3 4 14  Và Boaz nói unto her, At mealtime đến ngươi hither, Và eat of the bread, Và dip thy morsel in the vinegar. Và she sat beside the reapers: Và he reached her parched bắp, Và she did eat, Và was sufficed, Và left. 15  Và when she was risen up to glean, Boaz truyền lệnh his young men, nói [rằng], Let her glean even among the sheaves, Và reproach her not: 5 16  Và let fall also some of tayfuls of purpose for her, and leave họ, that she may glean họ, Và rebuke her not. 17  So she gleaned in the field until even, Và beat out that she had gleaned: Và it was about an ephah of barley. 18  Và she took it up, Và went into the city: Và her mother in law nhìn thấy what she had gleaned: Và she brought forth, Và gave to her that she had reserved after she was sufficed. 19  Và her mother in law nói unto her, Where hast ngươi gleaned to day? Và where wroughtest thou? blessed be he that did take knowledge of ngươi. And she shewed her mother in law with whom she had wrought, và nói, The man's name with whom I wrought to day is Boaz. 20  Và Naomi nói unto her daughter in law, phước be he of the CHÚA, who hath not left off his kindness to the living Và to kẻ chết. Và Naomi nói unto her, The man is near of kin unto us, one of our next kinsmen. 6 21  Và Ruth the Moabitess nói, He nói unto me also, ngươi shalt keep fast by my young men, until they have ended all my harvest. 22  Và Naomi nói unto Ruth her daughter in law, It is tốt, my daughter, that ngươi go out with his maidens, that they meet ngươi not in any other field. 7 23  So she kept fast by the maidens of Boaz to glean unto the end of barley harvest Và of wheat harvest; Và dwelt with her mother in law.
