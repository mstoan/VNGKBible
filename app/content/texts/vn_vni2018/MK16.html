16
1 Và when the sabbath was past, Ma-ri Mác-đơ-len, Và Ma-ri the mẹ of Gia-cơ, Và Salome, had bought sweet spices, that they might come Và anoint him. 2 Và very early in the morning the first day of the week, they came unto phần mộ at the rising of mặt trời. 3 Và they nói among themselves, Who shall roll us away the stone from the door of phần mộ? 4 Và when they looked, they saw that the stone was rolled away: for it was very great. 5 Và entering into phần mộ, they saw a young man sitting on the right side, clothed in a long white garment; Và they were affrighted. 6 Và he nói unto them, Be not affrighted: Ye seek ĐCGiê-su of Nazareth, which was crucified: Ngài đã sống lại; he is not here: kìa the place where they laid him. 7 But go your way, tell his disciples Và Phi-e-rơ that he goeth before you into Ga-li-lê: there họ sẽ thấy him, as he nói unto you. 8 Và they went out quickly, Và fled from phần mộ; for they trembled Và were amazed: neither nói they any thing to any man; for they were afraid.
9 Now when Giê-su was risen early the first day of the week, he appeared first to Mary Mác-đơ-len, out of whom he had cast seven devils. 10 Và she went Và told them that had been with him, as they mourned Và wept. 11 Và they, when they had heard that he was alive, Và had been seen of her, believed not.
12 After that he appeared in another form unto two of them, as they walked, Và went into the country. 13 Và they went Và told it unto the residue: neither believed they them.
14 Afterward he appeared unto the eleven as they sat at meat, Và upbraided them with their unbelief Và hardness of heart, because they believed not them which had seen him after he was risen. 15 Và he nói unto them, Go ye into all the world, Và preach phúc-âm to every creature. 16 He that believeth Và is baptized shall được cứu; but he that believeth not shall be damned. 17 Và these signs shall follow them that believe; In my name shall they cast out devils; they shall speak with new tongues; 18 They shall take up serpents; Và if they drink any deadly thing, it shall not hurt them; they shall lay hands on the sick, Và they shall recover.
19 So then after the Lord had spoken unto them, he was received up into heaven, Và sat on the right hand của ĐCT. 20 Và they went forth, Và preached every where, the Lord working with them, Và confirming the word with signs following. Amen.
