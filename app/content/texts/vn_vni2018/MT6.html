6
1 Take heed that ye do not your alms before men, to be seen of them: otherwise ye have no reward of your Father which is in heaven. 2 Therefore when thou doest thine alms, do not sound a trumpet before thee, as the hypocrites do in the các nhà hội Và in the streets, that they may have glory of men. Quả thật I say unto you, They have their reward. 3 But when thou doest alms, let not thy left hand know what thy right hand doeth: 4 That thine alms may be in secret: Và thy Father which seeth in secret himself shall reward thee openly.
5 Và when thou prayest, thou shalt not be as the hypocrites are: for they love to pray standing in the các nhà hội Và in the corners of the streets, that they may be seen of men. Quả thật I say unto you, They have their reward. 6 But thou, when thou prayest, enter into thy closet, Và when thou hast shut thy door, pray to thy Father which is in secret; Và thy Father which seeth in secret shall reward thee openly. 7 But when ye pray, use not vain repetitions, as the heathen do: for they think that they shall be heard for their much speaking. 8 Be not ye therefore like unto them: for your Father knoweth what things ye have need of, before ye ask him. 9 After this manner therefore pray ye: Our Father which art in heaven, Hallowed be thy name. 10 Thy kingdom come. Thy will be done in đất, as it is in heaven. 11 Give us this day our daily bread. 12 Và forgive us our debts, as chúng ta forgive our debtors. 13 Và lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil: For thine is the kingdom, Và the power, Và the glory, for ever. Amen. 14 For nếu ye forgive men their trespasses, your heavenly Father will also forgive you: 15 But nếu ye forgive not men their trespasses, neither will your Father forgive your trespasses.
16 Moreover when ye fast, be not, as the hypocrites, of a sad countenance: for they disfigure their faces, that they may appear unto men to fast. Quả thật I say unto you, They have their reward. 17 But thou, when thou fastest, anoint thine head, Và wash thy face; 18 That thou appear not unto men to fast, but unto thy Father which is in secret: Và thy Father, which seeth in secret, shall reward thee openly.
19 Lay not up for yourselves treasures upon đất, where moth Và rust doth corrupt, Và where thieves break through Và steal: 20 But lay up for yourselves treasures in heaven, where neither moth nor rust doth corrupt, Và where thieves do not break through nor steal: 21 For where your treasure is, there will your heart be also. 22 The light of the body is the eye: nếu therefore thine eye be single, thy whole body shall be full of light. 23 But nếu thine eye be evil, thy whole body shall be full of darkness. nếu therefore the light that is in thee be darkness, how great is that darkness!
24 không ai can serve two masters: for either he will hate the one, Và love the other; or else he will hold to the one, Và despise the other. Ye cannot serve ĐCT Và mammon. 25 Therefore I say unto you, Take no thought for your life, what ye shall eat, or what ye shall drink; nor yet for your body, what ye shall put on. Is not the life more than meat, Và the body than raiment? 26 kìa the fowls of the air: for they sow not, neither do they reap, nor gather into barns; yet your heavenly Father feedeth them. Are ye not much better than they? 27 Which of you by taking thought can add một cubit unto his stature? 28 Và why take ye thought for raiment? Consider the lilies of the field, how they grow; they toil not, neither do they spin: 29 Và yet I say unto you, That even Solomon in all his glory was not arrayed like một of these. 30 Wherefore, nếu ĐCT so clothe the grass of the field, which to day is, Và to morrow is cast into the oven, shall he not much more clothe you, O ye of little faith? 31 Therefore take no thought, saying, What shall we eat? or, What shall we drink? or, Wherewithal shall we be clothed? 32 (For after all these things do the Gentiles seek:) for your heavenly Father knoweth that ye have need of all these things. 33 But seek ye first the kingdom của ĐCT, Và his righteousness; Và all these things shall be added unto you. 34 Take therefore no thought for the morrow: for the morrow shall take thought for the things of itself. Sufficient unto the day is the evil thereof.
