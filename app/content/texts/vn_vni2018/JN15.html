15
1  Ta là gốc nho thật, và Cha Ta là người trồng [nho]. 2  Mọi nhánh [nào] trong me that beareth not fruit he taketh away: Và every branch that beareth fruit, he purgeth it, that it may bring forth more fruit. 3 bây giờ ye are clean through the word which I have spoken unto you. 4 Hãy cứ ở trong Ta và Ta trong các ngươi. As the branch cannot bear fruit of itself, except it abide in the vine; no more can ye, except ye abide in me. 5 I am the vine, ye are the branches: He that abideth in me, Và I in him, the same bringeth forth much fruit: for without me ye can do nothing. 6 nếu một người abide not in me, he is cast forth as a branch, Và is withered; Và men gather them, Và cast them into the fire, Và they are burned. 7 nếu ye abide in me, Và my words abide in you, ye shall ask what ye will, Và it shall be done unto you. 8 Herein is my Father glorified, that ye bear much fruit; so shall ye be my các môn đệ. 9 As the Father hath loved me, so have I loved you: continue ye in my love. 10 nếu ye keep my commandments, ye shall abide in my love; even as I have kept my Father's commandments, Và abide in his love. 11 These things have I spoken unto you, that my joy might remain in you, Và that your joy might be full. 12 This is my commandment, That ye love one another, as I have loved you. 13 Greater love hath không ai than this, that một người lay down his sự sống for his friends. 14 Ye are my friends, nếu ye do whatsoever I command you. 15 Henceforth I call you not servants; for the servant knoweth not what his lord doeth: Nhưng I have called you friends; for all things that I have heard of my Father I have made known unto you. 16 Ye have not chosen me, Nhưng I have chosen you, Và ordained you, that ye should go Và bring forth fruit, Và that your fruit should remain: that whatsoever ye shall ask of the Father in my tên, he may give it you. 17 These things I command you, that ye love one another. 18 nếu thế gian hate you, ye know that it hated me before it hated you. 19 nếu ye were of thế gian, thế gian would love his own: Nhưng because ye are not of thế gian, Nhưng I have chosen you out of thế gian, therefore thế gian hateth you. 20 Remember the word that I nói unto you, The servant is not greater than his lord. nếu they have persecuted me, they will also persecute you; nếu they have kept my Nói rằng, they will keep yours also. 21 Nhưng all these things will they do unto you for my name's sake, because they know not him that sent me. 22 nếu I had not come Và spoken unto them, they had not had sin: Nhưng bây giờ they have no cloke for their sin. 23 He that hateth me hateth my Father also. 24 nếu I had not done among them the works which none other man did, they had not had sin: Nhưng bây giờ have they both seen Và hated both me Và my Father. 25 Nhưng this cometh to pass, that the word might be fulfilled that is written in their law, They hated me without a cause. 26 Nhưng when the Comforter is come, whom I will send unto you from the Father, even the Spirit of truth, which proceedeth from the Father, he shall testify of me: 27 Và ye also shall bear witness, because ye have been with me from the beginning.
