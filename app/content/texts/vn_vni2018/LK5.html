5
1 Và xảy đến, that, as the people pressed upon him to hear the word của ĐCT, he stood by the lake of Gennesaret, 2 Và saw two ships standing by the lake: but the fishermen were gone out of them, and were washing their nets. 3 Và he entered into một of the ships, which was Si-môn's, Và prayed him that he would thrust out a little from the land. Và he sat down, Và taught the people out of the ship. 4 Now when he had left speaking, he said unto Si-môn, Launch out into the deep, Và let down your nets for a draught. 5 Và Si-môn answering said unto him, Master, we have toiled all the night, Và have taken nothing: nevertheless at thy word I will let down the net. 6 Và when they had this done, they inclosed a great multitude of fishes: Và their net brake. 7 Và they beckoned unto their partners, which were in the other ship, that they should come Và help them. Và they came, Và filled both the ships, đến đổi they began to sink. 8 When Si-môn Phi-e-rơ saw it, he fell down at Jesus' knees, saying, Depart from me; for I am a sinful man, O Lord. 9 For he was astonished, Và all that were with him, at the draught of the fishes which they had taken: 10 Và so was also Gia-cơ, Và Giăng, the sons of Xê-bê-đê, which were partners with Si-môn. Và ĐCGiê-su said unto Si-môn, Fear not; from henceforth thou shalt catch men. 11 Và when they had brought their ships to land, they forsook all, Và followed him.
12 Và xảy đến, when he was in a certain city, kìa a man full of leprosy: who seeing ĐCGiê-su rơi on his face, Và besought him, saying, Lord, nếu thou wilt, thou canst make me clean. 13 Và he put forth his hand, Và touched him, saying, I will: be thou clean. Và tức thì the leprosy departed from him. 14 Và he charged him to tell không ai: but go, Và shew thyself to the priest, Và offer for thy cleansing, according as Môi-se commanded, for a testimony unto them. 15 But so much the more went there a fame abroad of him: Và great multitudes came together to hear, Và to be healed by him of their infirmities.
16 Và he withdrew himself into the wilderness, Và prayed. 17 Và xảy đến on a certain day, as he was teaching, that there were những người Pha-ri-si Và doctors of the law sitting by, which were come out of every town of Ga-li-lê, Và xứ Giu-đê, Và Giê-ru-sa-lem: Và the power của Chúa was present to heal them.
18 Và, kìa, men brought in a bed a man which was taken with a palsy: Và they sought means to bring him in, Và to lay him before him. 19 Và when they could not find by what way they might bring him in because of đám đông, they went upon nhàtop, Và let him down through the tiling with his couch into the midst before ĐCGiê-su. 20 Và when he saw their faith, he said unto him, Man, thy tội lỗi are forgiven thee. 21 Và những thầy chép luật Và the những người Pha-ri-si began to reason, saying, Who is this which speaketh blasphemies? Who can forgive tội lỗi, but ĐCT alone? 22 But when ĐCGiê-su perceived their thoughts, he answering said unto them, What reason ye in your hearts? 23 Whether is easier, to say, Thy tội lỗi be forgiven thee; or to say, Rise up Và walk? 24 But that ye may know that the Son of man hath power upon đất to forgive tội lỗi, (he said unto the sick of the palsy,) I say unto thee, Hãy chổi dậy, Và take up thy couch, Và go into thine nhà. 25 Và tức thì he rose up before them, Và took up that whereon he lay, Và departed to his own nhà, glorifying ĐCT. 26 Và they were all amazed, Và they glorified ĐCT, Và were filled with fear, saying, We have seen strange things to day.
27 Và after these things he went forth, Và saw a publican, named Levi, sitting at the receipt of custom: Và he said unto him, Follow me. 28 Và he left all, rose up, Và followed him. 29 Và Levi made him a great feast in his own nhà: Và there was a great company of những người quan thuế Và of others that sat down with them. 30 But their scribes Và những người Pha-ri-si murmured against his disciples, saying, Why do ye eat Và drink with những người quan thuế Và sinners? 31 Và ĐCGiê-su answering said unto them, They that are whole need not a physician; but they that are sick. 32 I came not to call the righteous, but sinners to repentance.
33 Và they said unto him, Why do các môn đệ of John fast often, Và make prayers, Và likewise các môn đệ of the những người Pha-ri-si; but thine eat Và drink? 34 Và he said unto them, Can ye make the children of the bridechamber fast, while the bridegroom is with them? 35 But the days will come, when the bridegroom shall be taken away from them, Và then shall they fast in those days.
36 Và he spake also a parable unto them; không ai putteth a piece of a new garment upon an old; nếu otherwise, then both the new maketh a rent, Và the piece that was taken out of the new agreeth not with the old. 37 Và không ai putteth new wine into old bottles; else the new wine will burst the bottles, Và be spilled, Và the bottles shall perish. 38 But new wine must be put into new bottles; Và both are preserved. 39 không ai also having drunk old wine tức thì desireth new: for he saith, The old is better.
