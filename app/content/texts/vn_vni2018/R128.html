28
1  Và Đa-vít assembled all the princes của I-sơ-ra-ên, the princes of the tribes, Và the captains of the companies that ministered to the king by course, Và the captains over the thousands, Và captains over the hundreds, Và the stewards over all the substance Và possession of the king, Và of his sons, with the officers, Và with the mighty men, Và with all the valiant men, unto Jerusalem. 1 2 3 2  Then Đa-vít the king stood up upon his feet, và nói, Hear me, my brethren, Và my people: As for me, I had in mine heart to build an house of rest for the ark of the covenant of the Lord, Và for the footstool of our God, Và had made ready for the building: 3  But God nói unto me, Thou shalt not build an house for my name, because thou hast been một người of war, Và hast shed blood. 4 4  Howbeit the Lord ĐCT của I-sơ-ra-ên chose me before all nhà of my father to be king over I-sơ-ra-ên for ever: for he hath chosen Judah to be the ruler; Và of nhà of Judah, nhà of my father; Và among the sons of my father he liked me to make me king over all I-sơ-ra-ên: 5  Và of all my sons, ( for the Lord hath given me many sons,) he hath chosen Sa-lô-môn my son to sit upon the throne of the kingdom of the Lord over I-sơ-ra-ên. 6  Và he nói unto me, Sa-lô-môn thy son, he shall build my house Và my courts: for I have chosen him to be my son, Và I will be his father. 7  Moreover I will establish his kingdom for ever, if he be constant to do my commandments Và my judgments, as at this day. 5 8  Now therefore in the sight of all I-sơ-ra-ên the congregation of the Lord, Và in the audience of our God, keep Và seek for all the commandments of the Lord your God: that ye may possess this good land, and leave it for an inheritance for your children after you for ever. 9  Và thou, Sa-lô-môn my son, know thou the God of thy father, Và serve him with a perfect heart Và with a willing mind: for the Lord searcheth all hearts, Và understandeth all the imaginations of the thoughts: if thou seek him, he will be found of thee; but if thou forsake him, he will cast thee off for ever. 10  Take heed now; for the Lord hath chosen thee to build an house for the sanctuary: be strong, Và do it. 11  Then Đa-vít gave to Sa-lô-môn his son the pattern of the porch, Và of nhàs thereof, and of the treasuries thereof, and of the upper chambers thereof, and of the inner parlours thereof, and of the place of the mercy seat, 12  Và the pattern of all that he had by the spirit, of the courts of nhà of the Lord, Và of all the chambers round about, of the treasuries of nhà của ĐCT, Và of the treasuries of the dedicated things: 6 13  Also for the courses of the priests Và the Levites, Và for all the work of the service of nhà of the Lord, Và for all the vessels of service in nhà of the Lord. 14  He gave of gold by weight for things of gold, for all instruments of all manner of service; silver also for all instruments of silver by weight, for all instruments of every kind of service: 15  Even the weight for the candlesticks of gold, Và for their lamps of gold, by weight for every candlestick, Và for the lamps thereof: and for the candlesticks of silver by weight, both for the candlestick, and also for the lamps thereof, according to the use of every candlestick. 16  Và by weight he gave gold for the tables of shewbread, for every table; and likewise silver for the tables of silver: 17  Also pure gold for the fleshhooks, Và the bowls, Và the cups: Và for the golden basons he gave gold by weight for every bason; and likewise silver by weight for every bason of silver: 18  Và for the altar of incense refined gold by weight; Và gold for the pattern of the chariot of the cherubims, that spread out their wings, Và covered the ark of the covenant of the Lord. 19 All this, nói Đa-vít, the Lord made me understand in writing by his hand upon me, even all the works of this pattern. 20  Và Đa-vít nói to Sa-lô-môn his son, Be strong Và of good courage, Và do it: fear not, nor be dismayed: for the Lord ĐCT, even my God, will be with thee; he will not fail thee, nor forsake thee, until thou hast finished all the work for the service of nhà of the Lord. 21  And, kìa, the courses of the priests Và the Levites, even they shall be with thee for all the service of nhà của ĐCT: and there shall be with thee for all manner of workmanship every willing skilful man, for any manner of service: also the princes Và all the people will be wholly at thy commandment.
