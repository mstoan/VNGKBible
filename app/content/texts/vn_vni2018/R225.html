25
1  Amaziah was hai mươi Và five years old khi he began to reign, Và he reigned hai mươi Và nine years in Giê-ru-sa-lem. Và his mother's name was Jehoaddan of Giê-ru-sa-lem. 2  Và he did that which was right in the sight of the Lord, but not with a perfect heart. 3  Now xảy đến, when the kingdom was established to him, that he slew his servants that had killed vua his father. 1 4  But he slew not their children, but did as it is written in the law in the book of Môi-se, where the Lord commanded, saying, The fathers shall not die for the children, neither shall the children chết for the fathers, but every man shall die for his own sin. 5  Moreover Amaziah gathered Giu-đa together, Và made them captains over thousands, Và captains over hundreds, according to nhàs of their fathers, throughout all Giu-đa Và Bên-gia-min: Và he numbered them from hai mươi years old Và above, Và found them three trăm ngàn choice men, able to go forth to war, that could handle spear Và shield. 6  He hired also an hundred ngàn mighty men of valour out của I-sơ-ra-ên for an hundred talents of silver. 7  But there came một người của ĐCT to him, saying, O king, let not the army của I-sơ-ra-ên go with thee; for the Lord is not with I-sơ-ra-ên, to wit, with all the children of Ephraim. 8  But if thou wilt go, do it, be strong for the battle: ĐCT shall make thee fall before the enemy: for God hath power to help, Và to cast down. 9  Và Amaziah nói to the man của ĐCT, But what shall we do for the hundred talents which I have given to the army của I-sơ-ra-ên? Và the man của ĐCT trả lời, The Lord is able to give thee much more than this. 2 10  Then Amaziah separated them, to wit, the army that was come to him out of Ephraim, to go home again: wherefore their anger was greatly kindled against Giu-đa, Và they returned home in great anger. 3 4 11  Và Amaziah strengthened himself, and led forth his people, Và went to the valley of salt, Và smote of the children of Seir mười ngàn. 12 Và other mười ngàn left alive did the children of Giu-đa carry away captive, Và brought them unto the top of the rock, Và cast them down from the top of the rock, that they all were broken in pieces. 13  But quân lính of the army which Amaziah sent back, that they should not go with him to battle, rơi upon the cities of Giu-đa, from Sa-ma-ri even unto Beth–horon, Và smote ba ngàn of them, and took much spoil. 5 14  Now xảy đến, after that Amaziah was come from the slaughter of the Edomites, that he brought the gods of the children of Seir, Và set them up to be his gods, Và bowed down himself before them, and burned incense với họ. 15  Wherefore the anger of the Lord was kindled against Amaziah, Và he sent unto him a prophet, which nói unto him, Tại sao hast thou sought after the gods of the people, which could not deliver their own people out of thine hand? 16  Và xảy đến, as he talked with him, that vua nói unto him, Art thou made of the king's counsel? forbear; why shouldest thou be smitten? Then the prophet forbare, và nói, I know that God hath determined to destroy thee, because thou hast done this, and hast not hearkened unto my counsel. 6 17  Then Amaziah king of Giu-đa took advice, Và sent to Joash, the son of Jehoahaz, the son of Jehu, king của I-sơ-ra-ên, saying, Come, let us see lẫn nhau in the face. 18  Và Joash king của I-sơ-ra-ên sent to Amaziah king of Giu-đa, saying, The thistle that was in Lebanon sent to the cedar that was in Lebanon, saying, Give thy daughter to my son to wife: Và there passed by a wild beast that was in Lebanon, Và trode down the thistle. 7 8 19  Thou sayest, Lo, thou hast smitten the Edomites; Và thine heart lifteth thee up to boast: abide now at home; why shouldest thou meddle to thine hurt, that thou shouldest fall, even thou, and Giu-đa with thee? 20  But Amaziah would not hear; for it đến của ĐCT, that he might deliver them into tay of their enemies, because they sought after the gods of Edom. 21  So Joash vua của I-sơ-ra-ên went up; Và they saw lẫn nhau in the face, both he and Amaziah king of Giu-đa, at Beth–shemesh, which belongeth to Giu-đa. 22  Và Giu-đa was put to the worse before I-sơ-ra-ên, Và they fled every man to his tent. 9 23  Và Joash vua của I-sơ-ra-ên took Amaziah king of Giu-đa, the son of Joash, the son of Jehoahaz, at Beth–shemesh, Và brought him to Giê-ru-sa-lem, Và brake down the wall of Giê-ru-sa-lem from cổng of Ephraim to the corner gate, bốn trăm cubits. 10 24 Và he took all the gold Và the silver, Và all the vessels that were found in nhà của ĐCT with Obed–edom, Và the treasures of the king's nhà, the hostages also, and returned to Sa-ma-ri. 25  Và Amaziah the son of Joash king of Giu-đa lived after the death of Joash son of Jehoahaz king của I-sơ-ra-ên mười lăm years. 26  Now the rest of the acts of Amaziah, first Và last, kìa, are they not written in the book of the kings of Giu-đa Và I-sơ-ra-ên? 27  Now after the time that Amaziah did turn away from following the Lord they made a conspiracy against him in Giê-ru-sa-lem; Và he fled to Lachish: but they sent to Lachish after him, and slew him there. 11 12 28  Và they brought him upon horses, Và buried him with his fathers in the city of Giu-đa. 13
