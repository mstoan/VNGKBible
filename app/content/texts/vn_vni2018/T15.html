5
1  Rebuke không an elder, Nhưng intreat him as a father; Và the younger men as anh em; 2  The elder women as mothers; the younger as sisters, với all purity. 3  Honour widows that are widows indeed. 4  Nhưng nếu any bà góa have children hay nephews, let them learn first to shew piety at home, Và to requite their parents: for that is good Và acceptable before ĐCT. 5  bây giờ she that is một bà góa indeed, Và desolate, trusteth in ĐCT, Và continueth in supplications Và prayers night Và ngày. 6  Nhưng she that liveth in pleasure is dead while she liveth. 7  Và these things give in charge, that they may be blameless. 8  Nhưng nếu any provide không for his own, Và specially for those of his own house, he hath denied the faith, Và is worse than an infidel. 9  Let không một bà góa be taken into the number under sáu mươi tuổi, having been the wife of one man, 10  Well reported of for good works; nếu she have brought up children, nếu she have lodged strangers, nếu she have washed the saints' chân, nếu she have relieved the afflicted, nếu she have diligently đi theo every good work. 11  Nhưng the younger widows refuse: for khi they have begun to wax wanton against Christ, they will marry; 12  Having damnation, vì they have cast off their first đức tin. 13  Và withal they learn to be idle, wandering about from house to house; Và không only idle, Nhưng tattlers also Và busybodies, speaking things which they ought không. 14  I will therefore that the younger women marry, bear children, guide nhà, give none occasion to the adversary to speak reproachfully. 15  For some are already turned aside after Satan. 16  nếu any man hay woman that believeth have widows, let them relieve họ, Và let không hội thánh be charged; that it may relieve them that are widows indeed. 17  Let các trưởng lão that rule well be counted worthy of double honour, especially they who labour in the word Và doctrine. 18  For the scripture nói, Thou shalt không muzzle the ox that treadeth out the corn. Và, The labourer is xúng đáng of his phần thưởng. 19  Against an elder receive không an accusation, Nhưng before hai hay ba witnesses. 20  Them that sin rebuke before all, that others also may fear. 21  I charge thee before ĐCT, Và the Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ, Và the elect các thiên sứ, that thou observe these things without preferring one before another, doing nothing by partiality. 22  Lay hands bất chợt on không ai, neither be partaker of other men's tội lỗi: keep thyself pure. 23  uống no longer nước, Nhưng use a little rượu nho for thy stomach's sake Và thine often infirmities. 24  Some men's tội lỗi are open beforehand, going before to judgment; Và some men they follow after. 25  Likewise also the good works of some are manifest beforehand; Và they that are otherwise có thể không be hid.
