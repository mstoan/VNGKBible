2
1 Therefore we ought to give the more earnest heed to the things which we have heard, lest at any time we should let them slip. 2 For nếu the word spoken by các thiên sứ was stedfast, Và every transgression Và disobedience received a just recompence of reward; 3 How shall chúng ta escape, if we neglect so great salvation; which at the first began to be spoken by the Lord, and was confirmed unto us by them that heard him; 4 God also bearing them witness, both with signs Và wonders, Và with divers miracles, Và gifts of the Holy Ghost, according to his own will? 5 For unto các thiên sứ hath he not put in subjection the world to come, whereof we speak. 6 Nhưng một in a certain place testified, saying, What is man, that thou art mindful of him? hay the son of man, that thou visitest him? 7 Thou madest him a little lower than các thiên sứ; thou crownedst him with glory Và honour, Và didst set him over the works of thy hands: 8 Thou hast put all things in subjection under his feet. For in that he put all in subjection under him, he left nothing that is not put under him. Nhưng bây giờ we see not yet all things put under him. 9 Nhưng we see Jesus, who was made a little lower than các thiên sứ for the suffering of death, crowned with glory Và honour; that he by the grace của ĐCT should taste death for every man. 10 For it became him, for whom are all things, Và by whom are all things, in bringing many sons unto glory, to make the captain of their salvation perfect through sufferings. 11 For both he that sanctifieth Và they who are sanctified are all of một: for which cause he is not ashamed to call them anh em, 12 Saying, I will declare thy name unto my anh em, in the midst của hội thánh will I sing praise unto thee. 13 Và again, I will put my trust in him. Và again, kìa I Và the children which ĐCT hath given me. 14 Forasmuch then as the children are partakers of flesh Và blood, he also himself likewise took part of the same; that through death he might destroy him that had the power of death, that is, the devil; 15 Và deliver them who through fear of death were all their lifetime subject to bondage. 16 For Quả thật he took not on him the nature of các thiên sứ; Nhưng he took on him the seed of Áp-ra-ham. 17 Wherefore in all things it behoved him to be made like unto his anh em, that he might be a merciful Và faithful high priest in things pertaining to ĐCT, to make reconciliation for the tội lỗi of the people. 18 For in that he himself hath suffered being tempted, he is able to succour them that are tempted.
