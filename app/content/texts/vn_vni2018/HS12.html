12
1  Ép-ra-im feedeth on wind, Và followeth after the east wind: he daily increaseth lies Và desolation; Và they do make a covenant with the Assyrians, Và oil is carried into Ai-Cập. 2  The CHÚA hath also a controversy with Giu-đa, Và will punish Gia-cốp according to his ways; according to his doings will he recompense him. 1 3  He took his brother by the heel in the womb, Và by his strength he had power with God: 2 4  Yea, he had power over thiên sứ, Và prevailed: he wept, Và made supplication unto him: he found him trong Beth–el, Và there he spake with us; 5  Even the CHÚA ĐCT of hosts; the CHÚA is his memorial. 6  Therefore turn ngươi to thy God: keep mercy Và judgment, Và wait on thy God continually. 7  He is a merchant, the balances of deceit are in his hand: he loveth to oppress. 3 4 8  Và Ephraim nói, Yet I am become rich, I have found me out substance: trong all my labours they shall find none iniquity in me that were sin. 5 6 9 Và I that am the CHÚA thy God from the land of Ai-Cập will yet make ngươi to dwell in tabernacles, as trong ngàys of the solemn feast. 10  I have also spoken by các tiên tri, Và I have multiplied visions, Và used similitudes, by the ministry of các tiên tri. 7 11  Is there iniquity trong Gilead? surely they are hư không: they sacrifice bullocks in Gilgal; yea, their altars are as heaps in the furrows of the fields. 12  Và Gia-cốp fled into the country of Si-ri, Và I-sơ-ra-ên served for a wife, Và for a wife he kept chiên. 13  Và by a prophet the CHÚA brought I-sơ-ra-ên out of Ai-Cập, Và by a prophet was he preserved. 14  Ép-ra-im provoked him to anger most bitterly: therefore shall he leave his blood upon him, and his reproach shall his Lord return unto him. 8 9
