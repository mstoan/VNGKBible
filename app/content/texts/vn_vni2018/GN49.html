49
1  Và Gia-cốp called unto his sons, và nói, Gather yourselves together, that I may tell you that which shall befall you in cuối cùng ngày. 2  Gather yourselves together, Và hear, ye sons of Gia-cốp; Và hearken unto I-sơ-ra-ên your father. 3  Reuben, thou art my firstborn, my might, Và the beginning of my strength, the excellency of dignity, Và the excellency of power: 4  Unstable as water, ngươi shalt not excel; because ngươi wentest up to thy father's bed; then làm dơ dáydst thou it: he went up to my couch. 1 2 5  Si-mê-ôn Và Levi are anh em; instruments of cruelty are in their habitations. 3 6  O my soul, đến not ngươi into their secret; unto their assembly, mine honour, be not ngươi united: for in their anger they slew một người, Và in their selfwill they digged down a wall. 4 7  Cursed be their anger, for it was fierce; Và their wrath, for it was cruel: I will divide them in Gia-cốp, Và scatter them in I-sơ-ra-ên. 8  Giu-đa, thou art he whom thy brethren shall praise: thy hand shall be in the neck of thine enemies; thy father's children shall bow down before ngươi. 9  Giu-đa is a lion's whelp: from the prey, my son, ngươi art gone up: he stooped down, he couched as a lion, Và as an old lion; who shall rouse him up? 10  The sceptre shall not depart from Giu-đa, nor a lawgiver from between his feet, until Shiloh đến; and unto him shall the gathering của dân sự be. 11  Binding his foal unto the vine, Và his ass's colt unto the choice vine; he washed his garments in wine, Và his clothes in the blood of grapes: 12  His eyes shall be red with wine, Và his teeth trắng with milk. 13  Zebulun shall dwell at the haven of biển; and he shall be for an haven of ships; Và his border shall be unto Zidon. 14  Issachar is a strong ass couching down between two burdens: 15  Và he saw that rest was tốt, Và the land that it was pleasant; Và bowed his shoulder to bear, Và became a servant unto tribute. 16  Dan shall judge his people, as one of the tribes của I-sơ-ra-ên. 17  Dan shall be a serpent by the way, an adder in the path, that biteth the horse heels, so that his rider shall fall backward. 5 18  I have waited for thy salvation, O CHÚA. 19  Gad, a troop shall overcome him: but he shall overcome at cuối cùng. 20  Out of Asher his bread shall be fat, Và he shall yield royal dainties. 21  Naphtali is a hind let loose: he giveth goodly words. 22  Giô-sép is a fruitful bough, even a fruitful bough by a well; whose branches run over the wall: 6 23  The archers have sorely grieved him, Và shot at him, Và hated him: 24  But his bow abode in strength, Và the arms of his hands được dựng nên strong by tays of the mighty God of Gia-cốp; (from thence is người chăn chiên, Hòn đá của I-sơ-ra-ên:) 25  Even by the God of thy father, who shall help ngươi; and by the Almighty, who shall bless ngươi with blessings of heaven above, blessings of the deep that lieth under, blessings of the breasts, Và of the womb: 26  The blessings of thy father have prevailed above the blessings of my progenitors unto the utmost bound of the đời đời hills: they shall be on the head of Giô-sép, Và on the crown of the head of him that was separate from anh em người. 27  Bên-gia-min shall ravin as a wolf: in the morning he shall devour the prey, Và at night he shall divide the spoil. 28  All these are mười hai tribes của I-sơ-ra-ên: and this is it that their father nói với họ, and blessed them; every một according to his blessing he blessed them. 29  Và he charged them, and nói với họ, I am to be gathered unto my people: chôn me with my fathers in the cave that is in the field of Ephron the Hittite, 30  In the cave that is in the field of Machpelah, which is before Mamre, in the land of Canaan, which Áp-ra-ham bought with the field of Ephron the Hittite for a possession of a buryingplace. 31  There they buried Áp-ra-ham Và Sa-ra his wife; there they buried Y-sác Và Rê-be-ca his wife; Và there I buried Leah. 32  The purchase of the field Và of the cave that is therein was from the children of Heth. 33  Và when Gia-cốp had made an end of commanding his sons, he gathered up his feet into the bed, Và yielded up the ghost, Và was gathered unto his people.
