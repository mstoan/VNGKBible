10
1  For luật pháp having a shadow of good things to come, Và không the very image of the things, can never với those sacrifices which they offered year by year continually make the comers thereunto perfect. 2  For then would they không have ceased to be offered? vì that the worshippers once purged should have had no more conscience of tội lỗi. 3  Nhưng in those sacrifices there is a remembrance again made of tội lỗi every year. 4  For it is not possible that the blood of bulls Và of goats should take away tội lỗi. 5  Wherefore when he cometh into thế gian, he nói, Sacrifice Và offering thou wouldest không, Nhưng a body hast thou prepared me: 6  In burnt offerings Và sacrifices for sin thou hast had no pleasure. 7  Then nói I, Kìa, I come (in the volume of the book it is written of me,) to do thy will, O God. 8  Above when he nói, Sacrifice Và offering Và burnt offerings Và offering for sin thou wouldest không, neither hadst pleasure therein; which are offered by luật pháp; 9  Then nói he, Kìa, I come to do thy will, O God. He taketh away the first, that he may establish the second. 10  By the which will we are sanctified through the offering của thân thể của ĐCGiê-su Christ once for all. 11  Và every priest standeth daily ministering Và offering oftentimes the same sacrifices, which can never take away tội lỗi: 12  Nhưng this man, after he had offered một sacrifice for tội lỗi for ever, sat down on the right hand của ĐCT; 13  From henceforth expecting till his enemies be made his footstool. 14  For by one offering he hath perfected for ever them that are sanctified. 15  Whereof the Holy Ghost also is một nhân chứng to us: for after that he had nói before, 16  This is the covenant that I will make với them after those days, nói the Lord, I will put my laws into their hearts, Và in their minds will I write them; 17  Và their tội lỗi Và iniquities will I remember no more. 18  bây giờ nơi remission of these is, there is không còn offering for sin. 19  Having therefore, anh em, boldness to enter into the holiest by the blood của ĐCGiê-su, 20  By a new Và living way, which he hath consecrated for us, through tấm màn, that is to say, his flesh; 21  Và having an high priest over nhà của ĐCT; 22  Let us draw near với a true heart in full assurance of faith, having our hearts sprinkled từ an evil conscience, Và our bodies washed với pure nước. 23  Let us hold fast the profession of our đức tin without wavering; (for he is faithful that promised;) 24  Và let us consider lẫn nhau to provoke unto love Và to good works: 25  Not forsaking the assembling of ourselves together, as the manner of some is; Nhưng exhorting lẫn nhau: Và so much the more, as ye see the day approaching. 26  For nếu we sin wilfully after that we have received sự hiểu biết of the truth, there remaineth không còn sacrifice for tội lỗi, 27  Nhưng a certain fearful looking for of judgment Và fiery indignation, which shall devour the adversaries. 28  He that despised Môi-se' law died without mercy under hai hay ba witnesses: 29  Of how much sorer punishment, suppose ye, shall he be thought worthy, who hath trodden under foot Con của ĐCT, Và hath counted the blood of the covenant, where với he was sanctified, an unholy thing, Và hath done despite unto [Thánh] Linh of grace? 30  For we know him that hath nói, Vengeance belongeth unto me, I will recompense, nói the Lord. Và again, The Lord shall judge his people. 31  It is a fearful thing to fall into tays of the living ĐCT. 32  Nhưng call to remembrance the former days, in which, after ye were illuminated, ye endured a great fight of afflictions; 33  Partly, whilst ye được dựng nên a gazingstock both by reproaches Và afflictions; Và partly, whilst ye became companions of them that were so used. 34  For ye had compassion of me in my bonds, Và took joyfully the spoiling of your goods, knowing in yourselves that ye have in heaven a better Và an enduring substance. 35  Cast không away therefore your confidence, which hath great recompence of reward. 36  For ye have need of patience, that, after ye have done the will của ĐCT, ye might receive lời hứa. 37  For yet a little while, Vàhe that shall come will come, Và will không tarry. 38  bây giờ the just sẽ sống by đức tin: Nhưng nếu any man draw back, my soul shall have no pleasure in him. 39  Nhưng chúng ta are không of them who draw back unto perdition; Nhưng of them that believe to the saving of the soul.
