2
1  Tại sao do the heathen rage, Và the people imagine a vain thing? 1 2 2  các vua of đất set themselves, and the rulers take counsel together, against the CHÚA, Và against his anointed, nói [rằng], 3  Let us break their bands asunder, Và cast away their cords from us. 4  He that sitteth in the heavens shall laugh: the Lord shall have them in derision. 5  Then shall he speak với họ in his wrath, Và vex them in his sore displeasure. 3 6  Yet have I set my king upon my holy đồi of Zion. 4 5 7  I will declare the decree: the CHÚA hath nói unto me, Thou art my Son; this day have I begotten ngươi. 6 8  Ask of me, and I shall give ngươi the heathen for thine inheritance, Và the uttermost parts of đất for thy possession. 9  ngươi shalt break them with a rod of sắt; ngươi shalt dash them in pieces like a potter's vessel. 10  Be wise now therefore, O ye kings: be instructed, ye judges of đất. 11  Serve the CHÚA with fear, Và rejoice with trembling. 12  Kiss Con, lest he be angry, Và ye perish từ đường, when his wrath is kindled but a little. phước are all they that put their trust in him.
