9
1  Now there was a man of Bên-gia-min, whose name was Kish, the son of Abiel, the son of Zeror, the son of Bechorath, the son of Aphiah, a Benjamite, a mighty man of power. 1 2 2  Và he had một con trai, whose name was Sau-lơ, a choice young man, Và a goodly: and there was not among the children của I-sơ-ra-ên a goodlier person than he: from his shoulders Và upward he was higher than any của dân sự. 3  Và the asses of Kish Sau-lơ's cha were lost. Và Kish nói to Sau-lơ his son, Take now one of the servants with ngươi, and arise, go seek the asses. 4  Và he passed through mount Ép-ra-im, Và passed through the land of Shalisha, but they found họ not: then they passed through the land of Shalim, and there they were not: and he passed through the land of những người Bên-gia-min, but they found họ not. 5  Và when they were come to the land of Zuph, Sau-lơ nói to his servant that was with him, Come, Và let us return; lest my father leave caring for the asses, Và take thought for us. 6  Và he nói unto him, kìa now, there is in this city một người của ĐCT, and he is an honourable man; all that he nói cometh surely to pass: now let us go thither; peradventure he can shew us our way that we should go. 7  Then nói Sau-lơ to his servant, But, kìa, nếu we go, what shall we bring the man? for the bread is spent in our vessels, and there is not a present to bring to the man của ĐCT: what have we? 3 4 8  Và the servant trả lời Sau-lơ again, và nói, kìa, I have here at hand the fourth part of a shekel of silver: that will I give to the man của ĐCT, to tell us our way. 5 9 (Beforetime in I-sơ-ra-ên, when a man went to enquire của ĐCT, thus he spake, Come, Và let us go to the seer: for he that is bây giờ called một tiên tri was beforetime called a Seer.) 10  Then nói Sau-lơ to his servant, Well nói; come, let us go. So they went unto the city where the man của ĐCT was. 6 11  Và as they went up the hill to the city, they found young maidens going out to draw nước, và nói với họ, Is the seer here? 7 12  Và Họ trả lời them, and nói, He is; kìa, he is before you: make haste now, for he came to day to the city; for there is a sacrifice của dân sự to day in the high place: 8 13  As soon as ye be come into the city, ngươi sẽ straightway find him, before he go up to the high place to eat: for the people will not eat until he come, because he doth bless the sacrifice; Và afterwards they eat that be bidden. Now therefore get you up; for about this time ngươi sẽ find him. 9 14  Và they went up into the city: Và when they were come into the city, kìa, Samuel came out against them, for to go up to the high place. 15  Now the Lord had told Samuel in his ear a ngày before Sau-lơ đến, saying,10 16  ngày mai about this time I will send ngươi a man out of the land of Bên-gia-min, Và ngươi shalt anoint him to be captain over my people I-sơ-ra-ên, that he may save my people out of tay of the Philistines: for I have looked upon my people, because their cry is come unto me. 17  Và when Samuel nhìn thấy Sau-lơ, the Lord nói unto him, kìa the man whom I spake to ngươi of! this same shall reign over my people. 11 18  Then Sau-lơ drew near to Samuel trong cổng, và nói, Tell me, I pray ngươi, where the seer's nhà is. 19  Và Samuel trả lời Sau-lơ, và nói, I là the seer: go up before me unto the high place; for ngươi sẽ eat with me to day, Và to morrow I will let ngươi go, Và will tell ngươi all that is in thine heart. 20  Và as for thine asses that were lost ba ngày ago, set not thy mind on them; for they are found. And on whom is all the desire của I-sơ-ra-ên? Is it not on ngươi, and on all thy father's nhà? 12 21  Và Sau-lơ trả lời và nói, là not I a Benjamite, of the smallest of the tribes của I-sơ-ra-ên? Và my gia đình the least of all the families of the tribe of Bên-gia-min? wherefore then speakest ngươi so to me? 13 22  Và Samuel took Sau-lơ Và his servant, Và brought them into the parlour, Và made them sit in the chiefest place among them that were bidden, which were about ba mươi persons. 23  Và Samuel nói unto the cook, Bring the portion which I gave ngươi, of which I nói unto ngươi, Set it by ngươi. 24  Và the cook took up the shoulder, and that which was upon it, and set it before Sau-lơ. And Samuel nói, kìa that which is left! set it before ngươi, Và ăn: for unto this time hath it been kept for ngươi since I nói, I have invited the people. So Sau-lơ did eat with Samuel that day. 14 25  Và when they were come down from the high place into the city, Samuel communed with Sau-lơ upon the top of nhà. 26  Và they arose early: Và xảy đến about the spring of the day, that Samuel called Sau-lơ to the top of nhà, saying, Up, that I may send ngươi away. Và Sau-lơ arose, Và they went out both of them, he and Samuel, abroad. 27  Và as they were going down to the end of the city, Samuel nói to Sau-lơ, Bid the servant pass on before us, (and he passed on,) but stand ngươi still a while, that I may shew ngươi the word của ĐCT. 15
