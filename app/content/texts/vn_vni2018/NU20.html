1 Then came the children of Israel, even the whole congregation, into the desert of Zin in the first month: Và the people abode in Kadesh; Và Miriam died there, and was buried there. 2 Và there was no water for the congregation: Và they gathered themselves together against Môi-se Và against A-rôn. 3 Và the people chode with Môi-se, Và spake, saying, Would God that we had died when our brethren died before the Lord! 4 Và why have ye brought up the congregation of the Lord into this wilderness, that we and our cattle should die there? 5 Và wherefore have ye made us to come up out of Egypt, to bring us in unto this evil place? it is no place of seed, or of figs, or of vines, or of pomegranates; neither is there any water to drink. 6 Và Môi-se Và A-rôn went from the presence of the assembly unto the door of the tabernacle of the congregation, Và they fell upon their faces: Và the glory of the Lord appeared unto them.
7 Và the Lord spake unto Môi-se, saying, 8 Take the rod, Và gather thou the assembly together, thou, and A-rôn thy brother, Và speak ye unto the rock before their eyes; Và it shall give forth his water, Và thou shalt bring forth to them water out of the rock: so thou shalt give the congregation Và their beasts drink. 9 Và Môi-se took the rod from before the Lord, as he commanded him. 10 Và Môi-se Và A-rôn gathered the congregation together before the rock, Và he said unto them, Hear now, ye rebels; must we fetch you water out of this rock? 11 Và Môi-se lifted up his hand, Và with his rod he smote the rock twice: Và the water came out abundantly, Và the congregation drank, Và their beasts also.
12 Và the Lord spake unto Môi-se Và A-rôn, Because ye believed me not, to sanctify me in the eyes of the children of Israel, therefore ye shall not bring this congregation into the land which I have given them. 13 This is the water of Meribah; because the children of Israel strove with the Lord, Và he was sanctified in them.1
14 Và Môi-se sent messengers from Kadesh unto the king of Edom, Thus saith thy brother Israel, Thou knowest all the travail that hath befallen us:2 15 How our fathers went down into Egypt, Và we have dwelt in Egypt a long time; Và người Ê-díp-tô vexed us, and our fathers: 16 Và when we cried unto the Lord, he heard our voice, Và sent an angel, Và hath brought us forth out of Egypt: and, behold, we are in Kadesh, a city in the uttermost of thy border: 17 Let us pass, I pray thee, through thy country: we will not pass through the fields, or through the vineyards, neither will we drink of the water of the wells: we will go by the king's high way, we will not turn to the right hand nor to the left, until we have passed thy borders. 18 Và Edom said unto him, Thou shalt not pass by me, lest I come out against thee with the sword. 19 Và the children of Israel said unto him, We will go by the high way: Và if I and my cattle drink of thy water, then I will pay for it: I will only, without doing any thing else, go through on my feet. 20 Và he said, Thou shalt not go through. Và Edom came out against him with much people, Và with a strong hand. 21 Thus Edom refused to give Israel passage through his border: wherefore Israel turned away from him.
22 Và the children of Israel, even the whole congregation, journeyed from Kadesh, Và came unto mount Hor. 23 Và the Lord spake unto Môi-se Và A-rôn in mount Hor, by the coast of the land of Edom, saying, 24 A-rôn shall be gathered unto his people: for he shall not enter into the land which I have given unto the children of Israel, because ye rebelled against my word at the water of Meribah.3 25 Take A-rôn Và Eleazar his son, Và bring them up unto mount Hor: 26 Và strip A-rôn of his garments, Và put them upon Eleazar his son: Và A-rôn shall be gathered unto his people, Và shall die there. 27 Và Môi-se did as the Lord commanded: Và they went up into mount Hor in the sight of all the congregation. 28 Và Môi-se stripped A-rôn of his garments, Và put them upon Eleazar his son; Và A-rôn died there in the top of the mount: Và Môi-se Và Eleazar came down from the mount. 29 Và when all the congregation saw that A-rôn was dead, they mourned for A-rôn thirty days, even all the house of Israel.
