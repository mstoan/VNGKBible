5
1  Và thou, son of man, take ngươi a sharp knife, take ngươi a barber's razor, and cause it to pass upon thine head Và upon thy beard: then take ngươi balances to weigh, Và divide the hair. 2  ngươi shalt burn bằng lửa a third part in the midst of the city, when the days of the siege are fulfilled: Và ngươi shalt take a third part, Và smite about it with a knife: Và a third part ngươi shalt scatter in the wind; Và I will draw out a thanh gươm after them. 3  ngươi shalt also take thereof a few in number, Và bind them in thy skirts. 1 4  Then take of them again, Và cast them into the midst of the fire, Và burn them in the fire; for thereof shall a fire come forth into all nhà của I-sơ-ra-ên. 5  Thus nói the Lord God; This is Giê-ru-sa-lem: I have set it in the midst of the nations Và countries that are round about her. 6  Và she hath changed my judgments into wickedness more than the nations, Và my statutes more than the countries that are round about her: for they have refused my judgments Và my statutes, they have not walked in them. 7  Therefore thus nói the Lord God; Because ye multiplied more than the nations that are round about you, Và have not walked in my statutes, neither have kept my judgments, neither have done according to the judgments of the nations that are round about you; 8  Therefore thus nói the Lord God; kìa, I, even I, là against ngươi, and will execute judgments in the midst of ngươi in the sight of the nations. 9  Và I will do in ngươi that which I have not done, Và whereunto I will not do any more the like, because of all thine abominations. 10  Therefore the fathers shall eat the sons in the midst of ngươi, and the sons shall eat cha ông của họ; Và I will execute judgments in ngươi, and the whole remnant of ngươi will I scatter into all gió. 11 Wherefore, as I live, nói the Lord God; Surely, because ngươi hast defiled my sanctuary with all thy detestable things, Và with all thine abominations, therefore will I also diminish ngươi; neither shall mine eye spare, neither will I have any pity. 12  A third part of ngươi shall die with the pestilence, Và with famine shall they be consumed in the midst of ngươi: and a third part shall fall by the thanh gươm round about ngươi; and I will scatter a third part into all gió, Và I will draw out a thanh gươm after them. 13  Thus shall mine anger be accomplished, Và I will cause my fury to rest upon them, and I will được yên ủi: Và they shall know that I the CHÚA have spoken it in my zeal, when I have accomplished my fury in them. 14  Moreover I will make ngươi waste, Và a reproach among the nations that are round about ngươi, in the sight of all that pass by. 15  So it shall be a reproach Và a taunt, an instruction Và an astonishment unto the nations that are round about ngươi, when I shall execute judgments in ngươi in anger Và in fury Và in furious rebukes. I the CHÚA have spoken it. 16  When I shall send upon them the evil arrows of famine, which shall be for their destruction, Và which I will send to destroy you: and I will increase the famine upon you, and will break your staff of bread: 17  So will I send upon you famine Và evil beasts, Và they shall bereave ngươi; and pestilence Và blood shall pass through ngươi; and I will bring the thanh gươm upon ngươi. I the CHÚA have spoken it.
