4
1  bây giờ [Thánh] Linh speaketh expressly, rằng in the latter times some shall depart from the faith, giving heed to seducing spirits, Và doctrines of devils; 2  Speaking lies in hypocrisy; having their conscience seared with a hot iron; 3  Forbidding to marry, Và commanding to abstain from meats, which ĐCT hath created to be received with thanksgiving of them which believe Và know the truth. 4  For every creature của ĐCT is good, Và nothing to be refused, if it be received with thanksgiving: 5  For it is sanctified by the word của ĐCT Và prayer. 6  If thou put the brethren in remembrance of these things, thou shalt be a good minister của ĐCGiê-su Christ, nourished up in the words of faith Và of good doctrine, whereunto thou hast attained. 7  Nhưng refuse profane Và old wives' fables, Và exercise thyself rather unto godliness. 8  For bodily exercise profiteth little: Nhưng godliness is profitable unto all things, having promise of the sự sống that now is, Và of that which is to come. 9  This is a faithful Nói rằng Và worthy of all acceptation. 10  For therefore we both labour Và suffer reproach, because we trust in the living ĐCT, who is Cứu Tinh of all men, specially of those that believe. 11  These things command Và teach. 12  Let không ai despise thy youth; Nhưng be thou an example of the believers, in word, in conversation, in charity, in spirit, in đức tin, in purity. 13  Till I come, give attendance to reading, to exhortation, to doctrine. 14  Neglect not the gift that isin thee, which was given thee by prophecy, with the laying on of the hands of the presbytery. 15  Meditate upon these things; give thyself wholly to them; that thy profiting may appear to all. 16  Hãy thận trọng unto thyself, Và unto the doctrine; continue in them: for in doing this thou shalt both save thyself, Và them that hear thee.
