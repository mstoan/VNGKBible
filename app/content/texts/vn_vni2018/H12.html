2
1  For yourselves, anh em, know our entrance in unto you, rằng it was not in vain: 2  Nhưng even after that we had suffered before, Và were shamefully entreated, as ye know, at Phi-líppi, we were bold in our ĐCT to speak unto you phúc-âm của ĐCT with much contention. 3  For our exhortation was not of deceit, nor of uncleanness, nor in guile: 4  Nhưng as we were allowed of ĐCT to be put in trust with phúc-âm, even so we speak; not as pleasing men, Nhưng ĐCT, which trieth our hearts. 5  For neither at any time used we flattering words, as ye know, nor a cloke of covetousness; ĐCT is witness: 6  Nor of men sought we glory, neither of you, nor yet of others, when we might have been burden some, as the apostles của Đấng Christ. 7  Nhưng we were gentle among you, even as a nurse cherisheth her children: 8  So being affectionately desirous of you, we were willing to have imparted unto you, not phúc-âm của ĐCT only, Nhưng also our own souls, because ye were dear unto us. 9  For ye remember, anh em, our labour Và travail: for labouring night Và day, because we would not be chargeable unto any of you, we preached unto you phúc-âm của ĐCT. 10  Ye are witnesses, Và ĐCT also, how holily Và justly Và unblameably we behaved ourselves among you that believe: 11  As ye know how we exhorted Và comforted Và charged every một of you, as a father doth his children, 12  That ye would walk worthy của ĐCT, who hath called you unto his kingdom Và glory. 13  For this cause also thank chúng ta ĐCT without ceasing, because, when ye received the word ofĐCT which ye heard of us, ye received it not as the word of men, Nhưng as it is in truth, the word của ĐCT, which effectually worketh also in you that believe. 14  For ye, anh em, became followers of the churches của ĐCT which in Judaea are in Christ ĐCGiê-su: for ye also have suffered like things of your own countrymen, even as they have of người Giu-đa: 15  Who both killed the Lord ĐCGiê-su, Và their own prophets, Và have persecuted us; Và they please not ĐCT, Và arecontrary to all men: 16  Forbidding us to speak to the Gentiles that they might được cứu, to fill up their tội lỗi alway: for the wrath is come upon them to the uttermost. 17  Nhưng we, anh em, being taken from you for a short time in presence, not in heart, endeavoured the more abundantly to see your face with great desire. 18  Wherefore we would have come unto you, even I Phao-lô, once Và again; Nhưng Satan hindered us. 19  For what is our hy vọng, hay joy, hay crown of rejoicing? Are not even ye in the presence of our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ at his coming? 20  For ye are our glory Và joy.
