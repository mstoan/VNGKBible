18
1  At the same time đến các môn đệ cùng ĐCGiê-su, Nói rằng, Who is the greatest in the kingdom of heaven? 2  Và ĐCGiê-su called a little child unto him, Và set him in the midst of them, 3  Và nói, Quả thật I say unto you, Except ye be converted, Và become as little children, ye shall not enter into the kingdom of heaven. 4  Whosoever therefore shall humble himself as this little child, the same is greatest in the kingdom of heaven. 5  Và whoso shall receive một such little child in my name receiveth me. 6  Nhưng whoso shall offend một of these little ones which believe in me, it were better for him that a millstone were hanged about his neck, Và that he were drowned in the depth of biển. 7  Woe unto the world because of offences! for it must needs be that offences come; Nhưng woe to that man by whom the offence cometh! 8  Wherefore nếu thy hand hay thy foot offend thee, cut them off, Và cast them from thee: it is better for thee to enter into sự sống halt hay maimed, rather than having hai hands hay hai feet to be cast into everlasting fire. 9  Và nếu thine eye offend thee, pluck it out, Và cast it from thee: it is better for thee to enter into sự sống with one eye, rather than having hai eyes to be cast into hỏa ngục fire. 10  Take heed that ye despise not một of these little ones; for I say unto you, rằng in heaven their các thiên sứ do always kìa the face of my Father which is in heaven. 11  For the Son of man is come to save that which was lost. 12  How think ye? nếu a man have an hundred sheep, Và một of them be gone astray, doth he not leave the ninety and nine, Và goeth into the mountains, Và seeketh that which is gone astray? 13  Và nếu so be that he find it, Quả thật I say unto you, he rejoiceth more of that sheep, than of the ninety and nine which went not astray. 14  Even so it is not the will of your Father which is in heaven, that một of these little ones should perish. 15  Moreover nếu thy brother shall trespass against thee, go Và tell him his fault between thee Và him alone: nếu he shall hear thee, thou hast gained thy brother. 16  Nhưng nếu he will not hear thee, then take with thee một hay hai more, that in the mouth of hai hay ba witnesses every word may be established. 17  Và nếu he shall neglect to hear them, tell it unto the church: Nhưng nếu he neglect to hear the church, let him be unto thee as an heathen man Và a publican. 18  Quả thật I say unto you, Whatsoever ye shall bind on đất shall be bound in heaven: Và whatsoever ye shall loose on đất shall be loosed in heaven. 19  Again I say unto you, rằng nếu hai of you shall agree on đất as touching any thing that they shall ask, it shall be done for them of my Father which is in heaven. 20  For where hai hay ba are gathered together in my name, there am I in the midst of them. 21  Then đến Phi-e-rơ to him, và nói, Lord, how oft shall my brother sin against me, Và I forgive him? till seven times? 22  ĐCGiê-su nói unto him, I say not unto thee, Until seven times: Nhưng, Until bảy mươi times seven. 23  Therefore is the kingdom of heaven likened unto a certain king, which would take account of his servants. 24  Và when he had begun to reckon, một was brought unto him, which owed him ten thousand talents. 25  But forasmuch as he had not to pay, his lord commanded him to be sold, Và his vợ, Và children, Và all that he had, Và payment to be made. 26  The servant therefore fell down, Và worshipped him, Nói rằng, Lord, have patience with me, Và I will pay thee all. 27  Then the lord of that servant was moved with compassion, Và loosed him, Và forgave him the debt. 28  Nhưng the same servant went out, Và found một of his fellowservants, which owed him an hundred pence: Và he laid hands on him, Và took him by the throat, Nói rằng, Pay me that thou owest. 29  Và his fellowservant fell down at his feet, Và besought him, Nói rằng, Have patience with me, Và I will pay thee all. 30  Và he would not: Nhưng went and cast him into prison, till he should pay the debt. 31  So when his fellowservants saw what was done, they were very sorry, Và đến Và told unto their lord all that was done. 32  Then his lord, after that he had called him, nói unto him, O thou wicked servant, I forgave thee all that debt, because thou desiredst me: 33  Shouldest not thou also have had compassion on thy fellowservant, even as I had pity on thee? 34  Và his lord was wroth, Và delivered him to the tormentors, till he should pay all that was due unto him. 35  So likewise shall my heavenly Father do also unto you, nếu ye from your hearts forgive not every one his brother their trespasses.
