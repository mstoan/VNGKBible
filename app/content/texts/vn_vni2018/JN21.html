21
1  After these things ĐCGiê-su shewed himself again to các môn đệ at biển of Tiberias; Và on this wise shewed he himself. 2  There were together Si-môn Phi-e-rơ, Và Thô-ma called Didymus, Và Na-tha-na-ên of Cana in Ga-li-lê, Và the sons of Xê-bê-đê, Và hai other of his các môn đệ. 3  Si-môn Phi-e-rơ nói với họ, I go a fishing. They say unto him, chúng ta also go với thee. They went forth, Và entered into một chiếc thuyền tức thì; Và that night they caught nothing. 4  But when the morning was now come, ĐCGiê-su stood on the shore: Nhưng các môn đệ knew không rằng it was ĐCGiê-su. 5  Then ĐCGiê-su nói với họ, Children, have ye any meat? They trả lời him, No. 6  Và he nói với họ, Cast the net on the right side of the ship, Và ye shall find. They cast therefore, Và bây giờ they were không able to draw it for đám đông of fishes. 7  Therefore that disciple whom ĐCGiê-su loved nói unto Phi-e-rơ, It is the Lord. Now when Si-môn Phi-e-rơ heard rằng it was the Lord, he girt his fisher's coat unto him, ( for he was naked,) Và did cast himself into biển. 8  Và the other các môn đệ came in a little ship; (for they were không far từ land, Nhưng as it were hai trăm cubits,) dragging the net with fishes. 9  As soon then as they were come to land, they saw a fire of coals there, Và fish laid thereon, Và bánh. 10  ĐCGiê-su nói với họ, Bring of the fish which ye have bây giờ caught. 11  Si-môn Phi-e-rơ went up, Và drew the net to land full of great fishes, một trăm Và fifty and three: Và for all there were so many, yet was không the net broken. 12  ĐCGiê-su nói với họ, Come Và dine. Và none of các môn đệ durst ask him, Who art thou? knowing rằng it was the Lord. 13  ĐCGiê-su then cometh, Và taketh bánh, Và giveth họ, Và fish likewise. 14  This is now the third time that Jesus shewed himself to his các môn đệ, Sau đó he was risen từ the dead. 15  So khi they had dined, ĐCGiê-su nói to Si-môn Phi-e-rơ, Si-môn, son của Giô-na, lovest thou me more than these? He nói unto him, Yea, Lord; thou knowest rằng I love thee. He nói unto him, Feed my lambs. 16  He nói to him again the second time, Si-môn, son của Giô-na, lovest thou me? He nói unto him, Yea, Lord; thou knowest rằng I love thee. He nói unto him, Feed my chiên. 17  He nói unto him the third time, Si-môn, son của Giô-na, lovest thou me? Phi-e-rơ was grieved vì he nói unto him the third time, Lovest thou me? Và he nói unto him, Lord, thou knowest all things; thou knowest rằng I love thee. ĐCGiê-su nói unto him, Feed my chiên. 18  Quả thật, Quả thật, Ta nói cho ngươi, khi thou wast young, thou girdedst thyself, Và walkedst whither thou wouldest: Nhưng khi thou shalt be old, thou shalt stretch forth thy hands, Và another shall gird thee, Và carry thee whither thou wouldest không. 19  This spake he, signifying by what death he should glorify ĐCT. Và when he had spoken this, he nói unto him, Follow me. 20  Then Phi-e-rơ, turning about, thấy the disciple whom ĐCGiê-su loved following; which also leaned on his breast at supper, Và nói, Lord, which is he that betrayeth thee? 21  Phi-e-rơ seeing him nói to Jesus, Lord, Và what shall this man do? 22  ĐCGiê-su nói unto him, nếu I will that he tarry till I come, what is that to thee? follow thou me. 23  Then went this Nói rằng abroad among the brethren, rằng that disciple should không chết: yet ĐCGiê-su nói không unto him, He shall không chết; Nhưng, nếu Ta muốn he tarry till I come, what is that to thee? 24  This is the disciple which testifieth of these things, Và wrote these things: Và we know rằng his testimony is true. 25  Và there are also many other things which ĐCGiê-su did, the which, nếu they should be written every một, I suppose that even the world itself could không contain the books that should be written. Amen.
