Zechariah
1
1  In the eighth month, in the second year of Darius, came the word of the Lord unto Zechariah, the son of Berechiah, the son of Iddo tiên tri, saying, 2  The Lord hath been sore displeased with your fathers. 1 3  Therefore say thou với họ, Thus nói the Lord of hosts; Turn ye unto me, nói the Lord of hosts, Và I will turn unto you, nói the Lord of hosts. 4  Be ye not as your fathers, unto whom the former prophets have cried, saying, Thus nói the Lord of hosts; Turn ye now from your evil ways, and từ your evil doings: but they did not hear, nor hearken unto me, nói the Lord. 5  Your fathers, where are they? and các tiên tri, do they live for ever? 6  But my words Và my statutes, which I commanded my servants các tiên tri, did they not take hold of your fathers? Và they returned và nói, Like as the Lord of hosts thought to do unto us, according to our ways, Và according to our doings, so hath he dealt with us. 2 7  Upon the four Và twentieth day of the eleventh month, which is the month Sebat, in the second year of Darius, came the word of the Lord unto Zechariah, the son of Berechiah, the son of Iddo tiên tri, saying, 8  I saw by night, Và kìa a man riding upon a red horse, Và he stood among the myrtle trees that were in the bottom; Và behind him were there red horses, speckled, Và white. 3 9  Then nói I, O my lord, what are these? And thiên sứ that talked with me nói unto me, I will shew thee what these be. 10  Và the man that stood among the myrtle trees trả lời và nói, These are they whom the Lord hath sent to walk to and fro through đất. 11  Và they trả lời thiên sứ of the Lord that stood among the myrtle trees, và nói, We have walked to and fro through đất, and, kìa, all đất sitteth still, Và is at rest. 12  Then thiên sứ of the Lord trả lời và nói, O Lord of hosts, how long wilt thou not have mercy on Giê-ru-sa-lem Và on the cities of Giu-đa, against which thou hast had indignation these threescore and ten years? 13  Và the Lord trả lời thiên sứ that talked with me với good words Và comfortable words. 14  So thiên sứ that communed with me nói unto me, Cry thou, saying, Thus nói the Lord of hosts; I am jealous for Giê-ru-sa-lem Và for Zion with a great jealousy. 15  Và I am very sore displeased with the heathen that are at ease: for I was but a little displeased, Và they helped forward the affliction. 16  Therefore thus nói the Lord; I am returned to Giê-ru-sa-lem with mercies: my house shall be built in it, nói the Lord of hosts, Và a line shall be stretched forth upon Giê-ru-sa-lem. 17  Cry yet, saying, Thus nói the Lord of hosts; My cities through prosperity shall yet be spread abroad; Và the Lord shall yet comfort Zion, Và shall yet choose Giê-ru-sa-lem. 4 18  Then lifted I up mine eyes, Và saw, Và kìa four horns. 19  Và I nói unto thiên sứ that talked with me, What be these? And he trả lời me, These are the horns which have scattered Giu-đa, I-sơ-ra-ên, Và Giê-ru-sa-lem. 20  Và the Lord shewed me four carpenters. 21  Then nói I, What come these to do? Và he spake, saying, These are the horns which have scattered Giu-đa, đến đổi không ai did lift up his head: but these are come to fray them, to cast out the horns of the Gentiles, which lifted up their horn over the land of Giu-đa to scatter it.
