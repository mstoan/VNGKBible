7
1  Having therefore these promises, Hỡi kẻ yêu dấu, let us cleanse ourselves từ all filthiness of the flesh Và spirit, perfecting holiness in the fear của ĐCT. 2  Receive us; we have wronged không ai, we have corrupted không ai, we have defrauded không ai. 3  I speak không this to condemn you: for I have nói before, rằng ye are in our hearts to chết Và live with you. 4  Great is my boldness of speech toward you, great is my glorying of you: I am filled with comfort, I am exceeding joyful in all our tribulation. 5  For, when we were come into Macedonia, our flesh had no rest, Nhưng we were troubled on every side; without were fightings, within were fears. 6  Nevertheless ĐCT, that comforteth those that are cast down, comforted us by the coming of Titus; 7  Và không by his coming only, Nhưng by the consolation wherewith he was comforted in you, when he told us your earnest desire, your than khóc, your fervent mind toward me; đến đổi I rejoiced the more. 8  For though I made you sorryvới a letter, I do không repent, though I did repent: for I perceive rằng the same epistle hath made you sorry, though it were Nhưng for a season. 9  bây giờ I rejoice, không rằng ye được dựng nên sorry, Nhưng rằng ye sorrowed to repentance: for ye được dựng nên sorry after a godly manner, that ye might receive damage by us in nothing. 10  For godly sorrow worketh repentance to sự cứu rỗi not to be repented of: Nhưng the sorrow của thế gian worketh death. 11  For kìa this selfsame thing, that ye sorrowed after a godly sort, what carefulness it wrought in you, yea, what clearing of yourselves, yea, what indignation, yea, what fear, yea, what vehement desire, yea, what zeal, yea, what revenge! In all things ye have approved yourselves to be clear in this matter. 12  Wherefore, though I wrote unto you, I did it không for his cause that had done the wrong, nor for his cause that suffered wrong, Nhưng that our care for you in the sight của ĐCT might appear unto you. 13  Therefore we were comforted in your comfort: yea, and exceedingly the more joyed we for the joy of Titus, because his spirit was refreshed by you all. 14  For nếu I have boasted any thing to him of you, I am không ashamed; Nhưng as we spake all things to you in truth, even so our boasting, which I made before Titus, is found a truth. 15  Và his inward affection is more abundant toward you, whilst he remembereth the obedience of you all, how với fear Và trembling ye received him. 16  I rejoice therefore that I have confidence in you in all things.
