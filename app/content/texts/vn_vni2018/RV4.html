4
1 After this I looked, Và, kìa, a door was opened in heaven: Và the first voice which I heard was as it were of a trumpet talking with me; which nói, Come up hither, Và I will shew thee things which must be hereafter. 2 Và tức thì I was in the spirit: Và, kìa, a throne was set in heaven, Và one sat on the throne. 3 Và he that sat was to look upon like a jasper Và a sardine stone: Và there was a rainbow round about the throne, in sight like unto an emerald. 4 Và round about the throne were four Và twenty seats: Và upon biểnts I saw four Và twenty elders sitting, clothed in white raiment; Và they had on their heads crowns of gold. 5 Và out of the throne proceeded lightnings Và thunderings Và voices: Và there were seven lamps of fire burning before the throne, which are the seven Spirits của ĐCT. 6 Và before the throne there was a sea of glass like unto crystal: Và in the midst of the throne, Và round about the throne, were four beasts full of eyes before Và behind. 7 Và the first beast was like a lion, Và the second beast like a calf, Và the third beast had a face as a man, Và the fourth beast was like a flying eagle. 8 Và the four beasts had each of them six wings about him; Và they were full of eyes within: Và they rest not day Và night, saying, Holy, holy, holy, Lord ĐCT Almighty, which was, Và is, Và is to come. 9 Và when those beasts give glory Và honour Và thanks to him that sat on the throne, who liveth for ever Và ever, 10 The four Và twenty elders fall down before him that sat on the throne, Và worship him that liveth for ever Và ever, Và cast their crowns before the throne, saying, 11 Thou art worthy, O Lord, to receive glory Và honour Và power: for thou hast created all things, Và for thy pleasure they are Và were created.
