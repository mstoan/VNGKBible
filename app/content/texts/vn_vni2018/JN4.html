4
1  Vậy khi Ngài biết thế nào the những người Pha-ri-si có nghe rằng ĐCGiê-su đã làm và báp-tem nhiều môn đệ hơn là Giăng, 2 (Kỳ thật ĐCGiê-su chính mình không báp-tem, nhưng [là] các môn đệ của Ngài ,) 3 He left xứ Giu-đê, Và departed again into Ga-li-lê. 4 Và Ngài cần phải đi băng ngang Sa-ma-ri. 5 Rồi Ngài đi vào một thành của xứ Sa-ma-ri, nơi được gọi [là] Si-kha, gần to the parcel of ground that Jacob gave to his son Giô-sép. 6 bây giờ Jacob's well was there. ĐCGiê-su therefore, being wearied with his journey, sat thus on the well: Và it was about the sixth hour. 7 There cometh một người đờn bà of Samaria to draw water: ĐCGiê-su nói unto her, Give me to drink. 8 (For his disciples were gone away unto the city to buy meat.) 9 Then nói Người đờn bà of Samaria unto him, How is it that thou, being a Jew, askest drink of me, which am một người đờn bà of Samaria? for the Jews have no dealings with the Samaritans. 10 ĐCGiê-su trả lời Và nói unto her, nếu thou knewest the gift của ĐCT, Và who it is that nói to thee, Give me to drink; thou wouldest have asked of him, Và he would have given thee living water. 11 Người đờn bà nói unto him, Sir, thou hast nothing to draw with, Và the well is deep: from whence then hast thou that living water? 12 Art thou greater than our father Jacob, which gave us the well, Và drank thereof himself, Và his children, Và his cattle? 13 ĐCGiê-su trả lời Và nói unto her, Whosoever drinketh of this water shall thirst again: 14 Nhưng whosoever drinketh of the water that I shall give him shall never thirst; Nhưng the water that I shall give him shall be in him a well of water springing up into everlasting life. 15 Người đờn bà nói unto him, Sir, give me this water, that I thirst not, neither come hither to draw. 16 ĐCGiê-su nói unto her, Go, call thy husband, Và come hither. 17 Người đờn bà trả lời Và nói, I have no husband. ĐCGiê-su nói unto her, Thou hast well nói, I have no husband: 18 For thou hast had five husbands; Và he whom thou bây giờ hast is not thy husband: in that nóist thou truly. 19 Người đờn bà nói unto him, Sir, I perceive that thou art a prophet. 20 Our fathers worshipped in this mountain; Và ye say, that in Giê-ru-sa-lem is the place where men ought to worship. 21 ĐCGiê-su nói unto her, Woman, believe me, the hour cometh, when ye shall neither in this mountain, nor yet at Giê-ru-sa-lem, worship the Father. 22 Ye worship ye know not what: chúng ta know what we worship: for salvation is of the Jews. 23 Nhưng the hour cometh, Và bây giờ is, when the true worshippers shall worship the Father in spirit Và in truth: for the Father seeketh such to worship him. 24 ĐCT is a Spirit: Và they that worship him must worship him in spirit Và in truth. 25 Người đờn bà nói unto him, I know that Messias cometh, which is called Christ: when he is come, he will tell us all things. 26 ĐCGiê-su nói unto her, I that speak unto thee am he.
27 Và upon this đến his disciples, Và marvelled that he talked with Người đờn bà: yet không ai nói, What seekest thou? hay, Why talkest thou with her? 28 Người đờn bà then left her waterpot, Và went her way into the city, Và nói to the men, 29 Come, see a man, which told me all things that ever I did: is not this the Christ? 30 Then they went out of the city, Và đến unto him.
31 In the mean while his disciples prayed him, saying, Master, eat. 32 Nhưng he nói unto them, I have meat to eat that ye know not of. 33 Therefore nói các môn đệ một to another, Hath any man brought him ought to eat? 34 ĐCGiê-su nói unto them, My meat is to do the will of him that sent me, Và to finish his work. 35 Say not ye, There are yet four months, Và then cometh harvest? kìa, I say unto you, Lift up your eyes, Và look on the fields; for they are white already to harvest. 36 Và he that reapeth receiveth wages, Và gathereth fruit unto life eternal: that both he that soweth Và he that reapeth may rejoice together. 37 Và herein is that saying true, một soweth, Và another reapeth. 38 I sent you to reap that whereon ye bestowed no labour: other men laboured, Và ye are entered into their labours.
39 Và many of the Samaritans of that city believed on him for the saying of the woman, which testified, He told me all that ever I did. 40 So when the Samaritans were come unto him, they besought him that he would tarry with them: Và he abode there two days. 41 Và many more believed because of his own word; 42 Và nói unto the woman, bây giờ we believe, not because of thy saying: for we have heard him ourselves, Và know that this is indeed the Christ, the Saviour of the world.
43 bây giờ after two days he departed thence, Và went into Ga-li-lê. 44 For ĐCGiê-su himself testified, that a prophet hath no honour in his own country. 45 Then when he was come into Ga-li-lê, the Galilæans received him, having seen all the things that he did at Giê-ru-sa-lem at the feast: for they also went unto the feast. 46 So ĐCGiê-su đến again into Cana of Ga-li-lê, where he made the water wine. Và there was a certain nobleman, whose son was sick at Ca-bê-na-um. 47 When he heard that ĐCGiê-su was come out of xứ Giu-đê into Ga-li-lê, he went unto him, Và besought him that he would come down, Và heal his son: for he was at the point of death. 48 Then nói ĐCGiê-su unto him, Except ye see signs Và wonders, ye will not believe. 49 The nobleman nói unto him, Sir, come down ere my child die. 50 ĐCGiê-su nói unto him, Go thy way; thy son liveth. Và the man believed the word that ĐCGiê-su had spoken unto him, Và he went his way. 51 Và as he was bây giờ going down, his servants met him, Và told him, saying, Thy son liveth. 52 Then enquired he of them the hour when he began to amend. Và they nói unto him, Yesterday at the seventh hour the fever left him. 53 So the father knew that it was at the same hour, in the which ĐCGiê-su nói unto him, Thy son liveth: Và himself believed, Và his whole nhà. 54 This is again the second miracle that ĐCGiê-su did, when he was come out of xứ Giu-đê into Ga-li-lê.
