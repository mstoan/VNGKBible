39
1  Và Giô-sép was brought down to Ai-Cập; Và Potiphar, an officer of Pha-ra-ôn, captain of the guard, an Ai-Cậpian, bought him of tays of the Ishmeelites, which had brought him down thither. 2  Và the Lord was with Giô-sép, Và he was a prosperous man; Và he was in nhà of his master người Ai-cập. 3  Và his master nhìn thấy that the Lord was with him, and that the Lord made all that he did to prosper in his hand. 4  Và Giô-sép found ân-điển in his sight, Và he served him: and he made him overseer over his house, and all that he had he put into his hand. 5  Và xảy đến from the time that he had made him overseer in his house, Và over all that he had, that the Lord phước the Ai-Cậpian's nhà for Giô-sép's sake; Và the blessing of the Lord was upon all that he had in nhà, Và in the field. 6  Và he left all that he had in Giô-sép's tay; Và he knew not ought he had, save the bread which he did eat. Và Giô-sép was a goodly person, Và well favoured. 7  Và xảy đến after these things, that his master's vợ cast her eyes upon Giô-sép; Và she nói, Lie with me. 8  But he refused, và nói unto his master's vợ, kìa, my master wotteth not what is with me in nhà, Và he hath committed all that he hath to my hand; 9  There is none greater in this house than I; neither hath he kept back any thing from me but ngươi, because thou art his wife: how then can I do this great wickedness, Và sin against God? 10  Và xảy đến, as she spake to Giô-sép ngày by day, that he hearkened not unto her, to lie by her, or to be with her. 11  Và xảy đến about this time, that Giô-sép went into nhà to do his business; and there was none of the men của nhà there within. 12  Và she caught him by his garment, saying, Lie with me: and he left his garment in her hand, Và fled, Và got him out. 13  Và xảy đến, when she saw that he had left his garment in her hand, Và was fled forth, 14  That she called unto the men of her house, Và spake với họ, saying, thấy, he hath brought in an Hebrew unto us to mock us; he came in unto me to lie with me, and I cried with a loud tiếng: 1 15  Và xảy đến, when he heard that I lifted up my voice Và cried, that he left his garment with me, Và fled, Và got him out. 16  Và she laid up his garment by her, until his lord đến home. 17  Và she spake unto him according to these words, saying, The Hebrew đầy tớ, which ngươi hast brought unto us, came in unto me to mock me: 18  Và xảy đến, as I lifted up my voice Và cried, that he left his garment with me, Và fled out. 19  Và xảy đến, when his master heard the words of his wife, which she spake unto him, saying, After this manner did thy servant to me; that his wrath was kindled. 20  Và Giô-sép's master took him, and put him into the prison, một chỗ where the king's prisoners were bound: Và he was there in the prison. 21  But the Lord was with Giô-sép, Và shewed him mercy, Và gave him favour in the sight of the keeper of the prison. 2 22  Và the keeper of the prison committed to Giô-sép's tay all the prisoners that were in the prison; Và bất cứ điều chi they did there, he was the doer of it. 23  The keeper of the prison looked not to any thing that was under his hand; because the Lord was with him, and that which he did, the Lord made it to prosper.
