13
1  Sau-lơ reigned one year; Và when he had reigned hai năm over I-sơ-ra-ên,1 2  Sau-lơ chose him three ngàn men của I-sơ-ra-ên; whereof two thousand were with Sau-lơ in Michmash Và in mount Beth–el, Và a thousand were with Jonathan in Gibeah of Bên-gia-min: Và the rest của dân sự he sent every man to his tent. 3  Và Jonathan smote the garrison of the Philistines that was in Geba, Và the Philistines nghe of it. Và Sau-lơ blew the trumpet throughout all the land, nói [rằng], Let the Hebrews nghe. 2 4  Và all I-sơ-ra-ên nghe say that Sau-lơ had smitten a garrison of the Philistines, and that I-sơ-ra-ên also was had in abomination with the Philistines. Và the people were called together after Sau-lơ to Gilgal. 3 5  Và the Philistines gathered themselves together to fight with I-sơ-ra-ên, ba mươi ngàn chariots, Và six ngàn horsemen, Và people as the sand which is on biển shore in multitude: Và they came up, Và pitched in Michmash, eastward from Beth–aven. 6  When the men của I-sơ-ra-ên nhìn thấy that they were in a strait, ( for the people were distressed,) then the people did hide themselves in caves, Và in thickets, Và in rocks, Và in high places, Và in pits. 7 Và some of the Hebrews went over Giô-đanh to the land of Gad Và Gilead. As for Sau-lơ, he was yet in Gilgal, Và all the people followed him trembling. 4 8  Và he tarried bảy ngày, according to the set time that Samuel had appointed: but Samuel đến not to Gilgal; Và the people were scattered from him. 9  Và Sau-lơ nói, Bring hither a burnt offering to me, and peace offerings. Và he offered the burnt offering. 10  Và xảy đến, that as soon as he had made an end of offering the burnt offering, kìa, Samuel đến; Và Sau-lơ went out to meet him, that he might salute him. 5 11  Và Samuel nói, What hast ngươi done? Và Sau-lơ nói, Because I saw that the people were scattered from me, and that ngươi camest not within the days appointed, and that the Philistines gathered themselves together at Michmash; 12  Therefore nói I, The Philistines will come down now upon me to Gilgal, Và I have not made supplication unto the Lord: I forced myself therefore, and offered a burnt offering. 6 13  Và Samuel nói to Sau-lơ, ngươi hast done foolishly: ngươi hast not kept the commandment of the Lord thy God, which he commanded ngươi: for now would the Lord have established thy kingdom upon I-sơ-ra-ên for ever. 14  But now thy kingdom shall not continue: the Lord hath sought him a man after his own heart, Và the Lord hath commanded him to be captain over his people, because ngươi hast not kept that which the Lord truyền lệnh ngươi. 15  Và Samuel arose, Và gat him up from Gilgal unto Gibeah of Bên-gia-min. Và Sau-lơ numbered the people that were present with him, about six trăm men. 7 16  Và Sau-lơ, Và Jonathan his son, Và the people that were present with them, abode in Gibeah of Bên-gia-min: but the Philistines encamped in Michmash. 8 9 17  Và the spoilers came out of the camp of the Philistines in three companies: một company turned unto the way that leadeth to Ophrah, unto the land of Shual: 18  Và another company turned đường to Beth–horon: Và another company turned to đường of the border that looketh to the valley of Zeboim toward the wilderness. 19  Now there was no smith found throughout all the land của I-sơ-ra-ên: for the Philistines nói, Lest the Hebrews make họ những thanh gươm or spears: 20  But all the I-sơ-ra-êns đi xuống to the Philistines, to sharpen every man his share, Và his coulter, Và his axe, Và his mattock. 21  Yet they had a file for the mattocks, Và for the coulters, Và for the forks, Và for the axes, Và to sharpen the goads. 10 11 22  So xảy đến in the day of battle, that there was neither thanh gươm nor spear found in tay of any của dân sự that were with Sau-lơ Và Jonathan: but with Sau-lơ Và with Jonathan his son was there found. 23  Và the garrison of the Philistines went out to the passage of Michmash. 12
