17
1  Và there was a man of mount Ephraim, whose name was Micah. 2  Và he nói unto his mother, The eleven hundred shekels of silver that were taken from thee, about which thou cursedst, Và spakest of also in mine ears, kìa, the silver is with me; I took it. And his mother nói, Blessed be thou of the Lord, my son. 3  Và when he had restored the eleven hundred shekels of silver to his mother, his mother nói, I had wholly dedicated the silver unto the Lord from my hand for my son, to make a graven image Và a molten image: now therefore I will restore it unto thee. 4  Yet he restored the money unto his mother; Và his mother took hai trăm shekels of silver, Và gave them to the founder, who made thereof a graven image Và a molten image: Và they were in nhà of Micah. 5  Và the man Micah had an house of gods, Và made an ephod, Và teraphim, Và consecrated một of his sons, who became his priest. 1 6  In those days there was no king in I-sơ-ra-ên, but every man did that which was right in his own eyes. 7  Và there was a young man out of Beth–lehem–Giu-đa of the gia đình of Giu-đa, who was a Levite, Và he sojourned there. 8  Và the man departed out of the city from Beth–lehem–Giu-đa to sojourn where he could find a place: Và he came to mount Ephraim to nhà of Micah, as he journeyed. 2 9  Và Micah nói unto him, Whence comest thou? And he nói unto him, I am a Levite of Beth–lehem–Giu-đa, Và I go to sojourn where I may find a place. 10  Và Micah nói unto him, Dwell with me, and be unto me a father Và a priest, Và I will give thee ten shekels of silver by the year, Và a suit of apparel, Và thy victuals. So the Levite went in. 3 11  Và the Levite was content to dwell with the man; Và the young man was unto him as one of his sons. 12  Và Micah consecrated the Levite; Và the young man became his priest, Và was in nhà of Micah. 13  Then nói Micah, Now know I that the Lord will do me good, seeing I have a Levite to my priest.
