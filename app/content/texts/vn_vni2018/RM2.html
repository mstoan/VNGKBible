2
1  Therefore thou art inexcusable, O man, whosoever thou art that judgest: for wherein thou judgest another, thou condemnest thyself; for thou that judgest doest the same things. 2  Nhưng we are sure rằng the judgment của ĐCT is according to truth against them which commit such things. 3  Và thinkest thou this, O man, that judgest them which do such things, Và doest the same, rằng thou shalt escape the judgment của ĐCT? 4  hay despisest thou the riches of his goodness Và forbearance Và longsuffering; not knowing rằng the goodness của ĐCT leadeth thee to repentance? 5  Nhưng after thy hardness Và impenitent heart treasurest up unto thyself wrath against the day of wrath Và revelation of the righteous judgment của ĐCT; 6  Who will render to every man according to his deeds: 7  To them who by patient continuance in well doing seek for glory Và honour Và immortality, eternal sự sống: 8  Nhưng unto them that are contentious, Và do not obey the truth, Nhưng obey unrighteousness, indignation Và wrath, 9  Tribulation Và anguish, upon every soul of man that doeth evil, of the Jew first, Và also of the Gentile; 10  Nhưng glory, honour, Và peace, to every man that worketh good, to the Jew first, Và also to the Gentile: 11  For there is no respect of persons với ĐCT. 12  For as many as have sinned without law shall also perish without law: Và as many as have sinned in luật pháp shall be judged by luật pháp; 13 (For không the hearers of the law are just before ĐCT, Nhưng the doers of the law shall be justified. 14  For when the Gentiles, which have không luật pháp, do by nature the things contained in the law, these, having không luật pháp, are a law unto themselves: 15  Which shew the work of the law written in their hearts, their conscience also bearing witness, Và their thoughts the mean while accusing or else excusing one another;) 16  In the day when ĐCT shall judge the secrets of men by ĐCGiê-su Christ according to my gospel. 17  kìa, thou art called một người Giu-đa, Và restest in the law, Và makest thy boast of ĐCT, 18  Và knowest his will, Và approvest the things that are more excellent, being instructed out of luật pháp; 19  Và art confident that thou thyself art a guide of the blind, a light of them which are in tối tăm, 20  An instructor of the foolish, a teacher of babes, which hast the form of knowledge Và of the truth in luật pháp. 21  Thou therefore which teachest another, teachest thou không thyself? thou that preachest a man should not steal, dost thou steal? 22  Thou that sayest a man should not commit adultery, dost thou commit adultery? thou that abhorrest idols, dost thou commit sacrilege? 23  Thou that makest thy boast of luật pháp, through breaking luật pháp dishonourest thou ĐCT? 24  For the name của ĐCT is blasphemed among the Gentiles through you, as it is written. 25  For circumcision Quả thật profiteth, nếu thou keep luật pháp: Nhưng nếu thou be a breaker of the law, thy circumcision is made uncircumcision. 26  Therefore nếu the uncircumcision keep the righteousness of the law, shall not his uncircumcision be counted for circumcision? 27  Và shall not uncircumcision which isby nature, if it fulfil luật pháp, judge thee, who by the letter Và circumcision dost transgress luật pháp? 28  For he is không một người Giu-đa, which is one outwardly; neither is that circumcision, which is outward in the flesh: 29  Nhưng he is một người Giu-đa, which is one inwardly; Và circumcision is that of the heart, in the spirit, Và không in the letter; whose praise is không of men, Nhưng of ĐCT.
