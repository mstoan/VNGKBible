2
1  My little children, these things write I unto you, that ye sin not. Và nếu any man sin, we have an advocate with Cha, ĐCGiê-su Christ the righteous: 2  Và he is the propitiation for our tội lỗi: Và not for ours only, Nhưng also for the tội lỗi of the whole world. 3  Và hereby we do know rằng we know him, nếu we keep his commandments. 4  He that nói, I know him, Và keepeth not his commandments, is a liar, Và the truth is not in him. 5  Nhưng whoso keepeth his word, in him Quả thật is tình yêu ofĐCT perfected: hereby know we rằng we are in him. 6  He that nói he abideth in him ought himself also so to walk, even as he walked. 7  anh em, I write no new mạng lệnh unto you, Nhưng an old mạng lệnh which ye had from ban đầu. The old mạng lệnh is the word which ye have heard from ban đầu. 8  Again, a new mạng lệnh I write unto you, which thing is true in him Và in you: because the darkness is past, Và the true light bây giờ shineth. 9  He that nói he is in the light, Và hateth his brother, is in tối tăm even until bây giờ. 10  He that loveth his brother abideth in the light, Và there is none occasion of stumbling in him. 11  Nhưng he that hateth his brother is in tối tăm, Và walketh in tối tăm, Và knoweth not whither he goeth, because that darkness hath blinded his mắt. 12  I write unto you, little children, because your tội lỗi are forgiven you for his name's sake. 13  I write unto you, fathers, because ye have known him that is from ban đầu. I write unto you, young men, because ye have overcome the wicked one. I write unto you, little children, because ye have known Cha. 14  I have written unto you, fathers, because ye have known him that is from ban đầu. I have written unto you, young men, because ye are strong, Và the word of ĐCT abideth in you, Và ye have overcome the wicked one. 15  Love not thế gian, neither the things that are in thế gian. nếu any man love thế gian, tình yêu of Cha is not in him. 16  For all that is in thế gian, the lust of the flesh, Và the lust of the eyes, Và the pride of sự sống, is not of Cha, Nhưng is of thế gian. 17  Và thế gian passeth away, Và the lust thereof: Nhưng he that doeth the will của ĐCT abideth for ever. 18  Little children, it is the last time: Và as ye have heard rằng antichrist shall come, even bây giờ are there many antichrists; whereby we know rằng it is the last time. 19  They went out from us, Nhưng they were not of us; for nếu they had been of us, they would no doubt have continued with us: Nhưng they went out, that they might be made manifest rằng they were not all of us. 20  Nhưng ye have an unction from the Holy One, Và ye know all things. 21  I have not written unto you because ye know not the truth, Nhưng because ye know it, Và rằng no lie is of the truth. 22  Who is a liar Nhưng he that denieth rằng ĐCGiê-su is Đấng Christ? He is antichrist, that denieth Cha Và the Son. 23  Whosoever denieth the Son, the same hath not Cha: (but) he that acknowledgeth the Son hath Cha also. 24 Letthat therefore abide in you, which ye have heard from ban đầu. nếu that which ye have heard from ban đầu shall remain in you, ye also shall continue in the Son, Và in Cha. 25  Và this is lời hứa that he hath promised us, even eternal sự sống. 26  These things have I written unto you concerning them that seduce you. 27  Nhưng the anointing which ye have received of him abideth in you, Và ye need not that any man teach you: Nhưng as the same anointing teacheth you of all things, Và is truth, Và is no lie, Và even as it hath taught you, ye shall abide in him. 28  Và bây giờ, little children, abide in him; that, when he shall appear, we may have confidence, Và not be ashamed before him at his coming. 29  nếu ye know rằng he is công chính, ye know rằng every one that doeth righteousness is born of him.
