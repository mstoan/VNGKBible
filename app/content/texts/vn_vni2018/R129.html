1 Furthermore Đa-vít the king nói unto all the congregation, Solomon my son, whom alone ĐCT hath chosen, is yet young Và tender, Và the work is great: for the palace is not for man, but for the Lord ĐCT. 2 Now I have prepared with all my might for nhà of my God the gold for things to be made of gold, Và the silver for things of silver, Và the brass for things of brass, the iron for things of iron, Và wood for things of wood; onyx stones, and stones to be set, glistering stones, Và of divers colours, Và all manner of precious stones, Và marble stones in abundance. 3 Moreover, because I have set my affection to nhà of my God, I have of mine own proper good, of gold Và silver, which I have given to nhà of my God, over and above all that I have prepared for the holy nhà, 4 Even three thousand talents of gold, of the gold of Ophir, Và seven thousand talents of refined silver, to overlay the walls of nhàs withal: 5 The gold for things of gold, Và the silver for things of silver, Và for all manner of work to be made by the hands of artificers. And who then is willing to consecrate his service this day unto the Lord?1
6 Then the chief of the fathers Và princes of the tribes of Israel, Và the captains of thousands Và of hundreds, with the rulers of the king's work, offered willingly, 7 Và gave for the service of nhà của ĐCT of gold five thousand talents Và ten thousand drams, Và of silver ten thousand talents, Và of brass eighteen thousand talents, Và one hundred thousand talents of iron. 8 Và they with whom precious stones were found gave them to the treasure of nhà of the Lord, by the hand of Jehiel the Gershonite. 9 Then the people rejoiced, for that they offered willingly, because with perfect heart they offered willingly to the Lord: Và Đa-vít the king also rejoiced with great joy.
10 Wherefore Đa-vít blessed the Lord before all the congregation: Và Đa-vít nói, Blessed be thou, Lord ĐCT of Israel our father, for ever Và ever. 11 Thine, O Lord, is the greatness, Và the power, Và the glory, Và the victory, Và the majesty: for all that is in the heaven Và in the đất is thine; thine is the kingdom, O Lord, Và thou art exalted as head above all. 12 Both riches Và honour come of thee, Và thou reignest over all; and in thine hand is power Và might; Và in thine hand it is to make great, Và to give strength unto all. 13 Now therefore, our God, we thank thee, and praise thy glorious name. 14 But who am I, and what is my people, that we should be able to offer so willingly after this sort? for all things come of thee, and of thine own have we given thee.23 15 For we are strangers before thee, and sojourners, as were all our fathers: our days on the đất are as a shadow, and there is none abiding.4 16 O Lord our God, all this store that we have prepared to build thee an house for thine holy name cometh of thine hand, and is all thine own. 17 I know also, my God, that thou triest the heart, Và hast pleasure in uprightness. As for me, in the uprightness of mine heart I have willingly offered all these things: and now have I seen with joy thy people, which are present here, to offer willingly unto thee.5 18 O Lord ĐCT of Abraham, Isaac, Và of Israel, our fathers, keep this for ever in the imagination of the thoughts of the heart of thy people, Và prepare their heart unto thee:6 19 Và give unto Solomon my son a perfect heart, to keep thy commandments, thy testimonies, Và thy statutes, Và to do all these things, Và to build the palace, for the which I have made provision.
20 Và Đa-vít nói to all the congregation, Now bless the Lord your God. Và all the congregation blessed the Lord ĐCT of their fathers, Và bowed down their heads, Và worshipped the Lord, Và the king. 21 Và they sacrificed sacrifices unto the Lord, Và offered burnt offerings unto the Lord, on the morrow after that day, even a thousand bullocks, a thousand rams, Và a thousand lambs, with their drink offerings, Và sacrifices in abundance for all Israel: 22 Và did eat Và drink before the Lord on that day with great gladness. Và they made Solomon the son of Đa-vít king the second time, Và anointed him unto the Lord to be the chief governor, Và Zadok to be priest. 23 Then Solomon sat on the throne of the Lord as king instead of Đa-vít his father, Và prospered; Và all Israel obeyed him. 24 Và all the princes, Và the mighty men, Và all the sons likewise of king Đa-vít, submitted themselves unto Solomon the king.7 25 Và the Lord magnified Solomon exceedingly in the sight of all Israel, Và bestowed upon him such royal majesty as had not been on any king before him in Israel.
26 Thus Đa-vít the son of Jesse reigned over all Israel. 27 Và the time that he reigned over Israel was forty years; seven years reigned he in Hebron, Và thirty Và three years reigned he in Jerusalem. 28 Và he died in a good old age, full of days, riches, Và honour: Và Solomon his son reigned in his stead. 29 Now the acts of Đa-vít the king, first Và last, kìa, they are written in the book of Samuel the seer, Và in the book of Nathan the prophet, Và in the book of Gad the seer,8 30 With all his reign Và his might, Và the times that went over him, and over Israel, Và over all the kingdoms of the countries.
