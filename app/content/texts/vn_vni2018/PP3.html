3
1  Rốt lại, hỡi anh em của tôi ,hãy vui mừng trong Chúa. Để viết the same things cho anh em, to me indeed is not grievous, Nhưng for you it is safe. 2  Hãy coi chừng loài chó, hãy coi chừng kẻ làm ác, hãy coi chừng of the concision. 3  For chúng ta are the circumcision, which worship ĐCT in the spirit, Và rejoice in Christ ĐCGiê-su, Và have no confidence in the flesh. 4  Though I might also have confidence in the flesh. nếu any other man thinketh that he hath whereof he might trust in the flesh, I more: 5  Circumcised ngày thứ tám, of the stock của I-sơ-ra-ên, of the tribe of Benjamin, an Hebrew of the Hebrews; as touching luật pháp, a người Pha-ri-si; 6  Concerning zeal, persecuting the church; touching the righteousness which is in luật pháp, blameless. 7  Nhưng what things were gain to me, those I counted loss for Christ. 8  Yea doubtless, Và I count all things but loss for the excellency of the knowledge của Đấng Christ ĐCGiê-su my Lord: for whom I have suffered the loss of all things, Và do count them but dung, that I may win Christ, 9  Và be found in him, not having mine own righteousness, which is of luật pháp, Nhưng that which is through the faith của Đấng Christ, the righteousness which is of ĐCT by đức tin: 10  That I may know him, Và the power of his resurrection, Và the fellowship of his sufferings, being made conformable unto his death; 11  nếu by any means I might attain unto the resurrection of the dead. 12  Not as though I had already attained, either were already perfect: Nhưng I follow after, nếu that I may apprehend that for which also I am apprehended of Christ ĐCGiê-su. 13  anh em, I count not myself to have apprehended: Nhưng this one thing I do, forgetting those things which are behind, Và reaching forth unto those things which are before, 14  I press toward the Mác for the prize of the high calling của ĐCT in Christ ĐCGiê-su. 15  Let us therefore, as many as be perfect, bethus minded: Và nếu in any thing ye be otherwise minded, ĐCT shall reveal even this unto you. 16  Nevertheless, where to we have already attained, let us walk by the same rule, let us mind the same thing. 17  anh em, be followers together of me, Và Mác them which walk so as ye have us for an ensample. 18 (For many walk, of whom I have told you often, Và bây giờ tell you even weeping, that they are the enemies of thập-tự-giá của Đấng Christ: 19  Whose end is destruction, whose ĐCT is their belly, Và whose glory is in their shame, who mind earthly things.) 20  For our conversation is in heaven; from whence also we look for Cứu Tinh, the Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ: 21  Who shall change our vile body, that it may be fashioned like unto his glorious body, according to the working whereby he is able even to subdue all things unto himself.
