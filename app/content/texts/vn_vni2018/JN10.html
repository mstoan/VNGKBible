10
1  Quả thật, Quả thật, I say unto you, He that entereth not by the door into the sheepfold, Nhưng climbeth up some other way, the same is a thief Và a robber. 2  Nhưng he that entereth in by the door is the shepherd of the sheep. 3  To him the porter openeth; Và the sheep hear his voice: Và he calleth his own sheep by tên, Và leadeth them out. 4  Và when he putteth forth his own sheep, he goeth before them, Và the sheep follow him: for they know his voice. 5  Và a stranger will they not follow, Nhưng will flee from him: for they know not the voice of strangers. 6  This parable spake ĐCGiê-su unto them: Nhưng they understood not what things they were which he spake unto them. 7  Then nói ĐCGiê-su unto them again, Quả thật, Quả thật, I say unto you, I am the door of the sheep. 8  All that ever đến before me are thieves Và robbers: Nhưng the sheep did not hear them. 9  I am the door: by me nếu any man enter in, he shall được cứu, Và shall go in Và out, Và find pasture. 10  The thief cometh not, Nhưng for to steal, Và to kill, Và to destroy: I am come that they might have sự sống, Và that they might have it more abundantly. 11  I am the good shepherd: the good shepherd giveth his sự sống for the sheep. 12  Nhưng he that is an hireling, Và not the shepherd, whose own the sheep are not, seeth the wolf coming, Và leaveth the sheep, Và fleeth: Và the wolf catcheth them, Và scattereth the sheep. 13  The hireling fleeth, because he is an hireling, Và careth not for the sheep. 14  I am the good shepherd, Và know my sheep, Và am known of mine. 15  As the Father knoweth me, even so know I the Father: Và I lay down my sự sống for the sheep. 16  Và other sheep I have, which are not of this fold: them also I must bring, Và they shall hear my voice; Và there shall be một fold, Và một shepherd. 17  Therefore doth my Father love me, because I lay down my sự sống, that I might take it again. 18  không ai taketh it from me, Nhưng I lay it down of myself. I have power to lay it down, Và I have power to take it again. This commandment have I received of my Father. 19  There was a division therefore again among the Jews for these sayings. 20  Và many of them nói, He hath a devil, Và is mad; Tại sao hear ye him? 21  Others nói, These are not the words of him that hath a devil. Can a devil open the eyes of the blind? 22  Và it was at Giê-ru-sa-lem the lễ hội of the dedication, Và it was winter. 23  Và ĐCGiê-su walked in the temple in Sa-lô-môn's porch. 24  Then đến the Jews round about him, Và nói unto him, How long dost thou make us to doubt? nếu thou be Đấng Christ, tell us plainly. 25  ĐCGiê-su trả lời them, I told you, Và ye believed not: the works that I do in my Father's tên, they bear witness of me. 26  Nhưng ye believe not, because ye are not of my sheep, as I nói unto you. 27  My sheep hear my voice, Và I know them, Và they follow me: 28  Và I give unto them eternal sự sống; Và they shall never perish, neither shall any man pluck them out of my hand. 29  My Father, which cho them me, is greater than all; Và no man is able to pluck them out of my Father's hand. 30  I Và my Father are một. 31  Then the Jews took up stones again to stone him. 32  ĐCGiê-su trả lời them, Many good works have I shewed you from my Father; for which of those works do ye stone me? 33  The Jews trả lời him, Nói rằng, For a good work we stone thee not; Nhưng for blasphemy; Và because that thou, being một người, makest thyself ĐCT. 34  ĐCGiê-su trả lời them, Is it not written in your law, I nói, Ye are gods? 35  nếu he called them gods, unto whom the word của ĐCT đến, Và the scripture can not be broken; 36  Say ye of him, whom the Father hath sanctified, Và sent into thế gian, Thou blasphemest; because I nói, I am the Son của ĐCT? 37  nếu I do not the works of my Father, believe me not. 38  Nhưng nếu I do, though ye believe not me, believe the works: that ye may know, Và believe, that the Father is in me, Và I in him. 39  Therefore they sought again to take him: Nhưng he escaped out of their hand, 40  Và went away again beyond Giô-đanh into the place where Giăng atfirst baptized; Và there he abode. 41  Và many resorted unto him, Và nói, Giăng did no miracle: Nhưng all things that Giăng spake of this man were true. 42  Và many believed on him there.
