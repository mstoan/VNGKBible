13
1  Trong Ngày ấy ĐCGiê-su đi ra khỏi nhà, [và] ngồi bên bờ biển. 2 Và đám đông họp lại trước Ngài, đến đổi Ngài đi vào trong một chiếc thuyền để ngồi; và cả đám đông đứng trên bờ biển. 3  Và he spake many things unto them in parables, saying, kìa, a sower went forth to sow; 4  Và when he sowed, some seeds rơi by the way side, Và the fowls đến Và devoured them up: 5  Some rơi upon stony places, where they had not much đất: Và forthwith they sprung up, because they had no deepness of đất: 6  Và when mặt trời was up, they were scorched; Và because they had no root, they withered away. 7  Và some rơi among thorns; Và the thorns sprung up, Và choked them: 8  Nhưng other rơi into good ground, Và brought forth fruit, some an hundredfold, some sixtyfold, some thirtyfold. 9  Who hath ears to hear, let him hear. 10  Và các môn đệ đến, và nói unto him, Why speakest thou unto them in parables? 11  He trả lời và nói unto them, Because it is given unto you to know the mysteries of the kingdom of heaven, Nhưng to them it is not given. 12  For whosoever hath, to him shall be given, Và he shall have more abundance: Nhưng whosoever hath not, from him shall be taken away even that he hath. 13  Therefore speak I to them in parables: because they seeing see not; Và hearing they hear not, neither do they understand. 14  Và in them is fulfilled the prophecy of Esaias, which nói, By hearing ye shall hear, Và shall not understand; Và seeing ye shall see, Và shall not perceive: 15  For this people's heart is waxed gross, Và their ears are dull of hearing, Và their eyes they have closed; lest at any time they should see with their eyes, Và hear with their ears, Và should understand with their heart, Và should be converted, Và I should heal them. 16  Nhưng blessed are your eyes, for they see: Và your ears, for they hear. 17  For Quả thật I say unto you, That many prophets Và righteous men have desired to see those things which ye see, Và have not seen them; Và to hear those things which ye hear, Và have not heard them. 18  Hear ye therefore the parable of the sower. 19  When any one heareth the word of the kingdom, Và understandeth it not, then cometh the wicked one, Và catcheth away that which was sown in his heart. This is he which received seed by the way side. 20  Nhưng he that received the seed into stony places, the same is he that heareth the word, Và anon with joy receiveth it; 21  Yet hath he not root in himself, Nhưng dureth for a while: for when tribulation hay bắt bớ ariseth because of the word, by and by he is offended. 22  He also that received seed among the thorns is he that heareth the word; Và the care of this world, Và the deceitfulness of riches, choke the word, Và he becometh unfruitful. 23  Nhưng he that received seed into the good ground is he that heareth the word, Và understandeth it; which also beareth fruit, Và bringeth forth, some an hundredfold, some sixty, some thirty. 24  Another parable put he forth unto them, saying, The kingdom of heaven is likened unto a man which sowed good seed in his field: 25  Nhưng while men slept, his enemy đến Và sowed tares among the wheat, Và went his way. 26  Nhưng when the blade was sprung up, Và brought forth fruit, then appeared the tares also. 27 So the servants of nhàholder đến và nói unto him, Sir, didst not thou sow good seed in thy field? from whence then hath it tares? 28  He nói unto them, An enemy hath done this. The servants nói unto him, Wilt thou then that we go Và gather them up? 29 Nhưng he nói, Nay; lest while ye gather up the tares, ye root up also the wheat with them. 30 Let both grow together until the harvest: Và in the time of harvest I will say to the reapers, Gather ye together first the tares, Và bind them in bundles to burn them: Nhưng gather the wheat into my barn.
31 Another parable put he forth unto them, saying, The kingdom of heaven is like to a grain of mustard seed, which a man took, Và sowed in his field: 32 Which indeed is the least of all seeds: Nhưng when it is grown, it is the greatest among herbs, Và becometh a tree, đến đổi the birds of the air come Và lodge in the branches thereof.
33 Another parable spake he unto them; The kingdom of heaven is like unto leaven, which một người đờn bà took, Và hid in three measures of meal, till the whole was leavened. 34 All these things spake ĐCGiê-su unto đám đông in parables; Và without a parable spake he not unto them: 35 That it might be fulfilled which was spoken by the prophet, saying, I will open my mouth in parables; I will utter things which have been kept secret from the foundation of the world. 36 Then ĐCGiê-su sent đám đông away, Và went into nhà: Và his disciples đến unto him, saying, Declare unto us the parable of the tares of the field. 37  He trả lời và nói unto them, He that soweth the good seed is the Son of man; 38  The field is the world; the good seed are the children of the kingdom; Nhưng the tares are the children of the wicked one; 39  The enemy that sowed them is the devil; the harvest is the end of the world; Và the reapers are các thiên sứ. 40 As therefore the tares are gathered Và burned in the fire; so shall it be in the end of this world. 41 The Son of man shall send forth his các thiên sứ, Và they shall gather out of his kingdom all things that offend, Và them which do iniquity; 42 Và shall cast them into a furnace of fire: there shall be wailing Và gnashing of teeth. 43 Then shall the righteous shine forth as mặt trời in the kingdom of their Father. Who hath ears to hear, let him hear.
44 Again, the kingdom of heaven is like unto treasure hid in a field; the which when a man hath found, he hideth, Và for joy thereof goeth Và selleth all that he hath, Và buyeth that field.
45  Again, the kingdom of heaven is like unto a merchant man, seeking goodly pearls: 46 Who, when he had found một pearl of great price, went Và sold all that he had, Và bought it.
47 Again, the kingdom of heaven is like unto a net, that was cast into biển, Và gathered of every kind: 48 Which, when it was full, they drew to shore, Và sat down, Và gathered the good into vessels, Nhưng cast the bad away. 49 So shall it be at the end of the world: các thiên sứ shall come forth, Và sever the wicked from among the just, 50 Và shall cast them into the furnace of fire: there shall be wailing Và gnashing of teeth. 51  ĐCGiê-su nói unto them, Have ye understood all these things? They say unto him, Yea, Lord. 52 Then nói he unto them, Therefore every scribe which is instructed unto the kingdom of heaven is like unto a man that is an householder, which bringeth forth out of his treasure things new Và old.
53 Và xảy đến, that when ĐCGiê-su had finished these parables, he departed thence. 54 Và when he was come into his own country, he taught them in their nhà hội, insomuch that they were astonished, Và nói, Whence hath this man this wisdom, Và these mighty works? 55 Is not this the carpenter's son? is not his mẹ called Ma-ri? Và his anh em, Gia-cơ, Và Joses, Và Si-môn, Và Giu-đa? 56 Và his sisters, are they not all with us? Whence then hath this man all these things? 57 Và they were offended in him. Nhưng ĐCGiê-su nói unto them, A prophet is not without honour, save in his own country, Và in his own nhà. 58 Và Ngài không làm nhiều phép lạ [ở] đó vì cớ sự không tin của họ.
