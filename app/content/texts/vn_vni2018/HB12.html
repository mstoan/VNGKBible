12
1  Vậy nên seeing chúng ta also are compassed about with so great a cloud of witnesses, let us lay aside every weight, Và the sin which doth so easily beset us, andlet us run với patience the race that is set before us, 2  Looking unto ĐCGiê-su the author Và finisher of our đức tin; who for the joy that was set before him endured thập-tự-giá, despising the shame, Và is set down at the right hand of the throne của ĐCT. 3  For consider him that endured such contradiction of sinners against himself, lest ye be wearied Và faint in your minds. 4  Ye have not yet resisted unto huyết, striving against sin. 5  Và ye have forgotten the exhortation which speaketh cùng các ngươi as unto children, My son, despise không thou the chastening của Chúa, nor faint when thou art rebuked of him: 6  For whom the Lord loveth he chasteneth, Và scourgeth every son whom he nhận. 7  nếu ye endure chastening, ĐCT dealeth with you as with sons; for what son is he whom the father chasteneth không? 8  Nhưng nếu ye be without chastisement, whereof all are partakers, then are ye bastards, Và không sons. 9  Furthermore we have had fathers of our flesh which corrected us, Và we gave họ reverence: shall we không much rather be in subjection unto Cha of spirits, Và live? 10  For they Quả thật for a few ngày chastened us after their own pleasure; Nhưng he for our profit, that we might be partakers of his holiness. 11  bây giờ no chastening for the present seemeth to be joyous, Nhưng grievous: nevertheless afterward it yieldeth the peaceable fruit of sự công chính với họ which are exercised there by. 12  Vậy nên lift up tay which hang down, Và the feeble knees; 13  Và make straight paths for your chân, lest that which is lame be turned out of the way; Nhưng let it rather be healed. 14  Follow peace với all men, Và holiness, without which không ai sẽ thấy the Lord: 15  Looking diligently lest any man fail of the grace của ĐCT; lest any root of bitterness springing up trouble you, Và there by many be defiled; 16  Lest there be any fornicator, hay profane person, as Ê-sau, who for một morsel of meat sold his birthright. 17  For ye know how that afterward, when he would have inherited the blessing, he was rejected: for he found no place of repentance, though he sought it carefully với tears. 18  For ye are không come unto the mount that might be touched, Và that burned bằng lửa, nor unto blackness, Và tối tăm, Và tempest, 19  Và the sound of a trumpet, Và the voice of words; which tiếng they that heard intreated that the word should không be spoken to them any more: 20 (For they could không endure that which was commanded, Và if so much as a beast touch the mountain, it shall be stoned, hay thrust through with a dart: 21  Và so terrible was the sight, that Môi-se nói, I exceedingly fear Và quake:) 22  Nhưng ye are come unto mount Si-ôn, Và unto the city of the living ĐCT, the heavenly Giê-ru-sa-lem, Và to an innumerable company of angels, 23  To the general assembly Và hội thánh of the firstborn, which are written in heaven, Và to God the Judge of all, Và to the spirits of just men made perfect, 24  Và to Jesus the mediator of the new covenant, Và to the blood of sprinkling, that speaketh better things than that of Abel. 25  thấy that ye refuse không him that speaketh. For nếu they escaped không who refused him that spake on đất, much more shall not chúng ta escape, if we turn away from him that speaketh từ heaven: 26  Whose tiếng then shook đất: Nhưng bây giờ he hath promised, Nói rằng, Yet once more I shake không đất only, Nhưng also heaven. 27  Và này word, Yet once more, signifieth the removing of those things that are shaken, as of things that are made, that those things which không thể be shaken may remain. 28  Vậy nên we receiving một vương quốc which cannot be moved, let us have ân-điển, whereby we may serve ĐCT acceptably với reverence Và godly fear: 29  For our ĐCT is a consuming lửa.
