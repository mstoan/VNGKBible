12
1 Then ĐCGiê-su six days before the passover đến to Bê-tha-ni, where La-xa-rơ was which had been dead, whom he raised from the dead. 2 There they made him a supper; Và Ma-thê served: Nhưng La-xa-rơ was một of them that sat at the table with him. 3 Then took Ma-ri a pound of ointment of spikenard, very costly, Và anointed the feet của ĐCGiê-su, Và wiped his feet with her hair: Và nhà was filled with the odour of the ointment. 4 Then nói một of his các môn đệ, Giu-đa Ích-ca-ri-ốt, Si-môn's son, which should betray him, 5 Why was not this ointment sold for three hundred pence, Và given to the poor? 6 This he nói, not that he cared for the poor; Nhưng because he was a thief, Và had the bag, Và bare what was put therein. 7 Then nói ĐCGiê-su, Let her alone: against the day of my burying hath she kept this. 8 For the poor always ye have with you; Nhưng me ye have not always. 9 Much people of the Jews therefore knew that he was there: Và they came not for Jesus' sake only, Nhưng that they might see La-xa-rơ also, whom he had raised from the dead.
10 Nhưng các thầy tế lễ consulted that they might put La-xa-rơ also to death; 11 Because that by reason of him many of the Jews went away, Và believed on ĐCGiê-su.
12 On the next day much people that were come to the feast, when they heard that ĐCGiê-su was coming to Giê-ru-sa-lem, 13 Took branches of palm trees, Và went forth to meet him, Và cried, Hosanna: Blessed is vua của I-sơ-ra-ên that cometh in the name của Chúa. 14 Và ĐCGiê-su, when he had found a young ass, sat thereon; as it is written, 15 Fear not, daughter of Sion: kìa, thy King cometh, sitting on an ass's colt. 16 These things understood not his các môn đệ at the first: Nhưng when ĐCGiê-su was glorified, then remembered they that these things were written of him, Và that they had done these things unto him. 17 The people therefore that was with him when he called La-xa-rơ out of his grave, Và raised him from the dead, bare record. 18 For this cause the people also met him, for that they heard that he had done this miracle. 19 những người Pha-ri-si therefore nói among themselves, Perceive ye how ye prevail nothing? kìa, thế gian is gone after him.
20 Và there were certain Greeks among them that came up để thờ phượng at the feast: 21 The same đến therefore to Phi-líp, which was of Bethnóia of Ga-li-lê, Và desired him, Nói rằng, Sir, we would see ĐCGiê-su. 22 Phi-líp cometh Và telleth An-rê: Và again An-rê Và Phi-líp tell ĐCGiê-su.
23 Và ĐCGiê-su trả lời them, Nói rằng, The hour is come, that the Son of man should be glorified. 24 Quả thật, Quả thật, I say unto you, Except a corn of wheat fall into the ground and die, it abideth alone: Nhưng nếu it die, it bringeth forth much fruit. 25 He that loveth his sự sống shall lose it; Và he that hateth his sự sống in this world shall keep it unto sự sống eternal. 26 nếu any man serve me, let him follow me; Và where I am, there shall also my servant be: nếu any man serve me, him will my Father honour. 27 bây giờ is my soul troubled; Và what shall I say? Father, save me from this hour: Nhưng for this cause came I unto this hour. 28 Father, glorify thy tên. Then came there a voice from heaven, Nói rằng, I have both glorified it, Và will glorify it again. 29 The people therefore, that stood by, Và heard it, nói that it thundered: others nói, An angel spake to him. 30 ĐCGiê-su trả lời Và nói, This voice đến not because of me, Nhưng for your sakes. 31 bây giờ is the judgment of this world: bây giờ shall the prince of this world be cast out. 32 Và I, nếu I be lifted up from the đất, will draw all men unto me. 33 This he nói, signifying what death he should die. 34 The people trả lời him, chúng ta have heard out of the law that Christ abideth for ever: Và how sayest thou, The Son of man must be lifted up? who is this Son of man? 35 Then ĐCGiê-su nói unto them, Yet a little while is the light with you. Walk while ye have the light, lest tối tăm come upon you: for he that walketh in tối tăm knoweth not whither he goeth. 36 While ye have light, believe in the light, that ye may be the children of light. These things spake ĐCGiê-su, Và departed, Và did hide himself from them.
37 Nhưng though he had done so many miracles before them, yet they believed not on him: 38 That the saying of Esaias the prophet might be fulfilled, which he spake, Lord, who hath believed our report? Và to whom hath the arm của Chúa been revealed? 39 Therefore they could not believe, because that Ê-sai nói again, 40 He hath blinded their mắt, Và hardened their heart; that they should not see with their mắt, nor understand with their heart, Và be converted, Và I should heal them. 41 These things nói Ê-sai, when he saw his glory, Và spake of him.
42 Nevertheless among the chief rulers also many believed on him; Nhưng because of the những người Pha-ri-si they did not confess him, lest they should be put out of the nhà hội: 43 For they loved the praise of men more than the praise của ĐCT.
44 ĐCGiê-su cried Và nói, He that believeth on me, believeth not on me, Nhưng on him that sent me. 45 Và he that seeth me seeth him that sent me. 46 I am come a light into thế gian, that whosoever believeth on me should not abide in tối tăm. 47 Và nếu any man hear my words, Và believe not, I judge him not: for I came not to judge thế gian, Nhưng to save thế gian. 48 He that rejecteth me, Và receiveth not my words, hath one that judgeth him: the word that I have spoken, the same shall judge him in the last day. 49 For I have not spoken of myself; Nhưng the Father which sent me, he cho me a commandment, what I should say, Và what I should speak. 50 Và I know that his commandment is sự sống đời đời: whatsoever I speak therefore, even as the Father nói unto me, so I speak.
