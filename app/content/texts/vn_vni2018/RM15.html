15
1 chúng ta then that are strong ought to bear the infirmities of the weak, Và not to please ourselves. 2 Let every one of us please his neighbour for his good to edification. 3 For even Christ pleased not himself; Nhưng, as it is written, The reproaches of them that reproached thee rơi on me. 4  Vậy bất cứ những gì được viết trước cho chúng ta sự dạy dổ, hầu cho bởi sự nhịn nhục và yên ủi của Kinh Thánh chúng ta có sự hy vọng. 5 bây giờ the God of patience Và consolation grant you to be likeminded một toward another according to Christ ĐCGiê-su: 6 That ye may with one mind Và một mouth glorify ĐCT, even the Father of our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ. 7 Wherefore receive ye one another, as Christ also received us to the glory của ĐCT. 8 bây giờ I say that ĐCGiê-su Christ was a minister of the circumcision for the truth của ĐCT, to confirm lời hứas made unto the fathers: 9 Và that the Gentiles might glorify ĐCT for his mercy; as it is written, For this cause I will confess to thee among the Gentiles, Và sing unto thy name. 10 Và again he nói, Rejoice, ye Gentiles, with his people. 11 Và again, Praise the Lord, all ye Gentiles; Và laud him, all ye people. 12 Và again, Esaias nói, There shall be a root of Jesse, Và he that shall rise to reign over the Gentiles; in him shall the Gentiles trust. 13 bây giờ the God of hy vọng fill you with all joy Và peace in believing, that ye may abound in hy vọng, through the power of the Holy Ghost. 14 Và I myself also am persuaded of you, my anh em, that ye also are full of goodness, filled with all knowledge, able also to admonish one another. 15 Nevertheless, anh em, I have written the more boldly unto you in some sort, as putting you in mind, because of the grace that is given to me of ĐCT, 16 That I should be the minister của ĐCGiê-su Christ to the Gentiles, ministering phúc-âm của ĐCT, that the offering up of the Gentiles might be acceptable, being sanctified by the Holy Ghost. 17 I have therefore whereof I may glory through ĐCGiê-su Christ in those things which pertain to ĐCT. 18 For I will not dare to speak of any of those things which Christ hath not wrought by me, to make the Gentiles obedient, by word Và deed, 19 Through mighty signs Và wonders, by the power of the Spirit của ĐCT; đến đổi from Giê-ru-sa-lem, Và round about unto Illyricum, I have fully preached phúc-âm của Đấng Christ. 20 Yea, so have I strived to preach phúc-âm, not where Christ was named, lest I should build upon another man's foundation: 21 Nhưng as it is written, To whom he was not spoken of, they shall see: Và they that have not heard shall understand. 22 For which cause also I have been much hindered from coming to you. 23 Nhưng bây giờ having no more place in these parts, Và having a great desire these many years to come unto you; 24 Whensoever I take my journey into Spain, I will come to you: for I trust to see you in my journey, Và to be brought on my way thitherward by you, nếu first I be somewhat filled with your company. 25 Nhưng bây giờ I go unto Giê-ru-sa-lem to minister unto the saints. 26 For it hath pleased them of Macedonia Và Achaia to make a certain contribution for the poor saints which are at Giê-ru-sa-lem. 27 It hath pleased them Quả thật; Và their debtors they are. For nếu the Gentiles have been made partakers of their spiritual things, their duty is also to minister unto them in carnal things. 28 When therefore I have performed this, Và have sealed to them this fruit, I will come by you into Spain. 29 Và I am sure that, when I come unto you, I shall come in the fulness of the blessing of phúc-âm của Đấng Christ. 30 bây giờ tôi nài khuyên you, anh em, for the Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ's sake, Và for tình yêu of the Spirit, that ye strive together with me in your prayers to ĐCT for me; 31 That I may be delivered from them that do not believe in Judaea; Và that my service which I have for Giê-ru-sa-lem may be accepted of the saints; 32 That I may come unto you with joy by the will của ĐCT, Và may with you be refreshed. 33 bây giờ the God of peace be with you all. Amen.
