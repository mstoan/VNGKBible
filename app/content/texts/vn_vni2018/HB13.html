13
1  Let brotherly love continue. 2  Be not forgetful to entertain strangers: for there by some have entertained các thiên sứ unawares. 3  Remember them that are in bonds, as bound with them; Và them which suffer adversity, as being yourselves also in the body. 4  Marriage is honourable in all, Và the bed undefiled: Nhưng whoremongers Và adulterers ĐCT will judge. 5  Let your conversation be without covetousness; Và be content with such things as ye have: for he hath nói, I will never leave thee, nor forsake thee. 6  đến đổi we may boldly say, The Lord is my helper, Và I will not fear what man shall do unto me. 7  Remember them which have the rule over you, who have spoken unto you the word của ĐCT: whose đức tin follow, considering the end of their conversation. 8  ĐCGiê-su Christ the same yesterday, Và to day, Và for ever. 9  Be not carried about with divers Và strange doctrines. For it is a good thing that the heart be established with grace; not with meats, which have not profited them that have been occupied therein. 10  We have an altar, where of they have no right để ăn which serve the tabernacle. 11  For the bodies of those beasts, whose blood is brought into the sanctuary by thầy thượng phẩm for sin, are burned without the camp. 12  Wherefore ĐCGiê-su also, that he might sanctify the people with his own blood, suffered without the gate. 13  Let us go forth therefore unto him without the camp, bearing his reproach. 14  For here have we no continuing city, Nhưng we seek one to come. 15  By him therefore let us offer the sacrifice of praise to God continually, that is, the fruit of our lips giving thanks to his tên. 16  Nhưng to do good Và to communicate forget not: for with such sacrifices ĐCT is well pleased. 17  Obey them that have the rule over you, Và submit yourselves: for they watch for your souls, as they that must give account, that they may do it with joy, Và not with grief: for that is unprofitable for you. 18  cầu nguyện for us: for we trust we have a good conscience, in all things willing to live honestly. 19  Nhưng tôi nài khuyên you the rather to do this, that I may be restored to you the sooner. 20  bây giờ the God of peace, that brought again from the dead our Lord ĐCGiê-su, that great shepherd of the sheep, through the blood of the đời đời covenant, 21  Make you perfect in every good work to do his will, working in you that which is wellpleasing in his sight, through ĐCGiê-su Christ; to whom be glory for ever Và ever. Amen. 22  Và tôi nài khuyên you, anh em, suffer the word of exhortation: for I have written a letter unto you in few words. 23  Know ye that our brother Ti-mô-thê is set at liberty; with whom, nếu he come shortly, I will see you. 24  Salute all them that have the rule over you, Và all thánh đồ. They of Italy salute you. 25  ân-điển be with you all. Amen.
