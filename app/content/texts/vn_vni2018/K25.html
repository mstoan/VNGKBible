5
1  Now Naaman, captain of the host of the king of Si-ri, was a great man với his master, Và honourable, because by him the Lord had given deliverance unto Si-ri: he was also a mighty man in valour, but he was a leper. 1 2 3 2  Và the Syrians had gone out by companies, Và had brought away captive out of the land của I-sơ-ra-ên a little maid; Và she waited on Naaman's vợ. 4 3  Và she nói unto her mistress, Would God my lord were với tiên tri that is in Sa-ma-ri! for he would recover him of his leprosy. 5 6 4 Và one went in, Và told his lord, saying, Thus and thus nói the maid that is of the land của I-sơ-ra-ên. 5  Và the king of Si-ri nói, Go to, go, Và I will send a letter unto the king của I-sơ-ra-ên. Và he departed, Và took with him mười talents of silver, Và six ngàn pieces of gold, Và ten changes of raiment. 7 6  Và he brought the letter to the king của I-sơ-ra-ên, saying, Now when this letter is come unto thee, kìa, I have therewith sent Naaman my servant to thee, that thou mayest recover him of his leprosy. 7  Và xảy đến, when the king của I-sơ-ra-ên had read the letter, that he rent his clothes, và nói, Am I God, to kill Và to make alive, that this man doth send unto me to recover một người of his leprosy? wherefore consider, I pray you, and see how he seeketh a quarrel against me. 8  Và it was so, when Elisha the man của ĐCT had heard that the king của I-sơ-ra-ên had rent his clothes, that he sent to the king, saying, Wherefore hast thou rent thy clothes? let him come now to me, and he shall know that there is a prophet in I-sơ-ra-ên. 9  So Naaman đến with his horses Và with his chariot, Và stood at the door của nhà of Elisha. 10  Và Elisha sent a messenger unto him, saying, Go Và wash in Giô-đanh bảy times, Và thy flesh shall come again to thee, and thou shalt be clean. 11  But Naaman was wroth, Và went away, và nói, kìa, I thought, He will surely come out to me, and stand, Và call on the name of the Lord his God, Và strike his hand over the place, Và recover the leper. 8 9 10 12  Are not Abana Và Pharpar, rivers of Đa-mách, better than all the waters của I-sơ-ra-ên? may I not wash in them, and be clean? So he turned Và went away in a rage. 11 13  Và his servants came near, Và spake unto him, and nói, My father, nếu tiên tri had bid thee do some great thing, wouldest thou not have done it? how much rather then, when he nói to thee, Wash, Và be clean? 14  Then went he down, Và dipped himself seven times in Giô-đanh, according to the saying of the man của ĐCT: Và his flesh came again like unto the flesh of a little child, Và he was clean. 15  Và he returned to the man của ĐCT, he and all his company, Và came, Và stood before him: and he nói, kìa, now I know that there is no God in all đất, but in I-sơ-ra-ên: now therefore, I pray thee, take a blessing of thy servant. 16  But he nói, As the Lord liveth, before whom I stand, I will receive none. And he urged him to take it; but he refused. 17  Và Naaman nói, Shall there not then, I pray thee, be given to thy servant hai mules' burden of đất? for thy servant will henceforth offer neither burnt offering nor sacrifice unto other gods, but unto the Lord. 18  In this thing the Lord pardon thy servant, that when my master goeth into nhà of Rimmon to worship there, and he leaneth on my hand, Và I bow myself in nhà of Rimmon: when I bow down myself in nhà of Rimmon, the Lord pardon thy servant in this thing. 19  Và he nói unto him, Go in peace. So he departed from him a little way. 12 20  But Gehazi, the servant of Elisha the man của ĐCT, nói, kìa, my master hath spared Naaman this Syrian, in not receiving at his hands that which he brought: but, as the Lord liveth, I will run after him, and take somewhat of him. 21  So Gehazi followed after Naaman. Và when Naaman nhìn thấy him running after him, he lighted down from the chariot to meet him, and nói, Is all well? 13 22  Và he nói, All is well. My master hath sent me, saying, kìa, even now there be come to me from mount Ép-ra-im hai young men of the sons of các tiên tri: give them, I pray thee, a talent of silver, Và two changes of garments. 23  Và Naaman nói, Be content, take two talents. Và he urged him, and bound hai talents of silver in two bags, with two changes of garments, Và laid họ upon two of his servants; Và they bare họ before him. 24  Và when he came to the tower, he took họ from their hand, Và bestowed họ in nhà: Và he let the men go, Và they departed. 14 25  But he went in, Và stood before his master. Và Elisha nói unto him, Whence comest thou, Gehazi? Và he nói, Thy servant went no whither. 15 26  Và he nói unto him, Went not mine heart with thee, when the man turned again from his chariot to meet thee? Is it a time to receive tiền, Và to receive garments, Và oliveyards, Và vườn nhos, Và sheep, Và oxen, Và menservants, Và maidservants? 27  The leprosy therefore of Naaman shall cleave unto thee, and unto thy seed for ever. Và he went out from his presence a leper as white as snow.
