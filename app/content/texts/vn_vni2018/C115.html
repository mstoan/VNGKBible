15
1  Moreover, anh em, I declare unto you phúc-âm which I preached unto you, which also ye have received, Và wherein ye stand; 2  By which also ye are saved, nếu ye keep in memory what I preached unto you, unless ye have believed in vain. 3  For I delivered unto you first of all that which I also received, how that Christ died for our tội lỗi according to the scriptures; 4  Và rằng he was buried, Và rằng he rose again the third day according to the scriptures: 5  Và rằng he was seen of Sê-pha, then of the mười hai: 6  After that, he was seen of above five hundred anh em at once; of whom the greater part remain unto this present, Nhưng some are fallen asleep. 7  After that, he was seen of Gia-cơ; then of all các sứ đồ. 8  Và last of all he was seen of me also, as of one born out of due time. 9  For I am the least of the apostles, that am không meet to be called an apostle, because I persecuted hội thánh của ĐCT. 10  Nhưng by the grace của ĐCT I am what I am: Và his ân-điển which was bestowed upon me was không in vain; Nhưng I laboured more abundantly than they all: yet không I, Nhưng the grace của ĐCT which was với me. 11  Therefore hoặc it were I or they, so we preach, Và so ye believed. 12  bây giờ nếu Christ be preached rằng he rose từ the dead, how say some among you rằng there is no resurrection of the dead? 13  Nhưng nếu there be no resurrection of the dead, then is Christ không risen: 14  Và nếu Christ be không risen, then is our preaching vain, Và your đức tin is also vain. 15  Yea, Và we are found false witnesses của ĐCT; because we have testified of ĐCT rằng he raised up Christ: whom he raised không up, if so be that the dead rise không. 16  For nếu the dead rise không, then is không Christ raised: 17  Và nếu Christ be không raised, your đức tin is vain; ye are yet in your tội lỗi. 18  Then they also which are fallen asleep in Christ are perished. 19  nếu in this sự sống only we have hy vọng in Christ, we are of all men most miserable. 20  Nhưng bây giờ is Christ risen từ the dead, Và become the firstfruits of them that slept. 21  For since by man đến death, by man đến also sự sống lại of the dead. 22  For as in A-đam all chết, even so in Christ shall all be made alive. 23  Nhưng every man in his own order: Christ the firstfruits; afterward they that are Christ's at his coming. 24  Then cometh the end, when he shall have delivered up vương quốc to God, even Cha; when he shall have put down all rule Và all authority Và power. 25  For he must reign, till he hath put all enemies under his chân. 26  The last enemy that shall be destroyed is death. 27  For he hath put all things under his chân. Nhưng when he nói all things are put under him, it is manifest rằng he is excepted which did put all things under him. 28  Và when all things shall be subdued unto him, then shall Con also himself be subject unto him that put all things under him, that ĐCT may be all in all. 29  Else what shall they do which are baptized for the dead, nếu the dead rise không at all? Tại sao are they then baptized for the dead? 30  Và Tại sao stand chúng ta in jeopardy every hour? 31  I protest by your rejoicing which I have in Christ ĐCGiê-su our Lord, I die daily. 32  nếu after the manner of men I have fought with beasts at Ephesus, what advantageth it me, nếu the dead rise không? let us eat Và uống; for to morrow we die. 33  Be không deceived: evil communications corrupt good manners. 34  Awake to righteousness, Và sin không; for some have not the knowledge của ĐCT: I speak this to your shame. 35  Nhưng some man will say, How are the dead raised up? Và with what body do they come? 36  Thou fool, that which thou sowest is không quickened, except it die: 37  Và that which thou sowest, thou sowest không that body that shall be, Nhưng bare grain, it may chance of wheat, hay of some other grain: 38  Nhưng ĐCT giveth it a body as it hath pleased him, Và to every seed his own body. 39  All flesh is không the same flesh: Nhưng there is một kind of flesh of men, another flesh of beasts, another of fishes, Và another of birds. 40  There are also celestial bodies, Và bodies terrestrial: Nhưng sự vinh hiển of the celestial is một, Và the glory of the terrestrial is another. 41  There is một glory of mặt trời, Và another glory of mặt trăng, Và another glory of ngôi saos: for one ngôi sao differeth from another ngôi sao in glory. 42  So also is sự sống lại of the dead. It is sown in sự hư nát; it is raised in incorruption: 43  It is sown in dishonour; it is raised in glory: it is sown in weakness; it is raised in power: 44  It is sown a natural body; it is raised a spiritual body. There is a natural body, Và there is a spiritual body. 45  Và so it is written, The first man A-đam was made a living soul; the last A-đam was made a quickening spirit. 46  Howbeit that was không first which is spiritual, Nhưng that which is natural; Và afterward that which is spiritual. 47  The first man is of đất, earthy: the second man is the Lord từ heaven. 48  As is the earthy, such are they also that are earthy: Và as is the heavenly, such are they also that are heavenly. 49  Và as we have borne the image of the earthy, we shall also bear the image of the heavenly. 50  bây giờ this I say, anh em, rằng flesh Và huyết cannot inherit vương quốc của ĐCT; neither doth sự hư nát inherit incorruption. 51  kìa, I shew you a mystery; We shall không all sleep, Nhưng we shall all be changed, 52  In a moment, in the twinkling of an eye, at the last trump: for the trumpet shall sound, Và the dead shall be raised incorruptible, Và chúng ta shall be changed. 53  For this corruptible must put on incorruption, Và this mortal must put on immortality. 54  So when this corruptible shall have put on incorruption, Và this mortal shall have put on immortality, then shall be brought to pass the saying that is written, Death is swallowed up in victory. 55  O death, where is thy sting? O grave, where is thy victory? 56  The sting of death is sin; Và the strength of sin is luật pháp. 57  Nhưng thanks be to God, which giveth us the victory through our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ. 58  Therefore, my beloved anh em, be ye stedfast, unmoveable, always abounding in the work của Chúa, forasmuch as ye know rằng your labour is không in vain in the Lord.
