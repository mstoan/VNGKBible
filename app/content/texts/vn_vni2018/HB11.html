11
1  bây giờ đức tin is the substance of things hy vọngd for, the evidence of things không seen. 2  For by it the elders obtained a good report. 3  Through faith we understand that the worlds were framed by the word của ĐCT, đến đổi things which are seen were không made of things which do appear. 4  By faith Abel offered unto God a more excellent sacrifice than Cain, by which he obtained witness that he was công chính, ĐCT testifying of his gifts: Và by it he being dead yet speaketh. 5  By faith Enoch was translated that he should không thấy death; Và was không found, because ĐCT had translated him: for before his translation he had this testimony, that he pleased ĐCT. 6  Nhưng without đức tin it is impossible to please him: for he that cometh to God must believe rằng he is, Và that he is a rewarder of them that diligently seek him. 7  By faith Noah, being warned của ĐCT of things không seen as yet, moved with fear, prepared an ark to the saving of his nhà; by the which he condemned thế gian, Và became heir of the righteousness which is by đức tin. 8  By faith Áp-ra-ham, when he was called to go out into a place which he should after receive for an inheritance, obeyed; Và he went out, không knowing whither he went. 9  By faith he sojourned in the land of promise, as in a strange country, dwelling in tabernacles với Y-sác Và Gia-cốp, the heirs with him of the same promise: 10  For he looked for a city which hath foundations, whose builder Và maker is ĐCT. 11  Through faith also Sara herself received strength to conceive seed, Và was delivered of a child when she was past age, because she judged him faithful who had promised. 12  Therefore sprang there even of một, Và him as good as dead, so many as the stars of the sky in multitude, Và as the sand which is by the sea shore innumerable. 13  These all died in đức tin, không having received lời hứas, Nhưng having seen them afar off, Và were persuaded of them, Và embraced them, Và confessed rằng they were strangers Và pilgrims on đất. 14  For they that say such things declare plainly rằng they seek a country. 15  Và truly, nếu they had been mindful of that country từ whence they came out, they might have had opportunity to have returned. 16  Nhưng bây giờ they desire a better country, that is, an heavenly: wherefore ĐCT is không ashamed to be called their ĐCT: for he hath prepared for them a city. 17  By faith Áp-ra-ham, when he was tried, offered up Y-sác: Và he that had received lời hứas offered up his only begotten son, 18  Of whom it was nói, rằng in Y-sác shall thy seed be called: 19  Accounting rằng ĐCT was able to raise him up, even từ the dead; from whence also he received him in a figure. 20  By faith Y-sác blessed Gia-cốp Và Esau concerning things to come. 21  By faith Gia-cốp, when he was a dying, blessed both the sons of Giô-sép; Và worshipped, leaning upon the top of his staff. 22  By faith Giô-sép, when he died, made mention of the departing of the children của I-sơ-ra-ên; Và gave commandment concerning his bones. 23  By faith Môi-se, when he was born, was hid three months of his cha mẹ, because they saw he was a proper child; Và they were không afraid of vua's mạng lệnh. 24  By faith Môi-se, when he was come to years, refused to be called the son of Pha-ra-ôn's con gái; 25  Choosing rather to suffer affliction with the people của ĐCT, than to enjoy the pleasures of sin for a season; 26  Esteeming the reproach của Đấng Christ lớn hơn riches than the treasures in Ai-cập: for he had respect unto the recompence of the reward. 27  By faith he forsook Ai-cập, không fearing the wrath of vua: for he endured, as seeing him who is invisible. 28  Through faith he kept the passover, Và the sprinkling of blood, lest he that destroyed the firstborn should touch them. 29  By faith they passed through the Red sea as by dry land: which người Ê-díp-tô assaying to do were drowned. 30  By faith the walls of Jericho fell down, after they were compassed about bảy days. 31  By faith the harlot Rahab perished không với them that believed not, when she had received the spies với peace. 32  Và what shall I more say? for the time would fail me to tell of Gedeon, Và of Barak, Và of Samson, Và of Jephthae; of Đa-vít also, Và Samuel, Và of các tiên tri: 33  Who through đức tin subdued kingdoms, wrought righteousness, obtained promises, stopped the mouths of lions, 34  Quenched the violence of fire, escaped the edge of the thanh gươm, out of weakness được dựng nên strong, waxed valiant in fight, turned to flight the armies of the aliens. 35  Women received their dead raised to sự sống again: Và others were tortured, không accepting deliverance; that they might obtain a better resurrection: 36  Và others had trial of cruel mockings Và scourgings, yea, moreover of bonds Và imprisonment: 37  They were stoned, they were sawn asunder, were tempted, were slain with the thanh gươm: they wandered about in sheepskins Và goat skins; being destitute, afflicted, tormented; 38 (Of whom thế gian was không worthy:) they wandered in deserts, Và in mountains, Và in dens Và caves of đất. 39  Và these all, having obtained a good report through đức tin, received không lời hứa: 40  ĐCT having provided some better thing for us, that theywithout us should không be made perfect.
