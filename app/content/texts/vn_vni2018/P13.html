Việt-Hy-lạp 20181 Peter 3< = >
3
1  Likewise, ye wives, be in subjection to your own husbands; that, nếu any obey not the word, theyalso may without the word be won by the conversation of the wives; 2  While they kìa your chaste conversation coupled với fear. 3  Whose adorning let it không be that outward adorning of plaiting the hair, Và of wearing of gold, hay of putting on of apparel; 4  Nhưng let it be the hidden man of the heart, in that which is not corruptible, even the ornament of a meek Và quiet spirit, which is in the sight của ĐCT of great price. 5  For after this manner in the old time the holy women also, who trusted in ĐCT, adorned themselves, being in subjection unto their own husbands: 6  Even as Sara obeyed Áp-ra-ham, calling him lord: whose daughters ye are, as long as ye do well, Và are không afraid with any amazement. 7  Likewise, ye husbands, dwell with them according to knowledge, giving honour unto the wife, as unto the weaker vessel, Và as being heirs together of the grace of sự sống; that your prayers be không hindered. 8  Finally, be ye all of one mind, having compassion one of another, love as brethren, be pitiful, be courteous: 9  Not rendering evil for evil, hay railing for railing: Nhưng contrariwise blessing; knowing rằng ye are thereunto called, that ye should inherit a blessing. 10  For he that will love sự sống, Và thấy good days, let him refrain his tongue từ evil, Và his lips that they speak no guile: 11  Let him eschew evil, Và do good; let him seek peace, Và ensue it. 12  For the eyes của Chúa are over the righteous, Và his ears are open unto their prayers: Nhưng the face của Chúa is against them that do evil. 13  Và who is he that will harm you, nếu ye be followers of that which is good? 14  Nhưng Và nếu ye suffer for righteousness' sake, happy are ye: Và be không afraid of their terror, neither be troubled; 15  Nhưng sanctify the Lord ĐCT in your hearts: Và be ready always to give an answer to every man that asketh you a reason of hy vọng that is in you trong khiêm nhu Và fear: 16  Having a good conscience; that, whereas they speak evil of you, as of evildoers, they may be ashamed that falsely accuse your good conversation in Christ. 17  For it is better, nếu the will của ĐCT be so, that ye suffer for well doing, than for evil doing. 18  For Christ also hath once suffered for tội lỗi, the just for the unjust, that he might bring us to God, being put to death in the flesh, Nhưng quickened by the Spirit: 19  By which also he went Và preached unto the spirits in tù; 20  Which sometime were disobedient, when once the longsuffering của ĐCT waited in the days of Noah, while the ark was a preparing, wherein few, that is, tám souls were saved by nước. 21  The like figure whereunto even baptism doth also bây giờ save us (không the putting away of the filth of the flesh, Nhưng the answer of a good conscience toward ĐCT,) by sự sống lại của ĐCGiê-su Christ: 22  Who is gone into heaven, Và is on the right hand của ĐCT; các thiên sứ Và authorities Và powers being made subject unto him.
