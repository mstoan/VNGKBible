14
1  Follow after charity, Và desire spiritual gifts, Nhưng rather that ye may prophesy. 2  For he that speaketh in an unknown lưỡi speaketh không unto men, Nhưng unto God: for no man understandeth him; howbeit in the spirit he speaketh mysteries. 3  Nhưng he that prophesieth speaketh unto men to edification, Và exhortation, Và comfort. 4  He that speaketh in an unknown lưỡi edifieth himself; Nhưng he that prophesieth edifieth hội thánh. 5  I would that ye all spake with tongues, Nhưng rather that ye prophesied: for lớn hơn is he that prophesieth than he that speaketh with tongues, except he interpret, that hội thánh may receive edifying. 6  bây giờ, anh em, nếu I come unto you speaking with tongues, what shall I profit you, except I shall speak to you either by revelation, hay by knowledge, hay by prophesying, hay by doctrine? 7  Và even things without sự sống giving sound, hoặc pipe or harp, except they give a distinction in the sounds, how shall it be known what is piped hay harped? 8  For nếu the trumpet give an uncertain sound, who shall prepare himself to the battle? 9  So likewise ye, except ye utter by lưỡi words easy to be hiểu, how shall it be known what is spoken? for ye shall speak into the air. 10  There are, it may be, so many kinds of voices in thế gian, Và none of them is without signification. 11  Therefore nếu I know không the meaning of the voice, I shall be unto him that speaketh a barbarian, Và he that speaketh shall be a barbarian unto me. 12  Even so ye, forasmuch as ye are zealous of spiritual gifts, seek that ye may excel to the edifying của hội thánh. 13  Vậy nên let him that speaketh in an unknown lưỡi cầu nguyện that he may interpret. 14  For nếu I pray in an unknown lưỡi, my spirit prayeth, Nhưng my understanding is unfruitful. 15  What is it then? I will pray with the spirit, Và I will pray with the understanding also: I will sing with the spirit, Và I will sing with the understanding also. 16  Else khi thou shalt bless with the spirit, how shall he that occupieth the room of the unlearned say Amen at thy giving of thanks, seeing he understandeth không what thou sayest? 17  For thou Quả thật givest thanks well, Nhưng the other is không edified. 18  I thank my ĐCT, I speak with tongues more than ye all: 19  Yet in hội thánh I had rather speak năm (5) words với my understanding, that by my voice I might teach others also, than ten thousand words in an unknown lưỡi. 20  anh em, be không children in understanding: howbeit in malice be ye children, Nhưng in understanding be men. 21  In luật pháp có chép, With men of other tongues Và other lips will I speak unto this people; Và yet for all that will they không hear me, nói the Lord. 22  Vậy nên tongues are for a sign, không to them that believe, Nhưng to them that believe not: Nhưng prophesying serveth không for them that believe not, Nhưng for them which believe. 23  nếu therefore the whole church be come together into one place, Và all speak with tongues, Và there come in those that are unlearned, hay unbelievers, will they không say rằng ye are mad? 24  Nhưng nếu all prophesy, Và there come in một that believeth not, hay one unlearned, he is convinced of all, he is judged of all: 25  Và thus are the secrets of his heart made manifest; Và so falling down on his face he will worship ĐCT, Và report rằng ĐCT is in you of a truth. 26  How is it then, anh em? khi ye come together, every one of you hath a psalm, hath a doctrine, hath a tongue, hath a revelation, hath an interpretation. Let mọi sự be done unto edifying. 27  nếu any man speak in an unknown lưỡi, let it be by hai, hay at the most by ba, Và that by course; Và let một interpret. 28  Nhưng nếu there be no interpreter, let him keep silence in hội thánh; Và let himspeak to himself, Và to God. 29  Let các tiên tri speak hai hay ba, Và let the other xét đoán. 30  nếu any thing be revealed toanother that sitteth by, let the first hold his peace. 31  For ye may all prophesy một by một, that all may learn, Và all may được yên ủi. 32  Và the spirits of các tiên tri are subject to các tiên tri. 33  For ĐCT is không the author of confusion, Nhưng of peace, as in all churches of the saints. 34  Let your women keep silence in hội thánhes: for it is không permitted với họ to speak; Nhưng they are commanded to be under obedience, as also nói luật pháp. 35  Và nếu they will learn any thing, let them ask their husbands at home: for it is a shame for women to speak in hội thánh. 36  What? đến the word của ĐCT out từ you? hay came it unto you only? 37  nếu any man think himself to be một tiên tri, hay spiritual, let him acknowledge that the things rằng I write cùng các ngươi are the commandments của Chúa. 38  Nhưng nếu any man be ignorant, let him be ignorant. 39  Vậy nên, anh em, covet to prophesy, Và forbid không to speak with tongues. 40  Let mọi sự be done decently Và in order.
