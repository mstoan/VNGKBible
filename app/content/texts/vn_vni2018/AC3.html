3
1  bây giờ Phi-e-rơ Và Giăng went up together into the temple at the hour of prayer, being the ninth hour. 2  Và a certain man lame from his mother's womb was carried, whom they laid daily at the gate of the temple which is called Beautiful, to ask alms of them that entered into the temple; 3  Who seeing Phi-e-rơ Và Giăng about to go into the temple asked an alms. 4  Và Phi-e-rơ, fastening his eyes upon him with Giăng, nói, Look on us. 5  Và he gave heed unto them, expecting to receive something of them. 6  Then Phi-e-rơ nói, Silver Và gold have I none; Nhưng such as I have give I thee: In the name của ĐCGiê-su Christ of Nazareth rise up Và walk. 7  Và he took him by the right hand, Và lifted him up: Và tức thì his feet Và ancle bones received strength. 8  Và he leaping up stood, Và walked, Và entered with them into the temple, walking, Và leaping, Và praising ĐCT. 9  Và all the people thấy him walking Và praising ĐCT: 10  Và they knew rằng it was he which sat for alms at the Beautiful gate of the temple: Và they were filled with wonder Và amazement at that which had happened unto him. 11  Và asthe lame man which was healed held Phi-e-rơ Và Giăng, all the people ran together unto them in the porch that is called Sa-lô-môn's, greatly wondering. 12  Và when Phi-e-rơ thấy it, he trả lời unto the people, Ye men của I-sơ-ra-ên, Tại sao marvel ye at this? hay Tại sao look ye so earnestly on us, as though by our own power hay holiness we had made this man to walk? 13  The God của Áp-ra-ham, Và của I-sác, Và của Gia-cốp, the God of our fathers, hath glorified his Son ĐCGiê-su; whom ye delivered up, Và denied him in the presence of Pilate, when he was determined to let him go. 14  Nhưng ye denied the Holy One Và the Just, Và desired a murderer to be granted unto you; 15  Và killed the Prince of sự sống, whom ĐCT hath raised from the dead; whereof chúng ta are witnesses. 16  Và his tên through đức tin in his tên hath made this man strong, whom ye see Và know: yea, the faith which is by him hath given him this perfect soundness in the presence of you all. 17  Và bây giờ, anh em, I wot rằng through ignorance ye did it, as did also your rulers. 18  Nhưng those things, which ĐCT before had shewed by the mouth of all his prophets, that Christ should suffer, he hath so fulfilled. 19  Repent ye therefore, Và be converted, that your tội lỗi may be blotted out, when the times of refreshing shall come from the presence của Chúa; 20  Và he shall send ĐCGiê-su Christ, which before was preached unto you: 21  Whom the heaven must receive until the times of restitution of all things, which ĐCT hath spoken by the mouth of all his holy prophets since the world began. 22  For Môi-se truly nói unto the fathers, A prophet shall the Lord your ĐCT raise up unto you of your anh em, like unto me; him shall ye hear in all things whatsoever he shall say unto you. 23  Và it shall come to pass, that every soul, which will not hear that prophet, shall be destroyed from among the people. 24  Yea, Và all the prophets from Samuel Và those that follow after, as many as have spoken, have likewise foretold of these days. 25  Ye are the children of the prophets, Và of the covenant which ĐCT made with our fathers, Nói rằng unto Áp-ra-ham, Và in thy seed shall all the kindreds of the đất be blessed. 26  Unto you first ĐCT, having raised up his Son ĐCGiê-su, sent him to bless you, in turning away every one of you from his iniquities.
