6
1 After these things ĐCGiê-su went over biển of Ga-li-lê, which is biển of Tiberias. 2 Và a great multitude followed him, because they saw his miracles which he did on them that were diseased. 3 Và ĐCGiê-su went up into a mountain, Và there he sat with his disciples. 4 Và the passover, a feast of the Jews, was nigh.
5 When ĐCGiê-su then lifted up his eyes, Và saw a great company come unto him, he nói unto Phi-líp, Whence shall we buy bread, that these may eat? 6 Và this he nói to prove him: for he himself knew what he would do. 7 Phi-líp trả lời him, Two hundred pennyworth of bread is not sufficient for them, that every one of them may take a little. 8 một of his disciples, An-rê, Si-môn Phi-e-rơ's brother, nói unto him, 9 There is a lad here, which hath five barley loaves, Và two small fishes: Nhưng what are they among so many? 10 Và ĐCGiê-su nói, Make the men sit down. bây giờ there was much grass in the place. So the men sat down, in number about five thousand. 11 Và ĐCGiê-su took the loaves; Và when he had given thanks, he distributed to các môn đệ, Và các môn đệ to them that were set down; Và likewise of the fishes as much as they would. 12 When they were filled, he nói unto his disciples, Gather up the fragments that remain, that nothing be lost. 13 Therefore they gathered them together, Và filled mười hai baskets with the fragments of the five barley loaves, which remained over and above unto them that had eaten. 14 Then those men, when they had seen the miracle that ĐCGiê-su did, nói, This is of a truth that prophet that should come into the world.
15 When ĐCGiê-su therefore perceived that they would come Và take him by force, to make him a king, he departed again into a mountain himself alone. 16 Và when even was bây giờ come, his disciples went down unto biển, 17 Và entered into a ship, and went over biển toward Ca-bê-na-um. Và it was bây giờ dark, Và ĐCGiê-su was not come to them. 18 Và biển arose by reason of a great wind that blew. 19 So when they had rowed about five and twenty hay thirty furlongs, they see ĐCGiê-su walking on biển, Và drawing nigh unto the ship: Và they were afraid. 20 Nhưng he nói unto them, It is I; be not afraid. 21 Then they willingly received him into the ship: Và tức thì the ship was at the land whither they went.
22 The day following, when the people which stood on the other side of biển saw that there was none other boat there, save that một whereinto his disciples were entered, Và that ĐCGiê-su went not with his disciples into the boat, Nhưng that his disciples were gone away alone; 23 (Howbeit there came other boats from Tiberias nigh unto the place where they did eat bread, after that the Lord had given thanks:) 24 When the people therefore saw that ĐCGiê-su was not there, neither his disciples, they also took shipping, Và đến to Ca-bê-na-um, seeking for ĐCGiê-su. 25 Và when they had found him on the other side of biển, they nói unto him, Rabbi, when camest thou hither? 26 ĐCGiê-su trả lời them Và nói, Quả thật, Quả thật, I say unto you, Ye seek me, not because ye saw the miracles, Nhưng because ye did eat of the loaves, Và were filled. 27 Labour not for the meat which perisheth, Nhưng for that meat which endureth unto everlasting life, which the Son of man shall give unto you: for him hath ĐCT the Father sealed. 28 Then nói they unto him, What shall we do, that we might work the works của ĐCT? 29  ĐCGiê-su trả lời và nói với họ, Này là công việc của ĐCT, ấy là ngươi tin vào Đấng ấy [mà] Ngài đã sai [đến]. 30 Vậy Họ nói với Ngài, What sign shewest thou then, that chúng tôi có thể thấy, và tin thee? what dost thou work? 31 Our fathers did eat manna in the desert; as it is written, He gave them bread from heaven to eat. 32 Then ĐCGiê-su nói unto them, Quả thật, Quả thật, I say unto you, Môi-se gave you not that bread from heaven; Nhưng my Father giveth you the true bread from heaven. 33 For the bread của ĐCT is he which cometh down from heaven, Và giveth life unto the world. 34 Then nói they unto him, Lord, evermore give us this bread. 35 Và ĐCGiê-su nói unto them, I am the bread of life: he that cometh to me shall never hunger; Và he that believeth on me shall never thirst. 36 Nhưng I nói unto you, That ye also have seen me, Và believe not. 37 All that the Father giveth me shall come to me; Và him that cometh to me I will in no wise cast out. 38 For I came down from heaven, not to do mine own will, Nhưng the will of him that sent me. 39 Và this is the Father's will which hath sent me, that of all which he hath given me I should lose nothing, Nhưng should raise it up again at the last day. 40 Và this is the will of him that sent me, that every one which seeth the Son, Và believeth on him, may have everlasting life: Và I will raise him up at the last day. 41 The Jews then murmured at him, because he nói, I am the bread which came down from heaven. 42 Và they nói, Is not this ĐCGiê-su, the son of Giô-sép, whose father Và mẹ chúng ta know? how is it then that he nói, I came down from heaven? 43 ĐCGiê-su therefore trả lời Và nói unto them, Murmur not among yourselves. 44 không ai can come to me, except the Father which hath sent me draw him: Và I will raise him up at the last day. 45 It is written in the prophets, Và they shall be all taught của ĐCT. Every man therefore that hath heard, Và hath learned of the Father, cometh unto me. 46 Not that any man hath seen the Father, save he which is of ĐCT, he hath seen the Father. 47 Quả thật, Quả thật, I say unto you, He that believeth on me hath everlasting life. 48 I am that bread of life. 49 Your fathers did eat manna in the wilderness, Và are dead. 50 This is the bread which cometh down from heaven, that a man may eat thereof, Và not die. 51 I am the living bread which came down from heaven: nếu any man eat of this bread, he shall live for ever: Và the bread that I will give is my flesh, which I will give for the life of the world. 52 The Jews therefore strove among themselves, saying, How can this man give us his flesh to eat? 53 Then ĐCGiê-su nói unto them, Quả thật, Quả thật, I say unto you, Except ye eat the flesh of the Son of man, Và drink his blood, ye have no life in you. 54 Whoso eateth my flesh, Và drinketh my blood, hath eternal life; Và I will raise him up at the last day. 55 For my flesh is meat indeed, Và my blood is drink indeed. 56 He that eateth my flesh, Và drinketh my blood, dwelleth in me, Và I in him. 57 As the living Father hath sent me, Và I live by the Father: so he that eateth me, even he shall live by me. 58 This is that bread which came down from heaven: not as your fathers did eat manna, Và are dead: he that eateth of this bread shall live for ever. 59 These things nói he in the nhà hội, as he taught in Ca-bê-na-um. 60 Many therefore of his disciples, when they had heard this, nói, This is an hard saying; who can hear it? 61 When ĐCGiê-su knew in himself that his disciples murmured at it, he nói unto them, Doth this offend you? 62 What Và nếu ye shall see the Son of man ascend up where he was before? 63 It is the spirit that quickeneth; the flesh profiteth nothing: the words that I speak unto you, they are spirit, Và they are life. 64 Nhưng there are some of you that believe not. For ĐCGiê-su knew from the beginning who they were that believed not, Và who should betray him. 65 Và he nói, Therefore nói I unto you, that không ai can come unto me, except it were given unto him of my Father.
66 From that time many of his disciples went back, Và walked no more with him. 67 Then nói ĐCGiê-su unto the mười hai, Will ye also go away? 68 Then Si-môn Phi-e-rơ trả lời him, Lord, to whom shall we go? thou hast the words of eternal life. 69 Và chúng ta believe Và are sure that thou art that Christ, the Son of the living ĐCT. 70 ĐCGiê-su trả lời them, Have not I chosen you mười hai, Và một of you is a devil? 71 He spake of Judas Ích-ca-ri-ốt the son of Si-môn: for he it was that should betray him, being một of the mười hai.
