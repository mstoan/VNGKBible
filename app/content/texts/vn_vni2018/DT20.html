20
1  When ngươi goest out to battle against thine enemies, Và seest horses, Và chariots, Và a people more than thou, be not afraid of them: for the Lord thy God is with ngươi, which brought ngươi up out of the land of Ai-Cập. 2  Và it shall be, when ye are come nigh unto the battle, that the priest shall approach Và speak unto the people, 3  Và shall say với họ, Hear, O I-sơ-ra-ên, ye approach this day unto battle against your enemies: let not your hearts faint, fear not, and do not tremble, neither be ye terrified vì of them;1 2 4  For the Lord your God is he that goeth with you, to fight for you against your enemies, to save you. 5  Và the officers shall speak unto the people, nói [rằng], What man is there that hath built a new nhà, Và hath not dedicated it? let him go Và return to his house, lest he die in the battle, Và another man dedicate it. 6  Và what man is he that hath planted a vườn nho, and hath not yet eaten of it? let him also đi Và return unto his house, lest he die in the battle, Và another man ăn of it. 3 7  Và what man is there that hath betrothed a wife, Và hath not taken her? let him go Và return unto his house, lest he die in the battle, Và another man take her. 8  Và the officers shall speak further unto the people, Và they shall say, What man is there that is fearful Và fainthearted? let him go Và return unto his house, lest anh em người's heart faint as well as his heart. 4 9  Và it shall be, when the officers have made an end of speaking unto the people, that they shall make captains of the armies to lead the people. 5 10  When ngươi comest nigh unto a city to fight against it, then proclaim peace unto it. 11  Và it shall be, if it make ngươi answer of peace, Và open unto ngươi, then it shall be, that all the people that is found therein shall be tributaries unto ngươi, and they shall serve ngươi. 12  Và if it will make no peace with ngươi, but will make war against ngươi, then ngươi shalt besiege it: 13  Và when the Lord thy God hath delivered it into thine hands, ngươi shalt smite every male thereof with the edge of the thanh gươm: 14  But những người đàn bà, Và the little ones, Và the cattle, Và all that is in the city, even all the spoil thereof, shalt ngươi take unto thyself; and ngươi shalt eat the spoil of thine enemies, which the Lord thy God hath given ngươi. 6 15  Thus shalt ngươi do unto all the cities which are very far off from ngươi, which are not of the cities of these nations. 16  But of the cities of these people, which the Lord thy God doth give ngươi for một cơ nghiệp, ngươi shalt save alive nothing that breatheth: 17  But ngươi shalt utterly destroy them; namely, the Hittites, Và the Amorites, người Ca-na-an, Và the Perizzites, the Hivites, Và the Jebusites; as the Lord thy God hath commanded ngươi: 18  That they teach you not to do after all their abominations, which they have done unto their gods; so should ye sin against the Lord your God. 19  When ngươi shalt besiege a city a long time, in making war against it to take it, ngươi shalt not destroy the trees thereof by forcing an axe against them: for ngươi mayest eat of them, and ngươi shalt not cut them down (for the tree of the field is man's sự sống) to employ họ in the siege: 7 8 20  Only the trees which ngươi knowest that they be not trees for meat, ngươi shalt destroy Và cut them down; Và ngươi shalt build bulwarks against the city that maketh war with ngươi, until it be subdued. 9
