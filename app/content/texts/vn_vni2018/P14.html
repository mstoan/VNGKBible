4
1  Forasmuch then as Christ hath suffered for us in the flesh, arm yourselves likewise with the same mind: for he that hath suffered in the flesh hath ceased from sin; 2  That he no longer should live the rest of his time in the flesh to the lusts of men, Nhưng to the will của ĐCT. 3  For the time past of our sự sống may suffice us to have wrought the will of the Gentiles, when we walked in lasciviousness, dục vọng, excess of wine, revellings, banquetings, Và abominable idolatries: 4  Wherein họ nghĩ it strange that ye run không với họ to the same excess of riot, speaking evil of you: 5  Who shall give account to him that is ready to judge the quick Và the dead. 6  For for this cause was phúc-âm preached also to them that are dead, that they might be judged according to men in the flesh, Nhưng live according to ĐCT in the spirit. 7  Nhưng the end of all things is at hand: be ye therefore sober, Và watch unto prayer. 8  Và above all things have fervent charity among yourselves: for charity shall cover đám đông of tội lỗi. 9  Use hospitality một to another without grudging. 10  As every man hath received the gift, even so minister the same một to another, as good stewards of the manifold ân-điển của ĐCT. 11  nếu any man speak, let him speak as the oracles của ĐCT; nếu any man minister, let him do it as of the ability which ĐCT giveth: that ĐCT in all things may be glorified through ĐCGiê-su Christ, to whom be praise Và dominion for ever Và ever. Amen. 12  Beloved, think it không strange concerning the fiery trial which is to try you, as though some strange thing happened unto you: 13  Nhưng rejoice, inasmuch as ye are partakers của Đấng Christ's sufferings; that, khi his glory shall be revealed, ye may be glad also with exceeding joy. 14  nếu ye be reproached for the name của Đấng Christ, happy are ye; for the spirit of glory Và của ĐCT resteth upon you: on their part he is evil spoken of, Nhưng on your part he is glorified. 15  Nhưng let none of you suffer as a murderer, hay as a thief, hay as an evildoer, hay as a busybody in other men's matters. 16  Yet nếu any man suffer as a Christian, let him không be ashamed; Nhưng let him glorify ĐCT on this behalf. 17  For the time is come that judgment must begin at nhà của ĐCT: Và nếu it first begin at us, what shall the end be of them that obey not phúc-âm của ĐCT? 18  Và nếu the righteous scarcely được cứu, where shall the ungodly Và the sinner appear? 19  Wherefore let them that suffer according to the will của ĐCT commit the keeping of their souls to him in well doing, as unto a faithful Creator.
