5
1 After this there was a feast of the Jews; Và ĐCGiê-su went up to Giê-ru-sa-lem. 2 bây giờ there is at Giê-ru-sa-lem by the sheep market a pool, which is called in the Hebrew tongue Bethesda, having five porches. 3 In these lay a great multitude of impotent folk, of blind, halt, withered, waiting for the moving of the water. 4 For an angel went down at a certain season into the pool, Và troubled the water: whosoever then first after the troubling of the water stepped in was made whole of whatsoever disease he had. 5 Và a certain man was there, which had an infirmity thirty and eight years. 6 When ĐCGiê-su saw him lie, Và knew that he had been bây giờ a long time in that case, he nói unto him, Wilt thou be made whole? 7 The impotent man trả lời him, Sir, I have no man, when the water is troubled, to put me into the pool: Nhưng while I am coming, another steppeth down before me. 8 ĐCGiê-su nói unto him, Rise, take up thy bed, Và walk. 9 Và tức thì the man was made whole, Và took up his bed, Và walked: Và on the same day was the sabbath.
10 The Jews therefore nói unto him that was cured, It is the ngày Sa-bát: it is not lawful for thee to carry thy bed. 11 He trả lời them, He that made me whole, the same nói unto me, Take up thy bed, Và walk. 12 Then asked they him, What man is that which nói unto thee, Take up thy bed, Và walk? 13 Và he that was healed wist not who it was: for ĐCGiê-su had conveyed himself away, a multitude being in that place. 14 Afterward ĐCGiê-su findeth him in the temple, Và nói unto him, kìa, thou art made whole: sin no more, lest a worse thing come unto thee. 15 The man departed, Và told the Jews that it was ĐCGiê-su, which had made him whole. 16 Và therefore did the Jews persecute ĐCGiê-su, Và sought to slay him, because he had done these things on the ngày Sa-bát.
17 Nhưng ĐCGiê-su trả lời them, My Father worketh hitherto, Và I work. 18 Therefore the Jews sought the more to kill him, because he not only had broken the sabbath, Nhưng nói also that ĐCT was his Father, making himself equal with God. 19 Then trả lời ĐCGiê-su Và nói unto them, Quả thật, Quả thật, I say unto you, The Son can do nothing of himself, Nhưng what he seeth the Father do: for what things soever he doeth, these also doeth the Son likewise. 20 For the Father loveth the Son, Và sheweth him all things that himself doeth: Và he will shew him greater works than these, that ye may marvel. 21 For as the Father raiseth up the dead, Và quickeneth them; even so the Son quickeneth whom he will. 22 For the Father judgeth không ai, Nhưng hath committed all judgment unto the Son: 23 That all men should honour the Son, even as they honour the Father. He that honoureth not the Son honoureth not the Father which hath sent him. 24 Quả thật, Quả thật, I say unto you, He that heareth my word, Và believeth on him that sent me, hath everlasting sự sống, Và shall not come into condemnation; Nhưng is passed from death unto sự sống. 25 Quả thật, Quả thật, I say unto you, The hour is coming, Và bây giờ is, when the dead shall hear the voice of the Son của ĐCT: Và they that hear shall live. 26 For as the Father hath sự sống in himself; so hath he given to the Son to have sự sống in himself; 27 Và hath given him authority to execute judgment also, because he is the Son of man. 28 Marvel not at this: for the hour is coming, in the which all that are in the graves shall hear his voice, 29 Và shall come forth; they that have done good, unto the resurrection of sự sống; Và they that have done evil, unto the resurrection of damnation. 30 I can of mine own self do nothing: as I hear, I judge: Và my judgment is just; because I seek not mine own will, Nhưng the will of the Father which hath sent me. 31 nếu I bear witness of myself, my witness is not true.
32 There is another that beareth witness of me; Và I know that the witness which he witnesseth of me is true. 33 Ye sent unto Giăng, Và he bare witness unto the truth. 34 Nhưng I receive not testimony from man: Nhưng these things I say, that ye might được cứu. 35 He was a burning Và a shining light: Và ye were willing for a season to rejoice in his light.
36 Nhưng I have greater witness than that of John: for the works which the Father hath given me to finish, the same works that I do, bear witness of me, that the Father hath sent me. 37 Và the Father himself, which hath sent me, hath borne witness of me. Ye have neither heard his voice at any time, nor seen his shape. 38 Và ye have not his word abiding in you: for whom he hath sent, him ye believe not.
39 Search the scriptures; for in them ye think ye have eternal sự sống: Và they are they which testify of me. 40 Và ye will not come to me, that ye might have sự sống. 41 I receive not honour from men. 42 Nhưng I know you, that ye have not tình yêu của ĐCT in you. 43 I am come in my Father's name, Và ye receive me not: nếu another shall come in his own name, him ye will receive. 44 How can ye believe, which receive honour một of another, Và seek not the honour that cometh from ĐCT only? 45 Do not think that I will accuse you to the Father: there is one that accuseth you, even Môi-se, in whom ye trust. 46 For had ye believed Môi-se, ye would have believed me: for he wrote of me. 47 Nhưng nếu ye believe not his writings, how shall ye believe my words?
