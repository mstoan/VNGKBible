9
1  nghe, O I-sơ-ra-ên: Thou art to pass over Giô-đanh this day, to go in to possess nations lớn hơn Và mightier than thyself, cities great Và fenced up to heaven, 2  A people great Và tall, con cái of the Anakims, whom ngươi knowest, and of whom ngươi hast heard say, Who can stand before con cái of Anak! 3  Understand therefore this day, that the CHÚA thy God is he which goeth over before ngươi; as a consuming lửa he shall destroy them, and he shall bring them down before thy face: so shalt ngươi drive them out, Và destroy them quickly, as the CHÚA hath nói unto ngươi. 4  nói not ngươi in thine heart, Sau đó the CHÚA thy God hath cast them out from before ngươi, saying, For my righteousness the CHÚA hath brought me in to possess this land: but for the wickedness of these nations the CHÚA doth drive them out from before ngươi. 5  Not for thy righteousness, or for the uprightness of thine heart, dost ngươi go to possess their land: but for the wickedness of these nations the CHÚA thy God doth drive them out from before ngươi, and that he may perform lời which the CHÚA sware unto thy fathers, Áp-ra-ham, Y-sác, Và Gia-cốp. 6  Understand therefore, that the CHÚA thy God giveth ngươi not this good land to possess it for thy righteousness; for thou art a stiffnecked people. 7  nhớ, Và forget not, how ngươi provokedst the CHÚA thy God to wrath in the wilderness: từ ngày that ngươi didst depart out of the land of Ai-Cập, until ye came unto this place, ye have been rebellious against the CHÚA. 8  Also in Horeb ye provoked the CHÚA to wrath, so that the CHÚA was angry with you to have destroyed you. 9  When I was gone up into the mount to receive the tables of stone, even the tables of the covenant which the CHÚA made with you, then I abode in the mount bốn mươi ngày Và forty nights, I neither did eat bánh nor drink nước: 10  Và the CHÚA delivered unto me two tables of stone written with the finger của ĐCT; and on them was written according to all the words, which the CHÚA nói with you in the mount out of the midst of the fire trong ngày of the assembly. 11  Và xảy đến at the end of forty ngày Và forty nights, that the CHÚA gave me the two tables of stone, even the tables of the covenant. 12  Và the CHÚA nói unto me, Arise, get ngươi down quickly from hence; for thy people which ngươi hast brought forth out of Ai-Cập have corrupted themselves; they are quickly turned aside out of the way which I commanded them; they have made them a molten image. 13  Furthermore the CHÚA nói unto me, saying, I have seen this people, and, kìa, it is a stiffnecked people: 14  Let me alone, that I may destroy them, and blot out their name from under heaven: Và I will make of ngươi a nation mightier Và greater than they. 15  So I turned Và came down from the mount, Và the mount burned bằng lửa: Và the two tables of the covenant were in my two hands. 16  Và I looked, and, kìa, ye had sinned against the CHÚA your God, Và had made you a molten calf: ye had turned aside quickly out of the way which the CHÚA had commanded you. 17  Và I took the two tables, Và cast them out of my two hands, Và brake them before your eyes. 18  Và I fell down before the CHÚA, as at the first, bốn mươi ngày Và forty nights: I did neither eat bánh, nor drink nước, vì cớ all your tội lỗi which ye sinned, in doing wickedly in the sight of the CHÚA, to provoke him to anger. 19  For I was afraid of the anger Và hot displeasure, wherewith the CHÚA nổi giận against you to destroy you. But the CHÚA hearkened unto me at that time also. 20  Và the CHÚA was very angry with A-rôn to have destroyed him: and I prayed for A-rôn also the same time. 21  Và I took your sin, the calf which ye had made, Và burnt it bằng lửa, Và stamped it, Và ground it very small, even until it was as small as dust: Và I cast the dust thereof into the brook that descended out of the mount. 22  Và at Taberah, Và at Massah, Và at Kibroth–hattaavah, ye provoked the CHÚA to wrath. 23  cũng như vậy when the CHÚA sent you from Kadesh–barnea, nói [rằng], Go up Và possess the land which I have given you; then ye rebelled against the commandment of the CHÚA your God, Và ye believed him not, nor hearkened to his voice. 24  Ye have been rebellious against the CHÚA from the day that I knew you. 25  Thus I fell down before the CHÚA bốn mươi ngày Và forty nights, as I fell down at the first; because the CHÚA had nói he would destroy you. 26  I prayed therefore unto the CHÚA, và nói, O Lord God, destroy not thy people Và thine inheritance, which ngươi hast redeemed through thy greatness, which ngươi hast brought forth out of Ai-Cập with a mighty tay. 27  nhớ thy servants, Áp-ra-ham, Y-sác, Và Gia-cốp; look not unto the stubbornness of this people, nor to their wickedness, nor to their sin: 28  Lest the land whence ngươi broughtest us out say, Because the CHÚA was not able to bring them into the land which he promised them, and because he hated them, he hath brought them out to slay them in the wilderness. 29 Yet they are thy people Và thine inheritance, which ngươi broughtest out by thy mighty power Và by thy stretched out arm.
