15
1  Và xảy đến after this, that Absalom prepared him chariots Và horses, Và fifty men to run before him. 2  Và Absalom rose up early, Và stood beside đường of cổng: and it was so, that when any man that had a controversy đến to the king for judgment, then Absalom called unto him, and nói, Of what city art thou? And he nói, Thy servant is of one of the tribes của I-sơ-ra-ên. 1 3  Và Absalom nói unto him, See, thy matters are good Và right; but there is không ai deputed of the king to hear thee. 2 4  Absalom nói moreover, Oh that I được dựng nên judge in the land, that every man which hath any suit or cause might come unto me, and I would do him justice! 5 Và it was so, that when any man came nigh to him to do him obeisance, he put forth his hand, Và took him, and kissed him. 6  Và on this manner did Absalom to all I-sơ-ra-ên that came to the king for judgment: so Absalom stole the hearts of the men của I-sơ-ra-ên. 7  Và xảy đến after bốn mươi years, that Absalom nói unto the king, I pray thee, let me go Và pay my vow, which I have vowed unto the Lord, in Hebron. 8  For thy servant vowed a vow while I abode at Geshur in Si-ri, saying, If the Lord shall bring me again indeed to Giê-ru-sa-lem, then I will serve the Lord. 9  Và the king nói unto him, Go in peace. So he arose, Và went to Hebron. 10  But Absalom sent spies throughout all the tribes của I-sơ-ra-ên, saying, As soon as ye hear the sound of the trumpet, then ye shall say, Absalom reigneth in Hebron. 11  Và with Absalom went hai trăm men out of Giê-ru-sa-lem, that were called; Và they went in their simplicity, Và they knew not any thing. 12  Và Absalom sent for Ahithophel the Gilonite, Đa-vít's counsellor, from his city, even from Giloh, while he offered sacrifices. Và the conspiracy was strong; for the people increased continually with Absalom. 13  Và there came a messenger to Đa-vít, saying, The hearts of the men của I-sơ-ra-ên are after Absalom. 14  Và Đa-vít nói unto all his servants that were with him at Giê-ru-sa-lem, Hãy chổi dậy, Và let us flee; for we shall not else escape từ Absalom: make speed to depart, lest he overtake us bất chợt, Và bring evil upon us, and smite the city with the edge of the thanh gươm. 3 15  Và the king's các đầy tớ nói unto the king, kìa, thy servants are ready to do whatsoever my lord vua shall appoint. 4 16  Và the king went forth, Và all his household after him. And the king left mười women, which were concubines, to keep nhà. 5 17  Và the king went forth, Và all the people after him, and tarried in một chỗ that was far off. 18  Và all his servants passed on beside him; and all the Cherethites, Và all the Pelethites, Và all the Gittites, sáu trăm men which came after him from Gath, passed on before vua. 19  Then nói vua to Ittai the Gittite, Wherefore goest thou also with us? return to thy place, Và abide with the king: for thou art a stranger, Và also an exile. 20  Whereas thou camest but yesterday, should I this day make thee go up and down with us? seeing I go whither I may, return thou, and take back thy brethren: mercy Và truth be with thee. 6 21  Và Ittai trả lời vua, và nói, As the Lord liveth, and as my lord vua liveth, surely in what place my lord vua shall be, whether in death or sự sống, even there also will thy servant be. 22  Và Đa-vít nói to Ittai, Go Và pass over. Và Ittai the Gittite passed over, Và all his men, Và all the little ones that were with him. 23  Và all the country wept with a loud tiếng, Và all the people passed over: vua also himself passed over the brook Kidron, Và all the people passed over, toward đường of the wilderness. 7 24  Và lo Zadok also, and all the Levites were with him, bearing the ark of the covenant của ĐCT: Và they set down the ark của ĐCT; Và Abiathar went up, until all the people had done passing out of the city. 25  Và the king nói unto Zadok, Carry back the ark của ĐCT into the city: if I shall find favour in the eyes of the Lord, he will bring me again, Và shew me both it, and his habitation: 26  But if he thus say, I have no delight in thee; kìa, here am I, let him do to me as seemeth good unto him. 27  The king nói also unto Zadok the priest, Art not thou a seer? return into the city in peace, Và your two sons with you, Ahimaaz thy son, Và Jonathan the son of Abiathar. 28  thấy, I will tarry in the plain of the wilderness, until there come word from you to certify me. 29  Zadok therefore and Abiathar carried the ark của ĐCT again to Giê-ru-sa-lem: Và they tarried there. 30  Và Đa-vít went up by the ascent of mount Olivet, Và wept as he went up, Và had his head covered, Và he went barefoot: Và all the people that was with him covered every man his head, Và they went up, khóc lóc as they went up. 8 31  Và one told Đa-vít, saying, Ahithophel is among the conspirators with Absalom. Và Đa-vít nói, O Lord, I pray thee, turn the counsel of Ahithophel into foolishness. 32  Và xảy đến, that khi Đa-vít was come to the top of the mount, where he worshipped ĐCT, kìa, Hushai the Archite came to meet him with his coat rent, Và đất upon his head: 33  Unto whom Đa-vít nói, If thou passest on with me, then thou shalt be a burden unto me: 34  But if thou return to the city, Và say unto Absalom, I will be thy servant, O king; as I have been thy father's servant hitherto, so will I now also be thy servant: then mayest thou for me defeat the counsel of Ahithophel. 35 Và hast thou not there with thee Zadok Và Abiathar the priests? therefore it shall be, that what thing soever thou shalt hear out of the king's nhà, thou shalt tell it to Zadok Và Abiathar the priests. 36 kìa, they have there with them their two sons, Ahimaaz Zadok's son, Và Jonathan Abiathar's son; Và by them ye shall send unto me every thing that ye can hear. 37  So Hushai Đa-vít's friend đến into the city, Và Absalom đến into Giê-ru-sa-lem.
