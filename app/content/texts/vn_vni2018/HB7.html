7
1  For này Mên-chi-xê-đéc, vua của Salem, priest of the most high ĐCT, who met Áp-ra-ham returning từ the slaughter of vuas, Và phước him; 2  To whom also Áp-ra-ham cho a tenth part of all; first being by interpretation King of sự công chính, Và Sau đó also King of Salem, which is, Vua của Hòa Bình; 3  không cha, không mẹ, without descent, having cũng không beginning of days, nor end of sự sống; Nhưng made like unto the Son của ĐCT; abideth a priest continually. 4  bây giờ consider how great this man was, unto whom even the patriarch Áp-ra-ham cho the tenth of the spoils. 5  Và Quả thật they that are of the sons of Levi, who receive the office of the priesthood, have một mạng lệnh to take tithes of the people tùy theo luật pháp, that is, of their anh em, though they come out of the loins của Áp-ra-ham: 6  Nhưng he whose descent is không counted từ họ received tithes của Áp-ra-ham, Và phước him that had lời hứas. 7  Và without all contradiction the less is blessed of the better. 8  Và here men that die receive tithes; Nhưng there he nhận them, of whom it is witnessed rằng he liveth. 9  Và as I may so say, Levi also, who nhận tithes, payed tithes in Áp-ra-ham. 10  For he was yet in the loins of his father, khi Mên-chi-xê-đéc met him. 11  nếu therefore perfection were by the Levitical priesthood, ( for under it the people received the law,) what further need was there that another priest should rise after the order of Mên-chi-xê-đéc, Và không be called after the order of A-rôn? 12  For the priesthood being changed, there is made of necessity a change also of the law. 13  For he of whom những điều này are spoken pertaineth to another tribe, of which không ai gave attendance at the altar. 14  For it is evident rằng our Lord sprang out of Juda; of which tribe Môi-se spake nothing concerning priesthood. 15  Và it is yet far more evident: for that after the similitude of Mên-chi-xê-đéc there ariseth another priest, 16  Who is made, không after luật pháp of a carnal mạng lệnh, Nhưng after quyền năng of an endless sự sống. 17  For he testifieth, Thou art a priest for ever after the order of Mên-chi-xê-đéc. 18  For there is Quả thật a disannulling of the commandment going before for the weakness Và unprofitableness thereof. 19  For luật pháp made nothing trọn vẹn, Nhưng the bringing in of a better hy vọng did; by the which we draw nigh unto God. 20  Và in asmuch as không without an oath he was made priest: 21 (For thosepriests were made without an oath; Nhưng này với an oath by him that nói unto him, The Lord sware Và will không repent, Thou art a priest for ever after the order of Mên-chi-xê-đéc:) 22  By so much was ĐCGiê-su made a surety of a better testament. 23  Và thật là có nhiều thầy tế lễ, vì giới hạn để tiếp tục bởi sự chết: 24  Nhưng này man, vì he continueth ever, hath an unchangeable priesthood. 25  Vậy nên he is able also to save họ to the uttermost that come unto God by him, seeing he ever liveth to make intercession for họ. 26  For such an high priest became us, who is holy, harmless, không ô uế, separate từ sinners, Và made higher than the heavens; 27  Who needeth không daily, như those high priests, to offer up sacrifice, first for his own tội lỗi, Và then for the people's: for này he did once, when he offered up himself. 28  For luật pháp maketh men high priests which have infirmity; Nhưng the word of the oath, which was since luật pháp, maketh Con, who is consecrated for evermore.
