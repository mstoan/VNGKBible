4
1  Chúng ta sẽ nói gì then [that] Áp-ra-ham cha chúng ta as pertaining to xác thịt, hath found? 2  Vậy nếu Áp-ra-ham được xưng công chính từ việc làm, [thig] ông có sự hảnh diện; Nhưng không [phải] trước [mặt] ĐCT. 3  Vì nói gì *Kinh Thánh? Áp-ra-ham tin ĐCT, Và it was counted unto him for công chính. 4  bây giờ to him that worketh is the reward không reckoned of ân-điển, Nhưng of debt. 5  Nhưng to him that worketh không, Nhưng tinth on him that justifieth the ungodly, his đức tin is counted for sự công chính. 6  Even as Đa-vít also describeth the blessedness of the man, unto whom ĐCT imputeth sự công chính without works, 7  Nói rằng, phước are they whose iniquities are forgiven, Và whose tội lỗi are covered. 8  phước is the man to whom the Lord will không impute sin. 9  Cometh này blessedness then upon the circumcision only, hay upon the uncircumcision also? for we say rằng đức tin was reckoned to Áp-ra-ham for sự công chính. 10  How was it then reckoned? when he was in circumcision, hay in uncircumcision? Not in circumcision, Nhưng in uncircumcision. 11  Và người đã nhận dấu của sự cắt bì, ấn của sự công chính qua đức tin mà he had yet being uncircumcised: that he might be cha of all them that tin, though they be not circumcised; that sự công chính might be imputed với họ also: 12  Và cha of circumcision to them who are không of the circumcision only, Nhưng who also bước đi in the steps of that faith of cha chúng ta Áp-ra-ham, which he had being yet uncircumcised. 13  For lời hứa, that he should be the heir của thế gian, was không to Áp-ra-ham, hay to his hạt giống, through luật pháp, Nhưng through the sự công chính of faith. 14  For nếu they which are of luật pháp be heirs, đức tin is made void, Và lời hứa made of none effect: 15  Bởi vì luật pháp worketh wrath: for where no law is, there is no transgression. 16  Therefore it is of đức tin, that it might be by ân-điển; to the end lời hứa might be sure to all the seed; không to that only which is of luật pháp, Nhưng to that also which is of the faith của Áp-ra-ham; who is the father of us all, 17  ( As có chép, I have made thee a father of many nations,) before him whom he tind, even ĐCT, who quickeneth the dead, Và calleth those things which be không as though they were. 18  Who against hy vọng tin in hy vọng, that he might become the father of many nations; tùy theo that which was spoken, So shall thy hạt giống be. 19  Và being không weak in faith, he considered không his own thân thể bây giờ dead, when he wasabout an hundred tuổi, cũng không yet the deadness of Sara's womb: 20  He staggered không at lời hứa của ĐCT through unbelief; Nhưng was strong in faith, giving glory to God; 21  Và being fully persuaded rằng, what he had promised, he was able also to perform. 22  Và therefore it was imputed to him for sự công chính. 23  bây giờ it was không written for his sake alone, rằng it was imputed to him; 24  Nhưng for us also, to whom it shall be imputed, if we tin on him that raised up ĐCGiê-su our Lord từ the dead; 25  Who was delivered for our offences, Và was raised again for our justification.
