6
1  Therefore leaving the principles of the doctrine của Đấng Christ, let us go on unto perfection; không laying again the foundation of repentance từ dead works, Và of faith toward ĐCT, 2  Of the doctrine of baptisms, Và of laying on of hands, Và of resurrection of the dead, Và of eternal judgment. 3  Và this will we do, nếu ĐCT permit. 4  For it is impossible for those who were once enlightened, Và have tasted of the heavenly gift, Và được dựng nên partakers of the Holy Ghost, 5  Và have tasted the good word của ĐCT, Và the powers của thế gian to come, 6  nếu they shall fall away, to renew them again unto repentance; seeing they crucify to themselves Con của ĐCT afresh, Và put him to an open shame. 7  For đất which uống in the rain that cometh oft upon it, Và bringeth forth herbs meet for them by whom it is dressed, receiveth blessing từ ĐCT: 8  Nhưng that which beareth thorns Và briers is rejected, Và is nigh unto cursing; whose end is to be burned. 9  Nhưng, beloved, we are persuaded better things of you, Và things that accompany sự cứu rỗi, though we thus speak. 10  For ĐCT is không unrighteous to forget your work Và labour of love, which ye have shewed toward his tên, in that ye have ministered to thánh đồ, Và do minister. 11  Và we desire that every one of you do shew the same diligence to the full assurance of hy vọng unto the end: 12  That ye be không slothful, Nhưng followers of them who through đức tin Và patience inherit lời hứas. 13  For when ĐCT made promise to Áp-ra-ham, because he could swear by no lớn hơn, he sware by himself, 14  Nói rằng, Surely blessing I will bless thee, Và multiplying I will multiply thee. 15  Và so, after he had patiently endured, he obtained lời hứa. 16  For men Quả thật swear by the greater: Và an oath for confirmation is to them an end of all strife. 17  Where in ĐCT, willing more abundantly to shew unto the heirs of promise the immutability of his counsel, confirmed it by an oath: 18  That by hai immutable things, in which it was impossible for God to lie, we might have a strong consolation, who have fled for refuge to lay hold upon the hy vọng set before us: 19  Which hy vọng we have as an anchor of the soul, both sure Và stedfast, Và which entereth into that within tấm màn; 20  Whither the forerunner is for us entered, even ĐCGiê-su, made an high priest for ever after the order of Melchisedec.
