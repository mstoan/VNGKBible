2
1  Nhưng I determined this with myself, that I would không come again to you in heaviness. 2  For nếu I make you sorry, who is he then that maketh me glad, Nhưng the same which is made sorry by me? 3  Và I wrote this same unto you, lest, when I came, I should have sorrow từ them of whom I ought to rejoice; having confidence in you all, rằng my joy is the joy of you all. 4  For out of much affliction Và anguish of heart I wrote unto you với many tears; không that ye should be grieved, Nhưng that ye might know tình yêu which I have more abundantly unto you. 5  Nhưng nếu any have caused grief, he hath không grieved me, Nhưng in part: that I may không overcharge you all. 6  Sufficient to such a man is this punishment, which was inflicted of many. 7  đến đổi contrariwise ye ought rather to forgive him, Và comfort him, lest perhaps such a one should be swallowed up with overmuch sorrow. 8  Wherefore tôi nài khuyên you that ye would confirm your love toward him. 9  For to this end also did I write, that I might know the proof of you, hoặc ye be obedient in all things. 10  To whom ye forgive any thing, I tha thứ also: for nếu I forgave any thing, to whom I forgave it, for your sakes forgave I it in the person của Đấng Christ; 11  Lest Satan should get an advantage of us:for we are không ignorant of his devices. 12  Furthermore, when I came to Troas to preach Christ's gospel, Và a door was opened unto me of the Lord, 13  I had no rest in my spirit, because I found không Titus my brother: Nhưng taking my leave of them, I went from thence into Macedonia. 14  bây giờ thanks be unto God, which always causeth us to triumphin Christ, Và maketh manifest the savour of his knowledge by us in every place. 15  For we are unto God a sweet savour của Đấng Christ, in them that are saved, Và in them that perish: 16  To the one we are the savour of death unto death; Và to the other the savour of sự sống unto sự sống. Và who is sufficient for these things? 17  For we are không as many, which corrupt the word của ĐCT: Nhưng as of sincerity, Nhưng as of ĐCT, in the sight của ĐCT speak we in Christ.
