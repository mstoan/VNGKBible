3
1  Vậy nên, holy anh em, partakers of the heavenly calling, consider the Apostle Và High Priest of our profession, Christ ĐCGiê-su; 2  Who was faithful to him that appointed him, as also Môi-se was faithful trong all his nhà. 3  For này man was counted worthy of more glory than Môi-se, trong asmuch as he who hath builded nhà hath more honour than nhà. 4  For every nhà is builded by some man; Nhưng he that built mọi sự is ĐCT. 5  Và Môi-se Quả thật was faithful trong all his nhà, as a servant, for a testimony of those things which were to be spoken after; 6  Nhưng Christ as một con trai over his own nhà; whose nhà are we, nếu chúng ta hold fast the confidence Và the rejoicing of the hy vọng firm unto the end. 7  Vậy nên (as the Holy Ghost nói, To day nếu ye will hear his tiếng, 8  Harden không your hearts, as trong the provocation, trong ngày of temptation trong the wilderness: 9  khi your fathers tempted me, proved me, Và thấy my works bốn mươi năm. 10  Vậy nên I was grieved with that dòng dõi, Và nói, They do alway err trong their heart; Và they have không known my ways. 11  So I sware trong my wrath, They shall không enter into my rest.) 12  Hãy thận trọng, anh em, lest there be trong any of you an evil heart of unbelief, trong departing từ the living ĐCT. 13  Nhưng exhort lẫn nhau daily, while it is called To day; lest any of you be hardened through the deceitfulness of sin. 14  For we are made partakers của Đấng Christ, nếu we hold ban đầu of our confidence stedfast unto the end; 15  While it is nói, Ngày nay nếu ngươi có nghe tiếng của Ngài, đừng cứng lòng của ngươi, như trong cuộc nổi loạn. 16  For some, when they had heard, did provoke: howbeit không all that came out of Ai-cập by Môi-se. 17  Nhưng with whom was he grieved bốn mươi năm? was it không with them that had sinned, whose carcases rơi trong the wilderness? 18  Và to whom sware he that they should không enter into his rest, Nhưng to them that believed not? 19  So we see rằng they could không enter in vì cớ unbelief.
