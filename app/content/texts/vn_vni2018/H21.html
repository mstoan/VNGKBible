2 Thessalonians
1
1  Phao-lô, Và Silvanus, Và Ti-mô-thê, unto the church of the Thessalonians in ĐCT our Father Và the Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ: 2  ân-điển unto you, Và peace, from ĐCT our Father Và the Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ. 3  We are bound to thank ĐCT always for you, anh em, as it is meet, because that your đức tin groweth exceedingly, Và the charity of every một of you all toward each other aboundeth; 4  đến đổi we ourselves glory in you in the churches của ĐCT for your patience Và đức tin in all your persecutions Và tribulations that ye endure: 5  Which is a manifest token of the righteous judgment của ĐCT, that ye may be counted worthy of vương quốc của ĐCT, for which ye also suffer: 6  Seeing it is a righteous thing with ĐCT to recompense tribulation to them that trouble you; 7  Và to you who are troubled rest with us, when the Lord ĐCGiê-su shall be revealed from heaven with his mighty các thiên sứ, 8  In flaming fire taking vengeance on them that know not ĐCT, Và that obey not phúc-âm of our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ: 9  Who shall be punished with everlasting destruction from the presence của Chúa, Và from the glory of his power; 10  When he shall come to be glorified in his saints, Và to be admired in all them that believe (because our testimony among you was believed) in that day. 11  Wherefore also we pray always for you, that our ĐCT would count you worthy of this calling, Và fulfil all the good pleasure of his goodness, Và the work of faith with power: 12  That the name of our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ may be glorified in you, Và ye in him, according to the grace of our ĐCT Và the Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ.
