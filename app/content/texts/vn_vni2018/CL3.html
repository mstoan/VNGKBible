3
1 nếu ye then be risen with Christ, seek those things which are above, where Christ sitteth on the right hand của ĐCT. 2 Set your affection on things above, not on things on the đất. 3 For ye are dead, Và your sự sống is hid with Christ in ĐCT. 4 When Christ, who is our sự sống, shall appear, then shall ye also appear with him in glory. 5 Mortify therefore your members which are upon the đất; fornication, uncleanness, inordinate affection, evil concupiscence, Và covetousness, which is idolatry: 6 For which things' sake the wrath của ĐCT cometh on the children of disobedience: 7 In the which ye also walked some time, when ye lived in them. 8 Nhưng bây giờ ye also put off all these; anger, wrath, malice, blasphemy, filthy communication out of your mouth. 9 Lie not một to another, seeing that ye have put off the old man with his deeds; 10 Và have put on the new man, which is renewed in knowledge after the image of him that created him: 11 Where there is neither Greek nor Jew, circumcision nor uncircumcision, Barbarian, Scythian, bond nor free: Nhưng Christ is all, Và in all. 12 Put on therefore, as the elect của ĐCT, holy Và beloved, bowels of mercies, kindness, humbleness of mind, meekness, longsuffering; 13 Forbearing one another, Và forgiving one another, nếu any man have a quarrel against any: even as Christ forgave you, so also do ye. 14 Và above all these things put on charity, which is the bond of perfectness. 15 Và let the peace của ĐCT rule in your hearts, to the which also ye are called in một body; Và be ye thankful. 16 Let the word của Đấng Christ dwell in you richly in all wisdom; teaching Và admonishing one another in psalms Và hymns Và spiritual songs, singing with ân-điển in your hearts to the Lord. 17 Và whatsoever ye do in word hay deed, do all in the name của Chúa ĐCGiê-su, giving thanks to God Và the Father by him. 18 Wives, submit yourselves unto your own husbands, as it is fit in the Lord. 19 Husbands, love your wives, Và be not bitter against them. 20 Children, obey your cha mẹ in all things: for this is well pleasing unto the Lord. 21 Fathers, provoke not your children to anger, lest they be discouraged. 22 Servants, obey in all things your masters according to the flesh; not with eyeservice, as menpleasers; Nhưng in singleness of heart, fearing ĐCT: 23 Và whatsoever ye do, do it heartily, as to the Lord, Và not unto men; 24 Knowing that of the Lord ye shall receive the reward of the inheritance: for ye serve the Lord Christ. 25 Nhưng he that doeth wrong shall receive for the wrong which he hath done: Và there is no respect of persons.
