23
1  Khốn thay be unto the pastors that destroy Và scatter the sheep of my pasture! nói the CHÚA. 2  Therefore thus nói the CHÚA ĐCT của I-sơ-ra-ên against the pastors that feed my people; Ye have scattered my flock, Và driven them away, Và have not visited them: kìa, I will visit upon you the evil of your doings, nói the CHÚA. 3  Và I will gather the remnant of my flock out of all countries whither I have driven them, and will bring them again to their folds; Và they shall be fruitful Và increase. 4  Và I will set up shepherds over them which shall feed them: and they shall fear no more, nor be dismayed, neither shall they be lacking, nói the CHÚA. 5  kìa, the days đến, nói the CHÚA, that I will raise unto Đa-vít a righteous Branch, Và a King shall reign Và prosper, Và shall execute judgment Và justice in đất. 6  In his days Giu-đa shall được cứu, Và I-sơ-ra-ên shall dwell safely: and this is his name whereby he shall be called, THE CHÚA OUR RIGHTEOUSNESS. 1 7  Therefore, kìa, the days đến, nói the CHÚA, that they shall no more say, The CHÚA liveth, which brought up con cái của I-sơ-ra-ên out of the land of Ai-Cập; 8  But, The CHÚA liveth, which brought up Và which led the seed của nhà của I-sơ-ra-ên out of the north country, Và from all countries whither I had driven them; and they shall dwell in their own land. 9  Mine heart within me is broken vì cớ các tiên tri; all my bones shake; I am like a drunken man, Và like a man whom wine hath overcome, vì of the CHÚA, Và vì cớ the words of his holiness. 10  For the land is full of adulterers; for because of swearing the land mourneth; the pleasant places of the wilderness are dried up, Và their course is evil, Và their force is not right. 2 3 11  For both prophet Và priest are profane; yea, in my house have I found their wickedness, nói the CHÚA. 12  Wherefore their way shall be với họ as slippery ways in the darkness: they shall be driven on, Và fall therein: for I will bring evil upon them, even the year of their visitation, nói the CHÚA. 13  Và I have seen folly in các tiên tri of Sa-ma-ri; they prophesied in Baal, Và caused my people I-sơ-ra-ên to err. 4 14  I have seen also in các tiên tri of Giê-ru-sa-lem an horrible thing: they commit ngoại tình, Và walk in lies: they strengthen also tays of evildoers, that none doth return from his wickedness: they are all of them unto me as Sô-đôm, Và the inhabitants thereof as Gô-mô-rơ. 5 15  Therefore thus nói the CHÚA of hosts concerning các tiên tri; kìa, I will feed them with wormwood, Và make them drink nước of gall: for from các tiên tri of Giê-ru-sa-lem is profaneness gone forth into all the land. 6 16  Thus nói the CHÚA of hosts, Hearken not unto the words of các tiên tri that prophesy cùng các ngươi: they make you vain: they speak a vision of their own heart, Và not out of the mouth of the CHÚA. 17  They say still với họ that despise me, The CHÚA hath nói, ngươi sẽ have peace; Và they say unto every one that walketh after the imagination of his own heart, No evil shall come upon you. 7 18  For who hath stood in the counsel of the CHÚA, Và hath perceived Và heard his word? who hath marked his word, Và heard it? 8 19  kìa, a whirlwind of the CHÚA is gone forth in fury, even a grievous whirlwind: it shall fall grievously upon the head of the wicked. 20  The anger of the CHÚA shall not return, until he have executed, Và till he have performed the thoughts of his heart: in the latter ngày ngươi sẽ consider it perfectly. 21  I have not sent these các tiên tri, yet they ran: I have not spoken to them, yet they prophesied. 22  But if they had stood in my counsel, Và had caused my people to hear my words, then they should have turned them from their evil way, Và from the evil of their doings. 23  là I a God at hand, nói the CHÚA, Và not a God afar off? 24  Can any hide himself in secret places that I shall not see him? nói the CHÚA. Do not I fill trời Và đất? nói the CHÚA. 25  I have heard what các tiên tri nói, that prophesy lies in my name, nói [rằng], I have dreamed, I have dreamed. 26 How long shall này be in the heart of các tiên tri that prophesy lies? yea, họ là các tiên tri of the deceit of their own heart; 27  Which think to cause my people to forget my name by their dreams which they tell every man to his neighbour, as their fathers have forgotten my name for Baal. 28  tiên tri that hath a dream, let him tell một giấc chiêm bao; Và he that hath my word, let him speak my word faithfully. What is the chaff to the wheat? nói the CHÚA. 9 29  Is not my word like as a fire? nói the CHÚA; Và like a hammer that breaketh the rock in pieces? 30 Therefore, kìa, I là against các tiên tri, nói the CHÚA, that steal my words every one from his neighbour. 31 kìa, I là against các tiên tri, nói the CHÚA, that use their tongues, Và say, He nói. 10 32 kìa, I là against them that prophesy false dreams, nói the CHÚA, Và do tell them, and cause my people to err by their lies, Và by their lightness; yet I sent them not, nor commanded them: therefore they shall not profit this people at all, nói the CHÚA. 33  Và when this people, or the prophet, or a priest, shall ask ngươi, saying, What is the burden of the CHÚA? ngươi shalt then say với họ, What burden? I will even forsake you, nói the CHÚA. 34 Và as for tiên tri, Và thầy tế lễ, Và the people, that shall say, The burden of the CHÚA, I will even punish that man Và his house. 11 35  Thus shall ye say every one to his neighbour, Và every one to his brother, What hath the CHÚA trả lời? and, What hath the CHÚA spoken? 36  Và the burden of the CHÚA shall ye mention no more: for every man's word shall be his burden; for ye have perverted the words of the living ĐCT, of the CHÚA of hosts ĐCT chúng tôi. 37  Thus shalt ngươi say to the prophet, What hath the CHÚA trả lời ngươi? and, What hath the CHÚA spoken? 38  Nhưng since ye say, The burden of the CHÚA; therefore thus nói the CHÚA; Because ye say this word, The burden of the CHÚA, Và I have sent cùng các ngươi, saying, ngươi sẽ not say, The burden of the CHÚA; 39  Therefore, kìa, I, even I, will utterly forget you, and I will forsake you, and the city that I gave you and your fathers, Và cast you out of my presence: 40  Và I will bring an đời đời reproach upon you, and a perpetual shame, which shall not be forgotten.
