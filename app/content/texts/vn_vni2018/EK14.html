14
1  Then came certain of the elders của I-sơ-ra-ên unto me, and sat before me. 2  Và the word of the Lord came unto me, saying, 3  Son of man, these men have set up their idols in their heart, Và put the stumblingblock of their iniquity before their face: should I be enquired of at all by them? 4  Therefore speak với họ, and say với họ, Thus nói the Lord God; Every man of nhà của I-sơ-ra-ên that setteth up his idols in his heart, Và putteth the stumblingblock of his iniquity before his face, Và cometh to the prophet; I the Lord will answer him that cometh according to đám đông of his idols; 5  That I may take nhà của I-sơ-ra-ên in their own heart, because they are all estranged from me through their idols. 6  Therefore say unto nhà của I-sơ-ra-ên, Thus nói the Lord God; Repent, Và turn yourselves from your idols; Và turn away your faces from all your abominations. 1 7  For every one of nhà của I-sơ-ra-ên, or of the stranger that sojourneth in I-sơ-ra-ên, which separateth himself from me, Và setteth up his idols in his heart, Và putteth the stumblingblock of his iniquity before his face, Và cometh to a prophet to enquire of him concerning me; I the Lord will answer him by myself: 8  Và I will set my face against that man, Và will make him a sign Và a proverb, Và I will cut him off from the midst of my people; Và ye shall know that I am the Lord. 9  Và if the prophet be deceived when he hath spoken a thing, I the Lord have deceived that prophet, Và I will stretch out my hand upon him, and will destroy him from the midst of my people I-sơ-ra-ên. 10  Và they shall bear the punishment of their iniquity: the punishment of the prophet shall be even as the punishment of him that seeketh unto him; 11  That nhà của I-sơ-ra-ên may go no more astray from me, neither be polluted any more with all their transgressions; but that they may be my people, Và I may be their God, nói the Lord God. 12  The word of the Lord came again to me, saying, 13  Son of man, when the land phạm tội against me by trespassing grievously, then will I stretch out mine hand upon it, and will break the staff of the bread thereof, and will send famine upon it, and will cut off man Và beast from it: 14  Though these three men, Noah, Đa-ni-ên, Và Job, were in it, they should deliver but their own souls by their righteousness, nói the Lord God. 15  nếu I cause noisome beasts to pass through the land, Và they spoil it, so that it be desolate, that không ai may pass through vì of the beasts: 2 16  Though these three men were in it, as I live, nói the Lord God, they shall deliver neither sons nor daughters; they only shall be delivered, but the land shall be desolate. 3 17  Or nếu I bring a thanh gươm upon that land, Và say, thanh gươm, go through the land; so that I cut off man Và beast from it: 18  Though these three men were in it, as I live, nói the Lord God, they shall deliver neither sons nor daughters, but they only shall be delivered themselves. 19  Or nếu I send a pestilence into that land, Và pour out my fury upon it in blood, to cut off from it man Và beast: 20  Though Noah, Đa-ni-ên, Và Job, were in it, as I live, nói the Lord God, they shall deliver neither son nor con gái; they shall but deliver their own souls by their righteousness. 21  For thus nói the Lord God; How much more when I send my four sore judgments upon Giê-ru-sa-lem, the thanh gươm, Và the famine, Và the noisome beast, Và the pestilence, to cut off from it man Và beast? 4 22  Yet, kìa, therein shall be left a remnant that shall be brought forth, both sons Và daughters: kìa, they shall come forth unto you, and ye sẽ thấy their way Và their doings: Và ye shall được yên ủi concerning the evil that I have brought upon Giê-ru-sa-lem, even concerning all that I have brought upon it. 23  Và they shall comfort you, when ye see their ways Và their doings: Và ye shall know that I have not done without cause all that I have done in it, nói the Lord God.
