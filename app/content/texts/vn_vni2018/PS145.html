145
1  I will extol thee, my God, O king; Và I will bless thy name for ever Và ever. 2  Every day will I bless thee; and I will praise thy name for ever Và ever. 3  Great is the Lord, Và greatly to be praised; Và his greatness is unsearchable. 1 4  một dòng dõi shall praise thy works to another, and shall declare thy mighty acts. 5  I will speak of the glorious honour of thy majesty, Và of thy wondrous works. 2 6 Và men shall speak of the might of thy terrible acts: Và I will declare thy greatness. 3 7  They shall abundantly utter the memory of thy great goodness, Và shall sing of thy righteousness. 8  The Lord is gracious, Và full of compassion; slow to anger, Và of great mercy. 4 9  The Lord is good to all: and his tender mercies are over all his works. 10  All thy works shall praise thee, O Lord; Và thy saints shall bless thee. 11  They shall speak of sự vinh hiển of thy kingdom, Và talk of thy power; 12  To make known to the sons of men his mighty acts, Và the glorious majesty of his kingdom. 13  Thy kingdom is an đời đời kingdom, Và thy dominion endureth throughout all generations. 5 14  The Lord upholdeth all that fall, Và raiseth up all those that be bowed down. 15  The eyes of all wait upon thee; and thou givest them their meat in due season. 6 16  Thou openest thine hand, Và satisfiest the desire of every living thing. 17  The Lord is công chính in all his ways, Và holy in all his works. 7 18  The Lord is nigh unto all them that call upon him, to all that call upon him in truth. 19  He will fulfil the desire of them that fear him: he also will hear their cry, Và will save them. 20  The Lord preserveth all them that love him: but all the wicked will he destroy. 21  My mouth shall speak the praise of the Lord: Và let all flesh bless his holy name for ever Và ever.
