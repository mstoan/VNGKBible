16
1  I commend unto you Phebe our sister, which is a servant của hội thánh which is at Cenchrea: 2  That ye receive her in the Lord, as becometh saints, Và that ye assist her in whatsoever business she hath need of you: for she hath been a succourer of many, Và of myself also. 3  Greet Priscilla Và Aquila my helpers in Christ ĐCGiê-su: 4  Who have for my sự sống laid down their own necks: unto whom not only I give thanks, Nhưng also all the churches of the Gentiles. 5  Likewise greet the church that is in their nhà. Salute my wellbeloved Epaenetus, who is the firstfruits of Achaia unto Christ. 6  Greet Ma-ri, who bestowed much labour on us. 7  Salute Andronicus Và Junia, my kinsmen, Và my fellowprisoners, who are of note among the apostles, who also were in Christ before me. 8  Greet Amplias my beloved in the Lord. 9  Salute Urbane, our helper in Christ, Và Stachys my beloved. 10  Salute Apelles approved in Christ. Salute them which are of Aristobulus' gia đình. 11  Salute Hê-rốtion my kinsman. Greet them that be of the gia đình of Narcissus, which are in the Lord. 12  Salute Tryphena Và Tryphosa, who labour in the Lord. Salute the beloved Persis, which laboured much in the Lord. 13  Salute Rufus chosen in the Lord, Và his mẹ Và mine. 14  Salute Asyncritus, Phlegon, Hermas, Patrobas, Hermes, Và the brethren which are with them. 15  Salute Philologus, Và Julia, Nereus, Và his sister, Và Olympas, Và all thánh đồ which are with them. 16  Salute one another with an holy kiss. The churches của Đấng Christ salute you. 17  bây giờ tôi nài khuyên you, anh em, Mác them which cause divisions Và offences contrary to the doctrine which ye have learned; Và avoid them. 18  For they that are such serve not our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ, Nhưng their own belly; Và by good words Và fair speeches deceive the hearts of the simple. 19  For your obedience is come abroad unto all men. I am glad therefore on your behalf: but yet I would have you wise unto that which is good, Và simple concerning evil. 20  Và the God of peace shall bruise Satan under your feet shortly. The grace of our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ be with you. Amen. 21  Ti-mô-thê my workfellow, Và Lucius, Và Jason, Và Sosipater, my kinsmen, salute you. 22  I Tertius, who wrote this epistle, salute you in the Lord. 23  Gai-út mine host, Và of the whole church, saluteth you. Erastus the chamberlain of the city saluteth you, Và Quartus a brother. 24  The grace of our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ be with you all. Amen. 25  bây giờ to him that is of power to stablish you according to my gospel, Và the preaching của ĐCGiê-su Christ, according to the revelation of the mystery, which was kept secret since the world began, 26  Nhưng bây giờ is made manifest, Và by the scriptures of the prophets, according to the commandment of the đời đời ĐCT, made known to all nations for the obedience of faith: 27  To God only wise, be glory through ĐCGiê-su Christ for ever. Amen.
