71
1  In ngươi, O Lord, do I put my trust: let me never be put to confusion. 2  Deliver me in thy righteousness, Và cause me to escape: incline thine ear unto me, and save me. 3  Be ngươi my strong habitation, whereunto I may continually resort: ngươi hast given commandment to save me; for thou art my rock Và my fortress. 1 4  Deliver me, O my God, out of tay of the wicked, out of tay of the unrighteous Và cruel man. 5 For thou art my hy vọng, O Lord God: ngươi art my trust from my youth. 6  By ngươi have I been holden up from the womb: ngươi art he that took me out of my mother's bowels: my praise shall be continually of ngươi. 7  I am as a wonder unto many; but thou art my strong refuge. 8  Let my mouth be filled với thy praise Và with thy honour all the day. 9  Cast me not off in the time of old age; forsake me not when my strength faileth. 10  For mine enemies speak against me; and they that lay wait for my soul take counsel together,2 11  nói [rằng], ĐCT hath forsaken him: persecute Và take him; for there is none to deliver him. 12  O God, be not far from me: O my God, make haste for my help. 13  Let them be confounded Và consumed that are adversaries to my soul; let them be covered với reproach Và dishonour that seek my hurt. 14  But I will hy vọng continually, Và will yet praise ngươi more Và more. 15  My mouth shall shew forth thy righteousness Và thy salvation all the day; for I know not the numbers thereof. 16  I will go in the strength của ĐCT God: I will make mention of thy righteousness, even of thine only. 17  O God, ngươi hast taught me from my youth: Và hitherto have I declared thy wondrous works. 18  Now also when I am old Và grayheaded, O God, forsake me not; until I have shewed thy strength unto này dòng dõi, Và thy power to every one that is to come. 3 4 19  Thy righteousness also, O God, is very high, who hast done great things: O God, who is like unto ngươi! 20  Thou, which hast shewed me great Và sore troubles, shalt quicken me again, Và shalt bring me up again from the depths of đất. 21  ngươi shalt increase my greatness, Và comfort me on every side. 22  I will also praise ngươi with the psaltery, even thy truth, O my God: unto ngươi will I sing with the harp, O ngươi Holy One của I-sơ-ra-ên. 5 23  My lips shall greatly rejoice when I sing unto ngươi; and my soul, which ngươi hast redeemed. 24  My tongue also shall talk of thy righteousness all the day long: for they are confounded, for they are brought unto shame, that seek my hurt.
