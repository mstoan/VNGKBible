4
1 Và ĐCGiê-su being full of the Holy Ghost returned from Giô-đanh, Và was led by the Spirit into the wilderness, 2 Being forty days tempted of the devil. Và in those days he did eat nothing: Và when they were ended, he afterward hungered. 3 Và the devil nói unto him, nếu thou be the Son của ĐCT, command this stone that it be made bread. 4 Và ĐCGiê-su trả lời him, saying, It is written, That man shall not live by bread alone, but by every word của ĐCT. 5 Và the devil, taking him up into an high mountain, shewed unto him all the kingdoms of the world in a moment of time. 6 Và the devil nói unto him, All this power will I give thee, Và the glory of them: for that is delivered unto me; Và to whomsoever I will I give it. 7 nếu thou therefore wilt worship me, all shall be thine. 8 Và ĐCGiê-su trả lời và nói unto him, Get thee behind me, Satan: for it is written, Thou shalt worship the Lord thy ĐCT, Và him only shalt thou serve. 9 Và he brought him to Giê-ru-sa-lem, Và set him on a pinnacle of the temple, Và nói unto him, nếu thou be the Son của ĐCT, cast thyself down from hence: 10 For it is written, He shall give his các thiên sứ charge over thee, to keep thee: 11 Và in their hands they shall bear thee up, lest at any time thou dash thy foot against a stone. 12 Và ĐCGiê-su answering nói unto him, It is nói, Thou shalt not tempt the Lord thy ĐCT. 13 Và when the devil had ended all the temptation, he departed from him for a season.
14 Và ĐCGiê-su returned in the power of the Spirit into Ga-li-lê: Và there went out a fame of him through all the region round about. 15 Và he taught in their các nhà hội, being glorified of all.
16 Và he came to Nazareth, where he had been brought up: Và, as his custom was, he went into the nhà hội on the sabbath day, Và stood up for to read. 17 Và there was delivered unto him the book of the prophet Esaias. Và when he had opened the book, he found the place where it was written, 18 The Spirit của Chúa is upon me, because he hath anointed me to preach phúc-âm to the poor; he hath sent me to heal the brokenhearted, to preach deliverance to the captives, Và recovering of sight to the blind, to set at liberty them that are bruised, 19 To preach the acceptable year của Chúa. 20 Và he closed the book, Và he gave it again to the minister, Và sat down. Và the eyes of all them that were in the nhà hội were fastened on him. 21 Và he began to say unto them, This day is this scripture fulfilled in your ears. 22 Và all bare him witness, Và wondered at the gracious words which proceeded out of his mouth. Và they nói, Is not this Joseph's son? 23 Và he nói unto them, Ye will surely say unto me this proverb, Physician, heal thyself: whatsoever we have heard done in Ca-bê-na-um, do also here in thy country. 24 Và he nói, Quả thật I say unto you, No prophet is accepted in his own country. 25 But I tell you of a truth, many widows were in Israel in the days of Ê-li, when the heaven was shut up three years Và six months, when great famine was throughout all the land; 26 But unto none of them was Ê-li sent, save unto Sarepta, a city of Sidon, unto một người đờn bà that was a widow. 27 Và many lepers were in Israel in the time of Eliseus the prophet; Và none of them was cleansed, saving Naaman the Syrian. 28 Và all they in the nhà hội, when they heard these things, were filled with wrath, 29 Và rose up, Và thrust him out of the city, Và led him unto the brow of the hill whereon their city was built, that they might cast him down headlong. 30 But he passing through the midst of them went his way, 31 Và came down to Ca-bê-na-um, a city of Ga-li-lê, Và taught them on the ngày Sa-báts. 32 Và they were astonished at his doctrine: for his word was with power.
33 Và in the nhà hội there was a man, which had a spirit of an unclean devil, Và cried out with a loud voice, 34 Saying, Let us alone; what have we to do with thee, thou ĐCGiê-su of Nazareth? art thou come to destroy us? I know thee who thou art; the Holy One của ĐCT. 35 Và ĐCGiê-su rebuked him, saying, Hold thy peace, Và come out of him. Và when the devil had thrown him in the midst, he came out of him, Và hurt him not. 36 Và they were all amazed, Và spake among themselves, saying, What a word is this! for with authority Và power he commandeth the unclean spirits, Và they come out. 37 Và the fame of him went out into every place of the country round about.
38 Và he arose out of the nhà hội, Và entered into Si-môn's nhà. Và Si-môn's wife's mother was taken with a great fever; Và they besought him for her. 39 Và he stood over her, Và rebuked the fever; Và it left her: Và tức thì she arose Và ministered unto them.
40 Now when mặt trời was setting, all they that had any sick with divers diseases brought them unto him; Và he laid his hands on every một of them, Và healed them. 41 Và devils also came out of many, crying out, Và saying, Thou art Christ the Son của ĐCT. Và he rebuking them suffered them not to speak: for they knew that he was Christ. 42 Và when it was day, he departed Và went into a desert place: Và the people sought him, Và đến unto him, Và stayed him, that he should not depart from them. 43 Và he nói unto them, I must preach the kingdom của ĐCT to other cities also: for therefore am I sent. 44 Và he preached in the các nhà hội of Ga-li-lê.
