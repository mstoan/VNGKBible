17
1  There was also a lot for the tribe of Manasseh; for he was the firstborn of Giô-sép; to wit, for Machir the firstborn of Manasseh, the father of Gilead: because he was a man of war, therefore he had Gilead Và Bashan. 2 There was also a lot for the rest of the children of Manasseh by their families; for the children of Abiezer, Và for the children of Helek, Và for the children of Asriel, Và for the children of Shechem, Và for the children of Hepher, Và for the children of Shemida: these were the male children of Manasseh the son of Giô-sép by their families. 1 3  But Zelophehad, the son of Hepher, the son of Gilead, the son of Machir, the son of Manasseh, had no sons, but daughters: and these are the names of his daughters, Mahlah, Và Nô-ê, Hoglah, Milcah, Và Tirzah. 4  Và they came near before Eleazar the priest, Và before Giô-suê the son of Nun, Và before the princes, saying, The Lord commanded Môi-se to give us một cơ nghiệp among our brethren. Therefore according to the commandment of the Lord he gave them một cơ nghiệp among the brethren of their father. 5  Và there fell mười portions to Manasseh, beside the land of Gilead Và Bashan, which were on the other side Giô-đanh; 6  Because the daughters of Manasseh had một cơ nghiệp among his sons: Và the rest of Manasseh's sons had the land of Gilead. 7  Và the coast of Manasseh was from Asher to Michmethah, that lieth before Shechem; Và the border went along on the right hand unto the inhabitants of En–tappuah. 8  bây giờ Manasseh had the land of Tappuah: but Tappuah on the border of Manasseh belonged to the children of Ephraim; 9  Và the coast descended unto the river Kanah, southward of the river: these cities of Ephraim are among the cities of Manasseh: the coast of Manasseh also was on the north side of the river, Và the outgoings of it were at biển: 2 10  Southward it was Ephraim's, Và northward it was Manasseh's, Và biển is his border; Và they met together in Asher on the north, Và in Issachar on the east. 11  Và Manasseh had in Issachar Và in Asher Beth–shean Và her towns, Và Ibleam Và her towns, Và the inhabitants of Dor Và her towns, Và the inhabitants of Endor Và her towns, Và the inhabitants of Taanach Và her towns, Và the inhabitants of Megiddo Và her towns, even ba countries. 12  Yet the children of Manasseh could not drive out the inhabitants of those cities; but người Ca-na-an would dwell in that land. 13  Yet xảy đến, when the children của I-sơ-ra-ên were waxen strong, that they put người Ca-na-an to tribute; but did not utterly drive them out. 14  Và the children of Giô-sép spake unto Giô-suê, saying, Tại sao hast thou given me but một lot Và one portion to inherit, seeing I am a great people, forasmuch as the Lord hath blessed me hitherto? 15  Và Giô-suê trả lời them, If thou be a great people, then get thee up to the wood country, Và cut down for thyself there in the land of the Perizzites Và of the giants, if mount Ép-ra-im be too narrow for thee. 3 16  Và the children of Giô-sép nói, The hill is not enough for us: and all người Ca-na-an that dwell in the land of the valley have chariots of sắt, both they who are of Beth–shean Và her towns, and they who are of the valley of Jezreel. 17  Và Giô-suê spake unto nhà of Giô-sép, even to Ephraim Và to Manasseh, saying, Thou art a great people, Và hast great power: thou shalt not have one lot only: 18  But the mountain shall be thine; for it is a wood, Và thou shalt cut it down: Và the outgoings of it shall be thine: for thou shalt drive out người Ca-na-an, though they have sắt chariots, Và though they be strong.
