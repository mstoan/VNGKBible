Colossians
1
1 Phao-lô, là một sứ đồ của ĐCGiê-su Christ bởi ý muốn của ĐCT, và Ti-mô-thê người anh em của chúng ta, 2 To the saints Và faithful anh em in Christ which are at Colosse: Grace be unto you, Và peace, from ĐCT our Father Và the Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ. 3 Chúng tôi tạ ơn ĐCT và Cha của Chúa ĐCGiê-su Christ của chúng ta, praying always for you, 4  Khi nghe về đức tin của anh em trong Christ ĐCGiê-su, và tình yêu thương đến cho mọi thánh đồ, 5 For the hy vọng which is laid up for you in heaven, whereof ye heard before in the word of the truth of phúc-âm; 6 Which is come unto you, as it is in all the world; Và bringeth forth fruit, as it doth also in you, since the day ye heard of it, Và knew the grace của ĐCT in truth: 7 As ye also learned of Epaphras our dear fellowservant, who is for you a faithful minister của Đấng Christ; 8 Who also declared unto us your love in the Spirit. 9 For this cause chúng ta also, since the day we heard it, do not cease to pray for you, Và to desire that ye might be filled with the knowledge of his will in all wisdom Và spiritual understanding; 10 That ye might walk worthy của Chúa unto all pleasing, being fruitful in every good work, Và increasing in the knowledge của ĐCT; 11 Strengthened with all might, according to his glorious power, unto all patience Và longsuffering with joyfulness; 12 Giving thanks unto the Father, which hath made us meet to be partakers of the inheritance of the saints in light: 13 Who hath delivered us from the power of darkness, Và hath translated us into the kingdom of his dear Son: 14 In whom we have redemption through his blood, even the forgiveness of tội lỗi: 15 Who is the image of the invisible ĐCT, the firstborn of every creature: 16 For by him were all things created, that are in heaven, Và that are in đất, visible Và invisible, whether they be thrones, or dominions, or principalities, or powers: all things were created by him, Và for him: 17 Và he is before all things, Và by him all things consist. 18 Và he is the head of the body, the church: who is the beginning, the firstborn from the dead; that in all things he might have the preeminence. 19 For it pleased the Father that in him should all fulness dwell; 20 Và, having made peace through the blood of his cross, by him to reconcile all things unto himself; by him, I say, whether they be things in đất, or things in heaven. 21 Và you, that were sometime alienated Và enemies in your mind by wicked works, yet now hath he reconciled 22 In the body of his flesh through death, to present you holy Và unblameable Và unreproveable in his sight: 23 nếu ye continue in the faith grounded Và settled, Và be not moved away from the hy vọng of phúc-âm, which ye have heard, Và which was preached to every creature which is under heaven; whereof I Phao-lô am made a minister; 24 Who now rejoice in my sufferings for you, Và fill up that which is behind of the afflictions của Đấng Christ in my flesh for his body's sake, which is the church: 25 Whereof I am made a minister, according to the dispensation của ĐCT which is given to me for you, to fulfil the word của ĐCT; 26 Even the mystery which hath been hid from ages Và from generations, but now is made manifest to his saints: 27 To whom ĐCT would make known what is the riches of the glory of this mystery among the Gentiles; which is Christ in you, the hy vọng of glory: 28 Whom chúng ta preach, warning every man, Và teaching every man in all wisdom; that we may present every man perfect in Christ ĐCGiê-su: 29 Whereunto I also labour, striving according to his working, which worketh in me mightily.
