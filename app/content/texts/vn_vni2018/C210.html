10
1  bây giờ I Phao-lô myself beseech you by sự khiêm nhu và dịu dàng của Đấng Christ, who in presence am base among you, Nhưng being absent am bold toward you: 2  Nhưng tôi nài khuyên you, that I may không be bold when I am present with that confidence, wherewith I think to be bold against some, which think of us as if we walked tùy theo the flesh. 3  For though we walk in the flesh, we do không war after the flesh: 4 ( For the weapons of our warfare are không carnal, Nhưng mighty through God to the pulling down of strong holds;) 5  Casting down imaginations, Và every high thing that exalteth itself against sự hiểu biết của ĐCT, Và bringing into captivity every thought to the obedience của Đấng Christ; 6  Và having in a readiness to revenge all disobedience, khi your obedience is fulfilled. 7  Do ye look on things after the outward appearance? nếu any man trust to himself that he is Christ's, let him of himself think này again, rằng, as he is Christ's, even so are chúng ta Christ's. 8  For though I should boast somewhat more of our authority, which the Lord hath given us for edification, Và không for your destruction, I should không be ashamed: 9  That I may không seem as nếu I would terrify you by letters. 10  For his letters, say they, are weighty Và powerful; Nhưng his bodily presence is weak, Và his speech contemptible. 11  Let such an one think này, rằng, such as we are in word by letters when we are absent, such will we be also in deed when we are present. 12  For we dare không make ourselves of the number, hay compare ourselves with some that commend themselves: Nhưng they measuring themselves by themselves, Và comparing themselves among themselves, are không khôn ngoan. 13  Nhưng chúng ta will không boast of things without our measure, Nhưng tùy theo the measure of the rule which ĐCT hath distributed to us, a measure to reach even unto you. 14  For we stretch không ourselves bên kia our measure, as though we reached không unto you: for we are come as far as to you also in giảng dạy phúc-âm của Đấng Christ: 15  Not boasting of things without our measure, that is, of other men's labours; Nhưng having hy vọng, whenyour đức tin is increased, that we shall be enlarged by you according to our rule abundantly, 16  To preach phúc-âm in the regions bên kia you, Và không to boast in another man's line of things made ready to our hand. 17  Nhưng he that glorieth, let him glory in the Lord. 18  For không he that commendeth himself is approved, Nhưng whom the Lord commendeth.
