Deuteronomy
1
1 These be the words which Môi-se nói unto all I-sơ-ra-ên on this side Giô-đanh in the wilderness, in the plain over against the Red sea, between Paran, Và Tophel, Và Laban, Và Hazeroth, Và Dizahab. 1 2 (There are mười một days' hành trình from Horeb by the way of mount Seir unto Kadesh–barnea.) 3  Và xảy đến in the fortieth năm, in the eleventh month, on the first ngày of the month, that Môi-se nói unto the children của I-sơ-ra-ên, according unto all that the Lord had given him in commandment với họ; 4  After he had slain Sihon vua of the Amorites, which dwelt in Heshbon, Và Og vua of Bashan, which dwelt at Astaroth in Edrei: 5  On this side Giô-đanh, in the land of Moab, bắt đầu Môi-se to declare this law, nói [rằng], 6  The Lord our God nói unto us in Horeb, nói [rằng], Ye have dwelt long enough in this mount: 7  Turn you, and take your journey, Và go to the mount of the Amorites, and unto all the places nigh thereunto, in the plain, in the hills, Và in the vale, Và in the south, Và by biển side, to the land của người Ca-na-an, Và unto Lebanon, unto the great river, the river Euphrates. 2 8  kìa, I have set the land before you: go in and possess the land which the Lord sware cùng các ngươir fathers, Áp-ra-ham, Y-sác, Và Gia-cốp, để cho với họ and to their seed after them. 3 9  Và I spake cùng các ngươi at that time, nói [rằng], I am not able to bear you myself alone: 10  The Lord your God hath multiplied you, and, kìa, ye are this day as ngôi saos of heaven for multitude. 11 (The Lord ĐCT of your fathers make you a thousand times so many more as ye are, Và bless you, as he hath promised you!) 12  How can I myself alone bear your cumbrance, Và your burden, Và your strife? 13  Take you wise men, Và understanding, Và known among your tribes, Và I will make them rulers over you. 4 14  Và ye trả lời me, and nói, The thing which ngươi hast spoken is tốt for us to do. 15  So I took the chief of your tribes, khôn ngoan men, Và known, Và made them heads over you, captains over thousands, Và captains over hundreds, Và captains over fifties, Và captains over tens, Và officers among your tribes. 5 16  Và I charged your judges at that time, nói [rằng], nghe the causes between your brethren, Và judge righteously between every man Và his brother, Và the stranger that is with him. 17  ngươi sẽ not respect persons in judgment; but ngươi sẽ hear the small as well as the great; ngươi sẽ not be afraid of the face of man; for the judgment is God's: Và the cause that is too hard for you, bring it unto me, and I will hear it. 6 18  Và I commanded you at that time all the things which ye should do. 19  Và when we departed from Horeb, we went through all that great Và terrible wilderness, which ye saw by the way of núi of the Amorites, as the Lord our God truyền lệnh us; and we came to Kadesh–barnea. 20  Và I nói cùng các ngươi, Ye are come unto núi of the Amorites, which the Lord our God doth give unto us. 21  kìa, the Lord thy God hath set the land before ngươi: go up Và possess it, as the Lord ĐCT of thy fathers hath nói unto ngươi; fear not, neither be discouraged. 22  Và ye came near unto me every one of you, and nói, We will send men before us, and they shall search us out the land, Và bring us word again by what way we must go up, Và into what cities we shall come. 23  Và the saying pleased me well: Và I took mười hai men of you, one of a tribe: 24  Và they turned Và went up into núi, Và came unto the valley of Eshcol, Và searched it out. 25  Và they took of the fruit of the land in their hands, and brought it down unto us, and brought us word again, và nói, It is a good land which the Lord our God doth give us. 26  Notwithstanding ye would not go up, but rebelled against the commandment of the Lord your God: 27  Và ye murmured in your tents, và nói, Because the Lord ghét us, he hath brought us forth out of the land of Ai-Cập, to deliver us into tay of the Amorites, to destroy us. 28  Whither shall we go up? our brethren have discouraged our heart, nói [rằng], The people is lớn hơn Và taller than we; the cities are great Và walled up to heaven; Và moreover we have seen the sons of the Anakims there. 7 29  Then I nói cùng các ngươi, Dread not, neither be afraid of them. 30  The Lord your God which goeth before you, he shall fight for you, according to all that he did for you in Ai-Cập before your eyes; 31  Và in the wilderness, where ngươi hast seen how that the Lord thy God bare ngươi, as a man doth bear his son, in all the way that ye went, until ye came into this place. 32  Yet in this thing ye did not believe the Lord your God, 33  Who went in the way before you, to search you out một chỗ to pitch your tents trong, in fire by night, to shew you by what way ye should go, Và in a cloud by day. 34  Và the Lord nghe tiếng of your words, Và nổi giận, Và sware, nói [rằng], 35  Surely there shall not one of these men of this evil dòng dõi thấy that good land, which I sware để cho cùng các ngươir fathers, 36  Save Caleb the son of Jephunneh; he sẽ thấy it, and to him will I give the land that he hath trodden upon, Và to his children, because he hath wholly followed the Lord. 8 37  Also the Lord was angry with me for your sakes, nói [rằng], ngươi also shalt not go in thither. 38  But Giô-suê the son of Nun, which standeth before ngươi, he shall go in thither: encourage him: for he shall cause I-sơ-ra-ên to inherit it. 39  Moreover your little ones, which ye nói should be a prey, Và your children, which in that day had no knowledge between good Và evil, they shall go in thither, and với họ will I give it, and they shall possess it. 40 But as for you, turn you, and take your journey into the wilderness by the way of the Red sea. 41  Then ye trả lời và nói unto me, We have sinned against the Lord, we will go up Và fight, according to all that the Lord our God truyền lệnh us. And when ye had girded on every man his weapons of war, ye were ready to go up into the hill. 42  Và the Lord nói unto me, Say với họ, Go not up, neither fight; for I là not among you; lest ye be smitten before your enemies. 43  So I spake cùng các ngươi; and ye would not hear, but rebelled against the commandment of the Lord, Và went presumptuously up into the hill. 9 44  Và the Amorites, which dwelt in that mountain, came out against you, and chased you, as bees do, Và destroyed you in Seir, even unto Hormah. 45  Và ye returned Và wept before the Lord; but the Lord would not hearken to your voice, nor give ear cùng các ngươi. 46  So ye abode in Kadesh many ngày, according unto the days that ye abode there.
