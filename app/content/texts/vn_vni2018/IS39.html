1  At that time Merodach–baladan, the son of Baladan, vua of Ba-by-lôn, sent letters Và a present to Hezekiah: for he had heard that he had been sick, Và was recovered. 2  Và Hezekiah was glad of them, and shewed them nhà of his precious things, the silver, Và the gold, Và the spices, Và the precious ointment, Và all nhà of his armour, Và all that was found in his treasures: there was nothing in his house, nor in all his dominion, that Hezekiah đã bày tỏ them not. 1 2 3  Then came Isaiah tiên tri unto king Hezekiah, và nói unto him, What nói these men? Và from whence đến they unto ngươi? And Hezekiah nói, They are come from a far country unto me, even from Ba-by-lôn. 4  Then nói he, What have they seen in thine house? Và Hezekiah trả lời, All that is in mine house have they seen: there is nothing among my treasures that I have not shewed them. 5  Then nói Isaiah to Hezekiah, nghe lời of the CHÚA of hosts: 6  kìa, the days đến, that all that is in thine house, and that which thy fathers have laid up in store until this day, shall be carried to Ba-by-lôn: nothing shall be left, nói the CHÚA. 7  Và of thy sons that shall issue from ngươi, which ngươi shalt beget, shall they take away; Và they shall be eunuchs in the palace of the king of Ba-by-lôn. 8  Then nói Hezekiah to Isaiah, tốt is lời of the CHÚA which ngươi hast spoken. He nói moreover, For there shall be peace Và truth in my days.
