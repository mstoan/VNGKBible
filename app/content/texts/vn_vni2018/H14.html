4
1 Furthermore then we beseech you, anh em, Và exhort you by the Lord ĐCGiê-su, that as ye have received of us how ye ought to walk Và to please ĐCT, so ye would abound more and more. 2 For ye know what commandments we gave you by the Lord ĐCGiê-su. 3 For this is the will của ĐCT, even your sanctification, that ye should abstain from fornication: 4 That every one of you should know how to possess his vessel in sanctification Và honour; 5 Not in the lust of concupiscence, even as the Gentiles which know not ĐCT: 6 That no man go beyond Và defraud his brother in any matter: because that the Lord is the avenger of all such, as we also have forewarned you Và testified. 7 For ĐCT hath not called us unto uncleanness, Nhưng unto holiness. 8 He therefore that despiseth, despiseth not man, Nhưng ĐCT, who hath also given unto us his holy Spirit. 9 Nhưng as touching brotherly love ye need not that I write unto you: for ye yourselves are taught của ĐCT to love one another. 10 Và indeed ye do it toward all the brethren which are in all Macedonia: Nhưng we beseech you, anh em, that ye increase more and more; 11 Và that ye study to be quiet, Và to do your own business, Và to work with your own hands, as we commanded you; 12 That ye may walk honestly toward them that are without, Và that ye may have lack of nothing. 13 Nhưng I would not have you to be ignorant, anh em, concerning them which are asleep, that ye sorrow not, even as others which have no hy vọng. 14 For nếu we believe that ĐCGiê-su died Và rose again, even so them also which sleep in ĐCGiê-su will ĐCT bring with him. 15 For this we say unto you by the word của Chúa, that chúng ta which are alive Và remain unto the coming của Chúa shall not prevent them which are asleep. 16 For the Lord himself shall descend from heaven with a shout, with the voice of the archangel, Và with the trump của ĐCT: Và the dead in Christ shall rise first: 17 Then chúng ta which are alive Và remain shall be caught up together with them in the clouds, to meet the Lord in the air: Và so shall we ever be with the Lord. 18 Wherefore comfort one another with these words.
