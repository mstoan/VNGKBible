36
1  Then the people of the land took Jehoahaz the son of Josiah, Và made him king in his father's stead in Giê-ru-sa-lem. 2  Jehoahaz was hai mươi Và three years old when he began to reign, Và he reigned ba months in Giê-ru-sa-lem. 3  Và the king of Ai-Cập put him down at Giê-ru-sa-lem, Và condemned the land in an hundred talents of silver Và a talent of gold. 1 4  Và the king of Ai-Cập made Eliakim his brother king over Giu-đa Và Giê-ru-sa-lem, Và turned his name to Jehoiakim. Và Necho took Jehoahaz his brother, Và carried him to Ai-Cập. 5  Jehoiakim was hai mươi Và five years old when he began to reign, Và he reigned mười một years in Giê-ru-sa-lem: Và he did that which was evil in the sight of the Lord his God. 6  Against him came up Nebuchadnezzar king of Ba-by-lôn, Và bound him in fetters, to carry him to Ba-by-lôn. 2 7  Nebuchadnezzar also carried of the vessels của nhà of the Lord to Ba-by-lôn, Và put them in his temple at Ba-by-lôn. 8  Now the rest of the acts of Jehoiakim, Và his abominations which he did, Và that which was found in him, kìa, they are written in the book of the kings của I-sơ-ra-ên Và Giu-đa: Và Jehoiachin his son reigned in his stead. 3 9  Jehoiachin was tám years old when he began to reign, Và he reigned ba months Và ten ngày in Giê-ru-sa-lem: Và he did that which was evil in the sight of the Lord. 10  Và when the year was expired, king Nebuchadnezzar sent, Và brought him to Ba-by-lôn, with the goodly vessels của nhà of the Lord, Và made Zedekiah his brother king over Giu-đa Và Giê-ru-sa-lem. 4 5 6 11  Zedekiah was một Và hai mươi years old when he began to reign, Và reigned mười một years in Giê-ru-sa-lem. 12  Và he did that which was evil in the sight of the Lord his God, Và humbled not himself before Giê-rê-mi tiên tri speaking from the mouth of the Lord. 13  Và he also rebelled against king Nebuchadnezzar, who had made him swear by God: but he stiffened his neck, Và hardened his heart from turning unto the Lord ĐCT của I-sơ-ra-ên. 14  Moreover all the chief of the priests, Và the people, transgressed very much after all the abominations of the heathen; Và polluted nhà of the Lord which he had hallowed in Giê-ru-sa-lem. 15  Và the Lord ĐCT of their fathers sent to them by his messengers, rising up betimes, Và sending; because he động lòng thương xót on his people, Và on his dwelling place: 7 8 16  But they mocked the messengers của ĐCT, Và despised his words, Và misused his prophets, until cơn thịnh nộ of the Lord arose against his people, till there was no remedy. 9 17  Therefore he brought upon them the king of the Chaldees, who slew their young men with the thanh gươm in nhà of their sanctuary, Và had no compassion upon young man or maiden, old man, or him that stooped for age: he gave họ all into his hand. 18  Và all the vessels của nhà của ĐCT, great Và small, Và the treasures của nhà of the Lord, Và the treasures of the king, Và of his princes; all these he brought to Ba-by-lôn. 19  Và they burnt nhà của ĐCT, Và brake down the wall of Giê-ru-sa-lem, Và burnt all the palaces thereof bằng lửa, Và destroyed all the goodly vessels thereof. 20  Và them that had escaped from the thanh gươm carried he away to Ba-by-lôn; where they were servants to him and his sons until the reign of the kingdom of Persia: 10 21  To fulfil the word of the Lord by the mouth of Giê-rê-mi, until the land had enjoyed her sabbaths: for as long as she lay desolate she kept sabbath, to fulfil sáu mươi and ten years. 22  Now in the first year of Cyrus king of Persia, that the word of the Lord spoken by the mouth of Giê-rê-mi might be accomplished, the Lord stirred up the spirit of Cyrus king of Persia, that he made a proclamation throughout all his kingdom, and put it also in writing, saying, 23  Thus nói Cyrus king of Persia, All the kingdoms of đất hath the Lord ĐCT of heaven given me; and he hath charged me to build him một căn nhà in Giê-ru-sa-lem, which is in Giu-đa. Who is there among you of all his people? The Lord his God be with him, and let him go up.
