Ephesians
1
1  Phao-lô, an apostle của ĐCGiê-su Christ by the will của ĐCT, to the saints which are at Ephesus, Và to the faithful in Christ ĐCGiê-su: 2 ân-điển be to you, Và peace, from ĐCT our Father, Và from the Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ. 3  Ngợi khen ĐCT và Cha Chúa của chúng ta ĐCGiê-su Christ, Đấng ban phước chúng ta với mọi thứ phước thiêng liêng trong [mọi nơi] trên trời trong [Đấng] Christ: 4 According as he hath chosen us in him before the foundation of the world, that we should be holy Và without blame before him in love: 5 Having predestinated us unto the adoption of children by ĐCGiê-su Christ to himself, according to the good pleasure of his will, 6 To the praise of the glory of his ân-điển, wherein he hath made us accepted in the beloved. 7 In whom we have redemption through his blood, the forgiveness of tội lỗi, according to the riches of his ân-điển; 8 Wherein he hath abounded toward us in all wisdom Và prudence; 9 Having made known unto us the mystery of his will, according to his good pleasure which he hath purposed in himself: 10 That in the dispensation of the fulness of times he might gather together in one all things in Christ, both which are in heaven, Và which are on đất; even in him: 11 In whom also we have obtained an inheritance, being predestinated according to the purpose of him who worketh all things after the counsel of his own will: 12 That we should be to the praise of his glory, who first trusted in Christ. 13 In whom ye also trusted, after that ye heard the word of truth, phúc-âm of your salvation: in whom also after that ye believed, ye were sealed with that holy Spirit of promise, 14 Which is the earnest of our inheritance until the redemption of the purchased possession, unto the praise of his glory. 15 Wherefore I also, after I heard of your đức tin in the Lord ĐCGiê-su, Và love unto all thánh đồ, 16 Cease not to give thanks for you, making mention of you in my prayers; 17 That the God of our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ, the Father of glory, may give unto you the spirit of wisdom Và revelation in the knowledge of him: 18 The eyes of your understanding being enlightened; that ye may know what is the hy vọng of his calling, Và what the riches of the glory of his inheritance in thánh đồ, 19 Và what is the exceeding greatness of his power to us-ward who believe, according to the working of his mighty power, 20 Which he wrought in Christ, when he raised him from the dead, Và set him at his own right hand in the heavenly places, 21 Far above all principality, Và power, Và might, Và dominion, Và every name that is named, not only in this world, Nhưng also in that which is to come: 22 Và hath put all things under his feet, Và gave him to be the head over all things to the church, 23 Which is his body, the fulness of him that filleth all in all.
