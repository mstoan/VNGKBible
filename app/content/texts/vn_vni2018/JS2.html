1  Và Giô-suê the son of Nun sent out of Shittim hai men to spy secretly, saying, Go view the land, even Giê-ri-cô. Và they went, Và came into an harlot's nhà, named Rahab, Và lodged there. 1 2 2  Và it was told vua of Giê-ri-cô, saying, kìa, there came men in hither to night of the children của I-sơ-ra-ên to search out the country. 3  Và the king of Giê-ri-cô sent unto Rahab, saying, Bring forth the men that are come to thee, which are entered into thine house: for they be come to search out all the country. 4  Và the woman took the two men, Và hid them, and nói thus, There came men unto me, but I wist not whence they were: 5 Và xảy đến about the time of shutting of cổng, when it was dark, that the men went out: whither the men went I wot not: pursue after them quickly; for ye shall overtake them. 6  But she had brought them up to the roof of nhà, and hid them with the stalks of flax, which she had laid in order upon the roof. 7  Và the men pursued after them the way to Giô-đanh unto the fords: Và as soon as they which pursued after them were gone out, they shut cổng. 8  Và before they were laid down, she came up với họ upon the roof; 9  Và she nói unto the men, I know that the Lord hath given you the land, Và that your terror is fallen upon us, and that all the inhabitants of the land faint vì of you. 3 10  For we have heard how the Lord dried up nước of the Red sea for you, when ye came out of Ai-Cập; Và what ye did unto the two kings of the Amorites, that were on the other side Giô-đanh, Sihon Và Og, whom ye utterly destroyed. 11  Và as soon as we had heard these things, our hearts did melt, neither did there remain any more courage in any man, vì of you: for the Lord your God, he is ĐCT in heaven above, Và in đất beneath. 4 12  Now therefore, I pray you, swear unto me by the Lord, since I have shewed you kindness, that ye will also shew kindness unto my father's nhà, Và give me a true token: 13 Và that ye will save alive my father, Và my mother, Và my brethren, Và my sisters, Và all that they have, and deliver our lives from death. 14  Và the men trả lời her, Our sự sống for yours, if ye utter not this our business. Và it shall be, when the Lord hath given us the land, that we will deal kindly Và truly with thee. 5 15  Then she let them down by a cord through the window: for her house was upon the town wall, Và she dwelt upon the wall. 16  Và she nói với họ, Get you to the mountain, lest the pursuers meet you; and hide yourselves there three ngày, until the pursuers be returned: Và afterward may ye go your way. 17  Và the men nói unto her, We will be blameless of this thine oath which thou hast made us swear. 18 kìa, khi we come into the land, thou shalt bind this line of scarlet thread in the window which thou didst let us down by: Và thou shalt bring thy father, Và thy mother, Và thy brethren, Và all thy father's gia đình, home unto thee. 6 19 Và it shall be, that whosoever shall go out of the doors of thy house into the street, his blood shall be upon his head, and we will be guiltless: Và whosoever shall be with thee in nhà, his blood shall be on our head, if any tay be upon him. 20  Và if thou utter this our business, then we will be quit of thine oath which thou hast made us to swear. 21  Và she nói, According cùng các ngươir words, so be it. And she sent them away, Và they departed: Và she bound the scarlet line in the window. 22  Và they went, Và came unto the mountain, Và abode there three ngày, until the pursuers were returned: Và the pursuers sought họ throughout all the way, but found họ not. 23  So the two men returned, Và descended from the mountain, Và passed over, Và came to Giô-suê the son of Nun, Và told him all things that befell them: 24  Và họ nói unto Giô-suê, Truly the Lord hath delivered into our hands all the land; for even all the inhabitants of the country do faint vì of us. 7
