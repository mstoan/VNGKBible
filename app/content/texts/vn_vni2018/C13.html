3
1  Và I, anh em, could không speak unto you as unto spiritual, Nhưng as untocarnal, even as unto babes in Christ. 2  I have fed you with milk, Và không with meat: for hitherto ye were not able to bear it, neither yet bây giờ are ye able. 3  For ye are yet carnal: for whereas there is among you envying, Và strife, Và divisions, are ye không carnal, Và walk as men? 4  For while một nói, I am of Phao-lô; Và another, I am of Apollos; are ye không carnal? 5  Who then is Phao-lô, Và who is Apollos, Nhưng ministers by whom ye believed, even as the Lord gave to every man? 6  I have planted, Apollos watered; Nhưng ĐCT gave the increase. 7  So then neither is he that planteth any thing, neither he that watereth; Nhưng ĐCT that giveth the increase. 8  bây giờ he that planteth Và he that watereth are một: Và every man shall receive his own phần thưởng according to his own labour. 9  For we are labourers together with God: ye are God's husbandry, ye are God's building. 10  According to the grace của ĐCT which is given unto me, as a wise masterbuilder, I have laid the foundation, Và another buildeth thereon. Nhưng let every man Hãy thận trọng how he buildeth thereupon. 11  For other foundation có thể không ai lay than that is laid, which is ĐCGiê-su Christ. 12  bây giờ nếu any man build upon this foundation gold, silver, precious stones, wood, hay, stubble; 13  Every man's work shall be made manifest: for the day shall declare it, vì it shall be revealed by lửa; Và lửa shall try every man's work of what sort it is. 14  nếu any man's work abide which he hath built thereupon, he shallreceive a reward. 15  nếu any man's work shall be burned, he shall suffer loss: Nhưng he himself shall được cứu; yet so as by lửa. 16  Know ye không rằng ye are đền thờ của ĐCT, Và that [Thánh] Linh của ĐCT dwelleth in you? 17  nếu any man defile đền thờ của ĐCT, him shall ĐCT destroy; for đền thờ của ĐCT is holy, which đền thờ ye are. 18  Let không ai deceive himself. nếu any man among you seemeth to be khôn ngoan in this world, let him become a fool, that he may be khôn ngoan. 19  For sự khôn ngoan of this world is foolishness với ĐCT. For it is written, He taketh the wise in their own craftiness. 20  Và again, The Lord biết the thoughts of the wise, rằng họ là vain. 21  Therefore let không ai glory in men. For all things are yours; 22  hoặc Phao-lô, or Apollos, or Sê-pha, or thế gian, or sự sống, or death, or things present, or things to come; all are yours; 23  Và ye are Christ's; Và Christ is God's.
