9
1 Và as Giê-su passed by, he saw a man which was blind from his birth. 2 Và his disciples asked him, saying, Master, who did sin, this man, or his parents, that he was born blind? 3 ĐCGiê-su trả lời, Neither hath this man sinned, nor his parents: but that the works của ĐCT should be made manifest in him. 4 I must work the works of him that sent me, while it is day: the night cometh, when không ai can work. 5 As long as I am in the world, I am the light of the world. 6 When he had thus spoken, he spat on the ground, Và made clay of the spittle, Và he anointed the eyes of the blind man with the clay, 7 Và nói unto him, Go, wash in the pool of Siloam, (which is by interpretation, Sent.) He went his way therefore, Và washed, Và đến seeing.
8 The neighbours therefore, Và they which before had seen him that he was blind, nói, Is not this he that sat Và begged? 9 Some nói, This is he: others nói, He is like him: but he nói, I am he. 10 Therefore nói they unto him, How were thine eyes opened? 11 He trả lời Và nói, A man that is called ĐCGiê-su made clay, Và anointed mine eyes, Và nói unto me, Go to the pool of Siloam, Và wash: Và I went Và washed, Và I received sight. 12 Then nói they unto him, Where is he? He nói, I know not.
13 They brought to the những người Pha-ri-si him that aforetime was blind. 14 Và it was the ngày Sa-bát when ĐCGiê-su made the clay, Và opened his eyes. 15 Then again the những người Pha-ri-si also asked him how he had received his sight. He nói unto them, He put clay upon mine eyes, Và I washed, Và do see. 16 Therefore nói some of the những người Pha-ri-si, This man is not of ĐCT, because he keepeth not the ngày Sa-bát. Others nói, How can a man that is a sinner do such miracles? Và there was a division among them. 17 They say unto the blind man again, What sayest thou of him, that he hath opened thine eyes? He nói, He is a prophet. 18 But the Jews did not believe concerning him, that he had been blind, Và received his sight, until they called the parents of him that had received his sight. 19 Và they asked them, saying, Is this your son, who ye say was born blind? how then doth he now see? 20 His parents trả lời them Và nói, We know that this is our son, Và that he was born blind: 21 But by what means he now seeth, we know not; or who hath opened his eyes, chúng ta know not: he is of age; ask him: he shall speak for himself. 22 These words spake his parents, because they feared the Jews: for the Jews had agreed already, that nếu any man did confess that he was Christ, he should be put out of the nhà hội. 23 Therefore nói his parents, He is of age; ask him. 24 Then again called they the man that was blind, Và nói unto him, Give ĐCT the praise: chúng ta know that this man is a sinner. 25 He trả lời Và nói, Whether he be a sinner or no, I know not: one thing I know, that, whereas I was blind, now I see. 26 Then nói they to him again, What did he to thee? how opened he thine eyes? 27 He trả lời them, I have told you already, Và ye did not hear: wherefore would ye hear it again? will ye also be his disciples? 28 Then they reviled him, Và nói, Thou art his disciple; but chúng ta are Môi-se' disciples. 29 chúng ta know that ĐCT spake unto Môi-se: as for this fellow, we know not from whence he is. 30 The man trả lời Và nói unto them, Why herein is a marvellous thing, that ye know not from whence he is, Và yet he hath opened mine eyes. 31 Now we know that ĐCT heareth not sinners: but nếu any man be a worshipper của ĐCT, Và doeth his will, him he heareth. 32 Since the world began was it not heard that any man opened the eyes of one that was born blind. 33 nếu this man were not of ĐCT, he could do nothing. 34 They trả lời Và nói unto him, Thou wast altogether born in tội lỗi, Và dost thou teach us? Và they cast him out. 35 ĐCGiê-su heard that they had cast him out; Và when he had found him, he nói unto him, Dost thou believe on the Son của ĐCT? 36 He trả lời Và nói, Who is he, Lord, that I might believe on him? 37 Và ĐCGiê-su nói unto him, Thou hast both seen him, Và it is he that talketh with thee. 38 Và he nói, Lord, I believe. Và he worshipped him.
39 Và ĐCGiê-su nói, For judgment I am come into this world, that they which see not might see; Và that they which see might be made blind. 40 Và some of the những người Pha-ri-si which were with him heard these words, Và nói unto him, Are chúng ta blind also? 41 ĐCGiê-su nói unto them, nếu ye were blind, ye should have no sin: but now ye say, We see; therefore your sin remaineth.
