1  Đa-vít therefore departed thence, and escaped to the cave Adullam: Và when anh em người Và all his father's nhà heard it, they went down thither to him. 2  Và every one that was in distress, Và every one that was in debt, Và every one that was discontented, gathered themselves unto him; and he became a captain over them: and there were with him about four trăm men. 1 2 3  Và Đa-vít went thence to Mizpeh of Moab: Và he nói unto the king of Moab, Let my father Và my mother, I pray thee, come forth, Và be with you, till I know what God will do for me. 4  Và he brought them before vua of Moab: Và they dwelt with him all the while that Đa-vít was in the hold. 5  Và the prophet Gad nói unto Đa-vít, Abide not in the hold; depart, Và get thee into the land of Giu-đa. Then Đa-vít departed, Và came into the forest of Hareth. 6  When Sau-lơ heard that Đa-vít was discovered, Và the men that were with him, (now Sau-lơ abode in Gibeah under a tree in Ramah, having his spear in his hand, Và all his servants were standing about him;)3 7  Then Sau-lơ nói unto his servants that stood about him, Hear now, ye Benjamites; will the son of Jesse give every one of you fields Và vườn nhos, Và make you all captains of thousands, Và captains of hundreds; 8  That all of you have conspired against me, and there is none that sheweth me that my son hath made a league with the son of Jesse, and there is none of you that is sorry for me, or sheweth unto me that my son hath stirred up my servant against me, to lie in wait, as at this day? 4 9  Then trả lời Doeg the Edomite, which was set over the servants of Sau-lơ, và nói, I saw the son of Jesse coming to Nob, to Ahimelech the son of Ahitub. 10  Và he enquired of the Lord for him, and gave him victuals, Và gave him the thanh gươm of Goliath the Philistine. 11  Then the king sent to call Ahimelech the priest, the son of Ahitub, Và all his father's nhà, the priests that were in Nob: Và they came all of them to the king. 12  Và Sau-lơ nói, Hear now, thou son of Ahitub. Và he trả lời, Here I am, my lord. 5 13  Và Sau-lơ nói unto him, Tại sao have ye conspired against me, thou and the son of Jesse, in that thou hast given him bread, Và a thanh gươm, Và hast enquired của ĐCT for him, that he should rise against me, to lie in wait, as at this day? 14  Then Ahimelech trả lời vua, và nói, And who is so faithful among all thy servants as Đa-vít, which is the king's son in law, Và goeth at thy bidding, Và is honourable in thine house? 15  Did I then begin to enquire của ĐCT for him? be it far from me: let not the king impute any thing unto his servant, nor to all nhà of my father: for thy servant knew nothing of all this, less or more. 6 16  Và the king nói, Thou shalt surely chết, Ahimelech, thou, and all thy father's nhà. 17  Và the king nói unto the footmen that stood about him, Turn, Và slay the priests of the Lord; because their hand also is with Đa-vít, Và because they knew when he fled, Và did not shew it to me. But the servants of the king would not put forth their hand to fall upon the priests of the Lord. 7 18  Và the king nói to Doeg, Turn thou, and fall upon the priests. Và Doeg the Edomite turned, Và he fell upon the priests, Và slew on that day fourscore Và five persons that did wear a linen ephod. 19  Và Nob, the city of the priests, smote he with the edge of the thanh gươm, both men Và women, children Và sucklings, Và oxen, Và asses, Và sheep, with the edge of the thanh gươm. 20  Và one of the sons of Ahimelech the son of Ahitub, named Abiathar, escaped, Và fled after Đa-vít. 21  Và Abiathar shewed Đa-vít that Sau-lơ had slain the Lord's priests. 22  Và Đa-vít nói unto Abiathar, I knew it that day, when Doeg the Edomite was there, that he would surely tell Sau-lơ: I have occasioned the death of all the persons of thy father's nhà. 23  Abide thou with me, fear not: for he that seeketh my sự sống seeketh thy sự sống: but with me thou shalt be in safeguard.
