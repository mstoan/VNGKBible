1 Thessalonians
1
1 Phao-lô, Và Silvanus, Và Ti-mô-thê, unto the church of the Thessalonians which is in ĐCT the Father Và in the Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ: ân-điển be unto you, Và peace, from ĐCT our Father, Và the Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ. 2 We give thanks to God always for you all, making mention of you in our prayers; 3 Remembering without ceasing your work of faith, Và labour of love, Và patience of hy vọng in our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ, in the sight của ĐCT Và our Father; 4 Knowing, anh em beloved, your election of ĐCT. 5 For our gospel đến not unto you in word only, Nhưng also in power, Và in the Holy Ghost, Và in much assurance; as ye know what manner of men we were among you for your sake. 6 Và ye became followers of us, Và của Chúa, having received the word in much affliction, with joy of the Holy Ghost: 7 đến đổi ye were ensamples to all that believe in Macedonia Và Achaia. 8 For from you sounded out the word of the Lord not only in Macedonia Và Achaia, Nhưng also in every place your đức tin to ĐCT-ward is spread abroad; đến đổi we need not to speak any thing. 9 For they themselves shew of us what manner of entering in we had unto you, Và how ye turned to ĐCT from idols to serve the living Và true ĐCT; 10 Và to wait for his Son from heaven, whom he raised from the dead, even ĐCGiê-su, which delivered us from the wrath to come.
