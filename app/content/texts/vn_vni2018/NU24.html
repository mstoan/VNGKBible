1  Và when Balaam nhìn thấy that it pleased the Lord to bless I-sơ-ra-ên, he went not, as at other times, to seek for enchantments, but he set his face toward the wilderness. 1 2  Và Balaam lifted up his eyes, Và he saw I-sơ-ra-ên abiding in his tents according to their tribes; Và the spirit của ĐCT came upon him. 3  Và he took up his parable, và nói, Balaam the son of Beor hath nói, Và the man whose eyes are open hath nói: 2 4  He hath nói, which heard the words của ĐCT, which saw the vision of the Almighty, falling into a trance, but having his eyes open: 5  How goodly are thy tents, O Gia-cốp, Và thy tabernacles, O I-sơ-ra-ên! 6  As the valleys are they spread forth, as gardens by the river's side, as the trees of lign aloes which the Lord hath planted, Và as cedar trees beside the waters. 7  He shall pour nước out of his buckets, Và his seed shall be in many waters, Và his king shall be higher than Agag, Và his kingdom shall be exalted. 8  ĐCT brought him forth out of Ai-Cập; he hath as it were the strength of an unicorn: he shall eat up the nations his enemies, Và shall break their bones, Và pierce họ through with his arrows. 9  He couched, he lay down as a lion, Và as a great lion: who shall stir him up? phước is he that blesseth thee, and cursed is he that curseth thee. 10  Và Balak's anger was kindled against Balaam, Và he smote his hands together: Và Balak nói unto Balaam, I called thee to curse mine enemies, and, kìa, thou hast altogether phước họ these three times. 11  Therefore now flee thou to thy place: I thought to promote thee unto great honour; but, lo, the Lord hath kept thee back from honour. 12  Và Balaam nói unto Balak, nói I not also to thy messengers which thou sentest unto me, saying, 13  If Balak would give me his house full of silver Và gold, I cannot go bên kia the commandment of the Lord, to do either good or bad of mine own mind; but what the Lord nói, that will I speak? 14  Và now, kìa, I go unto my people: come therefore, and I will advertise thee what this people shall do to thy people in the latter ngày. 15  Và he took up his parable, và nói, Balaam the son of Beor hath nói, Và the man whose eyes are open hath nói: 16  He hath nói, which heard the words của ĐCT, Và knew sự hiểu biết of the most High, which nhìn thấy the vision of the Almighty, falling into a trance, but having his eyes open: 17  I sẽ thấy him, but not now: I shall kìa him, but not nigh: there shall come a Star out of Gia-cốp, Và a Sceptre shall rise out của I-sơ-ra-ên, Và shall smite the corners of Moab, Và destroy all the children of Sheth. 3 18  Và Edom shall be a possession, Seir also shall be a possession for his enemies; Và I-sơ-ra-ên shall do valiantly. 19  Out of Gia-cốp shall come he that shall have dominion, Và shall destroy him that remaineth of the city. 20  Và when he looked on Amalek, he took up his parable, và nói, Amalek was the first of the nations; but his latter end shall be that he perish for ever. 4 5 21  Và he looked on the Kenites, Và took up his parable, và nói, Strong is thy dwellingplace, Và thou puttest thy nest in a rock. 22  Nevertheless the Kenite shall be wasted, until Asshur shall carry thee away captive. 6 7 23  Và he took up his parable, và nói, Alas, who shall live when God doeth this! 24  Và ships shall come from the coast of Chittim, Và shall afflict Asshur, Và shall afflict Eber, Và he also shall perish for ever. 25  Và Balaam rose up, Và went Và returned to his place: Và Balak also went his way.
