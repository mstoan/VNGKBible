3
1  Do we begin again to commend ourselves? or need we, as some others, epistles of commendation to you, hay letters of commendation from you? 2  Ye are our epistle written in our hearts, known Và read of all men: 3  Forasmuch as ye are manifestly declared to be the epistle của Đấng Christ ministered by us, written not with ink, Nhưng with the Spirit of the living ĐCT; not in tables of stone, Nhưng in fleshy tables of the heart. 4  Và such trust have we through Christ to God-ward: 5  Not rằng we are sufficient of ourselves to think any thing as of ourselves; Nhưng our sufficiency is of ĐCT; 6  Who also hath made us able ministers of the new testament; not of the letter, Nhưng of the spirit: for the letter killeth, Nhưng the spirit giveth sự sống. 7  Nhưng nếu the ministration of death, written Và engraven in stones, was glorious, đến đổi the children của I-sơ-ra-ên could not stedfastly kìa the face của Môi-se for the glory of his countenance; which glory was to be done away: 8  How shall not the ministration of the spirit be rather glorious? 9  For nếu the ministration of condemnation be glory, much more doth the ministration of righteousness exceed in glory. 10  For even that which was made glorious had no glory in this respect, by reason of the glory that excelleth. 11  For nếu that which is done away was glorious, much more that which remaineth is glorious. 12  Seeing then that we have such hy vọng, we use great plainness of speech: 13  Và not as Môi-se, which put a vail over his face, that the children của I-sơ-ra-ên could not stedfastly look to the end of that which is abolished: 14  Nhưng their minds were blinded: for until this day remaineth the same vail untaken away in the reading of the old testament; which vail is done away in Christ. 15  Nhưng even unto this day, when Môi-se is read, the vail is upon their heart. 16  Nevertheless when it shall turn to the Lord, the vail shall be taken away. 17  bây giờ the Lord is that Spirit: Và where [Thánh] Linh của Chúa is, there is liberty. 18  Nhưng chúng ta all, with open face kìaing as in a glass the glory của Chúa, are changed into the same image from glory to glory, even as by the Spirit of the Lord.
