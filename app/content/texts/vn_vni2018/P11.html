1 Peter
1
1 Phi-e-rơ, Sứ Đồ của ĐCGiê-su Christ, cho những kẻ kiều ngụ rải rác [trong] [xứ] Bông, Ga-la-ti, Cáp-ba-đốc, A-si, và Bi-thi-ni, 2 Elect according to the foreknowledge của ĐCT the Father, through sanctification of the Spirit, unto obedience Và sprinkling of the blood của ĐCGiê-su Christ: Grace unto you, Và peace, be multiplied. 3 Blessed be the God Và Father of our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ, which according to his abundant mercy hath begotten us again unto a lively hy vọng by the resurrection của ĐCGiê-su Christ from the dead, 4 To an inheritance incorruptible, Và undefiled, Và that fadeth not away, reserved in heaven for you, 5 Who are kept by the power của ĐCT through faith unto salvation ready to be revealed in the last time. 6 Wherein ye greatly rejoice, though now for a season, nếu need be, ye are in heaviness through manifold temptations: 7 That the trial of your faith, being much more precious than of gold that perisheth, though it be tried with fire, might be found unto praise Và honour Và glory at the appearing của ĐCGiê-su Christ: 8 Whom having not seen, ye love; in whom, though now ye see him not, yet believing, ye rejoice with joy unspeakable Và full of glory: 9 Receiving the end of your faith, even the salvation of your souls. 10 Of which salvation the prophets have enquired Và searched diligently, who prophesied of the grace that should come unto you: 11 Searching what, or what manner of time the Spirit của Đấng Christ which was in them did signify, when it testified beforehand the sufferings of Christ, Và the glory that should follow. 12 Unto whom it was revealed, that not unto themselves, but unto us they did minister the things, which are now reported unto you by them that have preached phúc-âm unto you with the Holy Ghost sent down from heaven; which things các thiên sứ desire to look into. 13 Wherefore gird up the loins of your mind, be sober, Và hy vọng to the end for the grace that is to be brought unto you at the revelation của ĐCGiê-su Christ; 14 As obedient children, not fashioning yourselves according to the former lusts in your ignorance: 15 But as he which hath called you is holy, so be ye holy in all manner of conversation; 16 Because it is written, Be ye holy; for I am holy. 17 Và nếu ye call on the Father, who without respect of persons judgeth according to every man's work, pass the time of your sojourning here in fear: 18 Forasmuch as ye know that ye were not redeemed with corruptible things, as silver Và gold, from your vain conversation received by tradition from your fathers; 19 But with the precious blood của Đấng Christ, as of a lamb without blemish Và without spot: 20 Who Quả thật was foreordained before the foundation of the world, but was manifest in these last times for you, 21 Who by him do believe in ĐCT, that raised him up from the dead, Và gave him glory; that your faith Và hy vọng might be in ĐCT. 22 Seeing ye have purified your souls in obeying the truth through the Spirit unto unfeigned love of the brethren, see that ye love one another with a pure heart fervently: 23 Being born again, not of corruptible seed, but of incorruptible, by the word của ĐCT, which liveth Và abideth for ever. 24 For all flesh is as grass, Và all the glory of man as the flower of grass. The grass withereth, Và the flower thereof falleth away: 25 But the word của Chúa endureth for ever. Và this is the word which by phúc-âm is preached unto you.
