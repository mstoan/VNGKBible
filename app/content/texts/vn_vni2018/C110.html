10
1 Moreover, hỡi anh em, I would not that ye should be ignorant, how that all our fathers were under the cloud, Và all passed through biển; 2 Và were all baptized unto Môi-se in the cloud Và in biển; 3 Và did all eat the same spiritual meat; 4 Và did all drink the same spiritual drink: for they drank of that spiritual Rock that followed them: Và that Rock was Christ. 5 Nhưng with many of them ĐCT was not well pleased: for they were overthrown in the wilderness. 6 bây giờ these things were our examples, to the intent we should not lust after evil things, as they also lusted. 7 Neither be ye idolaters, as were some of them; as it is written, The people sat down to eat Và drink, Và rose up to play. 8 Neither let us commit fornication, as some of them committed, Và rơi in một day ba Và twenty thousand. 9 Neither let us tempt Christ, as some of them also tempted, Và were destroyed of serpents. 10 Neither murmur ye, as some of them also murmured, Và were destroyed of the destroyer. 11 bây giờ all these things happened unto them for ensamples: Và they are written for our admonition, upon whom the ends of the world are come. 12 Wherefore let him that thinketh he standeth take heed lest he fall. 13 There hath no temptation taken you but such as is common to man: Nhưng ĐCT is faithful, who will not suffer you to be tempted above that ye are able; Nhưng will with the temptation also make a way to escape, that ye may be able to bear it. 14 Wherefore, my Hỡi kẻ yêu dấu, flee from idolatry. 15 I speak as to wise men; judge ye what I say. 16 The cup of blessing which we bless, is it not the communion of the blood của Đấng Christ? The bread which we break, is it not the communion of the body của Đấng Christ? 17 For we being many are một bread, Và một body: for we are all partakers of that one bread. 18 kìa I-sơ-ra-ên after the flesh: are not they which eat of the sacrifices partakers of the altar? 19 What say I then? that the idol is any thing, hay that which is offered in sacrifice to idols is any thing? 20 Nhưng I say, that the things which the Gentiles sacrifice, they sacrifice to devils, Và not to God: Và I would not that ye should have fellowship with devils. 21 Ye cannot drink the cup của Chúa, Và the cup of devils: ye cannot be partakers của Chúa table, Và of the table of devils. 22 Do we provoke the Lord to jealousy? are we stronger than he? 23 All things are lawful for me, Nhưng all things are not expedient: all things are lawful for me, Nhưng all things edify not. 24 Let không ai seek his own, Nhưng every man another's wealth. 25 Whatsoever is sold in the shambles, that eat, asking no question for conscience sake: 26 For the đất is the Lord's, Và the fulness thereof. 27 nếu any of them that believe not bid you to a feast, Và ye be disposed to go; whatsoever is set before you, eat, asking no question for conscience sake. 28 Nhưng nếu any man say unto you, This is offered in sacrifice unto idols, eat not for his sake that shewed it, Và for conscience sake: for the đất is the Lord's, Và the fulness thereof: 29 Conscience, I say, not thine own, Nhưng of the other: for Tại sao is my liberty judged of another man's conscience? 30 For nếu I by grace be a partaker, Tại sao am I evil spoken of for that for which I give thanks? 31 Whether therefore ye eat, or drink, or whatsoever ye do, do all to the glory của ĐCT. 32 Give none offence, neither to the Jews, nor to the Gentiles, nor to the church của ĐCT: 33 Even as I please all men in all things, not seeking mine own profit, Nhưng the profit of many, that they may được cứu.
