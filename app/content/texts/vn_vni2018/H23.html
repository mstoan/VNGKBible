3
1  Finally, anh em, cầu nguyện for us, that the word của Chúa may have free course, Và be glorified, even as it is với you: 2  Và that we may be delivered từ unreasonable Và wicked men: for all men havekhông đức tin. 3  Nhưng the Lord is faithful, who shall stablish you, Và keep you từ evil. 4  Và we have confidence in the Lord touching you, rằng ye both do Và will do the things which we command you. 5  Và the Lord direct your hearts into tình yêu của ĐCT, Và into the patient waiting for Christ. 6  bây giờ we command you, anh em, in the name of our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ, that ye withdraw yourselves từ every brother that walketh disorderly, Và không after truyền thống which he received of us. 7  For yourselves know how ye ought to follow us: for we behaved không ourselves disorderly among you; 8  Neither did we eat any man's bánh for nought; Nhưng wrought với labour Và travail night Và day, that we might không be chargeable to any of you: 9  Not vì we have không power, Nhưng to make ourselves an ensample cùng các ngươi to follow us. 10  For even khi we were với you, this we commanded you, rằng nếu any would không work, neither should he eat. 11  For we hear that there are some which walk among you disorderly, working not at all, Nhưng are busybodies. 12  bây giờ them that are such we command Và exhort by our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ, that với quietness they work, and ăn their own bánh. 13  Nhưng ye, anh em, be không weary in well doing. 14  Và nếu any man vâng lời không our word by this epistle, note that man, Và have no company with him, that he may be ashamed. 15  Yet count him không as an enemy, Nhưng admonish him as a brother. 16  bây giờ the Lord of peace himself give you peace always by all means. The Lord be với you all. 17  The salutation of Phao-lô with mine own tay, which is the token in every epistle: so I write. 18  The grace of our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ be với you all. Amen.
