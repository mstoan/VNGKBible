19
1  Và xảy đến, that, while Apollos was at Cô-rin-tô, Phao-lô having passed through the upper coasts đến to Ephesus: Và finding certain các môn đệ, 2  He nói unto them, Have ye received the Holy Ghost since ye believed? Và they nói unto him, We have not so much as heard whether there be any Holy Ghost. 3  Và he nói unto them, Unto what then were ye baptized? Và they nói, Unto John's baptism. 4  Then nói Phao-lô, Giăng Quả thật baptized with the baptism of repentance, Nói rằng untothe people, that they should believe on him which should come after him, that is, on Christ ĐCGiê-su. 5  When they heard this, they were baptized in the name của Chúa ĐCGiê-su. 6  Và when Paul had laid his hands upon them, the Holy Ghost đến on them; Và they spake with tongues, Và prophesied. 7  Và all the men were about mười hai. 8  Và he went into nhà hội, Và spake boldly for the space of ba months, disputing Và persuading the things concerning vuadom của ĐCT. 9  Nhưng when divers were hardened, Và believed not, but spake evil of that way before đám đông, he departed from them, Và separated các môn đệ, disputing daily in the school of one Tyrannus. 10  Và this continued by the space of two years; đến đổi all they which dwelt in Asia heard the word của Chúa ĐCGiê-su, both Jews Và Greeks. 11  Và ĐCT wrought special miracles by the hands of Paul: 12  đến đổi from his body were brought unto the sick handkerchiefs hay aprons, Và the diseases departed from them, Và the evil spirits went out of them. 13  Then certain of the vagabond Jews, exorcists, took upon them to call over them which had evil spirits the name của Chúa ĐCGiê-su, Nói rằng, We adjure you by Jesus whom Phao-lô preacheth. 14  Và there were seven sons of one Sceva, một người Giu-đa, Và chief of the priests, which did so. 15  Và the evil spirit trả lời và nói, ĐCGiê-su I know, Và Phao-lô I know; Nhưng who are ye? 16  Và the man in whom the evil spirit was leaped on them, Và overcame them, Và prevailed against them, đến đổi they fled out of that nhà naked Và wounded. 17  Và this was known to all the Jews Và Greeks also dwelling at Ephesus; Và fear rơi on them all, Và the name của Chúa ĐCGiê-su was magnified. 18  Và many that believed đến, Và confessed, Và shewed their deeds. 19  Many of them also which used curious arts brought their books together, Và burned them before all men: Và they counted the price of them, Và found it fifty thousand pieces of silver. 20  So mightily grew the word của ĐCT Và prevailed. 21  After these things were ended, Phao-lô purposed in the spirit, when he had passed through Macedonia Và Achaia, to go to Giê-ru-sa-lem, Nói rằng, After I have been there, I must also see Rome. 22  So he sent into Macedonia two of them that ministered unto him, Ti-mô-thê Và Erastus; but he himself stayed in Asia for a season. 23  Và the same time there arose no small stir about that way. 24  For a certain man named Demetrius, a silversmith, which made silver shrines for Diana, brought no small gain unto the craftsmen; 25  Whom he called together with the workmen of like occupation, và nói, Sirs, ye know that by this craft we have our wealth. 26  Moreover ye see Và hear, that not alone at Ephesus, Nhưng almost throughout all Asia, this Phao-lô hath persuaded Và turned away much people, Nói rằng that they be no gods, which are made with hands: 27  đến đổi not only this our craft is in danger to be set at nought; Nhưng also that the temple of the great goddess Diana should be despised, Và her magnificence should be destroyed, whom all Asia Và thế gian worshippeth. 28  Và when they heard these sayings, they were full of wrath, Và cried out, Nói rằng, Great is Diana of the Ephesians. 29  Và the whole city was filled with confusion: Và having caught Gai-út Và Aristarchus, men of Macedonia, Paul's companions in travel, they rushed with one accord into the theatre. 30  Và when Paul would have entered in unto the people, các môn đệ suffered him not. 31  Và certain of the chief of Asia, which were his friends, sent unto him, desiring him that he would not adventure himself into the theatre. 32  Some therefore cried one thing, Và some another: for the assembly was confused; Và the more part knew not wherefore they were come together. 33  Và they drew Alexander out of đám đông, the Jews putting him forward. Và Alexander beckoned with the hand, Và would have made his defence unto the people. 34  Nhưng when they knew that he was một người Giu-đa, all with một voice about the space of two hours cried out, Great is Diana of the Ephesians. 35  Và when the townclerk had appeased the people, he nói, Ye men of Ephesus, what man is there that knoweth not how thatthe city of the Ephesians is a worshipper of the great goddess Diana, Và of the image which fell down from Jupiter? 36  Seeing then that these things cannot be spoken against, ye ought to be quiet, Và to do nothing rashly. 37  For ye have brought hither these men, which are neither robbers of churches, nor yet blasphemers of your goddess. 38  Wherefore nếu Demetrius, Và the craftsmen which are with him, have a matter against any man, luật pháp is open, Và there are deputies: let them implead one another. 39  Nhưng nếu ye enquire any thing concerning other matters, it shall be determined in a lawful assembly. 40  For we are in danger to be called in question for this day's uproar, there being no cause whereby we may give an account of this concourse. 41  Và when he had thus spoken, he dismissed the assembly.
