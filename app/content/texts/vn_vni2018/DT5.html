5
1  Và Môi-se called all I-sơ-ra-ên, và nói với họ, Hear, O I-sơ-ra-ên, the statutes Và judgments which I speak in your ears this day, that ye may learn them, and keep, Và do them. 1 2  The CHÚA ĐCT chúng tôi made a covenant with us in Horeb. 3  The CHÚA made not this covenant with our fathers, but with us, even us, who are all of us here alive this day. 4  The CHÚA talked with you face to face in the mount out of the midst of the fire, 5 (I stood between the CHÚA Và you at that time, to shew you the word of the CHÚA: for ye were afraid by reason of the fire, Và went not up into the mount;) nói [rằng], 6  I là the CHÚA thy God, which brought ngươi out of the land of Ai-Cập, from nhà of bondage. 2 7  ngươi shalt have none other gods before me. 8  ngươi shalt not make ngươi any graven image, or any likeness of any thing that is in heaven above, or that is in đất beneath, or that is in the waters beneath đất: 9  ngươi shalt not bow down thyself với họ, nor serve them: for I the CHÚA thy God là a jealous ĐCT, visiting the iniquity of the fathers upon the children unto the third Và fourth dòng dõi of them that hate me, 10  Và shewing mercy unto thousands of them that love me and keep my commandments. 11  ngươi shalt not take Tên of the CHÚA thy God vô ích: for the CHÚA will not hold him guiltless that taketh his name vô ích. 12  Keep ngày Sa-bát ngày to sanctify it, as the CHÚA thy God hath commanded ngươi. 13  sáu ngày ngươi shalt labour, Và do all thy work: 14  But the seventh ngày is ngày Sa-bát of the CHÚA thy God: in it ngươi shalt not do any work, thou, nor thy son, nor thy daughter, nor thy manservant, nor thy maidservant, nor thine ox, nor thine ass, nor any of thy cattle, nor thy stranger that is within thy gates; that thy manservant Và thy maidservant may rest as well as thou. 15  Và remember that ngươi wast a servant in the land of Ai-Cập, and that the CHÚA thy God brought ngươi out thence through a mighty tay Và by a stretched out arm: therefore the CHÚA thy God truyền lệnh ngươi to keep ngày Sa-bát ngày. 16  Honour thy father Và thy mother, as the CHÚA thy God hath commanded ngươi; that thy days may be prolonged, Và that it may go well with ngươi, in the land which the CHÚA thy God giveth ngươi. 17  ngươi shalt not kill. 18  Neither shalt ngươi commit ngoại tình. 19  Neither shalt ngươi steal. 20  Neither shalt ngươi bear false witness against thy neighbour. 21  Neither shalt ngươi desire thy neighbour's vợ, neither shalt ngươi covet thy neighbour's nhà, his field, or his manservant, or his maidservant, his ox, or his ass, or any thing that is thy neighbour's. 22  These words the CHÚA nói unto all your assembly in the mount out of the midst of the fire, of đám mây, Và of the thick darkness, with a great tiếng: Và he added no more. Và he wrote them in two tables of stone, Và delivered them unto me. 23  Và xảy đến, when ye heard tiếng out of the midst of the darkness, ( for núi did burn bằng lửa,) that ye came near unto me, even all the heads of your tribes, Và your elders; 24  Và ye nói, kìa, the CHÚA ĐCT chúng tôi hath shewed us his glory Và his greatness, Và we have heard his voice out of the midst of the fire: we have seen this day that God doth talk with man, Và he liveth. 25  Now therefore why should we die? for this great lửa will consume us: if we hear tiếng of the CHÚA ĐCT chúng tôi any more, then we shall die. 3 26 For who is there of all flesh, that hath heard tiếng of the living ĐCT speaking out of the midst of the fire, as we have, Và lived? 27  Go ngươi near, Và hear all that the CHÚA ĐCT chúng tôi shall say: Và speak ngươi unto us all that the CHÚA ĐCT chúng tôi shall speak unto ngươi; and we will hear it, Và do it. 28  Và the CHÚA nghe tiếng of your words, when ye spake unto me; and the CHÚA nói unto me, I have heard tiếng of the words of this people, which they have spoken unto ngươi: they have well nói all that they have spoken. 29  O that there were such an heart in them, that they would fear me, and keep all my commandments always, that it might be well with them, and with their children đến đời đời! 30  đi say to them, Get you into your tents again. 31  But as for ngươi, stand ngươi here by me, Và I will speak unto ngươi all the commandments, Và the statutes, Và the judgments, which ngươi shalt teach them, that they may do họ in the land which I give them to possess it. 32  ngươi sẽ observe to do therefore as the CHÚA your God hath commanded you: ngươi sẽ not turn aside to the tay phải or to the left. 33  ngươi sẽ walk in all the ways which the CHÚA your God hath commanded you, that ye may live, and that it may be well with you, and that ye may prolong your ngày in the land which ngươi sẽ possess.
