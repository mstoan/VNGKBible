2 Peter
1
1  Si-môn Phi-e-rơ, đầy tớ và Sứ Đồ của ĐCGiê-su Christ, to them that have obtained like precious đức tin with us through the righteousness của ĐCT Và our Saviour ĐCGiê-su Christ: 2  Ân điển Và bình an be multiplied unto you qua sự nhận biết ĐCT, Và của ĐCGiê-su Chúa chúng ta, 3  According as his divine power hath given unto us all things that pertain unto sự sống Và godliness, through the knowledge of him that hath called us to glory Và virtue: 4  Whereby are given unto us exceeding great Và precious promises: that by these ye might be partakers of the divine nature, having escaped the corruption that is in thế gian through lust. 5 Và beside this, giving all diligence, add to your đức tin virtue; Và to virtue knowledge; 6 Và to knowledge temperance; Và to temperance patience; Và to patience godliness; 7 Và to godliness brotherly kindness; Và to brotherly kindness charity. 8  For nếu these things be in you, Và abound, they make you that ye shall neither be barren nor unfruitful in the knowledge of our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ. 9  Nhưng he that lacketh these things is mù lòa, Và cannot see afar off, Và hath forgotten that he was purged from his old tội lỗi. 10  Wherefore the rather, anh em, give diligence to make your calling Và election sure: for if ye do these things, ye shall never fall: 11  For so an entrance shall be ministered unto you abundantly into the everlasting kingdom of our Lord Và Saviour ĐCGiê-su Christ. 12  Wherefore I will not be negligent to put you always in remembrance of these things, though ye know them, Và be established in the present truth. 13 Yea, I think it meet, as long as I am in this tabernacle, to stir you up by putting you in remembrance; 14  Knowing that shortly I must put off this my tabernacle, even as our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ hath shewed me. 15  Moreover I will endeavour that ye may be able after my decease to have these things always in remembrance. 16  For we have not followed cunningly devised fables, when we made known unto you the power Và coming of our Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ, Nhưng were eyewitnesses of his majesty. 17  For he received from ĐCT the Father honour Và glory, when there came such a voice to him from the excellent glory, This is my beloved Son, in whom I am well pleased. 18  Và this voice which came from heaven chúng ta heard, when we were with him in the holy mount. 19  We have also a more sure word of prophecy; whereunto ye do well that ye Hãy thận trọng, as unto a light that shineth in a dark place, until the day dawn, Và the day star Hãy chổi dậy in your hearts: 20 Knowing this first, that no prophecy of the scripture is of any private interpretation. 21  For the prophecy đến not in old time by the will of man: Nhưng holy men of ĐCT spake as they were moved by the Holy Ghost.
