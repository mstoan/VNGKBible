25
1  Và the Lord nói unto Môi-se in mount Sinai, saying, 2  Speak unto the children của I-sơ-ra-ên, Và say với họ, When ye come into the land which I give you, then shall the land keep a sabbath unto the Lord. 1 3  sáu years ngươi shalt sow thy field, Và six years ngươi shalt prune thy vườn nho, Và gather in the fruit thereof; 4  But in the seventh year shall be a sabbath of rest unto the land, a sabbath for the Lord: ngươi shalt neither sow thy field, nor prune thy vườn nho. 5  That which groweth of its own accord of thy harvest ngươi shalt not reap, neither gather the grapes of thy vine undressed: for it is a year of rest unto the land. 2 6  Và ngày Sa-bát of the land shall be meat for you; for ngươi, and for thy servant, Và for thy maid, Và for thy hired servant, Và for thy stranger that sojourneth with ngươi, 7  Và for thy cattle, Và for the beast that are in thy land, shall all the increase thereof be meat. 8  Và ngươi shalt number bảy sabbaths of years unto ngươi, seven times bảy years; Và the space of the seven sabbaths of years shall be unto ngươi forty Và nine years. 9  Then shalt ngươi cause the trumpet of the jubile to sound on the tenth ngày of the seventh month, in the day of atonement shall ye make the trumpet sound throughout all your land. 3 10  Và ngươi sẽ hallow the fiftieth year, Và proclaim liberty throughout all the land unto all the inhabitants thereof: it shall be a jubile cùng các ngươi; and ngươi sẽ return every man unto his possession, Và ngươi sẽ return every man unto his gia đình. 11  A jubile shall that fiftieth year be cùng các ngươi: ngươi sẽ not sow, neither reap that which groweth of itself in it, nor gather the grapes in it of thy vine undressed. 12 For it is the jubile; it shall be holy cùng các ngươi: ngươi sẽ eat the increase thereof out of the field. 13  In the year of this jubile ngươi sẽ return every man unto his possession. 14  Và if ngươi sell ought unto thy neighbour, or buyest ought of thy neighbour's tay, ngươi sẽ not oppress một another: 15  According to the number of years after the jubile ngươi shalt buy of thy neighbour, Và according unto the number of years of the fruits he shall sell unto ngươi: 16  According to đám đông of years ngươi shalt increase the price thereof, and according to the fewness of years ngươi shalt diminish the price of it: for according to the number of the years of the fruits doth he sell unto ngươi. 17  ngươi sẽ not therefore oppress một another; but ngươi shalt fear thy God: for I là the Lord your God. 18  Wherefore ngươi sẽ do my statutes, Và keep my judgments, Và do them; and ngươi sẽ dwell in the land in safety. 19  Và the land shall yield her fruit, Và ngươi sẽ eat your fill, Và dwell therein in safety. 20  Và if ngươi sẽ say, What shall we eat the seventh year? kìa, we shall not sow, nor gather in our increase: 21  Then I will command my blessing upon you in thứ sáu year, Và it shall bring forth fruit for three years. 22  Và ngươi sẽ sow the eighth year, Và eat yet of old fruit until the ninth year; until her fruits come in ngươi sẽ eat of the old store. 23  The land shall not be sold for ever: for the land is mine; for ye are strangers Và sojourners with me. 4 24  Và in all the land of your possession ngươi sẽ grant a redemption for the land. 25  If thy brother be waxen poor, Và hath sold away some of his possession, Và if any of his kin come to redeem it, then shall he redeem that which his brother sold. 26  Và if the man have none to redeem it, and himself be able to redeem it;5 27  Then let him count the years of the sale thereof, and restore the overplus unto the man to whom he sold it; that he may return unto his possession. 28  But if he be not able to restore it to him, then that which is sold shall remain in tay of him that hath bought it until the year of jubile: Và in the jubile it shall go out, Và he shall return unto his possession. 29  Và if a man sell a dwelling nhà in a walled city, then he may redeem it within a whole year after it is sold; within a full year may he redeem it. 30  Và if it be not redeemed within the space of a full year, then nhà that is in the walled city shall be established for ever to him that bought it throughout his generations: it shall not go out in the jubile. 31  But nhàs of làngs which have no wall round about them shall be counted as the fields of the country: they may be redeemed, Và they shall go out in the jubile. 6 32  Notwithstanding the cities of the Levites, Và nhàs of the cities of their possession, may the Levites redeem at any time. 33  Và if a man purchase of the Levites, then nhà that was sold, Và the city of his possession, shall go out in the year of jubile: for nhàs of the cities of the Levites are their possession among the children của I-sơ-ra-ên. 7 34  But the field of the suburbs of their cities may not be sold; for it is their perpetual possession. 35  Và if thy brother be waxen poor, Và fallen in decay with ngươi; then ngươi shalt relieve him: yea, though he be a stranger, or a sojourner; that he may live with ngươi. 8 9 36  Take ngươi no usury of him, or increase: but fear thy God; that thy brother may live with ngươi. 37  ngươi shalt not give him thy money upon usury, nor lend him thy victuals for increase. 38 I là the Lord your God, which brought you forth out of the land of Ai-Cập, để cho you the land of Canaan, Và to be your God. 39  Và if thy brother that dwelleth by ngươi be waxen poor, Và be sold unto ngươi; ngươi shalt not compel him to serve as a bondservant: 10 40  But as an hired servant, Và as a sojourner, he shall be with ngươi, Và shall serve ngươi unto the year of jubile: 41 Và then shall he depart from ngươi, both he and his children with him, and shall return unto his own gia đình, Và unto the possession of his fathers shall he return. 42 For they are my servants, which I brought forth out of the land of Ai-Cập: they shall not be sold as bondmen. 11 43  ngươi shalt not rule over him with rigour; but shalt fear thy God. 44  Both thy bondmen, Và thy bondmaids, which ngươi shalt have, shall be of the heathen that are round about you; of them shall ye buy bondmen Và bondmaids. 45  Moreover of the children of the strangers that do sojourn among you, of them shall ye buy, Và of their families that are with you, which they begat in your land: Và they shall be your possession. 46  Và ngươi sẽ take them as một cơ nghiệp for your children after you, to inherit them for a possession; they shall be your bondmen for ever: but over your brethren the children của I-sơ-ra-ên, ngươi sẽ not rule một over another with rigour. 12 47  Và if a sojourner or stranger wax rich by ngươi, Và thy brother that dwelleth by him wax poor, Và sell himself unto the stranger or sojourner by ngươi, or to the stock of the stranger's gia đình: 13 48  After that he is sold he may be redeemed again; một of anh em người may redeem him: 49  Either his uncle, or his uncle's son, may redeem him, or any that is nigh of kin unto him of his gia đình may redeem him; or if he be able, he may redeem himself. 50  Và he shall reckon with him that bought him from the year that he was sold to him unto the year of jubile: Và the price of his sale shall be according unto the number of years, according to the time of an hired servant shall it be with him. 51 If there be yet many years behind, according với họ he shall give again the price of his redemption out of the money that he was bought for. 52  Và if there remain but few years unto the year of jubile, then he shall count with him, Và according unto his years shall he give him again the price of his redemption. 53  Và as a yearly hired servant shall he be with him: Và the other shall not rule with rigour over him in thy sight. 54  Và if he be not redeemed in these years, then he shall go out in the year of jubile, both he, and his children with him. 14 55  For unto me the children của I-sơ-ra-ên are các đầy tớ; they are my servants whom I brought forth out of the land of Ai-Cập: I là the Lord your God.
