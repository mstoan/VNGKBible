7
1 Và the men of Kirjath–jearim came, Và fetched up the ark of the Lord, Và brought it into nhà of Abinadab in the hill, Và sanctified Eleazar his son to keep the ark of the Lord. 2 Và xảy đến, while the ark abode in Kirjath–jearim, that the time was long; for it was twenty years: Và all nhà of Israel lamented after the Lord.
3 Và Samuel spake unto all nhà of Israel, saying, If ye do return unto the Lord with all your hearts, then put away the strange gods Và Ashtaroth from among you, and prepare your hearts unto the Lord, Và serve him only: and he will deliver you out of the hand of the Philistines. 4 Then the children of Israel did put away Baalim Và Ashtaroth, Và served the Lord only. 5 Và Samuel said, Gather all Israel to Mizpeh, Và I will pray for you unto the Lord. 6 Và they gathered together to Mizpeh, Và drew water, and poured it out before the Lord, Và fasted on that day, và nói there, We have sinned against the Lord. Và Samuel judged the children of Israel in Mizpeh. 7 Và when the Philistines heard that the children of Israel were gathered together to Mizpeh, the lords of the Philistines went up against Israel. Và when the children of Israel heard it, they were afraid of the Philistines. 8 Và the children of Israel said to Samuel, Cease not to cry unto the Lord our God for us, that he will save us out of the hand of the Philistines.1
9 Và Samuel took a sucking lamb, Và offered it for a burnt offering wholly unto the Lord: Và Samuel cried unto the Lord for Israel; Và the Lord heard him.2 10 Và as Samuel was offering up the burnt offering, the Philistines drew near to battle against Israel: but the Lord thundered with a great thunder on that day upon the Philistines, Và discomfited them; and they were smitten before Israel. 11 Và the men of Israel went out of Mizpeh, Và pursued the Philistines, Và smote them, until they came under Beth–car. 12 Then Samuel took a stone, Và set it between Mizpeh Và Shen, Và called the name of it Eben–ezer, saying, Hitherto hath the Lord helped us.3
13 So the Philistines were subdued, Và they came no more into the coast of Israel: Và the hand of the Lord was against the Philistines all the days of Samuel. 14 Và the cities which the Philistines had taken from Israel were restored to Israel, from Ekron even unto Gath; Và the coasts thereof did Israel deliver out of the hands of the Philistines. Và there was peace between Israel Và the Amorites. 15 Và Samuel judged Israel all the days of his life. 16 Và he went from year to year in circuit to Beth–el, Và Gilgal, Và Mizpeh, Và judged Israel in all those places.4 17 Và his return was to Ramah; for there was his house; Và there he judged Israel; Và there he built an altar unto the Lord.
