12
1  Và Ngài bắt đẩu nói cùng họ bằng những ngụ ngôn. [Có một] người trồng một vườn nho, và người dựng rào chung quanh [nó], và người đào a place for the winefat, và xây một cái tháp, Và let it out to husbandmen, Và went into a far country. 2 Và at biểnson he sent to the husbandmen a servant, that he might receive from the husbandmen of the fruit of the vườn nho. 3 Và they caught him, Và beat him, Và sent him away empty. 4 Và again he sent unto them another servant; Và at him they cast stones, Và wounded him in the head, Và sent him away shamefully handled. 5 Và again he sent another; Và him they killed, Và many others; beating some, Và killing some. 6 Having yet therefore một son, his wellbeloved, he sent him also last unto them, saying, They will reverence my son. 7 Nhưng those husbandmen nói among themselves, This is the heir; come, let us kill him, Và the inheritance shall be ours. 8 Và they took him, Và killed him, Và cast him out of the vườn nho. 9 What shall therefore the lord of the vườn nho do? he will come Và destroy the husbandmen, Và will give the vườn nho unto others. 10 Và have ye not read this scripture; The stone which the builders rejected is become the head of the corner: 11 This was the Lord's doing, Và it is marvellous in our eyes? 12 Và they sought to lay hold on him, Nhưng feared the people: for they knew that he had spoken the parable against them: Và they left him, Và went their way.
13 Và they send unto him certain of the những người Pha-ri-si Và of the Herodians, to catch him in his words. 14 Và when they were come, they say unto him, Master, we know that thou art true, Và carest for không ai: for thou regardest not the person of men, Nhưng teachest the way của ĐCT in truth: Is it lawful to give tribute to Cæsar, hay not? 15 Shall we give, hay shall we not give? Nhưng he, knowing their hypocrisy, nói unto them, Why tempt ye me? bring me a penny, that I may see it. 16 Và they brought it. Và he nói unto them, Whose is this image Và superscription? Và they nói unto him, Cæsar's. 17 Và ĐCGiê-su answering nói unto them, Render to Cæsar the things that are Cæsar's, Và to God the things that are God's. Và they marvelled at him.
18 Then come unto him the Sadducees, which say there is no resurrection; Và they asked him, saying, 19 Master, Môi-se wrote unto us, nếu a man's brother die, Và leave his wife behind him, Và leave no children, that his brother should take his wife, Và raise up seed unto his brother. 20 Now there were seven anh em: Và the first took a wife, Và dying left no seed. 21 Và the second took her, Và died, neither left he any seed: Và the third likewise. 22 Và the seven had her, Và left no seed: last of all Người đờn bà died also. 23 In the resurrection therefore, when they shall rise, whose wife shall she be of them? for the seven had her to wife. 24 Và ĐCGiê-su answering nói unto them, Do ye not therefore err, because ye know not the scriptures, neither the power của ĐCT? 25 For when they shall rise from the dead, they neither marry, nor are given in marriage; Nhưng are as các thiên sứ which are in heaven. 26 Và as touching the dead, that they rise: have ye not read in the book of Môi-se, how in the bush ĐCT spake unto him, saying, I am the God of Áp-ra-ham, Và the God of Isaac, Và the God of Gia-cốp? 27 He is not the God of the dead, Nhưng the God of the living: ye therefore do greatly err.
28 Và một of những thầy chép luật đến, Và having heard them reasoning together, Và perceiving that he had trả lời them well, asked him, Which is the first commandment of all? 29 Và ĐCGiê-su trả lời him, The first of all the commandments is, Hear, O Israel; The Lord our ĐCT is một Lord: 30 Và thou shalt love the Lord thy ĐCT with all thy heart, Và with all thy soul, Và with all thy mind, Và with all thy strength: this is the first commandment. 31 Và the second is like, namely this, Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself. There is none other commandment greater than these. 32 Và the scribe nói unto him, Well, Master, thou hast nói the truth: for there is một ĐCT; Và there is none other Nhưng he: 33 Và to love him with all the heart, Và with all the understanding, Và with all the soul, Và with all the strength, Và to love his neighbour as himself, is more than all whole burnt offerings Và sacrifices. 34 Và when Jesus saw that he trả lời discreetly, he nói unto him, Thou art not far from the kingdom của ĐCT. Và không ai after that durst ask him any question.
35 Và ĐCGiê-su trả lời và nói, while he taught in the temple, How say những thầy chép luật that Christ is the Son of Đa-vít? 36 For Đa-vít himself nói by the Holy Ghost, The Lord nói to my Lord, Sit thou on my right hand, till I make thine enemies thy footstool. 37 Đa-vít therefore himself calleth him Lord; Và whence is he then his son? Và the common people heard him gladly.
38 Và he nói unto them in his doctrine, Hãy coi chừng of những thầy chép luật, which love to go in long clothing, Và love salutations in the marketplaces, 39 Và the chief seats in the các nhà hội, Và the uppermost rooms at feasts: 40 Which devour widows' houses, Và for a pretence make long prayers: these shall receive greater damnation.
41 Và ĐCGiê-su sat over against the treasury, Và beheld how the people cast money into the treasury: Và many that were rich cast in much. 42 Và there came a certain poor widow, Và she threw in two mites, which make a farthing. 43 Và he called unto him his disciples, Và nói unto them, Quả thật I say unto you, That this poor widow hath cast more in, than all they which have cast into the treasury: 44 For all they did cast in of their abundance; Nhưng she of her want did cast in all that she had, even all her living.
