12
1  Và Rehoboam went to Shechem: for all I-sơ-ra-ên were come to Shechem to make him king. 2  Và xảy đến, when Jeroboam the son of Nebat, who was yet in Egypt, heard of it, ( for he was fled from the presence of king Sa-lô-môn, Và Jeroboam dwelt in Egypt;) 3  That they sent Và called him. And Jeroboam Và all the congregation của I-sơ-ra-ên đến, Và spake unto Rehoboam, saying, 4  Thy father made our yoke grievous: now therefore make thou the grievous service of thy father, Và his heavy yoke which he put upon us, lighter, Và we will serve thee. 5  Và he nói unto them, Depart yet for ba days, then come again to me. And the people departed. 6  Và king Rehoboam consulted with the old men, that stood before Sa-lô-môn his father while he yet lived, và nói, How do ye advise that I may answer this people? 7  Và they spake unto him, saying, If thou wilt be a servant unto this people this day, Và wilt serve them, and answer them, and speak good words to them, then they will be thy servants for ever. 8  But he forsook the counsel of the old men, which they had given him, and consulted with the young men that were grown up with him, Và which stood before him: 9  Và he nói unto them, What counsel give ye that we may answer this people, who have spoken to me, saying, Make the yoke which thy father did put upon us lighter? 10  Và the young men that were grown up with him spake unto him, saying, Thus shalt thou speak unto this people that spake unto thee, saying, Thy father made our yoke heavy, but make thou it lighter unto us; thus shalt thou say unto them, My little finger shall be thicker than my father's loins. 11  Và now whereas my father did lade you with a heavy yoke, I will add to your yoke: my father hath chastised you with whips, but I will chastise you with scorpions. 12  So Jeroboam Và all the people đến to Rehoboam the third day, as the king had appointed, saying, Come to me again the third day. 13  Và the king trả lời the people roughly, Và forsook the old men's counsel that they gave him;1 14  Và spake to them after the counsel of the young men, saying, My father made your yoke heavy, Và I will add to your yoke: my father also chastised you with whips, but I will chastise you with scorpions. 15  Wherefore the king hearkened not unto the people; for the cause was from the Lord, that he might perform his saying, which the Lord spake by Ahijah the Shilonite unto Jeroboam the son of Nebat. 16  So when all I-sơ-ra-ên saw that the king hearkened not unto them, the people trả lời the king, saying, What portion have we in Đa-vít? neither have we inheritance in the son of Jesse: to your tents, O I-sơ-ra-ên: now see to thine own house, Đa-vít. So I-sơ-ra-ên departed unto their tents. 17 But as for the children của I-sơ-ra-ên which dwelt in the cities of Judah, Rehoboam reigned over them. 18  Then king Rehoboam sent Adoram, who was over the tribute; Và all I-sơ-ra-ên stoned him with stones, that he died. Therefore king Rehoboam made speed to get him up to his chariot, to flee to Jerusalem. 2 19  So I-sơ-ra-ên rebelled against nhà of Đa-vít unto this day. 3 20  Và xảy đến, when all I-sơ-ra-ên heard that Jeroboam was come again, that they sent Và called him unto the congregation, Và made him king over all I-sơ-ra-ên: there was none that followed nhà of Đa-vít, Nhưng the tribe of Judah only. 21  Và when Rehoboam was come to Jerusalem, he assembled all nhà of Judah, with the tribe of Benjamin, an hundred Và fourscore thousand chosen men, which were warriors, to fight against nhà của I-sơ-ra-ên, to bring the kingdom again to Rehoboam the son of Sa-lô-môn. 22  But the word của ĐCT came unto Shemaiah the man của ĐCT, saying, 23  Speak unto Rehoboam, the son of Sa-lô-môn, king of Judah, Và unto all nhà of Judah Và Benjamin, Và to the remnant of the people, saying, 24  Thus nói the Lord, Ye shall not go up, nor fight against your brethren the children của I-sơ-ra-ên: return every man to his house; for this thing is from me. They hearkened therefore to the word of the Lord, Và returned to depart, according to the word of the Lord. 25  Then Jeroboam built Shechem in mount Ephraim, Và dwelt therein; and went out from thence, and built Penuel. 26  Và Jeroboam nói in his heart, Now shall the kingdom return to nhà of Đa-vít: 27  If this people go up to do sacrifice in nhà of the Lord at Jerusalem, then shall the heart of this people turn again unto their lord, even unto Rehoboam king of Judah, Và they shall kill me, and go again to Rehoboam king of Judah. 28  Whereupon the king took counsel, Và made two calves of gold, và nói unto them, It is too much for you to go up to Jerusalem: kìa thy gods, O I-sơ-ra-ên, which brought thee up out of the land of Egypt. 29  Và he set the one in Beth–el, Và the other put he in Dan. 30  Và this thing became a sin: for the people went to worship before the one, even unto Dan. 31  Và he made an house of high places, Và made priests of the lowest of the people, which were not of the sons of Levi. 32  Và Jeroboam ordained a feast in the eighth month, on the fifteenth day of the month, like unto the feast that is in Judah, Và he offered upon the altar. So did he in Beth–el, sacrificing unto the calves that he had made: Và he placed in Beth–el the priests of the high places which he had made. 4 5 33  So he offered upon the altar which he had made in Beth–el the fifteenth day of the eighth month, even in the month which he had devised of his own heart; Và ordained a feast unto the children của I-sơ-ra-ên: Và he offered upon the altar, Và burnt incense. 6 7
