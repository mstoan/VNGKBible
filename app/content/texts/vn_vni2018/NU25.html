25
1  Và I-sơ-ra-ên abode in Shittim, Và the people bắt đầu to commit whoredom with the daughters of Moab. 2  Và they called the people unto the sacrifices of their gods: Và the people did eat, Và bowed down to their gods. 3  Và I-sơ-ra-ên joined himself unto Baal–peor: Và the anger of the Lord was kindled against I-sơ-ra-ên. 4  Và the Lord nói unto Môi-se, Take all the heads of the people, Và hang them up before the Lord against mặt trời, that the fierce anger of the Lord may be turned away from I-sơ-ra-ên. 5  Và Môi-se nói unto the judges của I-sơ-ra-ên, Slay ye every một his men that were joined unto Baal–peor. 6  And, kìa, one of the children của I-sơ-ra-ên đến Và brought unto anh em người a Midianitish woman in the sight of Môi-se, Và in the sight of all the congregation of the children của I-sơ-ra-ên, who were khóc lóc before the door of the tabernacle of the congregation. 7  Và when Phinehas, the son of Eleazar, the son of A-rôn the priest, nhìn thấy it, he rose up from among the congregation, Và took a javelin in his hand; 8  Và he went after the man của I-sơ-ra-ên into the tent, Và thrust both of them through, the man của I-sơ-ra-ên, Và the woman through her belly. So the plague was stayed from the children của I-sơ-ra-ên. 9  Và those that died in the plague were hai mươi Và four ngàn. 10  Và the Lord spake unto Môi-se, saying, 11  Phinehas, the son of Eleazar, the son of A-rôn the priest, hath turned my wrath away from the children của I-sơ-ra-ên, while he was zealous for my sake among them, that I consumed not the children của I-sơ-ra-ên in my jealousy. 1 12  Wherefore say, kìa, I give unto him my covenant of peace: 13  Và he shall have it, and his seed after him, even the covenant of an đời đời priesthood; vì he was zealous for his God, Và made an atonement for the children của I-sơ-ra-ên. 14  Now the name of the I-sơ-ra-ên that was slain, even that was slain with the Midianitish woman, was Zimri, the son of Salu, a prince of a chief nhà among the Si-mê-ônites. 2 15  Và the name of the Midianitish woman that was slain was Cozbi, con gái of Zur; he was đầu over a people, Và of a chief nhà in Midian. 16  Và the Lord spake unto Môi-se, saying, 17  Vex the Midianites, Và smite them: 18  For they vex you with their wiles, wherewith they have beguiled you in the matter of Peor, Và in the matter of Cozbi, con gái of a prince of Midian, their sister, which was slain in the day of the plague for Peor's sake.
