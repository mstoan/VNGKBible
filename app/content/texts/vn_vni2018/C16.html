6
1 Dare any of you, having a matter against another, go to law before the unjust, Và not before thánh đồ? 2 Do ye not know that thánh đồ shall judge the world? Và nếu the world shall be judged by you, are ye unworthy to judge the smallest matters? 3 Know ye not that we shall judge các thiên sứ? how much more things that pertain to this life? 4 nếu then ye have judgments of things pertaining to this life, set them to judge who are least esteemed in the church. 5 I speak to your shame. Is it so, that there is not a wise man among you? no, not một that shall be able to judge between his anh em? 6 Nhưng brother goeth to law with brother, Và that before the unbelievers. 7 Now therefore there is utterly a fault among you, because ye go to law one with another. Why do ye not rather take wrong? why do ye not rather suffer yourselves to be defrauded? 8 Nay, ye do wrong, Và defraud, Và that your anh em. 9 Know ye not that the unrighteous shall not inherit the kingdom của ĐCT? Be not deceived: neither fornicators, nor idolaters, nor adulterers, nor effeminate, nor abusers of themselves with mankind, 10 Nor thieves, nor covetous, nor drunkards, nor revilers, nor extortioners, shall inherit the kingdom của ĐCT. 11 Và such were some of you: Nhưng ye are washed, Nhưng ye are sanctified, Nhưng ye are justified in the name của Chúa ĐCGiê-su, Và by the Spirit of our ĐCT. 12 All things are lawful unto me, Nhưng all things are not expedient: all things are lawful for me, Nhưng I will not be brought under the power of any. 13 Meats for the belly, Và the belly for meats: Nhưng ĐCT shall destroy both it Và them. Now the body is not for fornication, Nhưng for the Lord; Và the Lord for the body. 14 Và ĐCT hath both raised up the Lord, Và will also raise up us by his own power. 15 Know ye not that your bodies are the members của Đấng Christ? shall I then take the members của Đấng Christ, Và make them the members of an harlot? ĐCT forbid. 16 What? know ye not that he which is joined to an harlot is một body? for two, nói he, shall be một flesh. 17 Nhưng he that is joined unto the Lord is một spirit. 18 Flee fornication. Every sin that a man doeth is without the body; Nhưng he that committeth fornication sinneth against his own body. 19 What? know ye not that your body is the temple of the Holy Ghost which is in you, which ye have of ĐCT, Và ye are not your own? 20 For ye are bought with a price: therefore glorify ĐCT in your body, Và in your spirit, which are God's.
