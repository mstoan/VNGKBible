34
1  Và Môi-se went up from the plains of Moab unto the mountain of Nebo, to the top of Pisgah, that is over against Jericho. Và the Lord shewed him all the land of Gilead, unto Dan,1 2  Và all Naphtali, Và the land of Ephraim, Và Manasseh, Và all the land of Judah, unto the utmost sea, 3  Và the south, Và the plain of the valley of Jericho, the city of palm trees, unto Zoar. 4  Và the Lord nói unto him, This is the land which I sware unto Áp-ra-ham, unto Isaac, Và unto Gia-cốp, saying, I will give it unto thy seed: I have caused thee to see it with thine eyes, but thou shalt not go over thither. 5  So Môi-se the servant of the Lord died there in the land of Moab, according to the word of the Lord. 6  Và he buried him in a valley in the land of Moab, over against Beth–peor: but không ai knoweth of his sepulchre unto this day. 7  Và Môi-se was an hundred Và twenty years old when he died: his eye was not dim, nor his natural force abated. 2 3 8  Và the children của I-sơ-ra-ên wept for Môi-se in the plains of Moab thirty days: so the days of weeping Và mourning for Môi-se were ended. 9  Và Giô-suê the son of Nun was full of the spirit of wisdom; for Môi-se had laid his hands upon him: and the children của I-sơ-ra-ên hearkened unto him, and did as the Lord commanded Môi-se. 10  Và there arose not a prophet since in I-sơ-ra-ên like unto Môi-se, whom the Lord knew face to face, 11  In all the signs Và the wonders, which the Lord sent him to do in the land of Egypt to Pha-ra-ôn, Và to all his servants, Và to all his land, 12  Và in all that mighty hand, Và in all the great terror which Môi-se shewed in the sight of all I-sơ-ra-ên.
