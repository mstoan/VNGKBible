15
1 Và tức thì in the morning các thầy tế lễ held a consultation with the elders Và scribes Và the whole council, Và bound ĐCGiê-su, Và carried him away, Và delivered him to Pilate. 2 Và Phi-lát asked him, Art thou the King of the Jews? Và he answering nói unto him, Thou sayest it. 3 Và các thầy tế lễ accused him of many things: but he trả lời nothing. 4 Và Phi-lát asked him again, saying, Answerest thou nothing? kìa how many things they witness against thee. 5 Nhưng ĐCGiê-su yet trả lời nothing; đến đổi Phi-lát marvelled. 6 Now at that feast he released unto them một prisoner, whomsoever they desired. 7 Và there was one named Barabbas, which lay bound with them that had made insurrection with him, who had committed murder in the insurrection. 8 Và đám đông crying aloud began to desire him to do as he had ever done unto them. 9 Nhưng Phi-lát trả lời them, saying, Will ye that I release unto you the King of the Jews? 10 For he knew that các thầy tế lễ had delivered him for envy. 11 Nhưng các thầy tế lễ moved the people, that he should rather release Barabbas unto them. 12 Và Phi-lát trả lời và nói again unto them, What will ye then that I shall do unto him whom ye call the King of the Jews? 13 Và they cried out again, Crucify him. 14 Then Phi-lát nói unto them, Why, what evil hath he done? Và they cried out the more exceedingly, Crucify him.
15 Và so Phi-lát, willing to content the people, released Barabbas unto them, Và delivered ĐCGiê-su, when he had scourged him, to be crucified. 16 Và quân lính led him away into the hall, called Prætorium; Và they call together the whole band. 17 Và they clothed him with purple, Và platted a crown of thorns, Và put it about his head, 18 Và began to salute him, Hail, King of the Jews! 19 Và they smote him on the head with a reed, Và did spit upon him, Và bowing their knees worshipped him. 20 Và when they had mocked him, they took off the purple from him, Và put his own clothes on him, Và led him out to crucify him. 21 Và they compel một Si-môn a Cyrenian, who passed by, coming out of the country, the father of Alexander Và Rufus, to bear his cross. 22 Và they bring him unto the place Golgotha, which is, being interpreted, The place of a skull. 23 Và they gave him to drink wine mingled with myrrh: Nhưng he received it not. 24 Và when they had crucified him, they parted his garments, casting lots upon them, what every man should take. 25 Và it was the third hour, Và they crucified him. 26 Và the superscription of his accusation was written over, THE KING OF THE JEWS. 27 Và with him they crucify two thieves; the one on his right hand, Và the other on his left. 28 Và the scripture was fulfilled, which nói, Và he was numbered with the transgressors. 29 Và they that passed by railed on him, wagging their heads, Và saying, Ah, thou that destroyest the temple, Và buildest it in three days, 30 Save thyself, Và come down from thập-tự-giá. 31 Likewise also các thầy tế lễ mocking nói among themselves with những thầy chép luật, He saved others; himself he cannot save. 32 Let Christ the King of Israel descend now from thập-tự-giá, that we may see Và believe. Và they that were crucified with him reviled him. 33 Và when the sixth hour was come, there was darkness over the whole land until the ninth hour. 34 Và at the ninth hour ĐCGiê-su cried with a loud voice, saying, Eloi, Eloi, lama sabachthani? which is, being interpreted, My ĐCT, my ĐCT, why hast thou forsaken me? 35 Và some of them that stood by, when they heard it, nói, kìa, he calleth Ê-li. 36 Và một ran Và filled a spunge full of vinegar, Và put it on a reed, Và gave him to drink, saying, Let alone; let us see whether Ê-li will come to take him down. 37 Và ĐCGiê-su cried with a loud voice, Và gave up the ghost. 38 Và the veil of the temple was rent in twain from the top to the bottom.
39 Và when the centurion, which stood over against him, saw that he so cried out, Và gave up the ghost, he nói, Truly this man was the Son của ĐCT. 40 There were also women looking on afar off: among whom was Ma-ri Mác-đơ-len, Và Ma-ri the mother of Gia-cơ the less Và of Joses, Và Salome; 41 (Who also, when he was in Ga-li-lê, followed him, Và ministered unto him;) Và many other women which came up with him unto Giê-ru-sa-lem.
42 Và now when the even was come, because it was the preparation, that is, the day before the sabbath, 43 Giô-sép of Arimathaea, an honourable counsellor, which also waited for the kingdom của ĐCT, đến, Và went in boldly unto Phi-lát, Và craved the body của ĐCGiê-su. 44 Và Phi-lát marvelled nếu he were already dead: Và calling unto him the centurion, he asked him whether he had been any while dead. 45 Và when he knew it of the centurion, he gave the body to Giô-sép. 46 Và he bought fine linen, Và took him down, Và wrapped him in the linen, Và laid him in a sepulchre which was hewn out of a rock, Và rolled a stone unto the door of phần mộ. 47 Và Ma-ri Mác-đơ-len Và Ma-ri the mother of Joses beheld where he was laid.
