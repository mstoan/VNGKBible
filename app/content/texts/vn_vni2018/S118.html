18
1  Và xảy đến, when he had made an end of speaking unto Sau-lơ, that the soul của Giô-nathan was knit with the soul of Đa-vít, Và Jonathan loved him as his own soul. 2  Và Sau-lơ took him that day, Và would let him go no more home to his father's nhà. 3  Then Jonathan Và Đa-vít made a covenant, because he loved him as his own soul. 4  Và Jonathan stripped himself of the robe that was upon him, and gave it to Đa-vít, Và his garments, even to his thanh gươm, Và to his bow, Và to his girdle. 5  Và Đa-vít went out whithersoever Sau-lơ sent him, Và behaved himself wisely: Và Sau-lơ set him over the men of war, Và he was accepted in the sight of all the people, Và also in the sight of Sau-lơ's các đầy tớ. 1 6  Và xảy đến as they came, when Đa-vít was returned from the slaughter of the Philistine, that the women came out of all cities của I-sơ-ra-ên, singing Và dancing, to meet king Sau-lơ, with tabrets, with joy, Và with instruments of musick. 2 3 7  Và the women trả lời lẫn nhau as they played, và nói, Sau-lơ hath slain his thousands, Và Đa-vít his ten thousands. 8  Và Sau-lơ was very wroth, Và the saying displeased him; and he nói, They have ascribed unto Đa-vít ten thousands, Và to me they have ascribed but thousands: and what can he have more but the kingdom? 4 9  Và Sau-lơ eyed Đa-vít from that day Và forward. 10  Và xảy đến on the morrow, that the evil spirit from God đến upon Sau-lơ, Và he prophesied in the midst của nhà: Và Đa-vít played with his hand, as at other times: and there was a javelin in Sau-lơ's tay. 11  Và Sau-lơ cast the javelin; for he nói, Ta sẽ đánh Đa-vít even to the wall with it. Và Đa-vít avoided out of his presence twice. 12  Và Sau-lơ was afraid of Đa-vít, because the Lord was with him, and was departed from Sau-lơ. 13  Therefore Sau-lơ removed him from him, and made him his captain over a thousand; Và he went out Và came in before the people. 14  Và Đa-vít behaved himself wisely in all his ways; Và the Lord was with him. 5 15  Wherefore when Sau-lơ nhìn thấy that he behaved himself very wisely, he was afraid of him. 16  But all I-sơ-ra-ên Và Giu-đa loved Đa-vít, because he went out Và came in before them. 17  Và Sau-lơ nói to Đa-vít, kìa my elder con gái Merab, her will I give thee to wife: only be thou valiant for me, and fight the Lord's battles. For Sau-lơ nói, Let not mine hand be upon him, but let tay of the Philistines be upon him. 6 18  Và Đa-vít nói unto Sau-lơ, Who am I? and what is my sự sống, or my father's gia đình in I-sơ-ra-ên, that I should be son in law to the king? 19  But xảy đến at the time when Merab Sau-lơ's con gái should have been given to Đa-vít, that she was given unto Adriel the Meholathite to wife. 20  Và Michal Sau-lơ's con gái loved Đa-vít: Và they told Sau-lơ, Và the thing pleased him. 7 21  Và Sau-lơ nói, I will give him her, that she may be a snare to him, and that tay of the Philistines may be against him. Wherefore Sau-lơ nói to Đa-vít, Thou shalt this day be my son in law in the one of the twain. 22  Và Sau-lơ truyền lệnh his servants, saying, Commune with Đa-vít secretly, Và say, kìa, the king hath delight in thee, and all his servants love thee: now therefore be the king's son in law. 23  Và Sau-lơ's các đầy tớ spake those words in the ears of Đa-vít. Và Đa-vít nói, Seemeth it to you a light thing to be a king's son in law, seeing that I am a poor man, Và lightly esteemed? 24  Và the servants of Sau-lơ told him, saying, On this manner spake Đa-vít. 8 25  Và Sau-lơ nói, Thus shall ye say to Đa-vít, The king desireth not any dowry, but an hundred foreskins of the Philistines, to be avenged of the king's enemies. But Sau-lơ thought to make Đa-vít fall by tay of the Philistines. 26  Và when his servants told Đa-vít these words, it pleased Đa-vít well to be the king's son in law: Và the days were not expired. 9 27  Wherefore Đa-vít arose Và went, he and his men, Và slew of the Philistines hai trăm men; Và Đa-vít brought their foreskins, Và they gave them in full tale to the king, that he might be the king's son in law. Và Sau-lơ gave him Michal his daughter to wife. 28  Và Sau-lơ nhìn thấy Và knew that the Lord was with Đa-vít, and that Michal Sau-lơ's con gái loved him. 29  Và Sau-lơ was yet the more afraid of Đa-vít; Và Sau-lơ became Đa-vít's kẻ thù continually. 30  Then the princes of the Philistines went forth: Và xảy đến, after they went forth, that Đa-vít behaved himself more wisely than all the servants of Sau-lơ; so that his name was much set by. 10
