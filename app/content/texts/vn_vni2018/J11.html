1  That which was from the beginning, which we have heard, which we have seen with our mắt, which we have looked upon, Và our hands have handled, of the Word of sự sống; 2 (For the sự sống was manifested, Và we have seen it, Và bear witness, Và shew unto you that eternal sự sống, which was with the Father, Và was manifested unto us;) 3  That which we have seen Và heard declare we unto you, that ye also may have fellowship with us: Và truly our fellowship is with the Father, Và with his Son ĐCGiê-su Christ. 4  Và these things write we unto you, that your joy may be full. 5  This then is the message which we have heard of him, Và declare unto you, rằng ĐCT is light, Và in him is no tối tăm at all. 6  nếu we say rằng we have fellowship with him, Và walk in tối tăm, we lie, Và do not the truth: 7  Nhưng nếu we walk in the light, as he is in the light, we have fellowship một with another, Và the blood của ĐCGiê-su Christ his Son cleanseth us from all sin. 8  nếu we say rằng we have no sin, we deceive ourselves, Và the truth is not in us. 9  nếu we confess our tội lỗi, he is faithful Và just to forgive us our tội lỗi, Và to cleanse us from all unrighteousness. 10  nếu we say rằng we have not sinned, we make him a liar, Và his word is not in us.
