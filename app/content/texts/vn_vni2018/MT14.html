14
1 At that time Hê-rốt the tetrarch heard of the fame của ĐCGiê-su, 2 Và nói unto his servants, This is Giăng the Baptist; he is risen from the dead; Và therefore mighty works do shew forth themselves in him.
3 For Hê-rốt had laid hold on Giăng, Và bound him, Và put him in prison for Herodias' sake, his brother Phi-líp's vợ. 4 For Giăng nói unto him, It is not lawful for thee to have her. 5 Và when he would have put him to death, he feared đám đông, because they counted him as a prophet. 6 But when Herod's birthday was kept, the daughter of Herodias danced before them, Và pleased Hê-rốt. 7 Whereupon he promised with an oath to give her whatsoever she would ask. 8 Và she, being before instructed of her mẹ, nói, Give me here Giăng Baptist's head in a charger. 9 Và the king was sorry: nevertheless for the oath's sake, Và them which sat with him at meat, he commanded it to be given her. 10 Và he sent, Và beheaded Giăng in the prison. 11 Và his head was brought in a charger, Và given to the damsel: Và she brought it to her mẹ. 12 Và his disciples đến, Và took up the body, Và buried it, Và went Và told ĐCGiê-su.
13 When ĐCGiê-su heard of it, he departed thence by ship into a desert place apart: Và when the people had heard thereof, they followed him on foot out of the cities. 14 Và ĐCGiê-su went forth, Và saw a great multitude, Và was moved with compassion toward them, Và he healed their sick.
15 Và when it was evening, his disciples đến to him, Nói rằng, This is a desert place, Và the time is bây giờ past; send đám đông away, that they may go into the villages, Và buy themselves victuals. 16 Nhưng ĐCGiê-su nói unto them, They need not depart; give ye them to eat. 17 Và they say unto him, We have here Nhưng five loaves, Và two fishes. 18 He nói, Bring them hither to me. 19 Và he commanded đám đông to sit down on the grass, Và took the five loaves, Và the two fishes, Và looking up to heaven, he blessed, Và brake, Và gave the loaves to his disciples, Và các môn đệ to đám đông. 20 Và they did all eat, Và were filled: Và they took up of the fragments that remained mười hai baskets full. 21 Và they that had eaten were about five thousand men, beside women Và children.
22 Và tức thì ĐCGiê-su constrained his disciples to get into a ship, Và to go before him unto the other side, while he sent the multitudes away. 23 Và when he had sent the multitudes away, he went up into a mountain apart to pray: Và when the evening was come, he was there alone. 24 Nhưng the ship was bây giờ in the midst of biển, tossed with waves: for the wind was contrary. 25 Và in the fourth watch of the night ĐCGiê-su went unto them, walking on biển. 26 Và when các môn đệ saw him walking on biển, they were troubled, Nói rằng, It is a spirit; Và they cried out for fear. 27 Nhưng tức thì ĐCGiê-su spake unto them, Nói rằng, Be of good cheer; it is I; be not afraid. 28 Và Phi-e-rơ trả lời him và nói, Lord, nếu it be thou, bid me come unto thee on the water. 29 Và he nói, Come. Và when Phi-e-rơ was come down out of the ship, he walked on the water, to go to ĐCGiê-su. 30 Nhưng when he saw the wind boisterous, he was afraid; Và beginning to sink, he cried, Nói rằng, Lord, save me. 31 Và tức thì ĐCGiê-su stretched forth his hand, Và caught him, Và nói unto him, O thou of little faith, wherefore didst thou doubt? 32 Và when they were come into the ship, the wind ceased. 33 Then they that were in the ship đến Và worshipped him, Nói rằng, Of a truth thou art the Son của ĐCT.
34 Và when they were gone over, they came into the land of Gennesaret. 35 Và when the men of that place had knowledge of him, they sent out into all that country round about, Và brought unto him all that were diseased; 36 Và besought him that they might only touch the hem of his garment: Và as many as touched được dựng nên perfectly whole.
