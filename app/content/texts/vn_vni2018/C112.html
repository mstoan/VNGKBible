12
1  bây giờ concerning spiritual gifts, anh em, I would không have you ignorant. 2  Ye know rằng ye were Gentiles, carried away unto these dumb idols, even as ye were led. 3  Wherefore I give you to understand, rằng không ai speaking by [Thánh] Linh của ĐCT calleth ĐCGiê-su accursed: Và that không ai can say that Jesus is the Lord, Nhưng by the Holy Ghost. 4  bây giờ there are diversities of gifts, Nhưng the same Spirit. 5  Và there are differences of administrations, Nhưng the same Lord. 6  Và there are diversities of operations, Nhưng it is the same ĐCT which worketh all in all. 7  Nhưng the manifestation of the Spirit is given to every man to profit withal. 8  For to one is given by [Thánh] Linh the word of wisdom; to another the word of knowledge by the same Spirit; 9  To another đức tin by the same Spirit; to another the gifts of healing by the same Spirit; 10  To another the working of miracles; to another prophecy; to another discerning of spirits; to another divers kinds of tongues; to another the interpretation of tongues: 11  Nhưng all these worketh thatmột Và the selfsame Spirit, dividing to every man severally as he will. 12  For as the body is một, Và hath many members, Và all the members of that one body, being many, are một body: so also is Christ. 13  For by một Spirit are chúng ta all baptized into một body, hoặc we be Jews or Gentiles, hoặc we be bond or free; Và have been all made to drink into một Spirit. 14  For the body is không một member, Nhưng many. 15  nếu the foot shall say, Because I am không the hand, I am không of the body; is it therefore không of the body? 16  Và nếu the ear shall say, Because I am không the eye, I am không of the body; is it therefore không of the body? 17  nếu the whole body were an eye, where were the hearing? nếu the whole were hearing, where were the smelling? 18  Nhưng bây giờ hath ĐCT set the members every một of them in the body, as it hath pleased him. 19  Và nếu they were all một member, where were the body? 20  Nhưng bây giờ are they many members, yet Nhưng một body. 21  Và the eye cannot say unto the hand, I have no need of thee: nor again the head to the feet, I have no need of you. 22  Nay, much more those members of the body, which seem to be more feeble, are necessary: 23  Và those members of the body, which we think to be less honourable, upon these we bestow more abundant honour; Và our uncomely parts have more abundant comeliness. 24  For our comely parts have no need: Nhưng ĐCT hath tempered the body together, having given more abundant honour to that part which lacked: 25  That there should be no schism in the body; Nhưng that the members should have the same care một for another. 26  Và hoặc một member suffer, all the members suffer with it; or một member be honoured, all the members rejoice with it. 27  bây giờ ye are the body của Đấng Christ, Và members in particular. 28  Và ĐCT hath set some in hội thánh, first sự đồ, secondarily prophets, thirdly teachers, after that miracles, then gifts of healings, helps, governments, diversities of tongues. 29  Are all sự đồ? are all prophets? are all teachers? are all workers of miracles? 30  Have all the gifts of healing? do all speak with tongues? do all interpret? 31  Nhưng covet earnestly the best gifts: Và yet shew I unto you a more excellent way.
