30
1  Và xảy đến, when Đa-vít Và his men were come to Ziklag on the third day, that the Amalekites had invaded the south, Và Ziklag, Và smitten Ziklag, Và burned it with fire; 2  Và had taken the women captives, that were therein: they slew not any, either great or small, but carried them away, Và went on their way. 3  So Đa-vít Và his men đến to the city, and, kìa, it was burned with fire; Và their wives, Và their sons, Và their daughters, were taken captives. 4  Then Đa-vít Và the people that were with him lifted up their voice Và wept, until they had no more power to weep. 5  Và Đa-vít's hai wives were taken captives, Ahinoam the Jezreelitess, Và Abigail the wife of Nabal the Carmelite. 6  Và Đa-vít was greatly distressed; for the people spake of stoning him, because the soul of all the people was grieved, every man for his sons Và for his daughters: but Đa-vít encouraged himself in the Lord his God. 1 7  Và Đa-vít nói to Abiathar the priest, Ahimelech's son, I pray thee, bring me hither the ephod. Và Abiathar brought thither the ephod to Đa-vít. 8  Và Đa-vít enquired at the Lord, saying, Shall I pursue after this troop? shall I overtake them? And he trả lời him, Pursue: for thou shalt surely overtake them, Và without fail recover all. 9  So Đa-vít went, he and the six trăm men that were with him, and came to the brook Besor, where those that were left behind stayed. 10  But Đa-vít pursued, he and four trăm men: for two hundred abode behind, which were so faint that they could not go over the brook Besor. 11  Và they found an Ai-Cậpian in the field, Và brought him to Đa-vít, Và gave him bread, Và he did eat; Và they made him drink nước; 12  Và they gave him a piece of a cake of figs, and two clusters of raisins: Và when he had eaten, his spirit came again to him: for he had eaten no bread, nor drunk any nước, ba days Và three nights. 13  Và Đa-vít nói unto him, To whom belongest thou? and whence art thou? And he nói, I am a young man of Ai-Cập, servant to an Amalekite; Và my master left me, because three days agone I fell sick. 14  We made an invasion upon the south of the Cherethites, and upon the coast which belongeth to Giu-đa, Và upon the south of Caleb; Và we burned Ziklag with fire. 15  Và Đa-vít nói to him, Canst thou bring me down to this company? Và he nói, Swear unto me by God, that thou wilt neither kill me, nor deliver me into the hands of my master, Và I will bring thee down to this company. 16  Và when he had brought him down, kìa, they were spread abroad upon all đất, eating Và drinking, Và dancing, because of all the great spoil that they had taken out of the land of the Philistines, Và out of the land of Giu-đa. 17  Và Đa-vít smote them from the twilight even unto buổi chiều of the next day: Và there escaped not a man of them, save four trăm young men, which rode upon lạc đàs, Và fled. 2 18  Và Đa-vít recovered all that the Amalekites had carried away: Và Đa-vít rescued his two wives. 19  Và there was nothing lacking to them, neither small nor great, neither sons nor daughters, neither spoil, nor any thing that they had taken to them: Đa-vít recovered all. 20  Và Đa-vít took all the flocks Và the herds, which they drave before those other cattle, và nói, This is Đa-vít's spoil. 21  Và Đa-vít đến to the two hundred men, which were so faint that they could not follow Đa-vít, whom they had made also to abide at the brook Besor: Và they went forth to meet Đa-vít, Và to meet the people that were with him: and when Đa-vít came near to the people, he saluted them. 3 22  Then trả lời all the wicked men and men of Belial, of those that went with Đa-vít, và nói, Because they went not with us, we will not give them ought of the spoil that we have recovered, save to every man his wife Và his children, that they may lead them away, Và depart. 4 23  Then nói Đa-vít, Ye shall not do so, my brethren, with that which the Lord hath given us, who hath preserved us, and delivered the company that came against us into our hand. 24  For who will hearken unto you in this matter? but as his part is that goeth down to the battle, so shall his part be that tarrieth by the stuff: they shall part alike. 25 Và it was so from that day forward, that he made it a statute Và an ordinance for I-sơ-ra-ên unto this day. 5 26  Và when Đa-vít đến to Ziklag, he sent of the spoil unto the elders of Giu-đa, even to his friends, saying, kìa a present for you of the spoil of the enemies of the Lord; 6 27 To them which were in Beth–el, and to them which were in south Ramoth, and to them which were in Jattir, 28 Và to them which were in Aroer, and to them which were in Siphmoth, and to them which were in Eshtemoa, 29 Và to them which were in Rachal, and to them which were in the cities of the Jerahmeelites, and to them which were in the cities of the Kenites, 30 Và to them which were in Hormah, and to them which were in Chor–ashan, and to them which were in Athach, 31 Và to them which were in Hebron, Và to all the places where Đa-vít himself and his men were wont to haunt.
