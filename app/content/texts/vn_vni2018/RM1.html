Romans
1
1 Phao-lô, a servant của ĐCGiê-su Christ, called to be an apostle, separated unto phúc-âm của ĐCT, 2 (Which he had promised afore by his prophets in the holy scriptures,) 3 Concerning his Son Jesus Christ our Lord, which was made of the seed of Đa-vít according to the flesh; 4 Và declared to be the Son của ĐCT with power, according to the spirit of holiness, by the resurrection from the dead: 5 By whom we have received grace Và apostleship, for obedience to the faith among all nations, for his name: 6 Among whom are ye also the called của ĐCGiê-su Christ: 7  To all that be in Rome, beloved của ĐCT, called to be saints: Grace to you Và peace from ĐCT our Father, Và the Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ. 8 First, I thank my ĐCT through ĐCGiê-su Christ for you all, that your faith is spoken of throughout the whole world. 9 For ĐCT is my witness, whom I serve with my spirit in phúc-âm of his Son, that without ceasing I make mention of you always in my prayers; 10 Making request, if by any means now at length I might have a prosperous journey by the will của ĐCT to come unto you. 11 For I long to see you, that I may impart unto you some spiritual gift, to the end ye may be established; 12 That is, that I may be comforted together with you by the mutual faith both of you Và me. 13 Now I would not have you ignorant, anh em, that oftentimes I purposed to come unto you, (but was let hitherto,) that I might have some fruit among you also, even as among other Gentiles. 14 I am debtor both to the Greeks, Và to the Barbarians; both to the wise, Và to the unwise. 15 So, as much as in me is, I am ready to preach phúc-âm to you that are at Rome also. 16 Vì tôi không hổ thẹn vì phúc-âm của Đấng Christ: for it is the power của ĐCT unto salvation to every one that believeth; to the Jew first, Và also to the Greek. 17 For therein is the righteousness của ĐCT revealed from faith to faith: as it is written, The just shall live by faith. 18 For the wrath của ĐCT is revealed from heaven against all ungodliness Và unrighteousness of men, who hold the truth in unrighteousness; 19 Because that which may be known của ĐCT is manifest in them; for ĐCT hath shewed it unto them. 20 For the invisible things of him from the creation of the world are clearly seen, being understood by the things that are made, even his eternal power Và Godhead; đến đổi they are without excuse: 21 Because that, when they knew ĐCT, they glorified him not as ĐCT, neither were thankful; but became vain in their imaginations, Và their foolish heart was darkened. 22 Professing themselves to be wise, they became fools, 23 Và changed the glory of the uncorruptible ĐCT into an image made like to corruptible man, Và to birds, Và fourfooted beasts, Và creeping things. 24 Wherefore ĐCT also gave them up to uncleanness through the lusts of their own hearts, to dishonour their own bodies between themselves: 25 Who changed the truth của ĐCT into a lie, Và worshipped Và served the creature more than the Creator, who is blessed for ever. Amen. 26 For this cause ĐCT gave them up unto vile affections: for even their women did change the natural use into that which is against nature: 27 Và likewise also the men, leaving the natural use of the woman, burned in their lust một toward another; men with men working that which is unseemly, Và receiving in themselves that recompence of their error which was meet. 28 Và even as they did not like to retain ĐCT in their knowledge, ĐCT gave them over to a reprobate mind, to do those things which are not convenient; 29 Being filled with all unrighteousness, fornication, wickedness, covetousness, maliciousness; full of envy, murder, debate, deceit, malignity; whisperers, 30 Backbiters, haters của ĐCT, despiteful, proud, boasters, inventors of evil things, disobedient to parents, 31 Without understanding, covenantbreakers, without natural affection, implacable, unmerciful: 32 Who knowing the judgment của ĐCT, that they which commit such things are worthy of death, not only do the same, but have pleasure in them that do them.
