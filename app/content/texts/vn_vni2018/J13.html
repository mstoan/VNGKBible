3
1  kìa, what manner of love Cha hath bestowed upon us, that we should be called the sons của ĐCT: therefore thế gian knoweth us not, because it knew him not. 2  Beloved, bây giờ are we the sons của ĐCT, Và it doth not yet appear what we shall be: Nhưng we know rằng, when he shall appear, we shall be like him; for we shall see him as he is. 3  Và every man that hath this hy vọng in him purifieth himself, even as he is pure. 4  Whosoever committeth sin transgresseth also luật pháp: for sin is the transgression of the law. 5  Và ye know rằng he was manifested to take away our tội lỗi; Và in him is no sin. 6  Whosoever abideth in him sinneth not: whosoever sinneth hath not seen him, neither known him. 7  Little children, let không ai deceive you: he that doeth righteousness is công chính, even as he is công chính. 8  He that committeth sin is of the devil; for the devil sinneth from ban đầu. For this purpose the Son của ĐCT was manifested, that he might destroy những công việc of the devil. 9  Whosoever is born of ĐCT doth not commit sin; for his seed remaineth in him: Và he cannot sin, because he is born of ĐCT. 10  In this the children của ĐCT are manifest, Và the children of the devil: whosoever doeth not righteousness is not of ĐCT, neither he that loveth not his brother. 11  For this is the message that ye heard from ban đầu, that we should love one another. 12  Not as Cain, who was of that wicked one, Và slew his brother. Và wherefore slew he him? Because his own works were evil, Và his brother's công chính. 13  Marvel not, my anh em, nếu thế gian hate you. 14  chúng ta know rằng we have passed from death unto sự sống, because we love the brethren. He that loveth not his brother abideth in death. 15  Whosoever hateth his brother is a murderer: Và ye know rằng no murderer hath eternal sự sống abiding in him. 16  Hereby perceive we tình yêu của ĐCT, because he laid down his sự sống for us: Và chúng ta ought to lay down our lives for the brethren. 17  Nhưng whoso hath this world's good, Và seeth his brother have need, Và shutteth up his bowels of compassion from him, how dwelleth tình yêu của ĐCT in him? 18  My little children, let us not love in word, neither in tongue; Nhưng in deed Và in truth. 19  Và hereby we know rằng we are of the truth, Và shall assure our hearts before him. 20  For nếu our heart condemn us, ĐCT is greater than our heart, Và knoweth all things. 21  Beloved, nếu our heart condemn us not, then have we confidence toward ĐCT. 22  Và whatsoever we ask, we receive of him, because we keep his commandments, Và do those things that are pleasing in his sight. 23  Và this is his mạng lệnh, That we should believe on the name of his Son ĐCGiê-su Christ, Và love one another, as he gave us mạng lệnh. 24  Và he that keepeth his commandments dwelleth in him, Và he in him. Và hereby we know rằng he abideth in us, by [Thánh] Linh which he hath given us.
