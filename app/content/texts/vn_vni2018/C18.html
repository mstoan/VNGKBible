8
1 bây giờ as touching things offered unto idols, we know that we all have knowledge. Knowledge puffeth up, Nhưng charity edifieth. 2 Và nếu any man think that he knoweth any thing, he knoweth nothing yet as he ought to know. 3 Nhưng nếu any man love ĐCT, the same is known of him. 4 As concerning therefore the eating of those things that are offered in sacrifice unto idols, we know that an idol is nothing in thế gian, Và that there is none other ĐCT Nhưng một. 5 For though there be that are called gods, whether in heaven or in đất, (as there be gods many, Và lords many,) 6 Nhưng to us there is but một ĐCT, the Father, of whom are all things, Và chúng ta in him; Và một Lord ĐCGiê-su Christ, by whom are all things, Và chúng ta by him. 7 Howbeit there is not in every man that knowledge: for some with conscience of the idol unto this hour eat it as a thing offered unto an idol; Và their conscience being weak is defiled. 8 Nhưng meat commendeth us not to God: for neither, nếu we eat, are we the better; neither, nếu we eat not, are we the worse. 9 Nhưng take heed lest by any means this liberty of yours become a stumblingblock to them that are weak. 10 For nếu any man see thee which hast knowledge sit at meat in the idol's temple, shall not the conscience of him which is weak be emboldened to eat those things which are offered to idols; 11 Và through thy knowledge shall the weak brother perish, for whom Christ died? 12 Nhưng when ye sin so against the brethren, Và wound their weak conscience, ye sin against Christ. 13 Wherefore, nếu meat make my brother to offend, I will eat no flesh while the world standeth, lest I make my brother to offend.
