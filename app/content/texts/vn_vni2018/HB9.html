9
1  Then Quả thật the first covenant had also ordinances of divine service, Và a worldly sanctuary. 2  For there was a tabernacle made; the first, where in was the candlestick, Và the table, Và the shewbread; which is called the sanctuary. 3  Và after the second veil, the tabernacle which is called the Holiest of all; 4  Which had the golden censer, Và the ark of the covenant overlaid round about with gold, where in was the golden pot that had manna, Và A-rôn's rod that budded, Và the tables of the covenant; 5  Và over it the cherubims of glory shadowing the mercyseat; of which we can không bây giờ speak particularly. 6  Now when these things were thus ordained, the priests went always into the first tabernacle, accomplishing the service của ĐCT. 7  Nhưng into the second went thầy thượng phẩm alone once every year, không without huyết, which he offered for himself, Và for the errors của dân sự: 8  The Holy Ghost this signifying, thatđường into the holiest of all was not yet made manifest, while as the first tabernacle was yet standing: 9  Which was a figure for the time then present, in which were offered both gifts Và sacrifices, that could không make him that did the service trọn vẹn, as pertaining to the conscience; 10  Which stood only in meats Và drinks, Và divers washings, Và carnal ordinances, imposed on them until the time of reformation. 11  Nhưng Christ being come an high priest of good things to come, by a lớn hơn Và more perfect tabernacle, không made with hands, that is to say, không of this building; 12  Neither by the blood of goats Và calves, Nhưng by his own huyết he entered in once into the holy place, having obtained đời đời redemption for us. 13  For nếu the blood of bulls Và of goats, Và the ashes of an heifer sprinkling the unclean, sanctifieth to the purifying of the flesh: 14  How much more shall the blood của Đấng Christ, who through the đời đời Spirit offered himself without spot to God, purge your conscience từ dead works to serve the living ĐCT? 15  Và for this cause he is the mediator of the new testament, that by means of death, for the redemption of the transgressions that were under the first testament, they which are called might receive lời hứa of đời đời inheritance. 16  For nơi a testament is, there must also of necessity bethe death of the testator. 17  For a testament is of force after men are dead: otherwise it is of no strength at all while the testator liveth. 18  Whereupon neither the first testament was dedicated without huyết. 19  For khi Môi-se had spoken every precept to all the people tùy theo luật pháp, he took the blood of calves Và of goats, với nước, Và scarlet wool, Và hyssop, andsprinkled both the book, Và all the people, 20  Nói rằng, This is the blood of the testament which ĐCT hath enjoined unto you. 21  Moreover he sprinkled with blood both the tabernacle, Và all the vessels of the ministry. 22  Và almost all things are by luật pháp purged với huyết; Và without shedding of blood is no remission. 23  It was therefore necessary that the patterns of things in các tầng trời should be purified with these; Nhưng the heavenly things themselves với better sacrifices than these. 24  For Christ is không entered into the holy places made with hands, which are the figures of the true; Nhưng into heaven itself, bây giờ to appear in the presence của ĐCT for us: 25  Nor yet that he should offer himself often, as thầy thượng phẩm entereth into the holy place every year với huyết of others; 26  For then phải he often have suffered since the foundation của thế gian: Nhưng bây giờ once in the end của thế gian hath he appeared to put away sin by the sacrifice of himself. 27  Và as it is appointed unto men once to die, Nhưng after this the judgment: 28  So Christ was once offered to bear the tội lỗi of many; Và với họ that look for him shall he appear the second time without sin unto sự cứu rỗi.
