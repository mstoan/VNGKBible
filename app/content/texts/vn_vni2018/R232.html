1  After these things, Và the establishment thereof, Sennacherib king of A-si-ri đến, Và entered into Giu-đa, Và encamped against the fenced cities, Và thought to win them for himself. 1 2  Và when Hezekiah nhìn thấy that Sennacherib was come, Và that he was purposed to fight against Giê-ru-sa-lem,2 3  He took counsel with his princes Và his mighty men to stop the waters of the fountains which were without the city: Và they did help him. 4  So there was gathered much people together, who stopped all the fountains, Và the brook that ran through the midst of the land, saying, Tại sao should the kings of A-si-ri come, Và find much nước? 3 5  Also he strengthened himself, and built up all the wall that was broken, and raised it up to the towers, Và another wall without, Và repaired Millo trong the city of Đa-vít, Và made darts Và shields in abundance. 4 6  Và he set captains of war over the people, Và gathered them together to him in the street of cổng of the city, Và spake comfortably to them, saying,5 7  Be strong Và courageous, be not afraid nor dismayed for vua of A-si-ri, nor for all đám đông that is with him: for there be more with us than with him: 8 With him is an arm of flesh; but with us is the Lord our God to help us, and to fight our battles. Và the people rested themselves upon the words of Hezekiah king of Giu-đa. 6 9  After this did Sennacherib king of A-si-ri send his servants to Giê-ru-sa-lem, (but he himself laid siege against Lachish, Và all his power with him,) unto Hezekiah king of Giu-đa, Và unto all Giu-đa that were at Giê-ru-sa-lem, saying,7 10  Thus nói Sennacherib king of A-si-ri, Whereon do ye trust, that ye abide in the siege in Giê-ru-sa-lem? 8 11  Doth not Hezekiah persuade you để cho over yourselves to die by famine Và by thirst, saying, The Lord our God shall deliver us out of tay of the king of A-si-ri? 12  Hath not the same Hezekiah taken away his high places Và his altars, Và commanded Giu-đa Và Giê-ru-sa-lem, saying, ngươi sẽ worship before một altar, Và burn incense upon it? 13  Know ye not what I and my fathers have done unto all the people of other lands? were the gods of the nations of those lands any ways able to deliver their lands out of mine hand? 14 Who was there among all the gods of those nations that my fathers utterly destroyed, that could deliver his people out of mine hand, that your God should be able to deliver you out of mine hand? 15  Now therefore let not Hezekiah deceive you, nor persuade you on this manner, neither yet believe him: for no god of any nation or kingdom was able to deliver his people out of mine hand, Và out of tay of my fathers: how much less shall your God deliver you out of mine hand? 16  Và his servants nói yet more against the Lord ĐCT, Và against his servant Hezekiah. 17  He wrote also letters to rail on the Lord ĐCT của I-sơ-ra-ên, Và to speak against him, saying, As the gods of the nations of other lands have not delivered their people out of mine hand, so shall not the God of Hezekiah deliver his people out of mine hand. 18  Then they cried with a loud tiếng in the Jews' speech unto the people of Giê-ru-sa-lem that were on the wall, to affright them, and to trouble them; that they might take the city. 19  Và they spake against the God of Giê-ru-sa-lem, as against the gods của dân sự of đất, which were the work of tays of man. 20 Và for this cause Hezekiah vua, Và the prophet Isaiah the son of Amoz, prayed Và cried to heaven. 21  Và the Lord sent một thiên sứ, which cut off all the mighty men of valour, Và the leaders Và captains in the camp of the king of A-si-ri. So he returned with shame of face to his own land. Và when he was come into nhà of his god, they that came forth of his own bowels giết him there with the thanh gươm. 9 22  Thus the Lord saved Hezekiah Và the inhabitants of Giê-ru-sa-lem from tay of Sennacherib vua of A-si-ri, Và from tay of all other, Và guided them on every side. 23  Và many brought gifts unto the Lord to Giê-ru-sa-lem, Và presents to Hezekiah king of Giu-đa: so that he was magnified in the sight of all nations from thenceforth. 10 24  In those days Hezekiah was sick to the death, Và prayed unto the Lord: Và he spake unto him, and he gave him a sign. 11 25  But Hezekiah rendered not again according to the benefit done unto him; for his heart was lifted up: therefore there was wrath upon him, and upon Giu-đa Và Giê-ru-sa-lem. 26  Notwithstanding Hezekiah humbled himself for the pride of his heart, both he and the inhabitants of Giê-ru-sa-lem, so that cơn thịnh nộ of the Lord đến not upon them in the days of Hezekiah. 12 27  Và Hezekiah had exceeding much riches Và honour: Và he made himself treasuries for silver, Và for gold, Và for precious stones, Và for spices, Và for shields, Và for all manner of pleasant jewels; 13 28  Storehouses also for the increase of corn, Và wine, Và oil; Và stalls for all manner of beasts, Và cotes for flocks. 29  Moreover he provided him cities, Và possessions of flocks Và herds in abundance: for God had given him substance very much. 30  This same Hezekiah also stopped the upper watercourse of Gihon, Và brought it straight down to phương tây side of the city of Đa-vít. Và Hezekiah prospered in all his works. 31  Howbeit in the business of the ambassadors of the princes of Ba-by-lôn, who sent unto him to enquire of the wonder that was done in the land, ĐCT left him, to try him, that he might know all that was in his heart. 14 32  Now the rest of the acts of Hezekiah, Và his goodness, kìa, they are written in the vision of Isaiah tiên tri, the son of Amoz, Và in the book of the kings of Giu-đa Và I-sơ-ra-ên. 15 33  Và Hezekiah slept with his fathers, Và they buried him in the chiefest of phần mộs của các con of Đa-vít: Và all Giu-đa Và the inhabitants of Giê-ru-sa-lem did him honour at his death. Và Manasseh his son reigned in his stead. 16
