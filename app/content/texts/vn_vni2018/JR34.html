34
1  The word which came unto Giê-rê-mi from the Lord, when Nebuchadnezzar vua of Ba-by-lôn, Và all his army, Và all the kingdoms of đất of his dominion, Và all the people, fought against Giê-ru-sa-lem, Và against all the cities thereof, saying,1 2  Thus nói the Lord, the God của I-sơ-ra-ên; Go Và speak to Zedekiah vua của Giu-đa, Và tell him, Thus nói the Lord; kìa, I will give this city into tay of the king of Ba-by-lôn, Và he shall burn it bằng lửa: 3  Và ngươi shalt not escape out of his hand, but shalt surely be taken, Và delivered into his hand; Và thine eyes shall kìa the eyes of the king of Ba-by-lôn, Và he shall speak with ngươi mouth to mouth, Và ngươi shalt go to Ba-by-lôn. 2 4  Yet hear the word of the Lord, O Zedekiah vua của Giu-đa; Thus nói the Lord of ngươi, ngươi shalt not die by the thanh gươm: 5  But ngươi shalt die in peace: Và with the burnings of thy fathers, the former kings which were before ngươi, so shall they burn odours for ngươi; and they will lament ngươi, nói [rằng], Ah lord! for I have pronounced the word, nói the Lord. 6  Then Giê-rê-mi tiên tri nói all these words unto Zedekiah vua của Giu-đa in Giê-ru-sa-lem, 7  When the king of Ba-by-lôn's army fought against Giê-ru-sa-lem, Và against all the cities của Giu-đa that were left, against Lachish, Và against Azekah: for these defenced các thành remained of the cities của Giu-đa. 8  This is the word that came unto Giê-rê-mi from the Lord, after that the king Zedekiah had made a covenant with all the people which were at Giê-ru-sa-lem, to proclaim liberty với họ; 9  That every man should let his manservant, Và every man his maidservant, being an Hebrew or an Hebrewess, go free; that none should serve himself of them, to wit, of a Jew his brother. 10  Now when all the princes, Và all the people, which had entered into the covenant, heard that every one should let his manservant, Và every one his maidservant, go free, that none should serve themselves of them any more, then they obeyed, and let họ go. 11  But afterward they turned, Và caused the servants Và taymaids, whom they had let go free, to return, Và brought them into subjection for servants Và for handmaids. 12  Therefore the word of the Lord came to Giê-rê-mi from the Lord, nói [rằng], 13  Thus nói the Lord, the God của I-sơ-ra-ên; I made a covenant with your fathers in the day that I brought them forth out of the land of Ai-Cập, out of nhà of bondmen, nói [rằng], 14  At the end of seven năm let ye go every man his brother an Hebrew, which hath been sold unto ngươi; and when he hath served ngươi six năm, ngươi shalt let him go free from ngươi: but your fathers hearkened not unto me, neither inclined their ear. 3 15  Và ye were now turned, Và had done right in my sight, in proclaiming liberty every man to his neighbour; Và ye had made a covenant before me in nhà which is called by my name: 4 5 16  But ye turned Và polluted my name, Và caused every man his servant, Và every man his handmaid, whom ye had set at liberty at their pleasure, to return, Và brought them into subjection, to be cùng các ngươi for servants Và for handmaids. 17  Therefore thus nói the Lord; Ye have not hearkened unto me, in proclaiming liberty, every one to his brother, Và every man to his neighbour: kìa, I proclaim a liberty for you, nói the Lord, to the thanh gươm, to the pestilence, Và to the famine; Và I will make you to be removed into all the kingdoms of đất. 6 18  Và I will give the men that have transgressed my covenant, which have not performed the words of the covenant which they had made before me, when they cut the calf in twain, Và passed between the parts thereof, 19  The princes của Giu-đa, Và the princes of Giê-ru-sa-lem, the eunuchs, Và the priests, Và all the people of the land, which passed between the parts of the calf; 20  I will even give them into tay of their enemies, Và into tay of them that seek their sự sống: Và their dead bodies shall be for meat unto the fowls of the heaven, Và to the beasts of đất. 21  Và Zedekiah vua của Giu-đa Và his princes will I give into tay of their enemies, Và into tay of them that seek their sự sống, Và into tay of the king of Ba-by-lôn's army, which are gone up from you. 22  kìa, I will command, nói the Lord, Và cause them to return to this city; Và they shall fight against it, and take it, and burn it bằng lửa: Và I will make the cities của Giu-đa a desolation without an inhabitant.
