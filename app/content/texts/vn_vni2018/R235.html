1  Moreover Josiah kept a passover unto the Lord in Jerusalem: Và they killed the passover on the mười bốnth day of the first month. 2  Và he set the priests in their charges, Và encouraged them to the service of nhà of the Lord, 3  và nói unto the Levites that taught all I-sơ-ra-ên, which were holy unto the Lord, Put the holy ark in nhà which Sa-lô-môn the son of Đa-vít king của I-sơ-ra-ên did build; it shall not be a burden upon your shoulders: serve now the Lord your God, Và his people I-sơ-ra-ên, 4  Và prepare yourselves by nhàs of your fathers, after your courses, according to the writing of Đa-vít king của I-sơ-ra-ên, Và according to the writing of Sa-lô-môn his son. 5  Và stand in the holy place according to the divisions of the families of the fathers of your brethren the people, and after the division of the families of the Levites. 1 2 6  So kill the passover, Và sanctify yourselves, and prepare your brethren, that they may do according to the word of the Lord by the hand of Môi-se. 7  Và Josiah gave to the people, of the flock, lambs Và kids, all for the passover offerings, for all that were present, to the number of thirty thousand, Và three thousand bullocks: these were of the king's substance. 3 8  Và his princes gave willingly unto the people, to the priests, Và to the Levites: Hilkiah Và Zechariah Và Jehiel, rulers of nhà của ĐCT, gave unto the priests for the passover offerings two thousand Và six trăm small cattle, Và three trăm oxen. 4 9  Conaniah also, and Shemaiah Và Nethaneel, his brethren, Và Hashabiah Và Jeiel Và Jozabad, chief of the Levites, gave unto the Levites for passover offerings năm thousand small cattle, Và five trăm oxen. 5 10  So the service was prepared, Và the priests stood in their place, Và the Levites in their courses, according to the king's mạng lệnh. 11  Và they killed the passover, Và the priests sprinkled the blood from their hands, Và the Levites flayed them. 12  Và they removed the burnt offerings, that they might give according to the divisions of the families of the people, to offer unto the Lord, as it is written in the book of Môi-se. And so did they with the oxen. 13  Và they roasted the passover with fire according to the ordinance: but the other holy offerings sod they in pots, Và in caldrons, Và in pans, and divided them speedily among all the people. 6 14  Và afterward they made ready for themselves, and for the priests: because the priests the sons of A-rôn were busied in offering of burnt offerings Và the fat until night; therefore the Levites prepared for themselves, and for the priests the sons of A-rôn. 15  Và the singers the sons of Asaph were in their place, according to the commandment of Đa-vít, Và Asaph, Và Heman, Và Jeduthun the king's seer; Và the porters waited at every gate; they might not depart from their service; for their brethren the Levites prepared for them. 7 16  So all the service of the Lord was prepared the same day, to keep the passover, Và to offer burnt offerings upon the altar of the Lord, according to the commandment of king Josiah. 17  Và the children của I-sơ-ra-ên that were present kept the passover at that time, Và the feast of unleavened bread bảy days. 8 18  Và there was no passover like to that kept in I-sơ-ra-ên from the days of Samuel the prophet; neither did all the kings của I-sơ-ra-ên keep such a passover as Josiah kept, Và the priests, Và the Levites, Và all Judah Và I-sơ-ra-ên that were present, Và the inhabitants of Jerusalem. 9 19  In the eighteenth year of the reign of Josiah was this passover kept. 20  After all this, when Josiah had prepared the temple, Necho king of Egypt came up to fight against Carchemish by Euphrates: Và Josiah went out against him. 10 21  But he sent ambassadors to him, saying, What have I to do with thee, thou king of Judah? I come not against thee this day, but against nhà wherewith I have war: for God commanded me to make haste: forbear thee from meddling with ĐCT, who is with me, that he destroy thee not. 11 22  Nevertheless Josiah would not turn his face from him, but disguised himself, that he might fight with him, and hearkened not unto the words of Necho from the mouth của ĐCT, Và came to fight in the valley of Megiddo. 23  Và the archers shot at king Josiah; Và the king nói to his servants, Have me away; for I am sore wounded. 12 24  His servants therefore took him out of that chariot, Và put him in the second chariot that he had; and they brought him to Jerusalem, Và he died, Và was buried in one of phần mộs of his fathers. Và all Judah Và Jerusalem mourned for Josiah. 13 25  Và Giê-rê-mi lamented for Josiah: Và all the singing men Và the singing women spake of Josiah in their lamentations to this day, Và made them an ordinance in I-sơ-ra-ên: and, kìa, they are written in the lamentations. 26  Now the rest of the acts of Josiah, Và his goodness, according to that which was written in the law of the Lord,14 27  Và his deeds, first Và last, kìa, they are written in the book of the kings của I-sơ-ra-ên Và Judah.
