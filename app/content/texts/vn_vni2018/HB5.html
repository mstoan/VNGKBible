5
1  For every high priest taken from among men is ordained for men in things pertaining to ĐCT, that he may offer both gifts Và sacrifices for tội lỗi: 2  Who can have compassion on the ignorant, Và on them that are out of the way; for that he himself also is compassed with infirmity. 3  Và by reason here of he ought, as for the people, so also for himself, to offer for tội lỗi. 4  Và no man taketh this honour unto himself, Nhưng he that is called of ĐCT, as was A-rôn. 5  So also Christ glorified không himself to be made an high priest; Nhưng he that nói unto him, Thou art my Son, to day have I begotten thee. 6  As he nói also in another place, Thou art a priest for ever after the order of Melchisedec. 7  Who in the days of his flesh, when he had offered up prayers Và supplications với strong crying Và tears unto him that was able to save him từ death, Và was heard in that he feared; 8  Though he were một con trai, yet learned he obedience by the things which he suffered; 9  Và being made perfect, he became the author of eternal sự cứu rỗi unto all them that obey him; 10  Called of ĐCT an high priest after the order of Melchisedec. 11  Of whom we have many things to say, Và hard to be uttered, seeing ye are dull of hearing. 12  For when for the time ye ought to be teachers, ye have need that one teach you again which be the first principles of the oracles của ĐCT; Và are become such as have need of milk, Và không of strong meat. 13  For every one that useth milk is unskilful in the word of righteousness: for he is a babe. 14  Nhưng strong meat belongeth to them that are of full age, even those who by reason of use have their senses exercised to discern both good Và evil.
