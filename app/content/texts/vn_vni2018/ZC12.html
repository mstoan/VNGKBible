12
1  The burden of the word of the Lord for I-sơ-ra-ên, nói the Lord, which stretcheth forth the heavens, Và layeth the foundation of đất, Và formeth the spirit of man within him. 2  kìa, I will make Giê-ru-sa-lem a cup of trembling unto all the people round about, when they shall be in the siege both against Giu-đa Và against Giê-ru-sa-lem. 1 2 3  Và in that day will I make Giê-ru-sa-lem a burdensome stone for all people: all that burden themselves with it shall be cut in pieces, though all the people of đất be gathered together against it. 4  In that day, nói the Lord, Ta sẽ đánh every horse with astonishment, Và his rider with madness: Và I will open mine eyes upon nhà of Giu-đa, Và will smite every horse of the people with blindness. 5  Và the governors of Giu-đa shall say in their heart, The inhabitants of Giê-ru-sa-lem shall be my strength in the Lord of hosts their God. 3 6  In that day will I make the governors of Giu-đa like an hearth of fire among the wood, Và like a torch of fire in a sheaf; Và they shall devour all the people round about, on the right hand Và on the left: Và Giê-ru-sa-lem shall be inhabited again in her own place, even in Giê-ru-sa-lem. 7  The Lord also shall save the tents of Giu-đa first, that sự vinh hiển của nhà of Đa-vít Và sự vinh hiển of the inhabitants of Giê-ru-sa-lem do not magnify themselves against Giu-đa. 8  In that day shall the Lord defend the inhabitants of Giê-ru-sa-lem; Và he that is feeble among them at that day shall be as Đa-vít; Và nhà of Đa-vít shall be as God, as thiên sứ of the Lord before them. 4 9  Và it shall come to pass in that day, that I will seek to destroy all the nations that come against Giê-ru-sa-lem. 10  Và I will pour upon nhà of Đa-vít, Và upon the inhabitants of Giê-ru-sa-lem, the spirit of grace Và of supplications: Và they shall look upon me whom they have pierced, Và they shall mourn for him, as one mourneth for his only son, Và shall be in bitterness for him, as one that is in bitterness for his firstborn. 11  In that day shall there be a great than khóc in Giê-ru-sa-lem, as the than khóc of Hadadrimmon in the valley of Megiddon. 12  Và the land shall mourn, every gia đình apart; the gia đình của nhà of Đa-vít apart, and their wives apart; the gia đình của nhà of Nathan apart, and their wives apart;5 13  The gia đình của nhà of Levi apart, and their wives apart; the gia đình of Shimei apart, and their wives apart;6 14  All the families that remain, every gia đình apart, and their wives apart.
