3
1  This is a true Nói rằng, nếu một người desire the office of a bishop, he desireth a good work. 2  A bishop then phải be blameless, chồng of one vợ, vigilant, sober, of good behaviour, given to hospitality, apt to teach; 3  Not given to wine, no striker, không greedy of filthy lucre; Nhưng patient, not a brawler, not covetous; 4  One that ruleth well his own nhà, having his children in subjection với all gravity; 5 ( For nếu một người know không how to rule his own nhà, how shall he take care của hội thánh của ĐCT?) 6  Not a novice, lest being lifted up with pride he fall into the condemnation of the devil. 7  Moreover he phải have a good report of them which are without; lest he fall into reproach Và the snare of the devil. 8  Likewise phải the deacons be grave, không doubletongued, không given to much rượu nho, không greedy of filthy lucre; 9  Holding the mystery of the faith in a pure conscience. 10  Và let these also first be proved; then let them use the office of a deacon, being found blameless. 11  Even so must their wives be grave, không slanderers, sober, faithful in mọi sự. 12  Let the deacons be chồngs of one vợ, ruling their children Và their own houses well. 13  For they that have used the office of a deacon well purchase to themselves a good degree, Và great boldness in the faith which is in Christ ĐCGiê-su. 14  These things write I cho ngươi, hoping to come unto thee shortly: 15  Nhưng nếu I tarry long, that thou mayest know how thou oughtest to behave thyself in nhà của ĐCT, which is hội thánh of the living ĐCT, the pillar Và ground of the truth. 16  Và without controversy great is the mystery of godliness: ĐCT was manifest in the flesh, justified in [Thánh] Linh, seen of angels, preached unto dân Ngoại, believed on in thế gian, received up into glory.
