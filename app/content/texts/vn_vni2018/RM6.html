6
1  What shall we say then? Shall we continue in sin, that ân-điển may abound? 2  God forbid. How shall we, that are dead to sin, live any longer therein? 3  Know ye not, rằng so many of us as were baptized into ĐCGiê-su Christ were baptized into his death? 4  Therefore we are buried with him by baptism into death: that like as Christ was raised up từ the dead by sự vinh hiển of the Father, even so chúng ta also should walk in newness of sự sống. 5  For nếu we have been planted together in the likeness of his death, we shall be also in the likeness of his resurrection: 6  Knowing this, rằng our old man is crucified with him, that xác of sin might be destroyed, that henceforth we should không serve sin. 7  For he that is dead is freed từ sin. 8  bây giờ nếu we be dead với Christ, we believe rằng we shall also live with him: 9  Knowing rằng Christ being raised từ the dead dieth không còn; death hath không còn dominion over him. 10  For in that he died, he died unto sin once: Nhưng in that he liveth, he liveth unto God. 11  Likewise reckon ye also yourselves to be dead indeed unto sin, Nhưng alive unto God through ĐCGiê-su Christ our Lord. 12  Let không sin therefore reign in your mortal body, that ye should vâng lời it in the lusts thereof. 13  Neither yield ye your members as instruments of unrighteousness unto sin: Nhưng yield yourselves unto God, as those that are alive từ the dead, Và your members as instruments of righteousness unto God. 14  For sin shall không have dominion over you: for ye are không under luật pháp, Nhưng under ân-điển. 15  What then? shall we sin, vì we are không under luật pháp, Nhưng under ân-điển? God forbid. 16  Know ye không, rằng to whom ye yield yourselves servants to vâng lời, his servants ye are to whom ye obey; hoặc of sin unto death, hay of obedience unto righteousness? 17  Nhưng ĐCT be thanked, rằng ye were the servants of sin, Nhưng ye have obeyed từ the heart that form of doctrine which was delivered you. 18  Being then made free từ sin, ye became the servants of righteousness. 19  I speak after the manner of men vì cớ the infirmity of your flesh: for as ye have yielded your members servants to uncleanness Và to iniquity unto iniquity; even so bây giờ yield your members servants to righteousness unto holiness. 20  For khi ye were the servants of sin, ye were free from righteousness. 21  What fruit had ye then in those things whereof ye are bây giờ ashamed? for the end of those things is death. 22  Nhưng bây giờ being made free từ sin, Và become servants to God, ye have your fruit unto holiness, Và the end đời đời sự sống. 23  Vì tiền công của tội lỗi là sự chết; nhưng món quà của ĐCT là sự sống đời đời qua ĐCGiê-su Christ Chúa chúng ta.
