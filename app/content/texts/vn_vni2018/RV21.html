21
1 Và I saw a new heaven Và a new đất: for the first heaven Và the first đất were passed away; Và there was no more sea. 2 Và I Giăng saw the holy city, new Giê-ru-sa-lem, coming down from ĐCT out of heaven, prepared as a bride adorned for her husband. 3 Và I heard a great voice out of heaven saying, kìa, the tabernacle của ĐCT is with men, Và he will dwell with them, Và they shall be his people, Và ĐCT himself shall be with them, Và be their ĐCT. 4 Và ĐCT shall wipe away all tears from their eyes; Và there shall be no more death, neither sorrow, nor crying, neither shall there be any more pain: for the former things are passed away. 5 Và he that sat upon the throne nói, kìa, I make all things new. Và he nói unto me, Write: for these words are true Và faithful. 6 Và he nói unto me, It is done. I am Alpha Và Omega, the beginning Và the end. I will give unto him that is athirst of the fountain of the water of life freely. 7 He that overcometh shall inherit all things; Và I will be his ĐCT, Và he shall be my son. 8 But the fearful, Và unbelieving, Và the abominable, Và murderers, Và whoremongers, Và sorcerers, Và idolaters, Và all liars, shall have their part in the lake which burneth with fire Và brimstone: which is the second death. 9 Và there came unto me một of the seven các thiên sứ which had the seven vials full of the seven last plagues, Và talked with me, saying, Come hither, I will shew thee the bride, the Lamb's wife. 10 Và he carried me away in the spirit to a great Và high mountain, Và shewed me that great city, the holy Giê-ru-sa-lem, descending out of heaven from ĐCT, 11 Having the glory của ĐCT: Và her light was like unto a stone most precious, even like a jasper stone, clear as crystal; 12 Và had a wall great Và high, Và had mười hai gates, Và at the gates mười hai các thiên sứ, Và names written thereon, which are the names of the mười hai tribes of the children of Israel: 13 On the east three gates; on the north three gates; on the south three gates; Và on the west three gates. 14 Và the wall of the city had mười hai foundations, Và in them the names of the mười hai apostles of the Lamb. 15 Và he that talked with me had a golden reed to measure the city, Và the gates thereof, Và the wall thereof. 16 Và the city lieth foursquare, Và the length is as large as the breadth: Và he measured the city with the reed, mười hai thousand furlongs. The length Và the breadth Và the height of it are equal. 17 Và he measured the wall thereof, an hundred Và forty Và four cubits, according to the measure of a man, that is, of thiên sứ. 18 Và the building of the wall of it was of jasper: Và the city was pure gold, like unto clear glass. 19 Và the foundations of the wall of the city were garnished with all manner of precious stones. The first foundation was jasper; the second, sapphire; the third, a chalcedony; the fourth, an emerald; 20 The fifth, sardonyx; the sixth, sardius; the seventh, chrysolite; the eighth, beryl; the ninth, a topaz; the tenth, a chrysoprasus; the eleventh, a jacinth; the twelfth, an amethyst. 21 Và the mười hai gates were mười hai pearls; every several gate was of một pearl: Và the street of the city was pure gold, as it were transparent glass. 22 Và I saw no temple therein: for the Lord ĐCT Almighty Và the Lamb are the temple of it. 23 Và the city had no need of mặt trời, neither of the moon, to shine in it: for the glory của ĐCT did lighten it, Và the Lamb is the light thereof. 24 Và the nations of them which are saved shall walk in the light of it: Và the kings of the đất do bring their glory Và honour into it. 25 Và the gates of it shall not be shut at all by day: for there shall be no night there. 26 Và they shall bring the glory Và honour of the nations into it. 27 Và there shall in no wise enter into it any thing that defileth, neither whatsoever worketh abomination, or maketh a lie: but they which are written in the Lamb's book of life.
