2
1 Và tôi, hỡi anh em, when I came to you, đến not with excellency of speech hay of wisdom, declaring unto you the testimony của ĐCT. 2 For I determined not to know any thing among you, save ĐCGiê-su Christ, Và him crucified. 3 Và I was with you in weakness, Và in fear, Và in much trembling. 4 Và my speech Và my preaching was not with enticing words of man's wisdom, Nhưng in demonstration of the Spirit Và of power: 5 That your đức tin should not stand in the wisdom of men, Nhưng in the power của ĐCT. 6 Howbeit we speak wisdom among them that are perfect: yet not the wisdom of this world, nor of the princes of this world, that come to nought: 7 Nhưng we speak the wisdom của ĐCT in a mystery, even the hidden wisdom, which ĐCT ordained before the world unto our glory: 8 Which none of the princes of this world knew: for had they known it, they would not have crucified the Lord of glory. 9 Nhưng as it is written, Eye hath not seen, nor ear heard, neither have entered into the heart of man, the things which ĐCT hath prepared for them that love him. 10 Nhưng ĐCT hath revealed them unto us by his Spirit: for the Spirit searcheth all things, yea, the deep things của ĐCT. 11 For what man knoweth the things of a man, save the spirit of man which is in him? even so the things của ĐCT knoweth không ai, Nhưng the Spirit của ĐCT. 12 bây giờ chúng ta have received, not the spirit of the world, Nhưng the spirit which is of ĐCT; that we might know the things that are freely given to us of ĐCT. 13 Which things also we speak, not in the words which man's wisdom teacheth, Nhưng which the Holy Ghost teacheth; comparing spiritual things with spiritual. 14 Nhưng the natural man receiveth not the things of the Spirit của ĐCT: for they are foolishness unto him: neither can he know them, because they are spiritually discerned. 15 Nhưng he that is spiritual judgeth all things, yet he himself is judged of không ai. 16 For who hath known the mind của Chúa, that he may instruct him? Nhưng chúng ta have the mind của Đấng Christ.
