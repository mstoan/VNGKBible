24
1 Now upon the first day of the week, rất sáng sớm, họ đi đến mộ phần, mang theo the spices which they had prepared, Và certain others with them. 2 Và they found the stone rolled away from phần mộ. 3 Và they entered in, Và found not the body của Chúa ĐCGiê-su. 4 Và xảy đến, as they were much perplexed thereabout, kìa, two men stood by them in shining garments: 5 Và as they were afraid, Và bowed down their faces to the đất, they nói unto them, Why seek ye the living among the dead? 6 He is not here, but is risen: remember how he spake unto you when he was yet in Ga-li-lê, 7 Saying, The Son of man must be delivered into the hands of sinful men, Và be crucified, Và the third day rise again. 8 Và họ nhớ lại những lời của Ngài, 9 Và returned from phần mộ, Và told all these things unto the eleven, Và to all the rest. 10 It was Ma-ri Mác-đơ-len, Và Joanna, Và Ma-ri the mother of Gia-cơ, Và other women that were with them, which told these things unto the apostles. 11 Và their words seemed to them as idle tales, Và they believed them not. 12 Then arose Phi-e-rơ, Và ran unto phần mộ; Và stooping down, he beheld the linen clothes laid by themselves, Và departed, wondering in himself at that which was come to pass.
13 Và, kìa, two of them went that same day to a village called Emmaus, which was from Giê-ru-sa-lem about threescore furlongs. 14 Và they talked together of all these things which had happened. 15 Và xảy đến, that, while they communed together Và reasoned, ĐCGiê-su himself drew near, Và went with them. 16 But their eyes were holden that they should not know him. 17 Và he nói unto them, What manner of communications are these that ye have one to another, as ye walk, Và are sad? 18 Và the one of them, whose name was Cleopas, answering nói unto him, Art thou only a stranger in Giê-ru-sa-lem, Và hast not known the things which are come to pass there in these days? 19 Và he nói unto them, What things? Và they nói unto him, Concerning ĐCGiê-su of Nazareth, which was a prophet mighty in deed Và word before ĐCT Và all the people: 20 Và how các thầy tế lễ Và our rulers delivered him to be condemned to death, Và have crucified him. 21 But chúng ta trusted that it had been he which should have redeemed Israel: Và beside all this, to day is the third day since these things were done. 22 Yea, Và certain women also of our company made us astonished, which were early at phần mộ; 23 Và when they found not his body, they came, saying, that they had also seen a vision of angels, which nói that he was alive. 24 Và certain of them which were with us went to phần mộ, Và found it even so as the women had nói: but him they saw not. 25 Then he nói unto them, O fools, Và slow of heart to believe all that the prophets have spoken: 26 Ought not Christ to have suffered these things, Và to enter into his glory? 27 Và beginning at Môi-se Và all the prophets, he expounded unto them in all the scriptures the things concerning himself. 28 Và they drew nigh unto the village, whither they went: Và he made as though he would have gone further. 29 But they constrained him, saying, Abide with us: for it is toward evening, Và the day is far spent. Và he went in to tarry with them. 30 Và xảy đến, as he sat at meat with them, he took bread, Và blessed it, Và brake, Và gave to them. 31 Và their eyes were opened, Và they knew him; Và he vanished out of their sight. 32 Và they nói one to another, Did not our heart burn within us, while he talked with us by the way, Và while he opened to us the scriptures? 33 Và they rose up the same hour, Và returned to Giê-ru-sa-lem, Và found the eleven gathered together, Và them that were with them, 34 Saying, The Lord is risen indeed, Và hath appeared to Si-môn. 35 Và they told what things were done in the way, Và how he was known of them in breaking of bread.
36 Và as they thus spake, ĐCGiê-su himself stood in the midst of them, Và nói unto them, Peace be unto you. 37 But they were terrified Và affrighted, Và supposed that they had seen a spirit. 38 Và he nói unto them, Why are ye troubled? Và why do thoughts Hãy chổi dậy in your hearts? 39 kìa my hands Và my feet, that it is I myself: handle me, Và see; for a spirit hath not flesh Và bones, as ye see me have. 40 Và when he had thus spoken, he shewed them his hands Và his feet. 41 Và while they yet believed not for joy, Và wondered, he nói unto them, Have ye here any meat? 42 Và they gave him a piece of a broiled fish, Và of an honeycomb. 43 Và he took it, Và did eat before them. 44 Và he nói unto them, These are the words which I spake unto you, while I was yet with you, that all things must be fulfilled, which were written in the law of Môi-se, Và in the prophets, Và in the psalms, concerning me. 45 Then opened he their understanding, that they might understand the scriptures, 46 Và nói unto them, Thus it is written, Và thus it behoved Christ to suffer, Và to rise from the dead the third day: 47 Và that repentance Và remission of tội lỗi should be preached in his name among all nations, beginning at Giê-ru-sa-lem. 48 Và ye are witnesses of these things.
49 Và, kìa, I send lời hứa of my Father upon you: but tarry ye in the city of Jerusalem, until ye be endued with power from on high.
50 Và he led them out as far as to Bê-tha-ni, Và he lifted up his hands, Và blessed them. 51 Và xảy đến, while he blessed them, he was parted from them, Và carried up into heaven. 52 Và they worshipped him, Và returned to Giê-ru-sa-lem with great joy: 53 Và were continually in the temple, praising Và blessing ĐCT. Amen.
