14
1 After two days was the feast of the passover, Và of unleavened bread: Và các thầy tế lễ Và những thầy chép luật sought how they might take him by craft, Và put him to death. 2 Nhưng they nói, Not on the feast day, lest there be an uproar of the people.
3 Và being in Bê-tha-ni in nhà of Si-môn the leper, as he sat at meat, there came một người đờn bà having an alabaster box of ointment of spikenard very precious; Và she brake the box, Và poured it on his head. 4 Và there were some that had indignation within themselves, Và nói, Why was this waste of the ointment made? 5 For it might have been sold for more than three hundred pence, Và have been given to the poor. Và they murmured against her. 6 Và ĐCGiê-su nói, Let her alone; why trouble ye her? she hath wrought a good work on me. 7 For ye have the poor with you always, Và whensoever ye will ye may do them good: Nhưng me ye have not always. 8 She hath done what she could: she is come aforehand to anoint my body to the burying. 9 Quả thật I say unto you, Wheresoever this gospel shall be preached throughout the whole world, this also that she hath done shall be spoken of for a memorial of her.
10 Và Giu-đa Ích-ca-ri-ốt, một of the mười hai, went unto các thầy tế lễ, to betray him unto them. 11 Và when they heard it, they were glad, Và promised to give him money. Và he sought how he might conveniently betray him.
12 Và the first day of unleavened bread, when they killed the passover, his disciples nói unto him, Where wilt thou that we go Và prepare that thou mayest eat the passover? 13 Và he sendeth forth two of his disciples, Và nói unto them, Go ye into the city, Và there shall meet you một người bearing a pitcher of water: follow him. 14 Và wheresoever he shall go in, say ye to the goodman of nhà, The Master nói, Where is the guestchamber, where I shall eat the passover with my disciples? 15 Và he will shew you a large upper room furnished Và prepared: there make ready for us. 16 Và his disciples went forth, Và đến into the city, Và found as he had nói unto them: Và they made ready the passover. 17 Và in the evening he cometh with the mười hai. 18 Và as they sat Và did eat, ĐCGiê-su nói, Quả thật I say unto you, một of you which eateth with me shall betray me. 19 Và they began to be sorrowful, Và to say unto him một by một, Is it I? Và another nói, Is it I? 20 Và he trả lời và nói unto them, It is một of the mười hai, that dippeth with me in the dish. 21 The Son of man indeed goeth, as it is written of him: Nhưng woe to that man by whom the Son of man is betrayed! good were it for that man nếu he had never been born.
22 Và as they did eat, ĐCGiê-su took bread, Và blessed, Và brake it, Và gave to them, Và nói, Take, eat: this is my body. 23 Và he took the cup, Và when he had given thanks, he gave it to them: Và they all drank of it. 24 Và he nói unto them, This is my blood of the new testament, which is shed for many. 25 Quả thật I say unto you, I will drink no more of the fruit of the vine, until that day that I drink it new in the kingdom của ĐCT.
26 Và when they had sung an hymn, they went out into the mount of Olives. 27 Và ĐCGiê-su nói unto them, All ye shall be offended because of me this night: for it is written, I will smite the shepherd, Và the sheep shall be scattered. 28 Nhưng after that I am risen, I will go before you into Ga-li-lê. 29 Nhưng Phi-e-rơ nói unto him, Although all shall be offended, yet will not I. 30 Và ĐCGiê-su nói unto him, Quả thật I say unto thee, That this day, even in this night, before the cock crow twice, thou shalt deny me thrice. 31 Nhưng he spake the more vehemently, nếu I should die with thee, I will not deny thee in any wise. Likewise also nói they all. 32 Và they came to a place which was named Ghết-sê-ma-nê: Và he nói to his disciples, Sit ye here, while I shall pray. 33 Và he taketh with him Phi-e-rơ Và Gia-cơ Và Giăng, Và began to be sore amazed, Và to be very heavy; 34 Và nói unto them, My soul is exceeding sorrowful unto death: tarry ye here, Và watch. 35 Và he went forward a little, Và fell on the ground, Và prayed that, nếu it were possible, the hour might pass from him. 36 Và he nói, Abba, Father, all things are possible unto thee; take away this cup from me: nevertheless not what I will, Nhưng what thou wilt. 37 Và he cometh, Và findeth them sleeping, Và nói unto Phi-e-rơ, Si-môn, sleepest thou? couldest not thou watch một hour? 38 Watch ye Và cầu nguyện, lest ye enter into temptation. The spirit truly is ready, Nhưng the flesh is weak. 39 Và again he went away, Và prayed, Và spake the same words. 40 Và when he returned, he found them asleep again, (for their eyes were heavy,) neither wist they what to answer him. 41 Và he cometh the third time, Và nói unto them, Sleep on bây giờ, Và take your rest: it is enough, the hour is come; kìa, the Son of man is betrayed into the hands of sinners. 42 Rise up, let us go; lo, he that betrayeth me is at hand.
43 Và tức thì, while he yet spake, cometh Giu-đa, một of the mười hai, Và with him a great multitude with những thanh gươm Và staves, from các thầy tế lễ Và những thầy chép luật Và the elders. 44 Và he that betrayed him had given them a token, saying, Whomsoever I shall kiss, that same is he; take him, Và lead him away safely. 45 Và as soon as he was come, he goeth tức thì to him, Và nói, Master, master; Và kissed him.
46 Và they laid their hands on him, Và took him. 47 Và một of them that stood by drew a thanh gươm, Và smote a servant of thầy thượng phẩm, Và cut off his ear. 48 Và ĐCGiê-su trả lời và nói unto them, Are ye come out, as against a thief, with những thanh gươm Và with staves to take me? 49 I was daily with you in the temple teaching, Và ye took me not: Nhưng the scriptures must be fulfilled. 50 Và they all forsook him, Và fled. 51 Và there followed him a certain young man, having a linen cloth cast about his naked body; Và the young men laid hold on him: 52 Và he left the linen cloth, Và fled from them naked.
53 Và they led ĐCGiê-su away to thầy thượng phẩm: Và with him were assembled all các thầy tế lễ Và the elders Và những thầy chép luật. 54 Và Phi-e-rơ followed him afar off, even into the palace of thầy thượng phẩm: Và he sat with the servants, Và warmed himself at the fire. 55 Và các thầy tế lễ Và all the council sought for witness against ĐCGiê-su to put him to death; Và found none. 56 For many bare false witness against him, Nhưng their witness agreed not together. 57 Và there arose certain, Và bare false witness against him, saying, 58 chúng ta heard him say, I will destroy this temple that is made with hands, Và within ba days I will build another made without hands. 59 Nhưng neither so did their witness agree together. 60 Và thầy thượng phẩm stood up in the midst, Và asked ĐCGiê-su, saying, Answerest thou nothing? what is it which these witness against thee? 61 Nhưng he held his peace, Và trả lời nothing. Again thầy thượng phẩm asked him, Và nói unto him, Art thou the Christ, the Son of the Blessed? 62 Và ĐCGiê-su nói, I am: Và ye shall see the Son of man sitting on the right hand of power, Và coming in the clouds of heaven. 63 Then thầy thượng phẩm rent his clothes, Và nói, What need we any further witnesses? 64 Ye have heard the blasphemy: what think ye? Và they all condemned him to be guilty of death. 65 Và some began to spit on him, Và to cover his face, Và to buffet him, Và to say unto him, Prophesy: Và the servants did strike him with the palms of their hands.
66 Và as Phi-e-rơ was beneath in the palace, there cometh một of the maids of thầy thượng phẩm: 67 Và when she saw Phi-e-rơ warming himself, she looked upon him, và nói, Và thou also wast with ĐCGiê-su of Nazareth. 68 Nhưng he denied, saying, I know not, neither understand I what thou sayest. Và he went out into the porch; Và the cock crew. 69 Và a maid saw him again, Và began to say to them that stood by, This is one of them. 70 Và he denied it again. Và a little after, they that stood by nói again to Phi-e-rơ, Surely thou art one of them: for thou art a Galilaean, Và thy speech agreeth thereto. 71 Nhưng he began to curse Và to swear, saying, I know not this man of whom ye speak. 72 Và the second time the cock crew. Và Phi-e-rơ called to mind the word that ĐCGiê-su nói unto him, Before the cock crow twice, thou shalt deny me thrice. Và when he thought thereon, he wept.
