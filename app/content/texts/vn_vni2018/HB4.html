4
1 Let us therefore fear, lest, a promise being left us of entering into his rest, any of you should seem to come short of it. 2 For unto us was phúc-âm preached, as well as unto them: but the word preached did not profit them, not being mixed with faith in them that heard it. 3 For we which have believed do enter into rest, as he nói, As I have sworn in my wrath, nếu they shall enter into my rest: although the works were finished from the foundation of the world. 4 For he spake in a certain place of the seventh day on this wise, Và ĐCT did rest the seventh day from all his works. 5 Và in this place again, nếu they shall enter into my rest. 6 Seeing therefore it remaineth that some must enter therein, Và they to whom it was first preached entered not in because of unbelief: 7 Again, he limiteth a certain day, saying in Đa-vít, To day, after so long a time; as it is nói, To day nếu ye will hear his voice, harden not your hearts. 8 For nếu ĐCGiê-su had given them rest, then would he not afterward have spoken of another day. 9 There remaineth therefore a rest to the people của ĐCT. 10 For he that is entered into his rest, he also hath ceased from his own works, as ĐCT did from his. 11 Let us labour therefore to enter into that rest, lest any man fall after the same example of unbelief. 12  Vì lời của ĐCT là sống, và năng quyền, và sắc bén hơn mọi gươm hai-lưỡi, và xuyên tận phân chia hồn và linh, và cốt và tủy, và xét định tư tưởng và ý định trong lòng. 13  Neither is there any creature that is not manifest in his sight: but all things are naked Và opened unto the eyes of him with whom we have to do. 14 Seeing then that we have a great high priest, that is passed into the heavens, ĐCGiê-su the Son của ĐCT, let us hold fast our profession. 15 For we have not an high priest which cannot be touched with the feeling of our infirmities; but was in all points tempted like as we are, yet without sin. 16 Let us therefore come boldly unto the throne of grace, that we may obtain mercy, Và find grace to help in time of need.
