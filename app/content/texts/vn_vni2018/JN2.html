2
1  Và the third day there was a marriage in Cana of Ga-li-lê; Và the mother của ĐCGiê-su was there: 2  Và both ĐCGiê-su was called, Và his disciples, to the marriage. 3  Và when they wanted wine, the mother của ĐCGiê-su nói unto him, They have no wine. 4  ĐCGiê-su nói unto her, Woman, what have I to do with thee? mine hour is not yet come. 5  His mẹ nói unto the servants, Whatsoever he nói unto you, do it. 6  Và there were set there six waterpots of stone, after the manner of the purifying of the Jews, containing hai hay ba firkins apiece. 7  ĐCGiê-su nói unto them, Fill the waterpots with water. Và they filled them up to the brim. 8  Và he nói unto them, Draw out bây giờ, Và bear unto thống đốc of the feast. Và they bare it. 9  When the ruler of the feast had tasted the water that was made wine, Và knew not whence it was: (Nhưng the servants which drew the water knew;) thống đốc of the feast called the bridegroom, 10  Và nói unto him, Every man at the beginning doth set forth good wine; Và when men have well drunk, then that which is worse: but thou hast kept the good wine until bây giờ. 11  This beginning of miracles did ĐCGiê-su in Cana of Ga-li-lê, Và manifested forth his glory; Và his disciples believed on him. 12  After this he went down to Ca-bê-na-um, he, Và his mẹ, Và his anh em, Và his disciples: Và they continued there not many days. 13  Và the Jews' passover was at hand, Và ĐCGiê-su went up to Giê-ru-sa-lem, 14  Và found in the temple those that sold oxen Và sheep Và doves, Và the changers of money sitting: 15  Và when he had made a scourge of small cords, he drove them all out of the temple, Và the sheep, Và the oxen; Và poured out the changers' money, Và overthrew the tables; 16  Và nói unto them that sold doves, Take these things hence; make not my Father's nhà an house of merchandise. 17  Và his disciples remembered rằng it was written, The zeal of thine nhà hath eaten me up. 18  Then trả lời những người Do Thái Và nói unto him, What sign shewest thou unto us, seeing that thou doest these things? 19  ĐCGiê-su trả lời Và nói unto them, Destroy this temple, Và in ba days I will raise it up. 20  Then nói những người Do Thái, Forty Và six years was this temple in building, Và wilt thou rear it up in ba days? 21  Nhưng he spake of the temple of his body. 22  When therefore he was risen from the dead, his disciples remembered rằng he had nói this unto them; Và they believed the scripture, Và the word which ĐCGiê-su had nói. 23  bây giờ when he was in Giê-ru-sa-lem at the passover, in the feast day, many believed in his tên, when they saw the miracles which he did. 24  Nhưng ĐCGiê-su did not commit himself unto them, because he knew all men, 25  Và needed not that any should testify of man: for he knew what was in man.
