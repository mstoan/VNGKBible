9
1  For as touching the ministering to thánh đồ, it is superfluous for me to write to you: 2  For I know the forwardness of your mind, for which I boast of you to them of Macedonia, rằng Achaia was ready a year ago; Và your zeal hath provoked very many. 3  Yet have I sent the brethren, lest our boasting of you should be in vain in this behalf; that, as tôi nói, ye may be ready: 4  Lest haply nếu they of Macedonia come với me, Và find you unprepared, chúng ta (that we say không, ye) should be ashamed in this same confident boasting. 5  Therefore I thought it necessary to exhort the brethren, that they would go before unto you, Và make up beforehand your bounty, whereof ye had notice before, that the same might be ready, as a matter of bounty, Và không as of covetousness. 6  Nhưng this I say, He which soweth sparingly shall reap also sparingly; Và he which soweth bountifully shall reap also bountifully. 7  Every man according as he purposeth in his heart, so let him give; không grudgingly, hay of necessity: for ĐCT loveth a cheerful giver. 8  Và ĐCT is able to makeall ân-điển abound toward you; that ye, always having all sufficiency in all things, may abound to every good work: 9 (As it is written, He hath dispersed abroad; he hath given to the poor: his righteousness remaineth for ever. 10  bây giờ he that ministereth seed to the sower both minister bánh for your food, Và multiply your seed sown, Và increase the fruits of your righteousness;) 11  Being enriched in every thing to all bountifulness, which causeth through us thanksgiving to God. 12  For the administration of this service không only supplieth the want of the saints, Nhưng is abundant also by many thanksgivings unto God; 13  Whiles by the experiment of this ministration they glorify ĐCT for your professed subjection unto phúc-âm của Đấng Christ, Và for your liberal distribution unto họ, Và unto all men; 14  Và by their prayer for you, which long after you for the exceeding ân-điển của ĐCT in you. 15  Thanks be unto ĐCT for his unspeakable gift.
