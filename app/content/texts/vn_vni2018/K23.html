3
1  Now Jehoram the son of Ahab began to reign over I-sơ-ra-ên in Samaria the eighteenth year of Jehoshaphat king of Judah, Và reigned mười hai years. 2  Và he wrought evil in the sight of the Lord; but not like his father, Và like his mother: for he put away the image of Baal that his father had made. 1 3  Nevertheless he cleaved unto the tội lỗi of Jeroboam the son of Nebat, which made I-sơ-ra-ên to sin; he departed not therefrom. 4  Và Mesha king of Moab was a sheepmaster, Và rendered unto the king của I-sơ-ra-ên an hundred thousand lambs, Và an hundred thousand rams, with the wool. 5  But xảy đến, when Ahab was dead, that the king of Moab rebelled against the king của I-sơ-ra-ên. 6  Và king Jehoram went out of Samaria the same time, Và numbered all I-sơ-ra-ên. 7  Và he went Và sent to Jehoshaphat the king of Judah, saying, The king of Moab hath rebelled against me: wilt thou go with me against Moab to battle? Và he nói, I will go up: I am as thou art, my people as thy people, Và my horses as thy horses. 8  Và he nói, Which way shall we go up? Và he trả lời, The way through the wilderness of Edom. 9  So the king của I-sơ-ra-ên went, Và the king of Judah, Và the king of Edom: Và they fetched a compass of seven days' journey: Và there was no water for the host, Và for the cattle that followed them. 2 10  Và the king của I-sơ-ra-ên nói, Alas! that the Lord hath called these three kings together, to deliver them into the hand of Moab! 11  But Jehoshaphat nói, Is there not here a prophet of the Lord, that we may enquire of the Lord by him? And one of the king của I-sơ-ra-ên's servants trả lời và nói, Here is Elisha the son of Shaphat, which poured nước on the hands of Elijah. 12  Và Jehoshaphat nói, The word of the Lord is with him. So the king của I-sơ-ra-ên Và Jehoshaphat Và the king of Edom went down to him. 13  Và Elisha nói unto the king của I-sơ-ra-ên, What have I to do with thee? get thee to the prophets of thy father, Và to the prophets of thy mother. Và the king của I-sơ-ra-ên nói unto him, Nay: for the Lord hath called these three kings together, to deliver them into the hand of Moab. 14  Và Elisha nói, As the Lord of hosts liveth, before whom I stand, surely, were it not that I regard the presence of Jehoshaphat the king of Judah, I would not look toward thee, nor see thee. 15  But now bring me a minstrel. Và xảy đến, when the minstrel played, that the hand of the Lord came upon him. 16  Và he nói, Thus nói the Lord, Make this valley full of ditches. 17  For thus nói the Lord, Ye shall not see wind, neither họ sẽ thấy mưa; yet that valley shall be filled with water, that ye may drink, both ye, and your cattle, Và your beasts. 18 Và this is but a light thing in the sight of the Lord: he will deliver the Moabites also into your hand. 19  Và ye shall smite every fenced city, Và every choice city, Và shall fell every good tree, Và stop all wells of water, Và mar every good piece of land with stones. 3 20  Và xảy đến in the morning, when the meat offering was offered, that, kìa, there came nước by the way of Edom, Và the country was filled with water. 21  Và when all the Moabites heard that the kings were come up to fight against them, they gathered all that were able to put on armour, Và upward, Và stood in the border. 4 5 22  Và they rose up early in the morning, Và mặt trời shone upon the water, Và the Moabites saw the water on the other side as red as blood: 23  Và they nói, This is blood: the kings are surely slain, Và they have smitten một another: now therefore, Moab, to the spoil. 6 24  Và when they came to the camp của I-sơ-ra-ên, the I-sơ-ra-ênites rose up Và smote the Moabites, so that they fled before them: but they went forward smiting the Moabites, even in their country. 7 25  Và they beat down the cities, Và on every good piece of land cast every man his stone, Và filled it; and they stopped all the wells of water, Và felled all the good trees: only in Kir–haraseth left they the stones thereof; howbeit the slingers went about it, Và smote it. 8 26  Và when the king of Moab saw that the battle was too sore for him, he took with him seven trăm men that drew những thanh gươm, to break through even unto the king of Edom: but they could not. 27  Then he took his eldest son that should have reigned in his stead, and offered him for a burnt offering upon the wall. Và there was great indignation against I-sơ-ra-ên: Và they departed from him, and returned to their own land.
