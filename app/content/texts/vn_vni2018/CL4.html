4
1  Masters, give unto your servants that which is just Và equal; knowing rằng ye also have a Master in heaven. 2  Continue in prayer, Và watch in the same với thanksgiving; 3  Withal praying also for us, that ĐCT would open unto us a door of utterance, to speak the mystery của Đấng Christ, for which I am also in bonds: 4  That I may make it manifest, as I ought to speak. 5  Walk in wisdom toward them that are without, redeeming the time. 6  Let your speech be alway với ân-điển, seasoned with salt, that ye may know how ye ought to answer every man. 7  All my state shall Tychicus declare unto you, who is a beloved brother, Và a faithful minister Và fellowservant in the Lord: 8  Whom I have sent unto you for the same purpose, that he might know your estate, Và comfort your hearts; 9  With Onesimus, a faithful Và beloved brother, who is one of you. They shall make known unto you all things which are done here. 10  Aristarchus my fellowprisoner saluteth you, Và Marcus, sister's son to Ba-na-ba, ( touching whom ye received commandments: nếu he come unto you, receive him;) 11  Và ĐCGiê-su, which is called Justus, who are of the circumcision. These only are my fellowworkers unto vương quốc của ĐCT, which have been a comfort unto me. 12  Epaphras, who is one of you, a servant của Đấng Christ, saluteth you, always labouring fervently for you in prayers, that ye may stand trọn vẹn Và complete in all the will của ĐCT. 13  For I bear him record, rằng he hath a great zeal for you, Và them that are in Laodicea, Và them in Hierapolis. 14  Lu-ca, the beloved physician, Và Demas, greet you. 15  Salute the brethren which arein Laodicea, Và Nymphas, Và hội thánh which isin his nhà. 16  Và when this epistle is read among you, cause that it be read also in hội thánh of the Laodiceans; Và that ye likewise read the epistle từ Laodicea. 17  Và say to Archippus, Hãy thận trọng to the ministry which thou hast received in the Lord, that thou fulfil it. 18  The salutation by tay of me Phao-lô. Remember my bonds. ân-điển be với you. Amen.
