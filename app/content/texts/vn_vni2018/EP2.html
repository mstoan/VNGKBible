2
1 Và you hath he quickened, who were dead in trespasses Và tội lỗi; 2 Wherein in time past ye walked according to the course of this world, according to the prince of the power of the air, the spirit that now worketh in the children of disobedience: 3 Among whom also chúng ta all had our conversation in times past in the lusts of our flesh, fulfilling the desires of the flesh Và of the mind; Và were by nature the children of wrath, even as others. 4 Nhưng ĐCT, who is rich in mercy, for his great love wherewith he loved us, 5 Even when we were dead in tội lỗi, hath quickened us together with Christ, (by grace ye are saved;) 6 Và hath raised us up together, Và made us sit together in heavenly places in Christ ĐCGiê-su: 7 That in the ages to come he might shew the exceeding riches of his ân-điển in his kindness toward us through Christ ĐCGiê-su. 8  Ấy là nhờ ân điển anh em được cứu qua đức tin; Và đây không phải từ anh em: nhưng là món quà của ĐCT: 9 không bởi việc làm, [để] không ai có thể khoe mình [được]. 10  Vì chúng ta là công việc của Ngài, [là] những kẻ được dựng nên trong ĐCGiê-su Christ cho công việc lành, [là] những việc ĐCT đã sắm sẳn hầu cho theo đó [mà] chúng ta tiến hành . 11 Vậy hãy nhớ lại, rằng ye being in time past Dân Ngoại trong xác thịt, who are called Uncircumcision by that which is called the Circumcision in the flesh made by hands; 12  That at that time ye were without Christ, being aliens from the commonwealth of Israel, Và strangers from the covenants of promise, having no hy vọng, Và without God in the world: 13 Nhưng now in Christ ĐCGiê-su ye who sometimes were far off are made nigh by the blood của Đấng Christ. 14  For he is our peace, who hath made both một, Và hath broken down the middle wall of partition between us; 15  Having abolished in his flesh the enmity, even the law of commandments contained in ordinances; for to make in himself of twain một new man, so making peace; 16 Và that he might reconcile both unto God in một body by thập-tự-giá, having slain the enmity thereby: 17 Và đến Và preached peace to you which were afar off, Và to them that were nigh. 18 For through him we both have access by một Spirit unto the Father. 19 Now therefore ye are no more strangers Và foreigners, Nhưng fellowcitizens with the saints, Và of nhàhold của ĐCT; 20 Và are built upon the foundation of the apostles Và prophets, ĐCGiê-su Christ himself being the chief corner stone; 21 In whom all the building fitly framed together groweth unto an holy temple in the Lord: 22 In whom ye also are builded together for an habitation của ĐCT through the Spirit.
